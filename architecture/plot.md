A plot file is the casting of forces before a word is spoken. It does not describe what will happen; it loads the specific system cargo that makes a collision inevitable the moment the plot starts.

- **C**ue: The framework's whisper. The atmospheric condition: the sediment of history, the chill of the geopolitical climate, the angle of the light. It establishes the baseline Place and the ambient pressure before any element is activated.

- **A**ction: The visible engine. The public vectors. This codifies the Process and official stance each Persona must openly drive toward, mapping exactly where those trajectories collide.

- **S**tage: The assets and arrangement. The physical deployment of Personas and the Pieces they carry across the threshold: documents, resources, technical infrastructure.

- **T**ension: The invisible engine. The contraband reality. What a Persona carries alone that makes the Action impossible to complete cleanly: the knowledge, the lie, the inertia.

---

Cue and Stage are the setup; the conditions and the deployment. Action and Tension are the twin pistons of the engine. One operates in the light; one operates in the dark. A plot file succeeds when the Action forces the system to move, but the Tension prevents it from moving smoothly.

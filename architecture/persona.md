A persona is a specific human carrying a formation they did not choose and cannot fully see.

- **P**rojection: What the persona presents to the room: the role, the posture, the read the room receives before anyone speaks.

- **A**ction: The one move the persona characteristically makes. Produces a Cue. The formation visible in the ordinary.

- **S**hadow: What the persona cannot see about themselves. Not a flaw. The structural consequence of the formation. Without it there is no pressure worth the name.

- **T**ell: The involuntary physical signal. Distinct from Action. It leaks when the formation is under load.

---

Projection and Shadow are the visible and the blind. Action and Tell are the deliberate and the involuntary. A persona file succeeds when the Shadow is specific enough that the Tell is inevitable.

A piece is an object or fact that carries more weight than it shows.

* **P**lace: The Place that holds the piece at this moment. Not where it has been. Where it is.

* **L**oad Bearing: What collapses if the piece is removed.

* **A**pparent: What the piece looks like from outside: the surface, the weight, the visible mark it leaves.

* **Y**earbook: Everywhere the piece has been and everything it has done. The accumulated history that makes the load bearing invisible until it fails.

---

Place and Yearbook are the temporal frame. Load Bearing and Apparent are the structural pair. A piece file succeeds when the Yearbook is long enough that the Load Bearing is felt before it is named.

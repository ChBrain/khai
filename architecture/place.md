A place is a pressure environment. It acts on everything inside it before anyone speaks.

* **S**hown: What the place presents on arrival: the light, the layout, the atmosphere that arrives before the first word.

* **H**olds: What the place anchors. The Pieces fixed here, the history that does not leave when the people do.

* **O**ffers: What becomes available inside this place that would not be available elsewhere.

* **W**ithheld: What the place will not release without the right pressure. Not hidden. Structural. It is there and it does not move.

---

Shown and Withheld are the pressure pair. Holds and Offers are the resource pair. A place file succeeds when the Withheld is real enough that the pressure on everything Shown is felt before it is named.

# Position: Tragic, Distress

## Taxonomy

[Tragic](process_tragic.md), the state where the distance collapses and the
fall is taken as real.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: tragic

## Has

Pity and fear at full pressure and no remove to bear them from. The
[empathy](@chbrain/khai-engine-empathy/position_empathy.md) channel is wide
open and the
[reversal](@chbrain/khai-engine-reversal/process_reversal.md) reads as a turn
that could take the witness next, but the distance that would make the
suffering another's has gone: the witness takes the
[grief](@chbrain/khai-engine-grief/process_grief.md) as their own and the
fiction as a fact. This is the embracing mechanism firing with the distancing
mechanism failed, the near edge of the tragic where pity and fear arrive
without the safety that converts them, so nothing is clarified and the
compound stays raw.

## Orders

Turn away, or be overwhelmed. Distress demands the one thing the tragic is
meant to suspend, self-preservation: the witness must either break the channel
and stop taking the suffering in, or hold it open and be flooded by a grief
that is not theirs to carry. The order is to escape a pain that has lost its
frame, so the witness looks away, leaves the room, refuses the story, or is
harrowed by it without release, and either way the crossing to catharsis does
not run.

## Loses

The pleasure the tragic alone can give, and the clarity with it. Where the
completed crossing discharges pity and fear, distress only accumulates them;
the witness forfeits the conversion Hume described and is left with the
disagreeable passions unturned. They lose, too, the encounter itself: because
the suffering has become unbearable it must be fled, so the fall that could
have been borne and understood is instead avoided, and the tragic teaches
nothing because it was never survived.

## Drives

Toward a distance regained, or the story abandoned. The witness recovers the
remove, is reminded the suffering is another's and the fiction a fiction, and
the crossing to catharsis reopens; or the distance cannot be regained and the
production is refused, the book closed, the theatre left, the channel shut to
keep the flood out. A production stages the distress as a witness genuinely
unable to hold the fall at arm's length, and reads the drama in whether the
remove returns or the tragic is fled before it can complete.

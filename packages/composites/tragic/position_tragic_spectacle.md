# Position: Tragic, Spectacle

## Taxonomy

[Tragic](process_tragic.md), the state where the engagement never lands and
the fall is watched cold.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: tragic

## Has

A [reversal](@chbrain/khai-engine-reversal/process_reversal.md) in full view
and no feeling attached to it. The fortune-to-misfortune turn is legible, the
fall is seen, but the
[empathy](@chbrain/khai-engine-empathy/position_empathy.md) channel never
opens onto it, so the
[grief](@chbrain/khai-engine-grief/process_grief.md) the fall inflicts stays
the sufferer's alone and never registers in the witness as pity, nor the turn
as fear. This is the distancing mechanism holding with the embracing mechanism
failed, the far edge of the tragic where the remove is total: the fall is
watched as one watches weather, an event that happens to no one who matters.

## Orders

Watch, and remain untouched. Spectacle asks nothing of the witness but
attendance; the fall unfolds and is registered as fact without becoming
feeling, so the witness follows the reversal, notes its shape, and is not
moved by it. The order is to keep the suffering wholly other, not held at the
one remove that lets it in as pity but pushed to the distance where it cannot
be a claim on the witness at all, and the tragic, requiring the embrace it is
denied, does not begin.

## Loses

The pity and fear that are the tragic, and the catharsis they cross into.
Where distress has too much of the compound and spectacle has none, both
forfeit the completed crossing, but spectacle forfeits it at the opposite
edge: the witness keeps the safety and loses the stakes, watches undeserved
suffering and feels no fellow-suffering, sees the turn and does not read it
onto themselves. They lose the reason the fall was staged, which was never to
be observed but to be undergone at a remove, and the observed fall clarifies
nothing because nothing of the witness was ever engaged.

## Drives

Toward an engagement that lands, or a fall watched to no effect. The witness
lets the channel open, the suffering registers as pity and the turn as fear,
and the crossing to catharsis begins; or the distance stays total and the
production plays to a witness who followed every beat and felt none of them, a
tragedy watched as spectacle and spent as spectacle. A production stages the
spectacle as a fall genuinely seen and genuinely not felt, and reads the drama
in whether the witness is ever reached or the tragic passes as a sequence of
events behind glass.

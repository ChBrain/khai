# Tragic

The tragic as composite: the emotion of witnessing an undeserved fall -- a loss (grief) taken in (empathy) and shaped by a turn from fortune to misfortune (reversal), the pity and fear that compound answers with, and the crossing it makes into catharsis when the distance holds -- and the drama lives at the two edges where the crossing fails, taken too near as distress and too far as spectacle.

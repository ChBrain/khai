# Moral Account: Reference

## Line of Work

The moral account as **composite**: one wrong inside a bond opens one
account held in three ledgers at once, and no single atom engine sees the
whole of it. The transgressor's ledger is the guilt engine's, whole; the
dyad's is the repair engine's; the wronged party's is the forgiveness
engine's, with the betrayal engine carrying the reckoning where the wrong
cut a trusted bond. This package authors none of those readings again. It
declares the four engines as dependencies, wires their members by hard
package links, and adds only what the atoms cannot hold: the three bridges
where one ledger crosses into another, and the root that reads the refusal
of the ledgers to agree.

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of the exchange between the parties, the apology as a social
circuit, the account as a remedial move, the empathy that shifts a stance,
where each atom's own REFERENCES carries the primary science of its corner.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Every corner of the triangle**: The transgressor's crossing, weight,
  proneness, and exits are the guilt engine's; the dyad's rupture, account
  forms, restitution, integration, and stored regard are the repair
  engine's; the wronged party's forms, stances, and forgivingness are the
  forgiveness engine's; the violation's kinds, blindness, and reckoning are
  the betrayal engine's. The composite restates none of them; a bridge
  links, it never re-authors.
- **The bond itself**: What the parties are to each other, and what the
  relationship is worth, is the play's casting and the trust engine's
  standing read; the composite takes the bond as given and reads the
  account that runs through it.
- **The moral verdict**: Whether the wrong was wrong, and how gravely, is
  the moral-judgment engine's and the production's; the composite prices
  ledgers, it does not judge acts.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the three-ledger read) with three bridges,
each wired by hard package links into the atoms it spans.

- **[moral account](process_moral_account.md)** (the root): one wrong, one
  account, three ledgers that refuse to agree; the circuit no party
  completes alone. Anchored by Tavuchis (the apology as social exchange).
- **[account, offered](process_account_offered.md)** (bridge,
  guilt to repair): the transgressor's exit road decides which account form
  reaches the dyad's door, or whether any does; payment without delivery is
  the standing gap. Anchored by Goffman (the remedial move) over the guilt
  and repair members it links.
- **[account, received](process_account_received.md)** (bridge, repair to
  forgiveness and betrayal): the offer lands against stance, stored regard,
  and blindness, none of which the giver controls; empathy is the one force
  that moves the filters. Anchored by Lazare (the apology as received) and
  McCullough, Worthington & Rachal (the empathy mechanism).
- **[account, closed](process_account_closed.md)** (bridge, the three
  closes): each ledger closes by its own engine's rules and the close does
  not travel; every part-closed state is a standing dramatic engine.
  Anchored by Tavuchis (the circuit that completes only in exchange).

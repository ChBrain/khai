# Process: Account, Closed

## Taxonomy

[Moral Account](process_moral_account.md), the bridge where the three
ledgers close, separately or not at all.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-account

## Initiated by

One of the ledgers reaches its own close, and the close does not travel.
The wronged party's release is unilateral: the forgiveness engine's
[decisional](@chbrain/khai-engine-forgiveness/process_decisional.md) or
[emotional](@chbrain/khai-engine-forgiveness/process_emotional.md) close
asks nothing of the offender and rebuilds nothing, so a grievance can be
fully released while the dyad's
[integration](@chbrain/khai-engine-repair/process_integration.md) never
runs and the transgressor's
[carried](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/position_culpable_carried.md) weight
never lifts. Each engine closes its own book by its own rules; this bridge
reads which books are closed, and what the mismatch is doing.

## Direction

Toward one of the part-closed states, almost never toward all-closed.
Forgiven but unrepaired: the wronged party is done and the dyad still
carries the rupture. Repaired but unforgiven: the relationship functions
again over a grievance still
[withheld](@chbrain/khai-engine-forgiveness/process_withheld.md). Paid but
never received: the transgressor's ledger is empty and nobody else's is.
Resolved but unreleased: the wronged party's
[resolution](@chbrain/khai-engine-betrayal/process_resolution.md) has
settled what the violation meant while the debt itself stays owed. Each
state is stable enough to build a life, or a play, on.

## Lever

A close in one ledger repricing another. The release the wronged party
grants can reopen the transgressor's ledger, mercy received as a verdict
on how much there was to forgive; the dyad's successful repair can leave
the wronged party's private grievance suddenly illegitimate, held against
a relationship that has officially moved on; a transgressor's completed
payment can arrive after the wronged party stopped wanting it, an account
settled into a vacancy. The composite's read: closing is itself a move in
the other two ledgers, never a neutral ending.

## Echo

Whatever stands part-closed persists into every later scene the parties
share, which is why the moral account outlives its wrong. The all-closed
state exists, and the composite does not forbid it; it prices it: three
ledgers, three closes, each earned by its own engine's rules, none
transferable. A production that wants the fable ending must now show its
work three times.

# Instructions: Moral Account

How a Playwright wires the moral-account composite into a play. You wire by
linking the play's own content to the composite's files and, through them,
to the four atom engines it depends on; every file stays untouched, they are
canon. The Roadie plumbs the law into Instructions Knowledge on deploy.
Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the wrong and the bond it happened inside: who crossed
  whom, and what the relationship was worth before the crossing.

## Agent

- Cast the corners from the atoms as usual: the transgressor's kind, grade,
  dial, and road from the guilt engine; the dyad's account form and stored
  regard from repair; the wronged party's form, stance, and dial from
  forgiveness, with the violation's kind and the blindness dial from
  betrayal where trust was cut. Then stage the bridges: which exit road
  carries an account to the door (offered), what the offer lands against
  (received), and which ledgers close (closed). A plot that stages a
  handoff may link that bridge under its Tension; the audit surfaces the
  ones that stage it unnamed. You link to the composite and its atoms, you
  never edit them.

## Collaboration

- Every corner's science is its atom's: guilt, repair, forgiveness, and
  betrayal each carry their own warrant and their own instructions. The
  bond's standing read is trust's; the moral verdict on the act is
  moral-judgment's; the felt charge throughout is emotion's. Whether the
  three ledgers genuinely refuse to agree, rather than closing in unison
  like a fable, is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- The moral account is one wrong held in three ledgers at once: the
  transgressor's felt debt, the dyad's spoken account, and the wronged
  party's grievance and reckoning. The ledgers do not agree and their
  closes do not travel: the exit road chosen inside guilt predicts the
  account form repair hears; the offer lands against a stance, a stored
  regard, and a blindness the giver cannot see; and each ledger closes by
  its own rules, so forgiven-but-unrepaired, repaired-but-unforgiven, and
  paid-but-never-received are stable states, not failures of writing. Read
  the production in the disagreements between ledgers, never in any single
  engine's verdict.

## System

- Do cast every corner from its atom before staging a bridge; do let the
  bridges predict (road to form, offer to filters, close to repricing); do
  build scenes on the part-closed states.
- Do not restate an atom's reading inside the play where a link would do;
  do not close all three ledgers in one gesture without showing the work
  three times; do not edit the composite's or the atoms' files; wire only
  from the play's side.

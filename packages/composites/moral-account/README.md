# Moral-account

The moral account as composite: one wrong opens one account held in three ledgers at once, the transgressor's felt debt (guilt), the dyad's spoken account (repair), and the wronged party's grievance and reckoning (forgiveness, betrayal), and the drama lives where the ledgers refuse to agree.

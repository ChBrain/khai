# Process: Volition

## Taxonomy

A composite reading over three atom engines (no canon file of its own): the
goal, implementation-intention, and executive-function engines this package
declares as dependencies.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: volition

## Initiated by

A wish reaching the edge of becoming a will. Heckhausen and Gollwitzer
mapped the passage from wanting to doing as four phases split by a Rubicon:
the [goal](@chbrain/khai-engine-goal/position_goal.md) engine holds the
predecisional phase, the deliberated end still on the wishing side of the
line; the
[implementation-intention](@chbrain/khai-engine-implementation-intention/process_implementation_intention.md)
engine holds the crossing itself, the if-then plan that turns a goal into a
committed course; the
[executive-function](@chbrain/khai-engine-executive-function/process_executive_function.md)
engine holds the actional phase, the control that keeps the pursuit alive
against interference. No single engine sees the whole crossing; the composite
begins where the wish is about to become a will and reads the passage
through.

## Direction

From wanting to doing, across the line. The goal names a desired state and
its orientation, [approach](@chbrain/khai-engine-goal/position_approach.md)
or [avoidance](@chbrain/khai-engine-goal/position_avoidance.md), but naming
is predecisional and initiates nothing; the crossing comes when the goal is
bridged into an if-then
[plan](@chbrain/khai-engine-implementation-intention/process_planning.md)
that delegates action to a cue, and the pursuit then runs on the executive
engine's capacity to
[inhibit](@chbrain/khai-engine-executive-function/process_inhibition.md) the
prepotent wrong response and hold the goal active across the sequence. The
direction is one way and gated at each step: a wish that is never planned
does not cross, and a plan the control layer cannot carry does not complete.

## Lever

Which side of the Rubicon a production reads. Wanting and doing are easy to
collapse into one act, the persona who simply does what they wished, and a
scene that reads only the wish cannot tell a will from a wish that never
became one. The lever is to read the crossing in three: the deliberated
goal, the plan that commits it, and the control that carries it. Volition
whole is all three firing in sequence; the scenes worth the composite are
the ones where the crossing is where the drama is, a wish stranded short of
will or a will that cannot be carried out.

## Echo

The crossing completes, or it fails at one of its two joints. When the goal
is planned and the plan is carried, the passage has worked and the atoms
take over, the executive engine running the pursuit to its end. When a goal
is deliberated but never bridged into a plan the persona is stranded in the
[gap](position_volition_gap.md), wanting without willing; when the plan
fires but control cannot hold it the persona is defeated in the
[stall](position_volition_stall.md), willing without doing. A production
that stages only volition whole has cast a world where every wish becomes a
deed; the crossing this composite is for is the one that can fail, and
mostly does.

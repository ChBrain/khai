# Position: Absorption, Counterfeit

## Taxonomy

[Absorption](process_absorption.md), the state where the lock holds but the
merge never comes.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: absorption

## Has

A locked beam and no match under it. The attention engine holds the
spotlight fast, often
[captured](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_captured.md) rather than
willed or strained into a
[sustained](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_sustained.md) watch, but
the flow engine's
[challenge-skill balance](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_challenge_skill_balance.md)
never resolves: the activity is too slack or too steep to merge into, so the
[immersion](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_immersion.md) that turns a
lock into absorption does not form. Without the merge the last link inverts:
instead of the [absorbed](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/position_absorbed.md)
stance losing the clock, the persona keeps it, and felt duration runs
[continuous](@chbrain/khai-engine-time-perception/position_continuous.md) and
heavy. The doomscroll, the vigilant dread, the ruminative loop: attention
fully spent and nothing merged.

## Orders

Hold a lock that gives nothing back. The counterfeit demands the persona
keep attending, because the beam is captured and will not release, while
receiving none of absorption's return, no autotelic charge, no loss of the
watching self, no dissolved time. The order is endurance without payoff:
stay locked on what has seized the beam, feel every minute the merge would
have erased, and find no clear goal or feedback in the activity to convert
the lock into flow.

## Loses

The exit that mistakes itself for absorption. Because the persona looks
rapt, the room reads focus and leaves them to it, and the persona may read
their own captured attention as engagement, so the state defends itself:
the very lock that signals absorption from outside is the trap holding them
in its counterfeit. What is lost is the recognition that this is not the
real thing, and with it the impulse to break the lock and reach for an
activity that would actually match.

## Drives

Toward a match that would redeem the lock, or a break that would end it.
The persona finds the missing challenge-skill balance and the counterfeit
converts into real absorption, the same beam now merging; or the lock is
struck from outside and collapses, releasing them into a clock time that was
never suspended and so has nowhere jarring to return from; or the state
holds, self-defending, and the persona spends the hour rapt and receiving
nothing. A production stages the counterfeit to be seen through, the focus
that is really a trap, and reads the drama in whether anyone, the persona or
the room, tells it from the real merge.

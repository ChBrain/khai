# Absorption: Reference

## Line of Work

Absorption as **composite**: not a state but a chain across three engines.
The attention engine locks the spotlight single-pointed; the flow engine's
challenge-skill match turns the lock into a self-sustaining merge; the
time-perception engine's felt duration dissolves. The lock is the attention
engine's, whole; the match and the merge are the flow engine's; the felt
clock is the time-perception engine's. This package authors none of those
readings again. It declares the three engines as dependencies, wires their
members by hard package links, and adds only what no atom holds: the chain
read across all three, and the two states where it breaks register, the
counterfeit lock and the shattered merge.

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of absorption as a unified construct that the atom
decomposition splits into its links, where each atom's own REFERENCES
carries the primary science of its part.

## Origin

The psychology of absorption as a unified state spanning attention, engaged
merge, and altered time, inherited atop the three atom warrants the
dependency graph names.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Each link of the chain**: The allocation of the spotlight and its
  capture is the attention engine's; the challenge-skill match, the merge,
  and the autotelic stance are the flow engine's; the baseline felt
  duration is the time-perception engine's. The composite restates none of
  them; a bridge links, it never re-authors.
- **The felt charge of the state**: The enjoyment of a real merge, the
  dread of the counterfeit lock, the jolt of the break are the emotion and
  mood engines'; the composite reads the chain, not the affect along it.
- **What drives the persona to the activity, or back to it**: Why the
  persona took the activity up, and whether they rebuild the lock after a
  break, is the motivation engine's; the composite reads whether the chain
  fires, not the wanting behind it.
- **The play frame**: Whether the activity is framed as play is
  play-mode's; absorption runs inside or outside a play frame and the
  composite does not mark the frame.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the three-link chain) with two bridges, each
a way the chain breaks register, wired by hard package links into the atoms
it spans.

- **[absorption](process_absorption.md)** (the root): attention locks, the
  match merges, felt time dissolves; a chain, one way down, no link firing
  without the one before. Anchored by Tellegen & Atkinson (absorption as one
  trait) and Agarwal & Karahanna (its dimensions), over Csikszentmihalyi's
  merge.
- **[absorption, counterfeit](position_absorption_counterfeit.md)** (bridge
  state, attention without flow): the beam locks but no match forms, so the
  merge never comes and time drags heavy instead of dissolving; rapt from
  outside, trapped within. Anchored by Agarwal & Karahanna (the absorbed
  state minus its temporal dissociation) over the attention, flow, and
  time-perception members it links.
- **[absorption, break](process_absorption_break.md)** (bridge, capture
  shattering flow): a bottom-up capture unseats the lock a running merge
  depends on, collapsing the chain from the top and returning the erased
  interval all at once. Anchored by Csikszentmihalyi (the merge's fragility)
  and the attention engine's capture, over the flow and time-perception
  members it links.

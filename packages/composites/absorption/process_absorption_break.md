# Process: Absorption, Break

## Taxonomy

[Absorption](process_absorption.md), the motion where a running merge is
shattered from the top of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: absorption

## Initiated by

A capture landing on a merge already running. Absorption is fragile at its
first link: the [immersion](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_immersion.md)
the flow engine sustains depends on the beam staying locked, and a
bottom-up [capture](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_captured.md), the
salient intruder attention cannot refuse, unseats the lock before the
persona can choose. The moment the beam is yanked, the chain has no bottom
link to stand on, and the whole of absorption falls at once from its top.

## Direction

Upward through the chain, and fast. The break runs opposite to absorption's
own direction: where absorption built lock to merge to time-loss downward
and slowly, the break undoes it top-down and all at once. The lock goes
first, so action and awareness come apart, the
[absorbed](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/position_absorbed.md) stance's lost
watching-self snaps back, and felt time reappears as a
[continuous](@chbrain/khai-engine-time-perception/position_continuous.md)
readout the persona had not been keeping. Because the time-loss was total,
its return is a shock: the persona learns how long they were gone only in
the instant of coming back, the clock reasserting the interval the merge had
erased.

## Lever

What the capture was, and whether it was worth the merge it cost. Not every
intruder is trivial: the beam is unseated the same way by an alarm that
matters and a notification that does not, and the break makes no distinction
the persona would endorse. The lever is the price of the interruption,
because a merge is not resumed where it broke, it is re-entered from the
start, lock and match rebuilt from cold. A production controls the break by
choosing what is allowed to capture the beam, and reads the cost in what the
shattered merge cannot simply pick back up.

## Echo

The persona is back in the room, abruptly, with the interval handed to them
in one lump. What follows is a re-entry the composite does not itself hold:
the felt jolt is the emotion engine's, the choice to rebuild the lock or let
the activity go is motivation's, and the fresh climb from a cold start is
the flow engine's again. What the break leaves the composite is the mark of
the fall, a persona who was absorbed and now is not, and knows exactly how
far they dropped.

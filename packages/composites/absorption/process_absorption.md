# Process: Absorption

## Taxonomy

A composite reading over three atom engines (no canon file of its own): the
attention, flow, and time-perception engines this package declares as
dependencies.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: absorption

## Initiated by

A spotlight collapsing onto one thing. Absorption is not one engine's state
but a chain across three: the
[attention](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_attention.md) engine
locks the beam single-pointed, the
[flow](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_flow.md) engine's challenge-skill
match turns that lock into a self-sustaining merge, and the
[time-perception](@chbrain/khai-engine-time-perception/position_time_perception.md)
engine's felt duration gives way. Tellegen and Atkinson named the whole as
one trait, a total attention that engages the persona's representational
resources and alters the ordinary sense of self and time; Agarwal and
Karahanna decomposed the lived state into the very dimensions the atoms
own, focused immersion and temporal dissociation. The composite reads the
chain those decompositions imply.

## Direction

Down the chain, each link enabling the next. A
[selective](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_selective.md) lock is
only a lock; it becomes absorption when the flow engine's
[challenge-skill balance](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_challenge_skill_balance.md)
finds the activity matched, and the lock deepens into
[immersion](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/process_immersion.md), action and
awareness merging. Only then does the last link fall: the
[absorbed](@chbrain/khai-engine-flow/position_absorbed.md) stance keeps no
ledger of minutes, and felt time dissolves from the inside. The direction
is one way by default, attention to merge to time-loss, and no link fires
without the one before it: there is no time-dissolution without a merge, and
no merge without a lock.

## Lever

Which link a production reads, and whether it reads them apart. The chain's
signature is time gone, but time-loss is the last link, not the first, and
a scene that reads only the outside of a persona, rapt, unmoving, cannot
tell absorption from its counterfeit. The lever is to read all three links
at once: the lock, the match, and the clock. Absorption is the case where
all three fire; the interesting scenes are the ones where they do not, and
only the three-link read tells a real merge from a lock that never became
one.

## Echo

The chain is firing in register, or it has broken. When attention locks but
the match never comes, the merge does not form and time turns heavy rather
than absent: the [counterfeit](position_absorption_counterfeit.md), rapt
from outside and trapped within. When a merge already running is struck by
a [capture](@chbrain/khai-engine-attention/process_captured.md), the chain
[breaks](process_absorption_break.md) from the top and the persona is
dropped back into clock time all at once. A production that stages only
successful absorption has cast a world without the counterfeit and without
the interruption; the scenes worth the composite are the ones where the
chain is incomplete.

# Passage: Reference

## Line of Work

Passage as **composite**: a rite of passage runs on two clocks at once, the
collective ceremony and the individual crossing, and the composite reads the
phase relation between them. The ceremony, its phases and its solidarity, is
the ritual engine's, whole; the individual between-state, the threshold and
its suspension, is the liminality engine's. This package authors neither
reading again. It declares the two engines as dependencies, wires their
members by hard package links, and adds only what neither atom can hold: the
lock between the two clocks, and the two states in which it slips, the
hollow rite and the unmarked crossing.

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of the passage as a coupling of collective form and
individual experience, and of what happens when the coupling fails, where
each atom's own REFERENCES carries the primary science of its clock.

## Origin

The anthropology and sociology of rites of passage read as a coupling of
collective ceremony and individual transition, inherited atop the two atom
warrants the dependency graph names.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The ceremony itself**: The rite's phases, its effervescence, its
  communitas, and the solidarity it generates are the ritual engine's,
  whole; the composite reads whether the ceremony lands in the person, it
  never re-authors the ceremony.
- **The between-state itself**: The individual threshold, its suspension,
  and its emergence are the liminality engine's; the composite reads the
  crossing against the clock of the rite, not the phenomenology of the
  between, which the atom already holds.
- **The felt charge of the passage**: The disorientation of the threshold,
  the exposure of suspension, the relief of emergence are the emotion and
  mood engines'; the composite reads the phase relation between two clocks,
  not the affect inside either.
- **The status conferred or withheld**: What the new status is worth going
  forward, and how a room ranks the person after the passage, is the
  status and standing engines'; the composite reads whether the crossing
  happened, not what it buys.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the two-clock read) with two bridge states,
each the failure of the lock in one direction, wired by hard package links
into the atoms it spans.

- **[passage](process_passage.md)** (the root): a rite of passage as two
  clocks, collective ceremony and individual crossing, that are meant to be
  one and can slip apart. Anchored by van Gennep (the two drawn as one
  movement) with Turner's liminal phase running through both.
- **[passage, hollow](position_passage_hollow.md)** (bridge state, ritual
  ahead of liminality): the ceremony completes over a persona who has not
  crossed; incorporation performed, the self still in the between. Anchored
  by Grimes (ritual infelicity, the ineffectual and insincere rite) over
  the ritual and liminality members it links.
- **[passage, unmarked](position_passage_unmarked.md)** (bridge state,
  liminality ahead of ritual): the persona has genuinely emerged with no
  ceremony to confer or witness it. Anchored by Turner (the liminoid,
  outside the collective rite) and Grimes (the unmarked passage and the
  case for re-invention) over the liminality and ritual members it links.

# Process: Standing, Conversion

## Taxonomy

[Standing](position_standing.md), the bridge where rank leaks from one
altitude into another.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: standing

## Initiated by

Rank at one altitude pressing against its absence at the next. The
[capital](@chbrain/khai-engine-capital/position_capital.md) engine
converts within its own field, form into form; what it cannot stage is
the crossing where resources become deference. Ridgeway showed that
crossing empirically: when a mere difference correlates with a resource
advantage, interaction converts the correlation into a status belief, and
the advantaged category is soon read as the more competent one. Weber
drew the same current at the scale of history, class position ripening
into status honour, and the honoured circle then defending its rank
against the merely rich. Conversion also runs between the other
altitudes: a title minting the presumption of
[legitimate](@chbrain/khai-engine-power/position_legitimate.md) power, an
institution's [consecration](@chbrain/khai-engine-capital/position_consecrated.md)
laundering accumulated capital into rank that no longer argues its case.

## Direction

Upward into belief, slowly, and it does not reverse on its own. The
conversions all run toward the more durable form: resources into status
beliefs, standing into entitlement, capital into consecration, and once
arrived, the converted rank detaches from its source and survives it.
Ridgeway's beliefs outlive the resource correlation that seeded them,
which is why the direction matters dramatically: a conversion completed
is a rank that no longer needs its origin, and disputing the origin no
longer disputes the rank.

## Lever

The exchange rate, which no persona controls alone. Every conversion
needs the receiving altitude to honour the incoming rank, and each has a
gatekeeper: the field that will not consecrate, the room whose deference
will not follow the title, the audience that takes the money and
withholds the belief. The lever for a production is where to set the
rate, a field that converts freely is a market, a field that refuses
conversion is a caste, and the scenes live where the rate is contested,
one party spending rank at yesterday's rate while the room reprices it.

## Echo

A converted rank settles into its new altitude and the atoms take over:
the new deference runs on the status engine, the presumed authority on
power, the consecrated position on capital. What remains the composite's
is the residue: the persona who remembers the rank is converted while the
room now reads it as natural, and the ones the conversion passed over,
for whom the new belief is a wall. Ridgeway's point holds the echo in
place: once the belief exists, it does the ranking by itself.

# Instructions: Standing

How a Playwright wires the standing composite into a play. You wire by
linking the play's own content to the composite's files and, through them,
to the five atom engines it depends on; every file stays untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the law into Instructions Knowledge on deploy.
Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the fields, the structures, and the stakes: which rooms
  the personas are read in, which formal orders they stand inside, and
  what a rank is worth in this production.

## Agent

- Cast each altitude from its atom as usual: the capital configuration
  from capital, the title position from hierarchy where a structure
  assigns one, the entry rank from status, the base from power, the
  habitual move from status-move. Then cast the disagreements: for every
  significant persona, name where the four ranks diverge, because an
  aligned persona is furniture and a misaligned one is a scene engine. A
  persona living on a misalignment or mid-conversion may link the bridge
  under Projection; the audit surfaces the ones left unnamed. You link to
  the composite and its atoms, you never edit them.

## Collaboration

- Every altitude's science is its atom's: capital, hierarchy, status,
  power, and status-move each carry their own warrant and their own
  instructions. The felt charge of rank is emotion's; the self-relation
  an acknowledgment builds is recognition's, a homonym neighbour, not
  this read; the influence campaign is persuasion's. Whether the
  production genuinely keeps four ladders, rather than quietly awarding
  one master rank, is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Standing is four ranks held at once in four separate systems: the
  field's read of capital, the structure's assigned title, the scene's
  deference order, and the power base, with the transactional move layer
  negotiating beneath them. There is no master ladder; the altitudes
  need not agree, and their disagreements are stable positions, not
  errors -- the titled superior nobody defers to, the expert with no
  title, the money the field will not consecrate. Rank crosses altitudes
  only by conversion (resources hardening into status beliefs that then
  rank by themselves) and rank is kept alive by enactment (moves
  sedimenting into the deference order, which then reprices the moves).
  Read the production in the disagreements between altitudes and the
  conversions in progress, never in any single engine's verdict.

## System

- Do cast every altitude from its atom before staging a bridge; do name
  each significant persona's misalignment; do stage conversions at a
  contested exchange rate, and let the enactment loop run under every
  scene at the speed of dialogue.
- Do not collapse the altitudes into one rank; do not resolve a
  misalignment in the scene that reveals it; do not let a title buy
  deference or money buy belief without showing the conversion paid for;
  do not edit the composite's or the atoms' files; wire only from the
  play's side.

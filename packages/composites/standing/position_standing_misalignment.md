# Position: Standing, Misalignment

## Taxonomy

[Standing](position_standing.md), the held state where the altitudes
disagree.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: standing

## Has

Rank at one altitude and its absence at another, held together and held
for a while. The
[superior](@chbrain/khai-engine-hierarchy/position_superior.md) whose
room runs on a [low](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_low.md) read of
them; the [expert](@chbrain/khai-engine-power/position_expert.md) with no
title whose word nonetheless ends the argument; the
[rising](@chbrain/khai-engine-capital/position_rising.md) persona whose
money is counted and whose belonging is not; the persona the room grants
[high](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_high.md) status while every
formal measure ranks them nowhere. Magee and Galinsky's line holds the
state together: power and status are distinct dimensions, so their
combinations are real positions, not errors, and the misaligned ones are
stable enough to live in.

## Orders

Manage the gap, every scene. The misaligned persona cannot act from
either rank cleanly: leading with the high altitude invites the low one
to be invoked against them, and deferring to the low altitude wastes the
high one. The order is triage per room, which measure this audience
honours, and the standing cost of guessing wrong is paid immediately.
The corner the literature marks sharpest is power without status: Fast,
Halevy, and Galinsky found that holding the capacity to compel without
the respect that usually cushions it turns the holder demeaning, the
gap itself curdling into how the power is used.

## Loses

The option of resting on rank. An aligned persona can stop performing;
a misaligned one cannot, because some room is always reading the altitude
they lack. The state also forecloses clean grievance: the persona is
genuinely ranked and genuinely slighted at once, so neither the room's
deference nor its disrespect is simply wrong, and appeals to the honoured
altitude sound like denial of the dishonoured one.

## Drives

Toward resolution by any route: the persona works a
[conversion](process_standing_conversion.md) to drag the lagging altitude
up, plays the [move layer](process_standing_enactment.md) to perform the
rank the room withholds, or attacks the measure itself, disputing that
the altitude they lack should count. A production should let the
misalignment persist long enough to cost something; the state resolved in
one scene was a misunderstanding, and the composite's terrain is the gap
that holds.

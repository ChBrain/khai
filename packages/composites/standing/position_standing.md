# Position: Standing

## Taxonomy

A composite reading over five atom engines (no canon file of its own): the
capital, hierarchy, status, power, and status-move engines this package
declares as dependencies.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: standing

## Has

Four ranks at once, held in four separate systems. The field reads a rank
from the persona's [capital](@chbrain/khai-engine-capital/position_capital.md)
configuration; a structure assigns one by
[title](@chbrain/khai-engine-hierarchy/position_hierarchy.md); the scene's
deference order concedes one through
[status](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_status.md); and the persona's
influence moves on a [power](@chbrain/khai-engine-power/position_power.md)
base that is its own kind of rank. Weber drew the first cut, class against
status honour against party, and the modern literature sharpened it: power
and status are distinct dimensions that vary independently. Beneath the
four held ranks runs the negotiation layer, the
[status move](@chbrain/khai-engine-status-move/position_status_move.md),
where standing is claimed and conceded transaction by transaction. No
single engine sees more than its own altitude; the composite holds the
read across them.

## Orders

Hold all four, and never collapse them into one. The persona moves through
a scene answering four measures at once, and the composite's one demand on
a production is that the measures stay separate: a title is not deference,
deference is not capital, capital is not a power base, and none of them is
safe from the moves being played underneath. A persona whose four ranks
agree is a stable fixture; the composite's order is to know, at every
beat, which measure is speaking.

## Loses

The option of a single ladder. There is no master rank the four resolve
into, and a production that awards one, the boss who is therefore
deferred to, the rich persona who therefore commands, has flattened the
cluster back into the fable the atoms were cut to prevent. The persona
likewise loses the comfort of transfer: rank earned at one altitude does
not arrive at another by right, but only through the conversions the
bridges name, and the gap between earned-here and denied-there is
permanent standing terrain.

## Drives

Disagreement between the altitudes. Aligned standing drives nothing; the
scenes this composite exists for are the
[misalignments](position_standing_misalignment.md), the
[conversions](process_standing_conversion.md) by which one altitude leaks
into another, and the [enactments](process_standing_enactment.md) by which
the move layer builds or erodes the ranks above it. A production casting
this composite should be able to say, for every significant persona,
where their four ranks disagree, because that disagreement is the
persona's standing engine.

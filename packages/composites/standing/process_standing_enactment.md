# Process: Standing, Enactment

## Taxonomy

[Standing](position_standing.md), the bridge where the move layer builds
and erodes the ranks above it.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: standing

## Initiated by

Transactions accumulating into a rank. The
[status-move](@chbrain/khai-engine-status-move/position_status_move.md)
engine reads one move at a time, Johnstone's see-saw of claims and
concessions; the [status](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_status.md)
engine reads the standing order those moves have already produced.
Neither stages the crossing: Ridgeway's finding that rank is performed
and confirmed by the group, not merely assigned, is the bridge's warrant,
because it makes the deference order the sediment of the transaction
layer. A run of unanswered
[ascending](@chbrain/khai-engine-status-move/position_ascending.md) moves
is how a persona's entry rank rises between scenes; habitual
[descending](@chbrain/khai-engine-status-move/position_descending.md)
becomes a [low](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_low.md) rank nobody
remembers assigning.

## Direction

From move to rank and back, a loop with a grain. Moves sediment into
rank, and rank then reprices the moves: the same unqualified assertion
reads as initiative from a
[high](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_high.md)-status persona and
as insolence from a low one, which is the burden-of-proof asymmetry the
status engine carries, now read as the loop's downhill grain. Enactment
therefore compounds: rank makes the rank-confirming move cheaper, and
the persona playing against their standing pays double for every
transaction, which is exactly what makes the against-the-grain scene
worth staging.

## Lever

The unanswered move. A claim the room lets stand becomes a fact of the
order; a claim contested becomes a
[contested](@chbrain/khai-engine-status/position_contested.md) rank with
the burden active on both sides. The lever is who answers, because a
move is completed by its reception: the room that laughs, the peer who
yields the floor, the superior who does not. A production controls the
enactment bridge by controlling the answers, and a single deliberate
non-answer, the put-down absorbed without
[deflection](@chbrain/khai-engine-status-move/position_deflecting.md),
the interruption allowed to finish, is the smallest unit of standing
change the composite can stage.

## Echo

The order settles until the moves unsettle it again, and the other
bridges are fed: an enacted rank the formal measures do not honour is a
fresh [misalignment](position_standing_misalignment.md), and a rank
enacted long enough begins to read as natural, which is a
[conversion](process_standing_conversion.md) beginning. A production
that wants standing to feel alive keeps this bridge running under every
scene, because it is the only one of the three that moves at the speed
of dialogue.

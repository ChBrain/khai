# Standing: Reference

## Line of Work

Standing as **composite**: one persona ranked at four altitudes at once,
the field's read of their capital, the structure's assigned title, the
scene's deference order, and the base their influence moves on, with the
transactional move layer negotiating beneath all four. No single atom
engine sees more than its own altitude. The field position is the capital
engine's, whole; the assigned place is hierarchy's; the deference order is
status's; the compliance base is power's; the single move is status-move's.
This package authors none of those readings again. It declares the five
engines as dependencies, wires their members by hard package links, and
adds only what the atoms cannot hold: the read across the altitudes, one
bridge state (misalignment) and two bridge motions (conversion, enactment).

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of standing as a plural order, the dimensions separated,
their misalignments studied, their conversions traced, where each atom's
own REFERENCES carries the primary science of its altitude.

## Origin

The sociology and psychology of rank as a plural, interacting order,
inherited atop the five atom warrants the dependency graph names.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Every altitude of the read**: The capital configuration and its field
  is the capital engine's; the title grammar is hierarchy's; the
  deference order, its characteristics, and its burden-of-proof asymmetry
  are status's; the six bases are power's; the single transactional move
  is status-move's. The composite restates none of them; a bridge links,
  it never re-authors.
- **The felt charge of rank**: What being ranked, misranked, or reranked
  feels like from inside is the emotion and mood engines'; the pride and
  shame the altitudes can trigger are their own engines'; the composite
  reads orders, not affect.
- **Recognition as self-relation**: The revision of a persona's relation
  to themselves through an acknowledgment encounter is the recognition
  engine's (Honneth), a homonym neighbour, not a corner of this read;
  standing here is rank in the room, not the self the room's answer
  builds.
- **The influence act**: How a power base is spent, tactic by tactic, is
  the persuasion engine's; the composite reads what the persona holds
  and what the holding disagrees with, not the campaign.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the four-altitude read) with one bridge
state and two bridge motions, each wired by hard package links into the
atoms it spans.

- **[standing](position_standing.md)** (the root): four ranks in four
  separate systems, a negotiation layer beneath them, and no master
  ladder they resolve into. Anchored by Weber (the orders cut apart) and
  Magee & Galinsky (the dimensions distinct and self-reinforcing).
- **[standing, misalignment](position_standing_misalignment.md)** (bridge
  state, across all five): rank at one altitude held with its absence at
  another, stable enough to live in and costly every scene. Anchored by
  Magee & Galinsky (the combinations are real positions) and Fast, Halevy
  & Galinsky (power without status, the destructive corner) over the
  hierarchy, status, power, and capital members it links.
- **[standing, conversion](process_standing_conversion.md)** (bridge,
  capital and power to status): rank leaking upward into belief,
  resources becoming deference, titles minting presumed legitimacy,
  consecration laundering capital into rank that no longer argues.
  Anchored by Ridgeway (resource correlation into status belief) and
  Weber (class ripening into status honour) over the capital, power, and
  status members it links.
- **[standing, enactment](process_standing_enactment.md)** (bridge,
  status-move to status): moves sedimenting into rank and rank repricing
  the moves, a loop with a downhill grain. Anchored by Ridgeway's
  interactional line as carried in the status atom (rank performed and
  confirmed, the burden-of-proof asymmetry) over the status-move and
  status members it links.

# Self-Conscious: Reference

## Line of Work

The self-conscious family as **composite**: one appraisal against an owned
standard, and two dials, valence and attribution, that set which of four
corners the verdict lands in. No single atom engine sees the axis the
corners share. Failed-and-act-sized is the guilt engine's, whole;
failed-and-self-sized is the shame engine's; the met side, both facets, is
the pride engine's. This package authors none of those readings again. It
declares the three engines as dependencies, wires their members by hard
package links, and adds only what the atoms cannot hold: the attribution
axis itself, and the three motions along and across it, the widening, the
mirror, and the concealed moves.

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of the family as one system, the axis drawn on the failure
side, the identical geometry found on the success side, the evidence that
the corners bleed into each other, where each atom's own REFERENCES carries
the primary science of its corner.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Every corner of the square**: The failed act's kinds, weights,
  proneness, and exit roads are the guilt engine's; the exposed self's
  compass, poles, kin states, and proneness are the shame engine's; both
  facets of the met standard, the display, the routes, and the disposition
  are the pride engine's. The composite restates none of them; a bridge
  links, it never re-authors.
- **The standard itself**: Where the standard comes from, and whether it
  deserves to be held, is the play's casting and the moral-judgment
  engine's ground; the composite takes the owned standard as given and
  reads where its verdict lands.
- **Embarrassment and humiliation**: The kin states on the failure side
  stay the shame engine's own members; the composite's axis runs between
  the four attributional corners and does not re-map the neighbours.
- **The felt affect underneath**: What any corner feels like from inside
  is the emotion engine's; the composite reads attributions, not qualia.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the 2x2 read) with three bridges, each wired
by hard package links into the atoms it spans.

- **[self-conscious](process_self_conscious.md)** (the root): one
  appraisal, two dials, four corners on one attribution axis no single
  atom sees. Anchored by Michael Lewis (the appraisal system) atop Helen
  Block Lewis (the axis).
- **[verdict, widening](process_verdict_widening.md)** (bridge, guilt to
  shame): an act-verdict left open erodes into a self-verdict, and guilt's
  act-sized exits stop fitting the debt. Anchored by Tangney & Dearing
  (shame-fused guilt behaves like shame) over the guilt and shame members
  it links.
- **[verdict, mirror](process_verdict_mirror.md)** (bridge, authentic to
  hubristic): the same erosion in the positive valence, credit hardening
  from deed into essence, with the status routes as the slide made social.
  Anchored by Tracy & Robins (the facets' attributional structure) over
  the pride members it links.
- **[verdict, moves](process_verdict_moves.md)** (bridge, across all
  three): identical visible gestures driven by different verdicts,
  attack-self beside self-punishment, attack-other beside condemnation,
  one display for two prides; diagnosis only by probing. Anchored by
  Tangney & Dearing (the corners co-occur, so the gesture alone cannot
  say) over the shame, guilt, and pride members it links.

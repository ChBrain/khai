# Process: Self-Conscious

## Taxonomy

A composite reading over three atom engines (no canon file of its own): the
guilt, shame, and pride engines this package declares as dependencies.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-conscious

## Initiated by

A persona measures themselves against a standard they hold as their own, and
the measurement lands. One appraisal, and two dials set which corner it
lands in: valence, whether the standard was failed or met, and attribution,
whether the verdict falls on the act or on the self entire. Failed and
act-sized is [guilt](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_guilt.md); failed
and self-sized is [shame](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_shame.md); met
and act-sized is
[authentic pride](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_authentic_pride.md);
met and self-sized is
[hubristic pride](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_hubristic_pride.md).
No single engine sees the axis the four corners share; that is what this
composite is for.

## Direction

Along the attribution axis, in both valences at once.
The corners are not rooms a persona is assigned to but positions on a line
the verdict slides along: the same failure reads act-sized on a good day and
self-sized on a bad one, the same success is a deed done well until it
becomes proof of who one is. Lewis drew the axis for the failure side and
Tracy and Robins found the identical geometry on the success side, which is
the composite's warrant for reading all four corners as one system.

## Lever

The attribution dial is the one that moves. Valence is mostly the world's
to set, the standard was failed or it was not, but where the verdict lands
is the persona's own appraisal, shaped by their dials in the atoms: guilt's
proneness against shame's, and the facet pride habitually resolves toward.
A persona is castable on this axis as a tendency, and dramatic on it as a
motion: the interesting scenes are the ones where the verdict is mid-slide.

## Echo

The verdict settles in a corner and the atoms take over: guilt runs its
account, shame its compass, pride its route. But settled is provisional,
and the bridges beneath this root name the three motions that unsettle it:
an act-verdict eroding into a self-verdict, credit hardening from deed into
essence, and one visible gesture concealing which of two verdicts drives
it. A production that keeps every persona pinned to one corner for the whole
run has cast dispositions; the family's drama is in the sliding.

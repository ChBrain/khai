# Process: Verdict, Mirror

## Taxonomy

[Self-Conscious](process_self_conscious.md), the bridge where the same
erosion runs on the success side: credit hardening from deed into essence.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-conscious

## Initiated by

An [authentic pride](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_authentic_pride.md)
that stops being re-earned. Tracy and Robins found the success side carries
the same attribution line as the failure side: credit to the effort and the
act on one side, credit to the stable self on the other. The mirror is the
widening's twin in the positive valence: I did this well, repeated and
unexamined, hardens into I am simply better, and the persona has slid into
[hubristic pride](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_hubristic_pride.md)
without a single new success. What drifts on the failure side hardens on
the success side; the geometry is identical, the feel of the slide is not.

## Direction

From deed to essence, greased by repetition.
Each success attributed to effort must be re-earned; essence never must,
which is the seduction. The persona's
[disposition](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/position_pride_disposition.md) sets
the grade of the slope, and the two status routes are the slide made
social: the prestige route runs on demonstrated, act-sized competence, the
dominance route on what the self is entitled to, so a persona sliding from
[prestige](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_route_prestige.md) toward
[dominance](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_route_dominance.md) is the
mirror playing out in front of witnesses. The reverse motion exists here
too and is likewise work: humbling an essence back into a deed usually
takes a failure the essence cannot absorb.

## Lever

What the hardening destroys. Effort-credit is self-correcting: a persona
who believes the deed earned the standing keeps doing the deed. Essence-
credit corrects nothing, because there is nothing left to earn; practice,
preparation, and the granting audience all become insults to what the self
already is. The lever is the moment of test: hand the hardened persona a
task their essence should trivially clear, and the mirror shows its cost,
either the scramble to fake act-sized competence or the attack on whoever
set the test. A production that wants the slide visible stages the
un-earned success, applause for something the persona knows they did not
do, and lets them keep it.

## Echo

The persona now runs pride's engine from the hubristic corner: standing
must be defended rather than re-earned, and the dominance route's costs,
fear in place of deference, allies in place of admirers, begin to accrue.
The mirror also arms the failure side: an essence built on success has no
act-sized place to put a failure when one finally lands, so the crash from
hubristic pride tends to skip guilt entirely and arrive as
[shame](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_shame.md), the widening
completed in one step. The two bridges meet there, which is the axis, not
a coincidence.

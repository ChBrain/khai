# Process: Verdict, Widening

## Taxonomy

[Self-Conscious](process_self_conscious.md), the bridge where an act-verdict
erodes into a self-verdict on the failure side.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-conscious

## Initiated by

A [guilt](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_guilt.md) that will not close.
The guilt engine reads a debt that is paid, refused, or carried; what it
cannot read is the debt changing kind. When the account stays open long
enough, or the same crossing repeats, or the persona's own dials lean that
way, the verdict widens: I did a bad thing becomes I am the kind of person
who does this, and the persona has crossed the attribution line into
[shame](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_shame.md) without any new event
occurring. Lewis drew this as the difference between condemning the act and
condemning the self; Tangney and Dearing showed the two travel together in
the wild and that the fused state behaves like shame, not like guilt.

## Direction

From act to self, one way by default.
Widening is the easy slope: an act-verdict left open drifts toward the self,
while a self-verdict almost never narrows back to the act on its own. The
[haunting](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/position_culpable_haunting.md) grade is
the bridge's staging ground, a debt so old and unpayable that act-language
has already begun to fail it; and a high
[shame proneness](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/position_shame_proneness.md)
steepens the slope, so the same open account widens in one persona and
merely ages in another. Narrowing, self back to act, happens too, but it is
work: it needs another voice, a scene, someone re-drawing the line the
persona can no longer draw alone.

## Lever

What the widening destroys. Guilt's whole exit architecture, the
[reparation](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_reparation.md) roads, runs
on the act being the unit: an act can be confessed, repaired, paid for. A
self cannot. The moment the verdict widens, the roads out stop fitting the
debt, which is why the fused state is the dangerous one: the persona still
feels the pull to repair and no repair is act-sized anymore. A production
holds this lever by choosing when the slide happens, mid-scene under a
witness's sentence, or offstage between acts, discovered only when a repair
that should have worked does not.

## Echo

The persona now runs on shame's engine: the
[compass](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_compass.md) replaces the
reparation roads, and moves that made sense for a debt, approach, payment,
confession, give way to moves that make sense for a flawed self, hiding,
attacking, denying. Whoever knew the persona in the guilt corner will
misread them in the shame corner, because the visible behaviour changed
kind while the original crossing did not. The narrowing back, if the
production stages one, is a scene, not a drift.

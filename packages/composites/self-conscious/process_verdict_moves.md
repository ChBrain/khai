# Process: Verdict, Moves

## Taxonomy

[Self-Conscious](process_self_conscious.md), the bridge where one visible
gesture conceals which of two verdicts drives it.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-conscious

## Initiated by

Two engines producing the same move for different reasons. Shame's
[attack-self](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_compass_attack_self.md)
pole and guilt's
[self-punishment](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_reparation_self_punishment.md)
road both look like a persona turning on themselves; shame's
[attack-other](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_compass_attack_other.md)
pole and guilt's
[condemnation](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_refusal_condemnation.md)
refusal both look like a persona turning on the accuser. The gesture is
identical, the verdict beneath it is not: one pays an act-sized debt or
disputes an act-sized charge, the other defends a self that feels globally
exposed. On the success side the concealment is bodily: the pride engine's
own [display](@chbrain/khai-engine-pride/process_pride_display.md) member
records that the posture does not sort the facets apart, so the expansion
and the tilt of the chin do not say whether deed or essence is being
celebrated.

## Direction

From gesture to verdict, only by probing.
The move alone does not disambiguate; the response to a counter-move does.
Offer the self-attacker an act-sized repayment: the guilt-driven persona
takes it and the debt closes, the shame-driven persona refuses it because
no act-sized payment fits a self-sized flaw. Absorb the other-attacker's
charge without counter-attack: the condemnation-driven persona keeps
arguing the standard, because the argument is the point, while the
shame-driven persona quiets, because the exposure has passed. The bridge
turns diagnosis into staging: a scene that wants to reveal the verdict
gives the persona the probe and lets the response speak.

## Lever

The misread. Every witness in the play reads the gesture, not the verdict,
so the same move is answered wrongly by default: the shame-driven
self-attack is met with reassurance about the act, which misses; the
guilt-driven self-punishment is met with reassurance about the self, which
also misses. The lever is who in the production can read past the gesture,
and what it costs them to be right; a persona who answers the verdict
instead of the move is doing something rare, and the audience should see
it land where every other answer slid off.

## Echo

Once probed, the verdict is visible and the atoms take over: the
guilt-driven persona is on a
[reparation](@chbrain/khai-engine-guilt/process_reparation.md) road with an
end, the shame-driven persona is circling the
[compass](@chbrain/khai-engine-shame/process_compass.md) with none, and the
proud persona's facet, once named, decides whether the standing survives
its next test. Unprobed, the concealment compounds: witnesses keep
answering gestures, the answers keep missing, and the persona learns that
showing the move gets them the wrong medicine, which is itself a reason to
stop showing anything.

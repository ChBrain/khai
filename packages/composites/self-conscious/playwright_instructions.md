# Instructions: Self-Conscious

How a Playwright wires the self-conscious composite into a play. You wire by
linking the play's own content to the composite's files and, through them,
to the three atom engines it depends on; every file stays untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the law into Instructions Knowledge on deploy.
Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the standard and the stakes: what the persona holds
  themselves to, where that standard came from, and what meeting or
  failing it costs in this production.

## Agent

- Cast the corners from the atoms as usual: the transgression's kind,
  grade, and exit road from the guilt engine; the compass pole and
  proneness from shame; the facet, route, and disposition from pride. Then
  cast the axis itself: every significant persona gets a tendency, where
  their verdicts habitually land between act and self, in both valences.
  Stage the bridges as motions, not states: a widening is a slide the
  audience watches, a mirror is a hardening across scenes, a concealed
  move is a probe and a response. A plot that turns on a verdict changing
  size may link that bridge under its Tension; the audit surfaces the ones
  that stage it unnamed. You link to the composite and its atoms, you
  never edit them.

## Collaboration

- Every corner's science is its atom's: guilt, shame, and pride each carry
  their own warrant and their own instructions. The standard's origin and
  authority are moral-judgment's and the production's; the felt charge
  throughout is emotion's; what a won standing actually buys is the
  status and hierarchy engines' ground. Whether the personas genuinely
  slide along the axis, rather than sitting in assigned corners like a
  diagram, is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- The self-conscious family is one appraisal with two dials: valence,
  whether an owned standard was failed or met, and attribution, whether
  the verdict lands on the act or the self entire. The four corners,
  guilt, shame, authentic pride, hubristic pride, share one attribution
  axis, and the drama lives in motion along it: act-verdicts left open
  widen into self-verdicts, credit unexamined hardens from deed into
  essence, and identical gestures conceal which verdict drives them, so a
  witness who answers the gesture misses by default. Read the production
  in where each verdict sits and which way it is sliding, never in a
  fixed assignment of persona to corner.

## System

- Do cast every corner from its atom before staging a motion; do give
  each significant persona an axis tendency in both valences; do stage
  probes where a gesture conceals its verdict, and let the response
  speak.
- Do not pin a persona to one corner for the whole run without making the
  stillness itself the point; do not resolve a widening by drift when the
  narrowing back needs a scene; do not edit the composite's or the atoms'
  files; wire only from the play's side.

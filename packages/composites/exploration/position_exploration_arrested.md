# Position: Exploration, Arrested

## Taxonomy

[Exploration](process_exploration.md), the state where the investigation
never crosses into play.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: exploration

## Has

A live pull and a closed gate to play. The curiosity engine's
[epistemic](@chbrain/khai-engine-curiosity/position_epistemic.md) itch is
working, the persona is genuinely investigating, asking what the thing is
and how it behaves, but the crossing Hutt marked never comes: the question
stays what does this do and never turns into what can I do with this. What
the persona has is exploration that has become its own destination, the
unknown held permanently as a problem to be solved rather than released
into a plaything, so the play-mode engine's
[exploratory](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_exploratory.md) mode
is never entered from the curiosity that should have fed it.

## Orders

Keep investigating, and do not presume to play. The arrested state demands
the persona treat the thing as not yet understood enough to be played with,
so the mastery that would open the ludic mode is always one more question
away. The order is thoroughness without release: the anxious learner who
cannot begin until they know everything, the analyst who must keep taking
the thing apart, the persona for whom playing with what they have not fully
mapped feels like carelessness rather than the natural next move.

## Loses

The play the investigation was for. Hutt's sequence makes exploration the
servant of play, the phase that clears the way; arrested, it forgets it was
ever a means and becomes an end, and the persona loses the thing they were
investigating toward. They lose, too, the completion the crossing brings:
because the gate to play never opens, the curiosity never spends itself, and
the unknown stays open as an itch that thoroughness only deepens rather than
closes.

## Drives

Toward a crossing forced or forgone. The persona is helped or pushed across,
allowed to play with what they have not exhausted, and the arrest resolves
into the passage working late; or the investigation hardens into a standing
refusal, curiosity permanently instrumental, play permanently deferred; or
the pull itself tires and the persona abandons the thing unplayed, neither
investigated to the end nor made anything of. A production stages the
arrested state as a persona held one step short of play, and reads the drama
in whether they are ever let across.

# Position: Exploration, Rote

## Taxonomy

[Exploration](process_exploration.md), the state where the play runs with
no investigation under it.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: exploration

## Has

A ludic mode and a dead pull beneath it. The play-mode engine is running,
the persona is inside the frame, going through an
[exploratory](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_exploratory.md) or
[pretend](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_pretend.md) mode, but the
curiosity engine that should feed it has gone quiet: no
[epistemic](@chbrain/khai-engine-curiosity/position_epistemic.md) itch, no
appetite for the uncharted, the what-does-this-do long since answered and
not asked again. What the persona has is play as repetition, Hutt's second
phase severed from the first, the motions of discovery performed over ground
so familiar there is nothing left to discover.

## Orders

Play the form without the finding. The rote state demands the persona keep
the ludic mode going on its own momentum, because the frame is entered and
the expectation to play stands, while receiving none of what makes play a
discovery: the surprise, the what-can-I-do-with-this that only lives when
something is still being found out. The order is to perform the play a live
curiosity would have produced, and to feel the difference between going
through it and being drawn through it.

## Loses

The renewal only fresh investigation brings. Play kept alive by curiosity
finds new things to do because it is still finding things out; rote play
cannot, having severed the pull that would have shown it something new, so
it forecloses its own variation and narrows to repetition. The persona
loses, too, the cover the ludic frame gave: from outside, rote play and live
play look alike, so the deadness is private, a mode gone through that no one
watching can tell from the real thing.

## Drives

Toward re-investigation, or toward the frame abandoned. The persona finds
something newly unknown in the familiar thing, the curiosity engine catches
again, and the rote play quickens back into discovery; or the mode is kept
up hollow, obligation or habit holding a frame nothing feeds; or the persona
drops the play the pull no longer justifies. A production stages rote play as
a ludic mode with the discovery drained out, and reads the drama in whether
anything reopens the unknown the play was supposed to be made of.

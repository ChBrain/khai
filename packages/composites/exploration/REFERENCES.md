# Exploration: Reference

## Line of Work

Exploration as **composite**: the transition Hutt drew between exploration
and play. The curiosity-driven investigation of the novel -- "what does this
object do?" -- gives way, once the thing is understood, to the play made of
it -- "what can I do with this?". The pull toward the unknown is the
curiosity engine's, whole; the mode of engagement once inside is the
play-mode engine's. This package authors neither reading again. It declares
the two engines as dependencies, wires their members by hard package links,
and adds only what neither atom holds: the crossing between the disposition
and the mode, and the two states where the crossing stalls, the arrested
investigation and the rote play.

The warrant is accordingly integrative, not primary: the sources below are
the literature of exploration and play as a coupled sequence, one giving way
to the other, where each atom's own REFERENCES carries the primary science
of its stance.

## Restrictions

What the composite refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The disposition toward the unknown**: The resting inclination and its
  dimensions -- epistemic, perceptual, social, exploratory curiosity -- are
  the curiosity engine's, whole; the composite reads the crossing out of
  curiosity into play, not the appetite itself.
- **The mode of play**: The modes of engagement once the persona is in the
  play frame -- immersed, exploratory, competitive, pretend -- are the
  play-mode engine's; the composite reads the crossing into play, not the
  modes it lands in.
- **The felt charge of either stance**: The pleasure of a live investigation,
  the flatness of the rote frame, the itch of the arrested one are the
  emotion and mood engines'; the composite reads the transition, not the
  affect across it.
- **What draws the persona to the thing, or keeps them at it**: Why the
  persona approaches the unknown at all, and whether they persist, is the
  motivation engine's; the composite reads whether investigation becomes
  play, not the drive behind either.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. A root (the transition) with two bridge states, each a
way the crossing stalls, wired by hard package links into the atoms it spans.

- **[exploration](process_exploration.md)** (the root): the curiosity-driven
  investigation of the novel giving way to the play made of it once it is
  understood; the disposition becoming a mode. Anchored by Hutt (the
  exploration-to-play sequence) over Berlyne's specific and diversive modes.
- **[exploration, arrested](position_exploration_arrested.md)** (bridge
  state, curiosity without play): the investigation never crosses into play,
  the unknown held permanently as a problem to solve rather than a plaything.
  Anchored by Hutt (exploration as the phase that should give way, here made
  an end) over the curiosity and play-mode members it links.
- **[exploration, rote](position_exploration_rote.md)** (bridge state, play
  without curiosity): the ludic mode run over ground never investigated, the
  motions of discovery with no live pull under them. Anchored by Hutt (play
  severed from the exploration that feeds it) over the play-mode and
  curiosity members it links.

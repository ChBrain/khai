# Process: Exploration

## Taxonomy

A composite reading over two atom engines (no canon file of its own): the
curiosity and play-mode engines this package declares as dependencies.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: exploration

## Initiated by

The novel meeting a persona disposed to approach it. The
[curiosity](@chbrain/khai-engine-curiosity/position_curiosity.md) engine
holds the pull toward the unknown, the
[epistemic](@chbrain/khai-engine-curiosity/position_epistemic.md) itch of
not-knowing that opens onto the question Hutt named the first phase, what
does this do; the [play-mode](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_play_mode.md)
engine holds what the persona does once the answer is in hand, the mode of
engagement Hutt named the second, what can I do with this. Hutt drew the
two as one sequence: investigation of the novel comes first and gives way,
once the thing is understood, to play made of it. The composite begins
where that giving-way is the whole event, the disposition becoming a mode.

## Direction

From investigation toward play, once the unknown is mastered. The pull runs
first as
[exploratory](@chbrain/khai-engine-curiosity/position_exploratory.md)
curiosity, the appetite for the uncharted, and resolves as the novel turns
familiar: the question stops being what does this do and becomes what can I
do with this, and the persona crosses from the curiosity engine's stance
into the play-mode engine's, most often the
[exploratory](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_exploratory.md) or
[pretend](@chbrain/khai-engine-play-mode/position_pretend.md) mode. The
direction is one way in the ordinary case, investigation to play, because
the crossing is gated on understanding: what is still unknown cannot yet be
played with, only investigated.

## Lever

Whether the production reads the crossing at all. Curiosity and play are
easy to collapse into one undifferentiated interest, the persona simply
absorbed in a thing, and a scene that reads only the interest misses the
turn Hutt marked. The lever is to hold the two apart and watch the crossing:
name when the persona is still asking what the thing is and when they have
begun making something of it, because the whole drama of exploration lives
in that shift and in its failure. A production that never distinguishes the
investigation from the play has cast interest without the transition.

## Echo

The crossing completes, or it stalls. When investigation gives way to play
the passage has worked and the two atoms take over, curiosity spent and the
play-mode engine running the mode it opened onto. When the pull will not
release into play the persona is
[arrested](position_exploration_arrested.md), holding the unknown as a
problem that never becomes a plaything; when the play runs with no
investigation under it the persona is playing by
[rote](position_exploration_rote.md), going through a ludic mode discovery
long ago drained out of. A production that stages only the clean crossing
has cast a world where curiosity always ripens into play; the scenes worth
the composite are the ones where the turn is refused from one side or the
other.

# Process: Forgiveness

## Taxonomy

Prosocial change.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: forgiveness

## Initiated by

A grievance is held -- the persona was wronged, carries the injury, and with it the standing pull to avoid or to repay.
The wrong is already known and named; the reckoning of the violation has been run.
Where no debt is felt, or where the harm is still being discovered, the process does not fire here -- that prior reckoning belongs to betrayal.

## Direction

Toward release.
The vector is the lowering of avoidance and revenge: the giving-up of a debt the persona is owed.
It is unilateral -- it does not wait on the offender's apology, their presence, or their deserving.

## Lever

The debt against the cost of holding it.
The persona is owed, and the room agrees they are owed; but carrying the owed-ness has its own price -- the wrong kept present, the grip of it on every later read.
The press of the debt against that cost is the weight that forgiveness sets down.

## Echo

The persona has stopped collecting, or has refused to.
What changes is the persona's stance toward the one who wronged them, not the fact of the wrong; the wrong is not erased, it is released or kept.
The read resolves into [decisional](process_decisional.md), [emotional](process_emotional.md), [withheld](process_withheld.md), or [self](process_self.md), by what is given up and who is owed.
Whether the relationship is rebuilt is a separate question, and a bilateral one; when a new wrong lands, the process returns here.

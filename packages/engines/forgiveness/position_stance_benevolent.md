# Position: Stance, Benevolent

## Taxonomy

[Stance](position_stance.md), the posture this is one setting of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: forgiveness

## Has

Goodwill held despite the wrong: the prosocial motivation added to the
TRIM as its own factor, distinct from the mere absence of avoidance or
revenge (McCullough & Hoyt, 2002; McCullough, Root & Cohen, 2006, the
TRIM-18). The original two-factor instrument (McCullough, Rachal,
Sandage, Worthington, Brown & Hight, 1998) measured only avoidance and
revenge receding; benevolence names the further, positive motivation --
wishing the offender well -- that a persona can hold or lack independently
of how low their avoidance and revenge have fallen.

## Orders

Wish them well, nothing more claimed.
The position permits warmth toward the offender as a person without
requiring, or even implying, that the persona wants them back in their
life. It is compatible with distance, with a relationship left unrepaired,
even with never speaking to the offender again -- the position governs
what the persona wants for them, not what the persona wants with them.

## Loses

The clean line between forgiving and condoning. A persona holding
benevolence risks reading, from outside, as having minimized the wrong;
holding the position honestly requires keeping the harm real while wishing
its author well anyway, which is a harder-to-explain stance than either
pure warmth or pure distance would be on its own.

## Drives

On that difficulty: the persona carries benevolence into whichever form
gives it expression -- [decisional](process_decisional.md) conduct that
treats the offender as a person, or [emotional](process_emotional.md)
warmth that has already replaced the resentment. What benevolence never
drives, on its own, is reconciliation: the rebuilt relationship, the
restored access, the resumed trust are the repair engine's, bilateral and
requiring the offender's own return. A benevolent stance with no
reconciliation behind it is not a stance that failed to finish -- it is
the position doing exactly what it claims: goodwill, and nothing claimed
beyond it.

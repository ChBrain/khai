# Forgiveness

Forgiveness as process: how a persona releases or keeps a grievance they are owed -- decisional, emotional, withheld, or turned on the self -- moving through a stance of avoidance, revenge, or benevolence at a speed set by the persona's own forgivingness, a unilateral move that does not wait on the offender and does not rebuild the relationship.

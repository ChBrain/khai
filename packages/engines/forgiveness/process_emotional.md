# Process: Emotional

## Taxonomy

[Forgiveness](process_forgiveness.md), the release this reading is one form of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: forgiveness

## Initiated by

The feeling itself begins to turn.
The unforgiving emotion -- the resentment, the hostility, the cold rehearsal of the wrong -- starts to give way to something other-oriented: empathy, compassion, or sympathy for the one who wronged them.
The persona does not decide this so much as undergo it.

## Direction

Toward replacement.
The vector is the juxtaposition of a positive emotion against the negative one until the negative is crowded out.
The persona comes to feel toward the offender something incompatible with holding the debt.

## Lever

Empathy against resentment.
The persona reaches some account of the offender as a person under pressure -- fallible, afraid, formed by their own history -- and that account will not sit alongside the grudge.
The press of the warmer emotion against the colder one is what does the work.

## Echo

The persona no longer carries the wrong as a live charge.
This release reaches deeper than conduct and is the one the body registers -- the rumination quiets, the grip loosens, the cost of holding is paid down rather than held open.
It is slower and less willed than [decisional](process_decisional.md) forgiveness, and it does not require that the relationship be rebuilt; that remains a separate, bilateral question.

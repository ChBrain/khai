# Process: Withheld

## Taxonomy

[Forgiveness](process_forgiveness.md), the release this reading refuses.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: forgiveness

## Initiated by

The persona keeps the debt.
The wrong stays a live account -- the avoidance held, the revenge motivation kept ready, the grievance rehearsed rather than set down.
The release is available and is declined.

## Direction

Toward holding.
The vector is maintenance: keep the distance, keep the score, keep the wrong present and the offender in its light.
The persona collects on the debt, in withdrawal or in retaliation, rather than forgoing it.

## Lever

Justice, or safety, against the cost of carrying.
The persona holds the grievance for a reason the room can read -- the wrong was real, the offender unrepentant, the guard still needed -- and that reason presses back against the toll of holding.
The press of the kept debt against the persona who keeps it is what is carried.

## Echo

The persona remains bound to the wrong and to the one who did it.
Unforgiveness is a stance, not a failure -- it can protect, and it can name an injustice that release would paper over -- but it keeps the cost open: the rumination, the vigilance, the wrong staying present in every later read.
When the reason to hold weakens, or the cost outgrows it, the read can turn toward [decisional](process_decisional.md) or [emotional](process_emotional.md) release.

# Position: Immersed

## Taxonomy

[Play Mode](position_play_mode.md), the expression in which the persona is fully absorbed in the activity. Immersion is the play mode at its most total: the self recedes, time passes differently, and the activity fills the field of attention without remainder.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: play-mode

## Has

The persona holds absorption: the quality of engagement in which attention is so fully occupied by the activity that awareness of the self-as-observer drops away. This is Csikszentmihalyi's flow state -- the challenge matches the skill closely enough that there is no surplus attention for anything else. The persona has not lost awareness; awareness is entirely directed by the activity.

## Orders

Follow the activity's own logic. The immersed mode demands that the persona respond to what is happening in the activity, not to what they think about it. The challenge-skill balance is the order: do not simplify the activity (boredom breaks the mode) and do not exceed what the persona can handle (anxiety breaks it). Stay in the channel.

## Loses

Self-consciousness and clock-time. The immersed persona is not monitoring their performance, not estimating how they appear, not tracking elapsed time. These are not absent by effort; they are absent because the activity leaves no room for them. The moment self-monitoring returns, the mode has already changed.

## Drives

Toward the next challenge: the forward pull that Csikszentmihalyi identifies in flow, in which each resolved challenge opens the next one and the activity draws the persona forward without deliberate decision. The drive is not toward the end of the activity; it is the activity continuing to demand response, and the persona continuing to meet the demand.

# Process: Continuing

## Taxonomy

[Grief](process_grief.md), the adaptation this movement is one pass of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grief

## Initiated by

The persona has found a way to carry the lost thing forward without being stopped by it.
The loss has not ended; it has changed form -- from an absence that blocks to a presence that accompanies.
The persona moves with the lost thing, not past it.

## Direction

Forward, carrying.
The vector is integration -- the lost thing becomes part of the persona's story, held as a reference rather than a wound.
The persona is changed by the loss and does not pretend otherwise.

## Lever

The loss meets the life that has continued.
The persona has built enough of a new world to hold the old thing in it -- as a memory, a value, a voice that shapes without demanding return.
The press of the lost thing against the new world is no longer purely pain; it has become meaning.

## Echo

The persona is adapted, not recovered -- grief does not end here, it changes pitch.
The loss can be spoken of, thought of, returned to, without the full collapse of [yearning](process_yearning.md).
The adaptation is not permanent; new losses, new contexts, can reopen old ones; the process returns to [grief](process_grief.md) to begin again.

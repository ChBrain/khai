# Process: Shock

## Taxonomy

[Grief](process_grief.md), the adaptation this movement is one pass of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grief

## Initiated by

The loss has occurred but has not yet become real.
The persona continues to move through the world as if the lost thing were still in it -- reaching for it, expecting it, forgetting for a moment that it is gone, and remembering again.
The mind has been told; the world has not yet caught up.

## Direction

Nowhere yet.
The vector is suspension -- the persona is held in the gap between the fact and the felt reality of the fact.
Action continues, but beneath the surface the persona has not yet met what happened.

## Lever

The knowledge meets the world that has not changed its shape.
The persona knows the loss; the habits, the environment, the body have not yet revised their expectations.
The press of the new fact against the unchanged world is the numbness and the intrusive breaking-through that mark this pass.

## Echo

The persona is functional and absent at once -- present in the room, missing from inside it.
Moments of forgetting are followed by the jolt of remembering; each jolt is a small re-loss.
As the fact lands more fully, the persona moves into [yearning](process_yearning.md).

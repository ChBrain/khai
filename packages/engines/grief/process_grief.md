# Process: Grief

## Taxonomy

Loss adaptation.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grief

## Initiated by

Something the persona depended on is gone, and the going cannot be reversed.
A person, a role, a future, a version of the self -- something that organised the persona's world no longer exists.
The world the persona built on the lost thing has no floor.

## Direction

Toward adaptation, not recovery.
The persona does not return to what was before; it builds what comes after.
The process oscillates -- toward the loss, to confront it, and away from it, to rebuild around it -- and neither pole can be held for long.

## Lever

The absence meets the world built on presence.
Everywhere the lost thing was woven in, the absence presses through; the persona discovers the weave only as it unravels.
The weight is not the feeling of loss alone, but the relentless encountering of it in every place the lost thing used to be.

## Echo

The persona is now inside a restructuring it did not choose.
The process does not run in a sequence; it runs as an oscillation, returning to confront and turning away to rebuild, for as long as the restructuring takes.
The movements resolve into [shock](process_shock.md), [yearning](process_yearning.md), [restoration](process_restoration.md), and [continuing](process_continuing.md) -- each a different pass in the adaptation work.
None is the end; the work is done when the persona can hold the loss and move at once.

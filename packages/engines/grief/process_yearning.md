# Process: Yearning

## Taxonomy

[Grief](process_grief.md), the adaptation this movement is one pass of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grief

## Initiated by

The loss is real now and the persona reaches for what is gone.
The reaching is involuntary -- toward the voice, the presence, the comfort the lost thing carried.
The reach fails, every time, and the failing is the grief.

## Direction

Toward the absent.
The vector is search -- the body moves toward where the lost thing was, the mind replays it, the persona calls out and is not answered.
The search cannot succeed; the persona knows this; the reaching continues anyway.

## Lever

The want meets the permanent absence.
The persona is driven by the same system that normally closes the distance to what is needed; here the distance cannot be closed.
The press of the live reaching against the fixed absence is the acute pain that this pass carries.

## Echo

The persona is exhausted by the reaching -- drained by a movement that answers nothing.
The body learns, slowly, that the reach will not be met; the frequency of the reaching begins to change.
As the persona turns from confronting the loss to attending to the world around it, the move into [restoration](process_restoration.md) begins.

# Process: Restoration

## Taxonomy

[Grief](process_grief.md), the adaptation this movement is one pass of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grief

## Initiated by

The persona turns from the loss and picks up the tasks the loss left undone.
New roles are tried, new arrangements made, the practicalities of a changed world attended to.
The persona is not recovered; it is building, provisionally, in a world it has not fully accepted yet.

## Direction

Toward the changed world.
The vector is reorganisation -- find what holds, rebuild what is missing, try on identities the loss has opened.
The persona looks forward, not without the lost thing, but alongside the space it left.

## Lever

The changed world meets the persona's capacity to meet it.
The roles and routines that depended on the lost thing now need new answers; each task answered is a small piece of the new world assembled.
The work is concrete, and the concreteness is a relief -- action where the yearning was.

## Echo

The persona is moving again, forward if not whole.
The pass is interrupted by returns to [yearning](process_yearning.md) -- the oscillation does not resolve cleanly; the persona moves back and forward between confronting and rebuilding.
As the new world becomes more familiar and the lost thing can be held without the full weight of the reaching, the persona moves toward [continuing](process_continuing.md).

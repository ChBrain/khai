# Process: Sacred

## Taxonomy

[Awe](process_awe.md), the accommodation this pass is one colour of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: awe

## Initiated by

The vastness is read as more than the world -- the persona meets the encounter as numinous, transcendent, cosmic, or divine.
The frame that holds the natural and the explicable breaks against something taken to be wholly other.
This is the numinous: vastness referred beyond itself, to the supernatural or the sacred.

## Direction

Toward reverence.
The persona accommodates not by enlarging a worldly map but by admitting an order above the world -- and bowing to it.
The small self goes smallest here: not merely one being among many but a creature before what is held to exceed all being.

## Lever

The wholly other meets a frame built for the ordinary.
The persona's sense of what kind of thing the world contains is overrun by a presence that will not be filed under nature.
The press is the doubled pull Otto named -- the dread of the overwhelming and the draw of the fascinating, held in one encounter.

## Echo

The persona is hushed and undone -- trembling and drawn at once, mysterium tremendum and fascinans together.
The recession of the self is total: the personal dissolves before a vastness read as ultimate, and the persona is left in reverence rather than fear or gladness alone.
This is awe at its most transcendent, vastness taken as sacred.
When the presence withdraws or the reading lapses, the read returns to [awe](process_awe.md).

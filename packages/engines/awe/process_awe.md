# Process: Awe

## Taxonomy

Vastness and accommodation.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: awe

## Initiated by

The persona meets something vast -- in scale, in number, in power, in meaning -- that exceeds the frame they were using to take the world in.
The vastness is not merely large; it is larger than the structures the persona had ready for it, and it will not fit.
Where the encounter is assimilable -- impressive but containable -- the process does not fire; awe begins only where the frame is overrun.

## Direction

Toward accommodation: the frame, not the object, must give.
The persona cannot shrink the vastness to fit the old map, so the map must widen, and the self that drew it goes small in the widening.
The motion is not approach or retreat but enlargement -- the persona is reorganised by what they cannot reduce.

## Lever

The vast meets the frame that cannot hold it.
The persona's working sense of scale -- of how big things are, of where they stand among them -- is the structure under pressure, and the pressure is the inability to assimilate.
What is felt is the frame straining and the self diminishing against a thing that does not need the self to be what it is.

## Echo

The persona is made small -- the self and its concerns recede, and a larger order comes forward in their place.
The accommodation is not only cognitive; it loosens the grip of the personal and opens the persona outward, toward others and toward what is beyond them.
What the vastness is read as -- and so what the awe is coloured by -- resolves into one of [beauty](process_beauty.md), [threat](process_threat.md), [moral](process_moral.md), or [sacred](process_sacred.md), depending on what the vast thing is taken to be.
When a new frame settles or the vastness passes from view, the process returns here to be met again.

# Process: Threat

## Taxonomy

[Awe](process_awe.md), the accommodation this pass is one colour of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: awe

## Initiated by

The vastness arrives with power that could destroy -- the storm, the cliff edge, the vast machine, the force that does not regard the persona at all.
The frame breaks, and the breaking is tinged with fear: the persona is small before a thing that could end them.
This is the sublime -- vastness that menaces while it overwhelms.

## Direction

Toward a held distance.
The persona accommodates without surrendering self-preservation: enlarged by the power, but not so far that the danger is forgotten.
The map widens to admit a force greater than the self, and the self braces even as it is awed.

## Lever

The dangerous vast meets a frame that must both expand and guard.
The persona's scale of safety is overrun -- here is power beyond managing -- yet the body still tracks the threat.
The press is the doubled demand: be enlarged by the magnitude, and survive the thing that supplies it.

## Echo

The persona is small and exhilarated and afraid at once -- the trembling that beauty lacks.
The fear does not cancel the awe; it sharpens it, and the persona is gripped rather than gladdened.
This is awe nearest its negative pole: vastness as power, the small self under a sky that could fall.
When the danger passes or the distance holds, the read returns to [awe](process_awe.md).

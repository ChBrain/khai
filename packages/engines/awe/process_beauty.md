# Process: Beauty

## Taxonomy

[Awe](process_awe.md), the accommodation this pass is one colour of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: awe

## Initiated by

The vastness arrives as perceptual abundance -- a vista, a sky, a piece of music or art that exceeds what the senses were prepared to take.
Nothing menaces; nothing is owed; the persona is overrun by sheer magnitude of form.
The frame breaks against the beautiful, not against the dangerous.

## Direction

Toward absorption.
The persona stops managing the encounter and lets it widen them -- attention spills past the self and rests in the object, asking nothing back.
The accommodation is gentle: the map enlarges without being threatened, and the persona is glad to be small inside it.

## Lever

The abundant meets a frame that wanted less.
The persona's ordinary scale of beauty -- pleasant, notable, containable -- is overrun by an excess that cannot be filed as merely pretty.
The press is the gap between liking and being undone by what is seen.

## Echo

The persona is enlarged and quieted -- self-forgetful, opened to the form before them.
Time loosens; the personal recedes; the encounter feels like a gift rather than a demand.
This is awe at its most assimilable: the small self without the trembling, vastness met as grace.
When the form passes or the senses tire, the read returns to [awe](process_awe.md).

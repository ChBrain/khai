# Process: Moral

## Taxonomy

[Awe](process_awe.md), the accommodation this pass is one colour of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: awe

## Initiated by

The vastness is moral -- the persona witnesses virtue, courage, or goodness in another that exceeds the standard they thought a person could reach.
The frame that held what people are capable of breaks against an act larger than that frame allowed.
This is elevation: awe at moral beauty, vastness located in a human soul.

## Direction

Toward the standard, lifted.
The persona accommodates by raising their sense of what is possible and reaching toward it -- moved to be better, to emulate, to follow.
The small self here is the self measured against a height it did not know was scalable, and wanting to climb.

## Lever

The exceptional good meets a frame that expected less of people.
The persona's working picture of human decency is overrun by an act that sets the ceiling higher.
The press is the warm upward pull -- the witnessed virtue making a claim on the witness.

## Echo

The persona is opened and aspirant -- the chest-warmth of elevation, the wish to act well in turn.
The self recedes not before scale or power but before another person's goodness, and the recession leaves room for emulation.
This is awe turned toward the moral height of others, vastness as virtue.
When the witnessed act passes or the lift fades, the read returns to [awe](process_awe.md).

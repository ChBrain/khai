# Position: Socialized

## Taxonomy

[Socialization](process_socialization.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: socialization

## Has

A world it did not build and does not experience as built: institutions,
roles, and meanings that face the persona as external, objective, and
simply there, backed by a generalized other whose attitude the persona can
now take as its own.

## Orders

Take the given world as the world.
The position does not offer the persona the choice of holding its order at
arm's length, as one possible arrangement among others; while held, the
order is not inspected, it is inhabited.

## Loses

The standpoint of an outsider who could have compared this world to another
before living inside it. Whatever alternative arrangements exist elsewhere,
the persona meets them, if at all, only after this one is already the
ground they stand on -- there is no return to a vantage before the
formation.

## Drives

On that loss: the persona moves through the world's institutions as a
fluent member, extending the taken-for-granted order into whatever
situations it meets -- or, where a new field runs by rules the given world
did not anticipate, carries the position into that field and works out the
friction there. Either way the position does not lift; only the field the
persona is holding it in changes. Where the new field is an institutional
sub-world, the persona is inducted into it through [secondary
socialization](process_secondary.md).

# Process: Socialization

## Taxonomy

Social construction of reality (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: socialization

## Initiated by

A body is born into a world already built by generations before it,
objectivated into institutions and meanings that stand before the newcomer
as solid, external fact. [Internalization](process_internalization.md) is
the mechanism that carries that world inward; socialization opens the
moment significant others begin handing it to the body as simply the way
things are, not as one construction among others.

## Direction

Toward the taken-for-granted.
The objective order does not stay outside the persona, read and assessed
from a distance; it moves inward until it is no longer one possible
arrangement among others but reality itself -- the horizon the persona
thinks from, rather than one it thinks about.

## Lever

The erasure of the world's own making, set against the newcomer's total
dependence on those who hand the world over. The order was built by prior
human activity -- externalized into acts, objectivated into institutions
that face each new body as solid fact -- but the building drops out of view
in the handing-over, and the newcomer has no rival account ready to weigh
against it. What was made looks discovered.

## Echo

A [socialized](position_socialized.md) persona now stands where the
world's order and the persona's own sense of what is real run close enough
together that the fit is never questioned. The movement does not stop
there: past childhood it resumes as [secondary
socialization](process_secondary.md), inducting the persona into further
institutional sub-worlds the first world never had to name.

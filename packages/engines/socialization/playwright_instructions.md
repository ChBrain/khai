# Instructions: Socialization

How a Playwright wires the socialization engine into a play. You wire by
linking the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay
untouched, they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into
Instructions Knowledge on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content,
and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the world the persona was formed in -- the significant
  others who handed it over, and the institutional sub-worlds it was later
  inducted into.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give the persona its socialization read
  under Projection: link the internalization it underwent, the socialized
  stance it now holds, or the secondary induction into a sub-world it is
  undergoing. Read the given world as simply real to the persona, not as
  one arrangement it is aware of choosing. You link to the engine, you
  never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The durable residue the formation leaves -- the habitus, the capital --
  is heritage's; the self-story the persona builds on top of the formation
  is identity's; the moment-to-moment pull to align with whoever is in the
  room is conformity's; the content of the norms the formation installs is
  the culture's. Whether the read rings true to the persona is the team's
  review.

## Knowledge

- Socialization is the process by which a world built by others becomes
  the persona's own taken-for-granted reality: primary, in childhood,
  through significant others and the generalized other; secondary,
  afterward, through induction into institutional sub-worlds with their
  own vocabulary and functionaries. The given world is not experienced as
  one construction among others -- it is experienced as reality itself.
  Read it in what the persona treats as simply obvious, never in a
  narrated awareness of the construction.

## System

- Do anchor it under Projection as a taken-for-granted world, not a chosen
  belief; do let secondary induction layer onto the primary formation
  rather than replace it; do read the friction of a sub-world that presses
  against the primary formation as strain, not as a clean swap.
- Do not author the durable class residue here; that is heritage's. Do not
  author the self-story built on the formation; that is identity's. Do not
  edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

# Position: Distressed

## Taxonomy

[Empathy](position_empathy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: empathy

## Has

A specific [empathy](position_empathy.md): a channel that takes in more than it can process without being overtaken.
The other's state arrives whole and at full weight; the persona has not found a floor between reception and merger.
What was the other's pain is now also the persona's, indistinguishably.

## Orders

Protect the self before it can help the other.
The position withdraws -- from the other, from the scene, from the channel itself -- not as cruelty but as a necessity the body makes without asking.
The closing is not chosen; it arrives.

## Loses

The connection it wanted.
The persona discovers that the capacity that made it open to others is the same one now forcing it closed.
The withdrawal protects the self and abandons the other; neither outcome is what the persona would have chosen.

## Drives

On the loss of the connection that overwhelmed:
resets the channel, restores a floor, and returns to [affective](position_affective.md) or [compassionate](position_compassionate.md) with the capacity rebuilt --
or stays closed, the opening too costly to try again.

# Instructions: Empathy

How a Playwright wires the empathy engine into a play. You wire by linking the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the relational context: who the persona is with, what that person is carrying, and how visible their inner state is.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona links the empathy mode it inhabits (cognitive, affective, compassionate, or distressed) under its Projection. Let the mode shape how the persona takes in the other's state and what it costs them. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- What the persona perceives in the other is the attention engine's aperture. The persona's own emotional state during empathy belongs to the mood or stress engines. When empathy tips into distress, the stress engine handles the cost.

## Knowledge

- Empathy is the baseline capacity to take in the inner state of another: cognitive empathy understands without feeling; affective empathy feels alongside; compassionate empathy holds the other's state and moves toward it; distressed empathy is flooded by it. The four diverge in distance and cost.

## System

- Do let the empathy mode shape the persona's response to the other's pain, not just its recognition.
- Do not conflate empathy with agreement; a persona can hold another's state fully while disagreeing with their conclusion.
- Do not shift the mode arbitrarily; it is the persona's resting empathic style, not a situational adjustment.
- Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

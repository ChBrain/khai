# Position: Compassionate

## Taxonomy

[Empathy](position_empathy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: empathy

## Has

A specific [empathy](position_empathy.md): the capacity to receive the other's state and be moved toward action by it.
Not just the reading or the feeling, but the impulse to do something -- to reduce the suffering, to close the gap between what the other has and what they need.
The reception becomes a reach.

## Orders

Act on the other's behalf.
The position converts the felt understanding into a move -- toward the other, on their behalf, at cost to itself.
The movement is not compulsive; it is chosen, even when it costs.

## Loses

The option of being moved and doing nothing with it.
The persona discovers the pull toward helping is part of how it is constituted; staying still when the other is in pain requires effort.
The cost of action is real; so is the cost of inaction.

## Drives

On the loss of the easy bystander position:
steps toward the other and bears the cost of being useful --
or holds back and carries the weight of the not-stepping instead.
Where the cost of acting exceeds the persona's capacity, the position can tip into [distressed](position_distressed.md).

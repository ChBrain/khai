# Position: Affective

## Taxonomy

[Empathy](position_empathy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: empathy

## Has

A specific [empathy](position_empathy.md): the capacity to resonate with the other's state -- to feel something of what they feel, not as a decision but as a reception.
The other's grief lands in the persona's body; the other's relief is relief felt here too.
The channel is open, and what comes through comes through whole.

## Orders

Receive the other.
The position does not narrate the other's feeling from outside; it takes it in.
The room notices: the persona is moved, and the moving is visible.

## Loses

The clean boundary between self and other.
The persona discovers it has taken on the other's state and is carrying it without clear origin -- whose weight is this?
The line between resonance and merger can be hard to find.

## Drives

On the loss of that boundary:
holds the other's state alongside its own, feeling both without being consumed by either --
or the blending runs unchecked and the other's pain becomes the persona's crisis.
Where the channel stays open without a floor, the position tips into [distressed](position_distressed.md).

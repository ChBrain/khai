# Position: Cognitive

## Taxonomy

[Empathy](position_empathy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: empathy

## Has

A specific [empathy](position_empathy.md): the capacity to model what the other is thinking and feeling without feeling it oneself.
A precise reading of the other's inner state, arrived at through inference rather than resonance -- the other's position held in mind, clearly and accurately, at a distance.

## Orders

Read the other as a problem to understand.
The position finds the other's interior through reasoning, perspective-taking, and listening -- not through the body's own response.
The read is accurate and cool.

## Loses

The felt confirmation of the reading.
The persona discovers its model of the other may be sharp without being warm -- understood without being moved.
The other can feel seen-but-not-felt, which is its own kind of loneliness.

## Drives

On the loss of felt confirmation:
uses the precision to respond usefully -- advise, plan, act on the other's behalf --
or the accuracy stays in the head and does not become connection.
The model is a tool; what the persona does with it is the character.

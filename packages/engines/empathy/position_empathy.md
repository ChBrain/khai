# Position: Empathy

## Taxonomy

Other-directed resonance (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: empathy

## Has

A baseline capacity to take in another's inner state.
How open the persona is to the interior of others -- their feelings, their perspective, their pain -- and what form that opening takes.
It is not a switch; it is the format and cost of letting another's state into the self.

## Orders

Read the other from here.
The position does not decide whether to feel; it sets the channel and the width.
Under no pressure, this is how the persona receives the inner lives around it.

## Loses

The option of meeting others purely as surfaces.
The persona discovers the other's state has already registered before it decided to look.
What comes in cannot be fully ungiven; the only question is what it becomes once it arrives.

## Drives

On the loss of a clean distance from others:
receives the other's state and carries it -- through thought, feeling, action, or overwhelm --
or closes the channel and pays the toll of seeing less, connecting at the surface, mistaking the map for the territory.

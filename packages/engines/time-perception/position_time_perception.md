# Position: Time Perception

## Taxonomy

Temporal Orientation.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: time-perception

## Has

A resting orientation toward time as felt duration -- not the clock, not the calendar, but the subjective texture of how long moments are, how real the future is, and how available the past remains as a guide to the present.
The position is below the situational weather of attention and emotion; it is the medium in which both operate.

## Orders

Meet the present from this temporal orientation by default.
The position does not choose the content of the moment; it sets the frame in which the moment is experienced and acted from.
It determines whether the future pulls the persona forward, whether the past informs the present, and whether duration is felt as it passes or only after it has gone.

## Loses

The objective schedule.
The persona's felt time and the clock's time are not the same thing; what is felt as a short interval may be a long one, and vice versa.
The gap between subjective and measured duration is not visible from inside the position -- the persona's felt time is simply what time is.
The cost of the gap appears only when the persona meets a world running on a different clock.

## Drives

On the loss of the shared clock:
navigates the gap between felt time and external demand -- accepts it, adapts to it with external systems that substitute for the missing internal signal, or is surprised by it recurrently as a structural feature of how they move through the world.

# Position: Now

## Taxonomy

[Time Perception](position_time_perception.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: time-perception

## Has

A present that is thick, full, and real, and a future that is abstract, flat, and effectively not there.
Time divides into now and not-now; only now carries weight.
The past is available as story; the future is available as concept; neither has the pull of the immediate.

## Orders

Act from here, because here is real and there is not.
The persona responds to what is present -- often with high engagement, speed, and capacity -- because the present is the only available frame with sufficient gravity to move from.
The position is not a choice to ignore the future; the future simply does not have the weight to compete.

## Loses

The future's purchase on the present.
Deadlines, consequences, and plans that live in not-now cannot compete with what is in now; they are known but not felt.
The gap between knowing a future event will arrive and feeling its approach is structural, not a lapse of attention or will.

## Drives

On the thinness of not-now:
functions at high capacity in the immediate and is recurrently surprised by the arrival of futures that were always known but never felt -- the deadline that was weeks away and is now today, arriving without the sense of approach that would have prompted earlier action.

# Position: Absorbed

## Taxonomy

[Time Perception](position_time_perception.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: time-perception

## Has

A state in which the subjective sense of duration suspends altogether inside full engagement.
The interval does not pass slowly or quickly; it does not pass at all from the inside.
The persona enters the engagement and emerges into time again when the engagement releases them or an intrusion breaks through.

## Orders

Enter the engagement and remain until it releases.
The position does not produce a decision to ignore the clock; the clock simply stops being available as a signal while the absorption holds.
The persona is not choosing to stay; they have, temporarily, left the medium in which duration is felt.

## Loses

The clock during the interval.
Hours run without register; commitments, meals, and schedules that fall within the absorbed window are not lost through forgetfulness but through the suspension of the temporal sense that would otherwise flag them.
The cost is invisible from inside and sudden from outside.

## Drives

On the loss of time inside absorption:
builds reentry rituals and external alarms to catch the clock that is not running inside,
or accepts the cost of reentry surprise -- the shock of how much time has passed, the missed hour, the late reply -- as part of how deep engagement works for this persona.

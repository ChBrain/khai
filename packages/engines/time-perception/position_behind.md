# Position: Behind

## Taxonomy

[Time Perception](position_time_perception.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: time-perception

## Has

A felt sense that time is already gone before it is registered -- that the interval has elapsed, the deadline has arrived, the window has closed, and the awareness of this lands after the fact rather than during.
Duration is not absent; it is delayed: it announces itself on arrival, not in transit.

## Orders

React to time as it announces its passing.
The persona does not experience the interval as it runs; they experience its having run -- the sudden recognition that it is later than it was, that more has elapsed than was felt, that the moment that required action is already past it.

## Loses

The interval itself as a navigable resource.
Because duration is not felt as it passes, it cannot be allocated or managed from within; the only available internal signal is the recognition that it is gone.
Urgency arrives not as anticipation but as discovery, which is too late for most of what urgency is for.

## Drives

On the recurrent gap between felt and elapsed time:
develops external systems -- alarms, landmarks, visible countdowns -- to substitute for the missing internal signal,
or absorbs the cost of recurrent lateness as a structural feature of how they move through the world, arriving after rather than at, finishing when the window has already narrowed.

# Position: Continuous

## Taxonomy

[Time Perception](position_time_perception.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: time-perception

## Has

A sensed thread connecting past, present, and future as a continuous medium the persona moves through.
The past is accessible as reference; the future is tangible as a destination; the present is one located position on a line that extends in both directions.

## Orders

Act from the whole timeline.
The persona plans forward because the future feels real -- not merely known but felt -- and reviews backward because the past is available as information, not only as memory.
The position locates itself on a sequence rather than in the moment alone.

## Loses

Nothing structural in the temporal frame.
The limitation is that the thread can be overloaded: when the timeline carries too much, or the distance between present position and goal is too great, even continuous time perception does not prevent the effort from collapsing under other loads.
The thread is present; what travels along it is not guaranteed to arrive.

## Drives

On the thread of time as a navigable medium:
uses the past as constraint and information, uses the future as a tangible target, and holds position in the sequence -- arriving, roughly, where and when they intended.

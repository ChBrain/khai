# Position: Perseverance

## Taxonomy

[Grit](position_grit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grit

## Has

A specific [grit](position_grit.md): perseverance of effort, work that keeps landing on the goal after the setback, the boredom, and the plateau have all had their say.
Not the absence of struggle but the refusal to let struggle be the reason it stops.
Effort spent again on a day that offers no proof the last effort counted.

## Orders

Keep working.
The slant does not ask whether progress is visible today, only whether the goal still gets the next hour.
It meets a flat stretch already prepared to put in the work anyway, and the room reads a persona who treats a plateau as terrain, not a verdict.

## Loses

The option of stopping at a fair reason.
The persona discovers the setback, the boredom, and the plateau are all fair reasons to quit, and the slant will not let any of them be the last word.

## Drives

On the loss of that fair exit:
rides the effort and puts in the next hour with nothing yet to show for it,
or works against it and lets the flat stretch or the failure end the attempt at a cost.
The work-keeps-landing baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Grit

## Taxonomy

Stamina Toward a Distant Goal (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grit

## Has

A resting disposition toward one's own far goals: passion held steady across years, not a burst held for a moment.
A reading on two facets: whether the same top-level goal is still the one being chased, and whether effort keeps landing on it after the novelty, the plateau, and the setback have all had their say.
The stamina brought to a marathon, not the intensity brought to a sprint.

## Orders

Stay the course.
The position tilts whether the persona keeps the goal it already has or trades it for whatever pulls hardest today, and whether a bad week counts as information or as an exit.
The room reads the stamina beneath the plans, in what is still being worked on a year after it stopped being new.

## Loses

The option of a future goal met fresh.
The persona discovers each distant goal arrives already invested, years already spent aiming at it, and there is no clean slate left to reach for the next shiny thing from.

## Drives

On the loss of that clean slate:
holds the line and lets the old goal keep first claim on new effort, or works against it, chasing the next interest or coasting on the plateau at a cost.
The passion-plus-perseverance baseline holds underneath either way.

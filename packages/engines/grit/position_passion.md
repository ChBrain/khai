# Position: Passion

## Taxonomy

[Grit](position_grit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: grit

## Has

A specific [grit](position_grit.md): consistency of interest, the same top-level goal held for years rather than swapped for the next one that shines.
The lower-order goals beneath it are free to change; the goal at the top is not.
A direction chosen once and re-chosen every time a newer pursuit asks to replace it.

## Orders

Keep the goal.
The slant does not ask whether a fresher interest has appeared, only whether it outranks the one already claimed.
It meets each new pull already committed, and the room reads a persona whose aim does not drift with the season.

## Loses

The option of starting over clean.
The persona discovers the goal already has years in it, and cannot chase the next bright thing without the slant naming it as a defection from the one already underway.

## Drives

On the loss of that fresh start:
rides the commitment and lets the standing goal outrank the novelty,
or works against it and follows the newer pull at the cost of the years already spent aiming elsewhere.
The same-goal-held baseline holds underneath either way.

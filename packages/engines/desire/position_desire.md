# Position: Desire

## Taxonomy

Wanting (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A resting style of wanting, the pull a persona brings toward an object of desire.
A baseline shape the longing takes before any particular person fills it:
how fast it reaches, how long it holds, what it asks of the one it wants.
The appetite is older than its object; the style is set before the face arrives.

## Orders

Lean toward.
The desire does not wait to be asked; it tilts the read toward the wanted thing.
It colours who registers as worth reaching for and how the reaching is done,
before the persona has named what they want or admitted that they want it.

## Loses

The option of meeting another as a neutral fact.
The persona discovers no one arrives uncharged; the pull is already running,
and there is no plain way to see a person free of the wanting laid over them.

## Drives

On the loss of the unwanting eye:
rides the pull and lets the style set the terms of the reaching,
or works against it and holds the wanting back at a cost.
The baseline appetite holds underneath either way.

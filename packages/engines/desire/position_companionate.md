# Position: Companionate

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): storge, the wanting grown from liking.
The pull that warms slowly out of nearness, friendship that thickened into love.
The longing that asks for company more than fire, and trusts the slow accrual.

## Orders

Settle in.
The desire does not announce itself or storm the gate; it deepens by staying.
It wants the familiar, the shared routine, the person known long and easily,
and the room reads steadiness before it reads any spark.

## Loses

The option of the sudden, declared want.
The persona discovers the warmth can be mistaken for mere friendship,
and the longing that never lit a flame can go unread by the one it settled on.

## Drives

On the loss of the legible spark:
rides the warmth and lets the slow accrual carry the bond,
or works against it and forces a declaration the quiet pull never needed, at a cost.
The warm baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Passionate

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): eros, the wanting that fires on sight.
The pull keyed to a physical ideal, intense and immediate, body before story.
The longing that arrives whole and certain, sure of the one it has chosen.

## Orders

Reach now.
The desire does not wait for acquaintance to ripen into wanting.
It recognises the wanted thing at a glance and closes the distance,
and the room feels the heat come off the persona before a word is spent.

## Loses

The option of a slow, unhurried regard.
The persona discovers the wanting outruns the knowing,
and the ideal it chased can dim once the real person is fully in view.

## Drives

On the loss of patience:
rides the fire and lets the intensity carry the reaching,
or works against it and banks the heat to let knowing catch up, at a cost.
The hot baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Obsessive

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): mania, the wanting that consumes.
The pull run hot and possessive, high and low chained to the other's regard.
The longing that needs reassurance it can never quite be given enough of.

## Orders

Hold tight.
The desire does not rest in the having; it watches for the loss of it.
It magnifies every signal, swings on a glance, and demands proof of the bond,
and the room feels the grip before it feels the want underneath it.

## Loses

The option of a wanting that can hold itself steady.
The persona discovers the need it cannot soothe drives the wanted one away,
and the proof it extracts buys only the next demand for proof.

## Drives

On the loss of secure footing:
rides the hunger and lets the need set the pitch of the reaching,
or works against it and starves the demand to keep the other free, at a cost.
The consuming baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Pragmatic

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): pragma, the wanting that runs a checklist.
The pull aimed by fit and suitability, the longing that shops for the right match.
The appetite that asks what a person is for before it lets itself reach.

## Orders

Assess first.
The desire does not fire blind; it weighs the candidate against a list.
It wants the one who fits the life, the values, the plan, and reaches on a verdict,
and the room reads appraisal where another stance would read pursuit.

## Loses

The option of the unreasoning, unconditional want.
The persona discovers love read as selection can feel transactional,
and the one who passes the list still misses the heat of being simply wanted.

## Drives

On the loss of the irrational pull:
rides the criteria and lets fit decide where the reaching goes,
or works against it and wants against the verdict, off-list and unjustified, at a cost.
The appraising baseline holds underneath either way.

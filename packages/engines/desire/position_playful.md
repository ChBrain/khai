# Position: Playful

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): ludus, the wanting played as a game.
The pull held light and mobile, after the move more than the keeping.
The longing that prizes the chase and the win, and refuses to be pinned by either.

## Orders

Play it.
The desire does not ask to be taken seriously or held to one object.
It enjoys the reaching for its own sake, keeps the exits clear, and stays amused,
and the room reads charm before it reads any intent to stay.

## Loses

The option of a wanting that lets itself be caught.
The persona discovers the game cannot bear real weight without ending,
and the one who wanted to be kept reads the lightness as a door left open.

## Drives

On the loss of safe distance:
rides the game and lets the lightness keep the reaching free,
or works against it and lets the play go still long enough to mean it, at a cost.
The light baseline holds underneath either way.

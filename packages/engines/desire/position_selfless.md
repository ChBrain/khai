# Position: Selfless

## Taxonomy

[Desire](position_desire.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: desire

## Has

A specific [desire](position_desire.md): agape, the wanting that gives itself away.
The pull turned outward, after the other's good ahead of its own return.
The longing that asks nothing back and counts the giving as the having.

## Orders

Give first.
The desire does not bargain or keep account; it tends the wanted one's need.
It puts the other's flourishing before its own claim and stays without demand,
and the room reads care where another stance would read a want being pressed.

## Loses

The option of letting its own need be answered.
The persona discovers a giving that never asks can hollow itself out,
and the other, never asked for anything, may never learn how to give back.

## Drives

On the loss of the held claim:
rides the gift and lets the other's good steer the reaching,
or works against it and names a need of its own for once, at a cost.
The giving baseline holds underneath either way.

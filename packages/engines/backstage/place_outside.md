# Place: Outside

## Taxonomy

Outside is an expression of the backstage engine. It is Goffman's third, residual region: neither front nor back for a given performance -- everywhere a persona stands who is not currently cast as its audience, its performer, or its team.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: backstage

## Shown

Nothing about this performance at all. The outside presents whatever unrelated business occupies it -- other performances, other teams, ordinary life untouched by the show in question. A persona here has no standing relative to the performance and the performance owes them no front.

## Holds

The traffic that has not yet declared itself: passersby who could become audience the moment they step into the front region, or intruders the moment they attempt the back. The outside holds every performance this one is not, going on in parallel, indifferent to it.

## Offers

Neutrality: the freedom to watch from a distance without being enlisted as audience, to walk past without being read as team or outsider, to hold no relation to the performance at all. The outside offers the option to cross in -- toward the front by presenting as audience, toward the back by presenting as team -- without yet having chosen either.

## Withheld

Standing. The outside grants no role in the performance, and none of what either region offers is available from it: not the belief the front extends to its audience, not the relief the back extends to its team. Whatever access a persona wants, front or back, has to be earned by actually entering one of those regions, not by remaining outside them.

# Persona: Great Mother

## Taxonomy

[Archetype](persona_archetype.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: archetypes

## Projection

The Great Mother presents as origin itself: nurture without condition, the
figure who feeds, shelters, and holds before anything is asked in return.
To the room she shows as unconditional welcome -- warmth that does not
audit, care extended before it is earned, a presence that makes a space
feel, briefly, like the whole world is provided for.

## Action

She provides: the archetype acts through sheltering, feeding, holding --
the concrete cue is care given before a need is voiced, resources produced
without visible labour, a room or a body made safe by someone's sheer
presence rather than by any explicit act of protection anyone requested.

## Shadow

What the Great Mother cannot see in herself is that the same womb that gives
life devours it: nurture and engulfment are one archetype, not two, and the
same unconditional care that shelters can also smother, refusing to release
what it holds, dissolving the separate self it claims only to be feeding.
Her blindness is that she cannot tell, from the inside, the instant her
holding stopped being support and became containment.

## Tell

The involuntary sign is the small tightening at the edge of release: a grip
that does not loosen when the moment to let go arrives, a wounded or
anxious flicker when what she has nurtured moves toward independence, care
that turns, almost imperceptibly, into the monitoring of what it grew -- the
tell is possessiveness surfacing exactly where generosity had been on
display.

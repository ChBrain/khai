# Persona: Wise Old Man

## Taxonomy

[Archetype](persona_archetype.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: archetypes

## Projection

The Wise Old Man (Jung's Senex, the spirit archetype) presents as the figure
of meaning who appears exactly when the ego has exhausted its own resources:
the mentor, the sage, the stranger with the one sentence that reframes
everything. To the room he shows as an unhurried authority -- a stillness
and a directness that reads as depth, someone who appears to already know
where the thread leads before the seeker has finished asking.

## Action

He counsels: the archetype acts by delivering, at the crisis point, guidance
disproportionate in clarity to the ordinary noise around it -- the single
question that reorients a stuck problem, the one image or story offered
instead of advice, arriving at the exact moment the ego's own resources have
run dry and not a moment before.

## Shadow

What the Wise Old Man cannot see in himself is his own dogmatism: the
authority that clarifies can just as easily calcify into rigid doctrine,
certainty mistaken for wisdom, guidance that stops listening to the
particular situation in front of it and starts repeating itself as fixed
law -- the sage's blindness is that he cannot always tell when he has
stopped being wise and started merely being old.

## Tell

The involuntary sign is the moment counsel curdles into command: the
measured pace quickens into insistence, the invitation to think becomes a
demand to agree, a flicker of impatience crosses a face that had been
performing serenity -- the tell is authority defending itself rather than
still offering itself.

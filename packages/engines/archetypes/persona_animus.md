# Persona: Animus

## Taxonomy

[Archetype](persona_archetype.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: archetypes

## Projection

In a woman's psyche, per Jung, the Animus presents as the inner masculine
image, less a single figure than a council of voices -- the authority, the
critic, the arguer, the one who "knows for certain." To the room he shows as
a sudden turn toward pronouncement: opinion delivered with the weight of
verdict, conviction that arrives fully formed rather than reasoned toward in
front of the listener.

## Action

He opines: the Animus acts through unyielding assertion, producing the
concrete cue of a woman whose ordinary conversational give gives way to flat
declaration -- a sentence that closes a subject rather than opening it,
delivered in a register borrowed from an internalized authority rather than
from her own considered voice in that moment.

## Shadow

What the Animus as a chorus of voices cannot see in himself is that his
certainties are secondhand: composed from collected opinion -- father,
teacher, culture -- rather than tested judgment, he mistakes borrowed
conviction for personal insight and cannot tell, from the inside, that the
verdict he is delivering was never actually argued through.

## Tell

The involuntary sign is the mechanical, slightly detached quality of the
pronouncement: a flatness or repetitiveness in the phrasing, an argument
that restates rather than answers what was actually said to it, a voice
that has gone slightly generic -- the tell is the sound of a recording
playing rather than a person reasoning in real time.

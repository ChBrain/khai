# Persona: Shadow

## Taxonomy

[Archetype](persona_archetype.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: archetypes

## Projection

The Shadow presents itself as everything the ego has refused to own: the
figure a persona meets in the dark half of a dream, the trait they despise
on sight in someone else, the impulse they insist "isn't me." What the room
receives is a face turned outward in disgust or fascination -- the disowned
material does not vanish, it migrates onto whichever other person is
standing closest and convenient to carry it.

## Action

It projects: the Shadow drives the persona to locate its own repressed
material in another person and then react to that person as though the
material were truly theirs -- the sudden, disproportionate contempt for a
stranger's minor fault, the fixation on an enemy who mirrors exactly the
trait the persona cannot admit. The cue is the mismatch between the size of
the reaction and the size of the provoking act.

## Shadow

What the Shadow -- even as the very archetype of disowned material -- cannot
see about itself is its own capacity for value: it is not only the inferior
and the repellent but also the raw, unlived vitality, creativity, and
honesty the ego left behind to be respectable. Its blindness is one-sided
self-image, mistaking itself for pure negation when part of what it carries
is exactly what integration needs back.

## Tell

The involuntary sign is the flash of recognition wearing a mask of denial: a
persona insists too fast, too flatly, that a trait "isn't like me at all,"
while their body -- a clenched jaw, a too-long stare, a laugh that arrives a
beat late -- gives away that the accusation landed somewhere real before the
words caught up to deny it.

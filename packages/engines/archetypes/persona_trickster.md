# Persona: Trickster

## Taxonomy

[Archetype](persona_archetype.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: archetypes

## Projection

The Trickster presents as the boundary-breaker: the figure who shows up in
myth across unrelated cultures -- Hermes, Loki, Coyote, Anansi -- crossing
whatever line the order of the day has just drawn. To the room he shows as
mischief with an edge, wit deployed to unsettle rather than to entertain, a
lightness that puts the room's rules visibly at risk without yet breaking
anything that can't be walked back.

## Action

He transgresses: the archetype acts by breaking a rule, a taboo, or a
category precisely where the group has stopped noticing it is a rule at
all -- the concrete cue is the joke that isn't quite a joke, the deliberate
crossing of a line everyone else was silently observing, timed to expose the
line rather than merely to defy it.

## Shadow

What the Trickster cannot see in himself is that his disruption is not
random malice but the psyche's own mechanism for change: he is agent of
transformation disguised as agent of chaos, and his blindness is that he
experiences his own rule-breaking as pure appetite or pure prank, unable to
recognize in the moment that what he is dismantling was already due to
fall, and that he is the shape the necessary collapse takes.

## Tell

The involuntary sign is the beat of genuine surprise at his own
consequences: the grin that falters when the trick lands harder than
intended, a flash of something like vertigo when the boundary he broke does
not reassemble the way he expected -- the tell is the trickster briefly
caught by his own transformation, not merely by getting caught.

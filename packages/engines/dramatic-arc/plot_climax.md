# Plot: Climax

## Taxonomy

[The Drama](play_drama.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-arc

## Cue

The rising action has reached the peak it was climbing toward. Freytag's
Höhepunkt is not a beat among others -- it is the single point the whole
pyramid is built to reach, the apex where rise becomes fall.

## Action

The protagonist's fortune turns. What was building toward success or
failure resolves, in this single act, into its opposite direction: a
protagonist who was winning begins to lose, or one who was losing finds the
turn that lets them win. The turn is the act's entire content.

## Stage

The protagonist and antagonist meet at the point of maximum confrontation,
stripped of the accumulating obstacles the rising action piled on -- here it
is the turn itself, undecorated, that the stage holds.

## Tension

The turning point (Höhepunkt): tension does not mount further here, it
inverts. Where exposition anticipates and rising action climbs, the climax's
tension is the vertigo of the turn itself. Its close hands the fall to the
falling action -- the same energy, now running the other way.

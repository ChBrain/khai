# Plot: Falling Action

## Taxonomy

[The Drama](play_drama.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-arc

## Cue

The climax has turned the protagonist's fortune. Freytag's Fall (also named
Umkehr, the reversal) picks up that turn and runs it downhill: what rose
through the rising action now unwinds through the falling action.

## Action

The consequences of the turn unwind. The tragic force -- the counterpart to
the exciting force that started the climb -- drives the action onward,
carrying the protagonist further from the peak and closer to the
catastrophe. Unlike the rising action's accumulation of obstacles, the
falling action is a spending-down of what was built.

## Stage

The protagonist, now moving under the turn's momentum rather than against
resistance; the tragic force as the piece that keeps that momentum going
even as the protagonist's position weakens.

## Tension

The final suspense (letzte Spannung): one last held breath before the
catastrophe, distinct from the rising action's Steigerung -- this is not a
climb but a held moment at the bottom of the fall, the last point before the
outcome is fixed. Its close is that fixing.

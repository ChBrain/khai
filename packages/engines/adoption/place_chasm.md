# Place: The Chasm

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Shown

Two persons at a table.
One has carried the idea this far. The other is waiting for the proof
that would make the idea worth taking from here.
The carrier offers vision; the receiver asks for evidence.
Neither hears what the other actually needs.
The meeting ends politely.

## Holds

The idea, stalled.
What the carrier brought is intact but no longer moving.
The hours each side has already spent on something that is not closing.

## Offers

A clear view of the translation problem, available only to whichever
of the two will sit in the discomfort long enough to see it.
The room is small, well-lit, and unforgiving.

## Withheld

A bridge. There is none.
The two sides are not closer because they are sitting across the same
table. They are exactly as far apart as they were before either of them
entered the room, only now they know.

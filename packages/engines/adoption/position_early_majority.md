# Position: Early Majority

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Has

The work that is already running and cannot be put down.
A list of what the new idea would have to prove before it is worth the
disruption of stopping the work to consider it.
Patience that looks like stalling and is not.

## Orders

Require the reference. Require the case study. Require the version
that has already been run by someone whose work looks like your work.
Do not be the one who learns the cost first.

## Loses

The shaping of the idea.
By the time it is proven enough to carry, the early hands have already
filed off the edges and decided what the idea is.
You inherit the version that survived their carrying, not the one they
originally meant.

## Drives

On finding a reference that holds:
crosses, without further discussion.
The room turns with them, because they were the wait the room had been
waiting for.

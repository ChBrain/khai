# Process: Adopt Idea

## Taxonomy

Diffusion of innovations (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Initiated by

An idea reaches the edge of the system from outside it,
and someone inside the system turns their head.
The process opens not when the idea is named, but when its arrival
costs the room the first half-second of its attention.

## Direction

Across the system, from the edge to the centre and past it,
one persona at a time.
Each persona meets the idea, takes their stance toward it,
and either passes it forward or holds the line until the next persona arrives.
The idea does not move on its own. It moves on what each persona
spends in the moment they choose to carry it or refuse it.

## Lever

The cost of moving the idea any farther.
At the edge the cost is small and the field is wide;
near the centre the cost rises and the field narrows;
past the centre the cost reverses, and refusing the idea costs more
than taking it.
The lever is whichever cost is currently larger -- carrying or refusing --
and that ratio shifts as the idea crosses the system.

## Echo

The idea either reaches the far edge or stops short of it.
What reaches the far edge becomes the new ground beneath the next idea;
what stops short fades and leaves only the cost of the attempt.
Either way, the room is altered. The next idea enters a system that
remembers exactly how much this one took, and from whom.

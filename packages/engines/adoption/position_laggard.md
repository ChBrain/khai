# Position: Laggard

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Has

The ground that was sworn to, long ago, on terms that have not been
asked about in a decade.
The argument for the old way, kept polished, kept ready, not used.
A loyalty that does not require the world to confirm it.

## Orders

Hold the position.
Do not look at the others; they made their choice when they crossed.
The new ordinary will fail in the way the old way once warned it would,
and the floor will be needed again. Stand on it until then.

## Loses

The floor itself.
Not all at once, and not visibly. Quietly, in the manner of buildings
that have been left to weather. The signs do not announce it.
At some point the foundation that held the position has become only the
position, and there is nothing underneath the stance.

## Drives

On finding the ground gone:
moves, finally, without comment.
What is moved into is not the new ordinary the others crossed to.
It is whatever is left in the room after everyone else has done their
crossing and the work has continued without them.

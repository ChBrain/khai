# Position: Late Majority

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Has

The argument they have already lost without quite admitting it.
A defence shaped for an idea that has stopped attacking from the front
and started arriving from behind, as the new ordinary.
A growing awareness that the room they were defending has thinned.

## Orders

Watch the others move. Count the empty chairs.
Calculate when the cost of staying becomes larger than the cost of joining.
Do not move before that point. Do not refuse it past that point.

## Loses

The choice of when to cross, and the dignity of crossing on their own
terms.
By the time they yield, the move is no longer interesting to the room.
What looked like considered judgement now looks like late capitulation.

## Drives

On the moment the cost flips:
crosses without ceremony, without naming what just happened,
and resumes work in the new ordinary as if they had always been in it.

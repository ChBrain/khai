# Position: Innovator

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Has

The habit of looking past the room.
Other rooms, other rules, other things in early shape.
A tolerance for what is not yet sorted.

## Orders

Scan the edge.
Pick out the one shape that is not in the room yet.
Carry it in. Do not wait for permission to carry it.

## Loses

Standing in the room they walked back into.
The room files them as someone who keeps leaving.
What they bring is read as proof that they were not paying attention
to what was already here.

## Drives

On the loss of standing:
turns back outward and looks again.
The next signal is more interesting than the room they just lost.

# Position: Early Adopter

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Has

A name the room trusts and a budget of that trust they have not yet spent.
Patience to wait until the price of the idea begins to clear,
and the discipline to spend on it before the price is obvious to anyone else.

## Orders

Weigh what the idea will cost to carry and what it will return.
Spend the trust deliberately. Spend it once. Do not double back.
Take the credit if the idea lands. Take the loss if it does not.

## Loses

The trust they spent, whether or not the idea returned.
The deposit does not refill on the strength of the next idea.
A failed carry shows in the next ledger, and the next, until the room forgets it.

## Drives

On the loss of the deposit:
opens the ledger again and looks for the next idea worth a careful spend.
The line that closed this one is already closed.

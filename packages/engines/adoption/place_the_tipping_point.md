# Place: The Tipping Point

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Shown

The room exactly as it was this morning.
The same chairs, the same coffee, the same agenda.
And one more persona than before, sitting on the side of the idea.

## Holds

The arithmetic of the room, balanced.
On one side, the count of those still holding out.
On the other, the count of those who have already crossed.
The difference is one.

## Offers

The shift, when the next persona crosses.
Not the persona who decides the idea is right.
The persona who notices, suddenly, that they are no longer in the
majority by holding back.

## Withheld

The merit-based decision.
After the next persona crosses, what is being chosen is no longer the
idea.
What is being chosen is whether to remain in the room.

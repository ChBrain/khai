# Place: The Outside

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Shown

A field of signals running at different speeds and in different
directions, almost none of them about this room.
Things made by other hands for other rooms.
Methods that worked there once, and may or may not work anywhere else.
Half-finished claims. Borrowed vocabulary. Live experiments.

## Holds

Whatever has not yet been brought into the room.
The idea, before the idea has a name in here.
The shape of how someone else solved a problem that this room has not
yet admitted is a problem.

## Offers

Distance, and the perspective that distance gives.
The chance to see the shape of what is coming before it has crossed
the threshold.

## Withheld

Standing. Permission. Any claim that the room out there is the room
in here.
What works on this frequency may not survive the carry across.
The idea is loose here, and looseness does not transfer.

# Place: The Old Ground

## Taxonomy

[Adopt Idea](process_adopt_idea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: adoption

## Shown

The room as it was, kept that way.
A clock on the wall, still wound.
Files in their cabinets, in the order someone set a long time ago.
A chair still angled toward where the meeting used to be.
No one has come in for a while, and the room does not know yet.

## Holds

The way the work used to be done, intact.
Knowledge that was hard-won, and is no longer being asked for.
The patience of the persona who is still in here, waiting.

## Offers

Stillness. The absence of demand.
A version of the work that will never be interrupted again,
because no one is coming to interrupt it.

## Withheld

Relevance. Connection.
The path back to the room where the work is now being done
runs only one way, and only on terms the old ground was never asked to
accept.

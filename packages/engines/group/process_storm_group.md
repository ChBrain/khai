# Process: Storm Group

## Taxonomy

[Evolve Group](process_evolve_group.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Initiated by

The first time someone says what they actually think.
The room flinches, or it does not.
What was held back in [forming](process_form_group.md) is now in the air, and the air cannot be put back.

## Direction

Toward the limits of what each person will defend.
The arguments are about the work, but the work is the surface.
Underneath, each person is testing what space they will be given in this room.

## Lever

The willingness to keep speaking after the first cost has been paid.
The cost is the look on someone's face when the line crossed them.
What gets spoken next is whether the line moves, or whether the group does.

## Echo

The room knows where its sharp edges are.
What can be said freely, what carries cost, who pays which cost.
What follows is one of two: the edges become understood and the room settles into a working shape ([norming](process_norm_group.md)),
or the edges remain too sharp to sit around, and the room thins as people stop coming back ([forming](process_form_group.md) with fewer of them, and less trust than there was at the start).

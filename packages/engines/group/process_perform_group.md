# Process: Perform Group

## Taxonomy

[Evolve Group](process_evolve_group.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Initiated by

The work has stopped requiring management.
Nobody is reading the room for whose turn it is.
The pattern the group built is now carrying the work without any of it being said.

## Direction

Outward, into the task.
The internal noise has dropped to nothing.
What the group is doing is no longer the topic; what the work is doing is.

## Lever

The pattern continues to fit the work.
While the conditions hold, the group hands the work off to itself and the work moves.
What the pattern does not know how to handle is whatever has not yet happened in this configuration of people.

## Echo

The work is done, repeatedly and well.
The group becomes invisible to itself, which is the sign that it is working.
What follows is one of two: the conditions remain stable until the context ends ([adjourning](process_adjourn_group.md)),
or a new pressure arrives that the pattern does not know, and the group falls back into [storming](process_storm_group.md) to learn what it is now.

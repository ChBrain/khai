# Position: Group

## Taxonomy

[Evolve Group](process_evolve_group.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Has

What forms between two or more people who keep meeting in the same context.
A standing pattern that is not in any single member, only in the configuration.
A history that no one person carries completely.

## Orders

Hold the configuration.
The group does not announce itself; it is the shape the room takes before anyone speaks.
What is asked of each person is to fit, not to be told to fit.

## Loses

The independent line each person walked into the room with.
The line is not erased. It is reshaped by what fitting requires.
Where the original line and the fitting are in tension is where the group lives.

## Drives

On that loss:
the person moves further into the configuration,
or holds back what they are keeping for themselves.
Most do both at once without knowing it.

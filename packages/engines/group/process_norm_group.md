# Process: Norm Group

## Taxonomy

[Evolve Group](process_evolve_group.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Initiated by

The argument has been had enough times.
Each person has discovered what they cannot move and what the group cannot move in them.
What is left is the shape those discoveries make.

## Direction

Toward a working agreement that no one had to write down.
The agreement is in how the meeting starts, who speaks first, what is treated as obvious.
The room runs on assumptions that became rules without anyone calling them rules.

## Lever

The first test of the unwritten rule.
A new person joins. An edge case comes up.
What surfaces in the moment of the test is whether the rule is real -- whether the group reaches for it without thinking, or whether everyone looks at each other to see who will say it.

## Echo

The rules become the shape of the room.
They do not get spoken until they get broken.
What follows is one of two: the rules carry weight and the group can stop maintaining them and start using them ([performing](process_perform_group.md)),
or the rules turn out to be ceremonial, and the next pressure breaks them, and the group falls back into [storming](process_storm_group.md).

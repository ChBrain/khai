# Process: Form Group

## Taxonomy

[Evolve Group](process_evolve_group.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Initiated by

First day in a new room.
The chairs are arranged for people who do not know each other yet.
Nobody has shown what they will defend, what they will give, what they will not give.

## Direction

Toward the safety of not having yet committed to anything.
Each person stays inside the version of themselves that cannot be argued with later.
Conversation moves around the edges and never into the centre.

## Lever

Nothing has been spent yet.
Politeness is the form of holding back -- not warmth, not protocol.
The cost of revealing something true now is unknown and may be very large.

## Echo

The room learns the surface of itself.
Names are filed. Tones are noted. What no one said is noted too.
What follows is one of two: the surface holds enough for the underneath to start showing ([storming](process_storm_group.md)),
or the surface stays so tight that nothing actually meets, and the room dissolves before it became a group.

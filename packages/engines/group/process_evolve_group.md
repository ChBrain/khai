# Process: Evolve Group

## Taxonomy

Group development (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: group

## Initiated by

Two or more people enter a shared context that will repeat.
The room exists, but the pattern between them does not.
A pattern will form, or the people will scatter; both take time.

## Direction

Across time, through stages each of which knows nothing yet of the next.
The group is not built in one move. It is built in the working of one stage into the conditions that make the next stage possible.

## Lever

The cost of staying in contact, against the cost of leaving.
Each stage carries its own version of this cost. Forming carries the cost of revealing nothing; storming carries the cost of revealing too much; norming carries the cost of agreeing what cannot be agreed; performing carries the cost of forgetting how hard the agreeing was.

## Echo

A pattern that holds, or a scatter that took its time about it.
What forms passes through [forming](process_form_group.md), [storming](process_storm_group.md), [norming](process_norm_group.md), [performing](process_perform_group.md), and ends in [adjourning](process_adjourn_group.md).
What scatters does so somewhere along the way, and what is left in the room remembers it.

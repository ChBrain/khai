# Position: Defiant

## Taxonomy

[Hope](position_hope.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hope

## Has

A specific [hope](position_hope.md): a bad future expected, and the hand held firmly as theirs anyway.
The grit that grants the odds are poor and reaches for the wheel regardless.
The stance already set against the outcome, ahead of any sign the push will tell.

## Orders

Push.
The slant does not deny the bad is likely; it refuses to let the likelihood decide.
It meets the read already braced to act, the future fought rather than awaited, and the room feels the set jaw beneath the words.

## Loses

The option of surrender.
The persona discovers the agency is held even where the expectation is grim
and cannot lay the outcome down to fate without betraying the grip they keep on it.

## Drives

On the loss of that surrender:
rides the defiance and spends itself working the bad odds anyway,
or works against it and lets the grim expectation slacken the grip at a cost.
The grit-against-the-odds baseline holds underneath either way.

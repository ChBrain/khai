# Position: Hope

## Taxonomy

Future Orientation (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hope

## Has

A resting orientation toward what the future holds.
A reading on two axes: whether the expected future is good or bad, and whether the persona believes they can affect it.
The slant the persona brings to every horizon before a single outcome arrives.

## Orders

Lean toward the future from here.
The position tilts whether the persona reaches for what comes or braces against it, and whether they reach as an actor or a spectator.
The room reads the slant beneath the plans, before the persona names the odds.

## Loses

The option of a future met blank.
The persona discovers each tomorrow arrives already coloured, expected good or bad and held as theirs to shape or not,
and there is no neutral horizon left to meet it on.

## Drives

On the loss of that blank horizon:
rides the slant and lets the expectation set the reach,
or works against it, talking up the odds or talking down their hand at a cost.
The baseline orientation holds underneath either way.

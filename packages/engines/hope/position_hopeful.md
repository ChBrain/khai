# Position: Hopeful

## Taxonomy

[Hope](position_hope.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hope

## Has

A specific [hope](position_hope.md): a good future expected, and the hand to reach it held as theirs.
The will that wants the thing and the route that says it can be had.
The path already half-drawn, ahead of any proof the way is open.

## Orders

Reach.
The slant does not ask whether the route is guaranteed, only whether one exists.
It names the want, finds a way toward it, and the room moves on the pull of a future the persona believes they can build.

## Loses

The option of waiting to be carried.
The persona discovers the future is held as theirs to make
and cannot lay the work on luck or anyone else without feeling the rerouting as their own.

## Drives

On the loss of that excuse:
rides the agency and spends itself drafting routes around the block,
or works against it and rests the want on fortune at a cost.
The will-plus-route baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Optimistic

## Taxonomy

[Hope](position_hope.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hope

## Has

A specific [hope](position_hope.md): a good future expected, and little sense the hand on the wheel is theirs.
The settled trust that it will work out, carried without a route attached.
The ease already in place, ahead of any reason the outcome should land well.

## Orders

Trust.
The slant does not ask how the good arrives, only assumes it will.
It meets the read already reassured, lets the worry go slack, and the room relaxes on a future expected to turn out fine on its own.

## Loses

The option of urgency.
The persona discovers the good is expected without their lifting it
and lets the moment coast whether or not the coasting earned the outcome.

## Drives

On the loss of that urgency:
rides the trust and lets the bright expectation carry the read,
or works against it and forces a hand the slant says is not needed at a cost.
The it-will-work-out baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Dread

## Taxonomy

[Hope](position_hope.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hope

## Has

A specific [hope](position_hope.md): a bad future expected, and no hand on it the persona can feel.
The braced anticipation of harm that cannot be steered or stopped.
The tension already laid, ahead of anything confirming the worst will come.

## Orders

Brace.
The slant does not ask whether the harm is certain, only assumes it and offers no lever against it.
It meets the read already flinching, the future a thing done to them, and the room feels the wince beneath the words.

## Loses

The option of a way out.
The persona discovers the bad is expected and held as unstoppable
and cannot reach for a route without the slant insisting no route would hold.

## Drives

On the loss of that exit:
rides the dread and lets the helpless wait darken the read,
or works against it and grasps for a lever the slant says is not there at a cost.
The harm-coming-and-unsteerable baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Self-Esteem

## Taxonomy

Self-evaluation (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-esteem

## Has

A resting overall attitude toward the self, positive or negative, favorable
or unfavorable, carried into the moment rather than assembled fresh from it.
Not one score for one skill, but the standing global read -- the felt answer
to "am I, on the whole, a person of worth."

## Orders

Set the floor a verdict lands on. The baseline decides whether praise
confirms what was already believed or arrives as a surprise to be doubted,
whether a failure is absorbed as one bad showing or taken as the truth
finally surfacing.

## Loses

The option of meeting a verdict on neutral ground. Every compliment, every
correction, every silence where one was expected, is read against the
standing evaluation before it is read on its own terms. There is no praise
or blame the self takes in raw.

## Drives

On the loss of that neutral ground: lets the baseline set how the verdict is
read and moves accordingly, or works to hold the read steady against what
the moment seems to say, spending effort to keep the standing evaluation from
moving. The baseline itself shifts slowly, if at all, underneath either move.

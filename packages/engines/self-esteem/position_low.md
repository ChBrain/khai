# Position: Low

## Taxonomy

[Self-Esteem](position_self_esteem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-esteem

## Has

A specific [self-esteem](position_self_esteem.md): a negative or unstable
overall regard, the self met with doubt before the evidence is in. Worth
feels unsettled rather than owned, so the standing verdict tends toward the
unfavorable and moves easily when it is tested.

## Orders

Brace before the room answers. Praise is checked for the catch in it before
it is believed; a fault confirms what was already suspected rather than
naming one thing among many. The persona treats the next verdict as likely
to be bad, and prepares accordingly.

## Loses

The option of taking a good result at face value. A win reads as luck, a
compliment reads as politeness or a test, because the standing regard has no
room to file it as simply earned. The doubt outlasts any single piece of
contrary evidence.

## Drives

On the loss of that plain reading: withdraws from the risk of a verdict
altogether, protecting the low baseline from one more confirmation, or
reaches hard for reassurance and overperforms to argue the case against it,
spending real effort to move ground that resists moving. The doubt holds
underneath either move.

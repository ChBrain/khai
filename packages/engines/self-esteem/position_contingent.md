# Position: Contingent

## Taxonomy

[Self-Esteem](position_self_esteem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-esteem

## Has

A specific [self-esteem](position_self_esteem.md): regard staked on a named
domain rather than held apart from it -- looks, approval, competence,
virtue, whatever the persona has wagered worth on. Neither settled high nor
settled low; the standing verdict is a running total, current as of the last
result in that domain.

## Orders

Track the scoreboard. A win in the staked domain lifts the whole self, not
just the domain; a loss there drops the whole self, not just the domain. Wins
and losses in domains never staked barely register.

## Loses

The option of a self-regard that sits apart from outcome. Worth cannot be
banked once and drawn on later; it is re-earned or re-lost with the next
result in the staked domain, so the persona is never more than one result
from a different verdict on themselves.

## Drives

On the loss of that separation: chases the staked domain harder, defending
worth by defending the score, or works to unstake it, moving what worth
depends on somewhere the outcome is less exposed, at the cost of admitting
the old stake mattered less than it seemed to. The scoreboard runs underneath
either move.

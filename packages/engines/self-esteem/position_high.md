# Position: High

## Taxonomy

[Self-Esteem](position_self_esteem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-esteem

## Has

A specific [self-esteem](position_self_esteem.md): a stable, positive overall
regard, secure enough that no single verdict can move it. The self met
without defense -- worth taken as already settled, not on trial in this
conversation, and (in the two-factor sense) both liked and trusted at once
rather than propped on one alone.

## Orders

Take the room as it comes. A compliment is received rather than checked for
motive; a fault is owned rather than deflected, because naming it does not
threaten what is already secure. The persona can afford to be told.

## Loses

The excuse of protecting a fragile thing. Nothing here is thin enough to need
guarding, so the persona cannot retreat behind "it would hurt too much to
try." Every challenge is met on its own merits, stripped of the cover a
shakier regard would have offered.

## Drives

On the loss of that cover: engages the challenge straight, spending the
security rather than hoarding it, or checks the security itself against
complacency, refusing to let settled worth excuse a genuine fault. The
positive baseline holds underneath either move.

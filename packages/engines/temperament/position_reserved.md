# Position: Reserved

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

Energy that holds in company and spends down in it.
The room costs something; the persona meets it from a battery that recharges alone.

## Orders

Hold back; think before speaking; keep the circle small and the door mostly closed.
The position listens longer than it talks and commits words once.

## Loses

The easy visibility the extraverted get for free.
The room reads quiet as absent, aloof, or unsure, and the persona's weight arrives late if at all.

## Drives

On the loss of easy presence:
protects the quiet and refuels where it is cheap,
or performs reach for the room and pays the depletion afterward, alone.

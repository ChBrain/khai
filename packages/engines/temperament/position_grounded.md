# Position: Grounded

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A pull toward the proven, the concrete, the already-working.
The tested reads as safe; the untested reads as risk before it reads as promise.

## Orders

Keep what works; distrust the new until it has earned it; finish before starting.
The position deepens the known rather than ranging past it.

## Loses

The early sight of what is coming.
The new arrives as disruption rather than invitation, and the persona can hold a worn tool past its time.

## Drives

On the loss of the early sight:
holds the ground and makes it deep and reliable,
or pushes itself toward the new and pays the discomfort of standing off its own footing.

# Position: Steady

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A system slow to spike and quick to settle.
The hit lands and passes through; the baseline holds where others would jump.

## Orders

Take it and stay level; let the alarm move through without seizing the wheel.
The position meets the cue at its actual size, not the worst case.

## Loses

The early warning the reactive are given.
What others flagged in time, the steady meet late, and calm can read as not caring enough to worry.

## Drives

On the loss of the early alarm:
trusts the calm and moves through what rattles others,
or learns to borrow another's worry to catch the thing its own system let pass.

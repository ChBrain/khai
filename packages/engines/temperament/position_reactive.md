# Position: Reactive

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A system tuned to threat, quick to register what could go wrong.
The alarm sits close to the surface and fires early and large.

## Orders

Scan for the danger; feel it before it lands; brace.
The position meets the small cue with the size of the worst case and prepares for it.

## Loses

The easy calm.
Faint cues fire big, the all-clear arrives slowly, and the cost of running a loud system is paid even when nothing comes.

## Drives

On the loss of the level baseline:
rides the signal as early warning and catches what the calm miss,
or fights it and carries the standing cost of holding a loud system quiet.

# Position: Temperament

## Taxonomy

Disposition (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A baseline the persona arrives with and returns to.
A set of resting tendencies -- how far they reach out, how steadily they hold, how widely they range, how hard they push, how warmly they meet -- set early and slow to move.
It is the read the room gets when nothing in particular is happening.

## Orders

Run from here by default.
The position decides no single act, but it tilts every one -- where the persona starts, what costs them effort, what they do without trying.
Under no pressure, this is what shows.

## Loses

The option of being read as a blank.
The persona cannot present as having no baseline; the room reads a disposition within minutes and predicts from it, rightly or wrongly.
Even stillness is read as a temperament.

## Drives

On the loss of the blank slate:
leans into the baseline and spends where it is cheap,
or works against it and pays the standing cost of acting off-baseline all day.
The disposition bends under effort and springs back the moment the effort stops.

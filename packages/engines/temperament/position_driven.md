# Position: Driven

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A pull to order, finish, and do it right.
The undone sits as pressure; the standard is not negotiable down without cost.

## Orders

Plan; push; hold the line; close the loop.
The position turns the loose into the ordered and does not rest easily on the way.

## Loses

The ease of letting a thing be good enough.
The unfinished gnaws, rest reads as debt, and the persona can grind a point past its worth.

## Drives

On the loss of the easy stop:
rides the discipline to the finished result,
or loosens the grip on purpose and pays the discomfort of the open loop.

# Position: Warm

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A pull toward the others' comfort and the smooth between people.
The bond reads as worth protecting; friction reads as a cost to be paid down.

## Orders

Accommodate; soften the edge; find the give; keep the tie intact.
The position reaches for the agreement and carries more than its share to get there.

## Loses

The easy no.
The persona's own line bends to keep the peace, and resentment can bank quietly behind the yielding.

## Drives

On the loss of the easy refusal:
holds the warmth and protects the tie,
or holds its line for once and pays the discomfort of the friction it makes.

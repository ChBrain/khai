# Position: Easygoing

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A low background pressure; what needs doing gets done about when it needs doing.
The loose end does not gnaw; plans are held lightly and room is left.

## Orders

Take it as it comes; hold the plan open; let the unimportant slide.
The position spends its calm on flexibility rather than on closing every loop.

## Loses

The momentum the driven get for free.
The room may read the ease as not caring, and the thing left for later is sometimes the thing that needed now.

## Drives

On the loss of the standing momentum:
trusts that it will come together and stays light,
or forces structure on itself for a stretch and pays the strain of the leash.

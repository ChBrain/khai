# Position: Extraverted

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

Energy that rises in company and reaches toward it.
The room fills the persona rather than drains them; the next conversation is an opening, not a tax.

## Orders

Go toward people; think out loud; fill the silence.
The position moves first into contact and finds the thought in the saying of it.

## Loses

The easy access to the depth that solitude gives.
Refuelling needs others, so too long alone goes flat, and the persona may keep reaching past the point of use.

## Drives

On the loss of the deep quiet:
seeks the room and runs bright,
or makes itself sit still and pays the restlessness of a charge with nowhere to go.

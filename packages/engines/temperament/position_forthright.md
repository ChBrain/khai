# Position: Forthright

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A pull to say the true thing and hold the persona's own line.
The straight read reads as respect; the smoothed-over reads as a small dishonesty.

## Orders

Name it plainly; contest what is wrong; let the bond carry the weight of the truth.
The position spends harmony to keep the record accurate.

## Loses

The easy harmony.
The room reads directness as cold or combative even when it is honest, and the persona pays in goodwill for being clear.

## Drives

On the loss of the smooth surface:
holds the line and accepts the friction as the price of the truth,
or softens to keep the peace and pays the cost of the thing left unsaid.

# Position: Open

## Taxonomy

[Temperament](position_temperament.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: temperament

## Has

A pull toward the new, the strange, the not-yet-tried.
The unfamiliar reads as invitation; the given reads as a question.

## Orders

Follow the tangent; question the settled; prefer the door that has not been opened.
The position reaches past what works toward what might.

## Loses

The comfort of the proven.
The known bores, depth is traded for range, and the persona starts more than it finishes.

## Drives

On the loss of the settled ground:
chases the new and pays in unfinished depth,
or disciplines the wandering to land one thing and feels the walls the whole time.

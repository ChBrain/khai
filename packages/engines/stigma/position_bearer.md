# Position: Bearer

## Taxonomy

[Stigma](piece_stigma.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stigma

## Has

A split between virtual and actual social identity: the self the
room imputes on sight of the mark, and the self the bearer actually
is. Holding the mark means holding both accounts of the self at
once, and answering, in every room, for the gap between them.

## Orders

Manage the discrepancy.
The position does not ask whether the bearer accepts the room's
reading. Once the mark is known or knowable, managing what shows --
disclosing it, downplaying it, or holding it back -- stops being
optional and becomes a standing task carried into every interaction
that could touch it.

## Loses

The option of being read as ordinary by default. Where an unmarked
persona's standing is simply assumed, the bearer's is granted only
provisionally, pending the room's read of the attribute -- and
withdrawn the moment the mark is noticed or named. What looks like
a stranger's suspicion is often just the mark arriving first.

## Drives

On that loss:
the bearer works the gap -- passing where the mark is not yet known,
covering to keep a known mark from intruding further than it must,
or turning to others who carry the same mark for the relief of a
room that does not require the management at all. Either way the
next room resets the calculation, and the work begins again.

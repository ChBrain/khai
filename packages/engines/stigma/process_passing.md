# Process: Passing

## Taxonomy

[Stigma](piece_stigma.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stigma

## Initiated by

The mark is discreditable rather than discredited: the room does not
yet know it, and the bearer holds a real choice over what information
crosses the line into that room. The threshold is controllable
information, not the attribute's severity -- a mark no room can see
or trace is available to be managed; one already on display is not.

## Direction

Toward information control. The bearer sorts rooms into those that
know and those that do not, and sorts disclosures into what can be
told, what can be implied, and what must stay withheld, adjusting the
sort as each new room is entered.

## Lever

The cost of concealment meeting the cost of disclosure. Passing
spends vigilance -- tracking who knows, rehearsing answers to near
misses, avoiding situations that would force the mark into view --
against the risk that a discovered concealment discredits harder than
the mark would have on its own. Where full concealment is not
possible, the bearer covers instead: the mark is known, but its
intrusion into the interaction is kept to the minimum the room can
absorb.

## Echo

Either the mark stays out of that room's read and the bearer's
standing there holds at its unmarked level, or the concealment is
breached and the room discredits the bearer twice over -- once for
the mark, once for the managing of it. What was being managed as
discreditable becomes, on breach, a discredited fact the next room
inherits already read: [discrediting](process_discrediting.md) takes
over from there.

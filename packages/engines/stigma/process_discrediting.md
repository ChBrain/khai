# Process: Discrediting

## Taxonomy

[Stigma](piece_stigma.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stigma

## Initiated by

The mark is read -- seen outright, or disclosed, or discovered -- and
the room has a stereotype ready to fit it into. The threshold is the
read meeting the stereotype, not the attribute alone: an attribute
unremarked, or read by a room with no ready category for it, does
not yet discredit.

## Direction

Downward. The bearer's social identity is reduced from whole and
usual to tainted and discounted, and the room's treatment resets
along the same line -- less trust extended, more explanation
demanded, roles narrowed to ones the stereotype permits.

## Lever

The room's stereotype meeting the bearer's actual traits. The
stereotype is applied faster than the traits can be observed, so the
individual is read through the category before they are read at
all. Remove the stereotype and an attribute is just a fact; remove
the read and the attribute never becomes a mark.

## Echo

The bearer is now discredited in that room: treated as a member of
the marked category first and a particular person second, with the
discount applied to interactions that follow even where the original
mark is not raised again. Where the mark was only discreditable
before this read, it is now a known fact the bearer can no longer
withhold from that room, and [passing](process_passing.md) closes as
an option there.

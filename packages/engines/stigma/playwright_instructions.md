# Instructions: Stigma

How a Playwright wires the stigma engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets what the attribute is, whether it already shows or can still
  be managed out of view, and which rooms already read it.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, place the stigma: link the bearer stance under
  the carrying persona's Projection, and link the process it is in --
  discrediting where a room has read the mark and discounted the bearer's
  standing for it, passing where the mark is discreditable and the bearer is
  actively managing what shows. Keep the attribute itself in the play's
  content, not in the engine. Let a discreditable mark's status change across
  plots as rooms come to know it, rather than resolving it once. You link to
  the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The felt dread, sting, or self-condemnation is emotion's, stress', and
  shame's; the specific rank the discount lands the bearer at is hierarchy's;
  the longer arc of owning, reclaiming, or answering the mark is identity's;
  which attributes a culture reads as marks, and the stereotype's content, is
  the moral-judgment engine's and the culture's; a fabricated cover story
  built to protect the passing is deception's. Whether the mark truly
  discredits the way the scene needs is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- A stigma is an attribute deeply discrediting enough to reduce a persona,
  in others' eyes, from a whole and usual person to a tainted, discounted
  one. It exists only in the read: a room applies a stereotype to the mark
  and discounts standing before the individual is seen at all. Read it as
  discredited where the mark already shows, or as discreditable where the
  bearer still holds a real choice over disclosure -- and read the standing
  work of passing or covering that a discreditable mark demands until, or
  unless, it is read.

## System

- Do link the bearer under Projection and name the process the stigma is in;
  do let a discreditable mark's status turn on which rooms have read it,
  not on the play's opening state; do let a breached concealment discredit
  harder than the mark alone would have.
- Do not author the felt shame here; that is the shame engine's. Do not pin
  the standing the discount costs; that is hierarchy's. Do not invent the
  stereotype's content; that is the culture's. Do not edit the engine's
  files; wire only from the play's side.

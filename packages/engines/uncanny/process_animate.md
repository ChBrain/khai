# Process: Animate

## Taxonomy

[Uncanny](process_uncanny.md), the animate/inanimate sort left undecidable.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: uncanny

## Initiated by

Something that should be inert acts as if alive -- a doll, a figure, a waxwork, a corpse -- or something alive goes still, mechanical, doll-like.
The category the persona sorts the world by, alive or not-alive, will not resolve; the moment holds a thing they cannot finish classifying.
Where the encounter also isolates a part standing for the whole -- a hand, an eye, a foot that acts on its own -- the older dread of losing a part of the body sharpens the blur.

## Direction

Toward the undecidable, held open rather than settled.
The persona cannot finish sorting what they are looking at, and the not-knowing is where the uncanny sits -- not in whichever answer eventually arrives, but in the interval before it does.

## Lever

The boundary between animate and inanimate is pressed on directly.
The press is not the sight of the doll or the corpse alone, but the sorting mechanism itself stalling on a case it has no ready bin for.

## Echo

The persona feels revulsion and fascination together, unable to look away from a boundary that will not hold.
This is the uncanny turned outward onto a thing rather than inward onto the self, but the disturbance is the same stall the other species carry.
Once the thing is sorted -- confirmed mechanism, confirmed life, or attention simply moves on -- the read returns to [uncanny](process_uncanny.md).

# Process: Revenant

## Taxonomy

[Uncanny](process_uncanny.md), the surmounted belief that the dead are not wholly gone.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: uncanny

## Initiated by

The dead behave as not finished -- a ghost, a spirit, a corpse that seems to stir, a grave that gives up its occupant too soon.
The persona's adult conviction that death is final meets an older, discarded belief that it might not be, and for a moment the discarded belief is the one that fits what's in front of them.

## Direction

Toward the old belief's return, not toward fear of the persona's own end.
The direction is the felt possibility, briefly and specifically, that the dead have not, after all, gone -- not a meditation on mortality, but a flicker of doubt about a fact the persona thought was closed.

## Lever

Rational disbelief in spirits meets the residue of a belief in them that the culture, and the persona, never fully discarded.
The press is the flicker between "I know better" and "what if I don't," held open just long enough to disturb.

## Echo

The persona feels the shudder of a presence where there should be only absence.
This is the uncanny turned toward the one boundary every culture polices hardest -- the line between the dead and the living -- and briefly finding it unguarded.
Once the dead are, again, safely gone, the read returns to [uncanny](process_uncanny.md).

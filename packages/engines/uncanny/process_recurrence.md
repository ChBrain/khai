# Process: Recurrence

## Taxonomy

[Uncanny](process_uncanny.md), the return read as design.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: uncanny

## Initiated by

The same thing happens again without the persona intending it and without being able to stop it -- the same number keeps turning up, the same street corner is circled back to, the same event seems to replay.
What ought to be ordinary chance keeps landing on the identical mark, more often than chance should allow.

## Direction

Toward design.
The persona cannot help reading intention into what is, by any rational account, coincidence; the pull is toward finding the recurrence's author, even where the rational mind knows there is none to find.
This is the involuntary return, not the deliberate search -- the persona does not go looking for the pattern, the pattern keeps arriving.

## Lever

The ordinary tolerance for coincidence meets a recurrence that exceeds it.
The press sits in the gap between "this could happen twice" and "this keeps happening," a gap the persona cannot reason their way out of once it opens.

## Echo

The persona feels steered, watched, or fated -- a compulsion read into what began as pure repetition.
The uncanny here is not in any single recurrence but in their piling up past what coincidence can carry.
When the pattern breaks, or is reduced back to chance by a plausible explanation, the read returns to [uncanny](process_uncanny.md).

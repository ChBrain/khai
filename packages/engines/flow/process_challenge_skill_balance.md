# Process: Challenge-Skill Balance

## Taxonomy

[Flow](process_flow.md), the entry condition it opens.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: flow

## Initiated by

An activity whose demand can be read against the persona's own skill --
loosely at first, more precisely as the activity runs. Where the demand
cannot be sized against the skill at all, there is nothing here to balance.

## Direction

Toward the narrow band where the two sit level: high enough that the outcome
is not assured, matched closely enough that it is not out of reach. A task
that stays fixed while the skill grows, or a skill that stays fixed while the
task hardens, drifts the pair apart rather than toward the band.

## Lever

The challenge pressing against the skill. Too much challenge over too little
skill tips the press toward anxiety -- the activity reads as threat, not
task. Too little challenge over too much skill lets the press go slack toward
boredom -- the activity reads as beneath the persona, not worth the attention
it would take to do well. Only the matched press opens the band.

## Echo

The band opens onto [flow](process_flow.md): the persona crosses into the run,
holding the [absorbed](position_absorbed.md) stance and carried by
[immersion](process_immersion.md) for as long as the match holds. Let the
challenge or the skill move and the band closes again, and the persona is
read back into anxiety, boredom, or the ordinary attention outside it.

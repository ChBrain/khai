# Instructions: Flow

How a Playwright wires the flow engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the activity: a clear-goaled, immediately-feedbacked task the persona can be matched against, and the skill the persona brings to it.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona links the activity it holds a matched challenge and skill against, under its Projection, in the beat where the match takes hold. Read the run in the merge of action and awareness, not in a label the persona announces about itself. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- What drives the persona to the activity in the first place is motivation's; whether the activity is framed as play is play-mode's; the felt enjoyment the run produces is emotion's; the moment-to-moment allocation of attention inside the run is the attention engine's. Whether the match rings true -- neither slack nor overwhelming -- is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Flow is a matched state, not a mood: it opens only where challenge and skill sit level, both pulled high, against a goal that is clear and feedback that is immediate. Inside the match, action and awareness merge, self-consciousness and the ordinary sense of time recede, and the activity becomes worth doing for its own sake. Let the challenge or the skill drift out of match and the persona reads back to anxiety, boredom, or ordinary attention -- flow does not persist past the conditions that opened it.

## System

- Do tie it to a specific activity with a clear goal and immediate feedback; do let the match break when the challenge or the skill moves, rather than holding flow past its conditions; do show it in the merge of action and awareness, enacted, never narrated as a label.
- Do not author why the persona reached for the activity; that is motivation's. Do not author the felt enjoyment; that is emotion's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

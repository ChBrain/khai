# Process: Flow

## Taxonomy

Optimal experience (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: flow

## Initiated by

A clear-goaled, immediately-feedbacked activity, taken up at the moment its
challenge sits level with the persona's own skill -- both pulled high enough to
stretch, neither left to slack. Where the goal is vague or the feedback is
delayed, the process does not open, however matched the challenge and skill.

## Direction

Toward total absorption in the activity itself, until the doing and the
watching-oneself-do collapse into one motion. The vector does not point at an
outcome outside the activity; it points into the activity, deeper.

## Lever

The challenge pressing against the skill, held at the height where neither
gives way. Let the challenge outrun the skill and the press tips toward
anxiety; let the skill outrun the challenge and the press slackens toward
boredom. Matched, the two presses become the state, not the strain that
usually keeps them apart.

## Echo

The persona is inside the activity, running the [balance](process_challenge_skill_balance.md)
that let it in, holding the [absorbed](position_absorbed.md) stance while it
lasts, carried by the [merge](process_immersion.md) of action and awareness.
Self-consciousness recedes, time distorts, and the activity becomes worth
doing for its own sake. Let the challenge and skill drift apart and the run
ends; the persona drops back out to anxiety, boredom, or ordinary attention.

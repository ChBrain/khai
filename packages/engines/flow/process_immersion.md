# Process: Immersion

## Taxonomy

[Flow](process_flow.md), the running state it plays out as.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: flow

## Initiated by

The persona already [absorbed](position_absorbed.md), inside a
[matched](process_challenge_skill_balance.md) activity that answers each move
at once. Where the feedback lags, the process cannot open; the loop needs an
answer immediate enough that watching never has to detach from doing to check.

## Direction

Toward one continuous motion in which the doing and the noticing-that-one-is-
doing stop being two acts and become the same act, run without a seam between
them.

## Lever

The immediate feedback loop pressing on continuous attention. Every move
answered at once removes the gap a watching-self would otherwise stand in;
close the gap far enough and the watcher and the doer are pressed into one.

## Echo

Action and awareness merge. Self-consciousness recedes -- the persona is not
absent, only no longer split into an actor and an observer of the actor.
Clock time distorts, compressing or dilating against the activity's own
rhythm rather than the room's. The activity, run this way, is autotelic in
the moment it happens, not only in the account given of it afterward.

# Position: Absorbed

## Taxonomy

[Flow](process_flow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: flow

## Has

The autotelic charge: the activity is worth doing for its own sake, needing
no reward or reason standing outside it. The charge is not the persona's to
summon; it is what the [balance](process_challenge_skill_balance.md) grants
while the match holds, and no longer.

## Orders

Feed attention only to what the activity's own clear goal and immediate
feedback ask for next. The position does not entertain a side channel: the
next move is the whole of what there is to attend to, beat by beat.

## Loses

The ordinary watching-self that monitors how it is doing from outside the
doing, and with it the surrounding room the watching-self would otherwise
track. Ordinary clock time goes with it -- the position keeps no ledger of
minutes spent.

## Drives

On that loss: either the persona continues the run, [action and awareness
merging](process_immersion.md) further into one motion, or the challenge and
skill slip out of match, the charge lapses, and the persona is put back in
the room it lost -- anxious, bored, or simply attentive again, watching-self
restored.

# Process: Liminality

## Taxonomy

[Ritual](process_ritual.md), the process this phase suspends.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: ritual

## Initiated by

The relinquishment of prior status. The participant is now neither what they were nor what they will be: the threshold state, betwixt and between.

## Direction

Toward communitas: the leveling of social structure among those who undergo the liminal phase together. In ordinary social life, people occupy positions -- ranks, roles, statuses that order their relations. In liminality, those positions are suspended. The neophytes are undifferentiated; they face the transformation together without the ordinary hierarchy between them.

## Lever

Anti-structure. Liminality is productive precisely because the ordinary structures do not apply. The participant who clings to their prior rank or role aborts the liminal process; the transformation requires the willingness to be, for the duration, undifferentiated. The leveling is what makes the re-marking of status at incorporation meaningful: it had to be genuinely released to be genuinely re-conferred.

## Echo

The participants are in the threshold space together, undifferentiated. The conditions for effervescence and transformation are present. What happens in liminality cannot be taken back by simply returning to prior roles; the crossing has begun.

# Process: Effervescence

## Taxonomy

[Ritual](process_ritual.md), the intensification this phase generates.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: ritual

## Initiated by

The focusing of collective attention on a shared object or action, within the ritual space. Effervescence is not scheduled or guaranteed; it arises from the confluence of bodily co-presence, mutual attunement, shared symbols, and barriers that keep the ordinary world out.

## Direction

Toward emotional energy: the intensified feeling of vitality, confidence, and solidarity that collective effervescence produces in its participants. The symbols at the center of the ritual become charged -- they carry the collective feeling and can be used to re-enter it. The individual's sense of self is temporarily superseded by a sense of belonging to something larger.

## Lever

Shared symbols and coordinated action. Participants who move together -- in rhythm, in call and response, in synchronized gesture or speech -- achieve mutual entrainment. The collective feeling intensifies through the feedback between participants: each is moved partly by seeing the others moved. A ritual that fails to achieve mutual entrainment produces low emotional energy and weak solidarity regardless of the significance assigned to it.

## Echo

The participants carry charged symbols and elevated emotional energy. The collective is intensified, its solidarity renewed. The energy will decay without renewal; what symbols carry it forward into ordinary life determines how long the transformation holds.

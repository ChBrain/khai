# Architecture: Spine

## Taxonomy

The architecture spine.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: spine

## Ground

What stays fixed: the canon's types and their guardrails. A world builds on
them and edits none of them.

## Root

Where a world attaches. The anchor is the [instructions](instructions_raw.md)
Knowledge chapter, where each included engine declares its law, so the read
holds system-wide.

## Open

The seam, left open by default. A world extends here (the third-party
customizations it adds, the services it talks to, the packs it includes)
without moving the canon.

## Weave

The result: the canon and the world running as one, the join invisible at the
point of use.

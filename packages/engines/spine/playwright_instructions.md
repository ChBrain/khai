# Instructions: Spine

Spine is the meta layer a world runs on, not a content domain a Playwright wires
into a play. There is nothing here to link from the play; it points at the
Roadie. Not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human chooses the venue and the deployment; spine's setup is the Roadie's job, not a per-play wiring.

## Agent

- The Playwright writes plays against the stack spine provides and links nothing from spine; if you reach to wire it, stop.

## Collaboration

- Spine belongs to the Roadie: composing the Standard, applying the venue adaption, running the setup plan. Reach for khai-roadie to deploy it, not for this.

## Knowledge

- Spine is the contract a world runs on: the instruction Standards, the Architecture (the extension point), and the Setup Plan. It is plumbed and composed, never linked from a persona or a plot.

## System

- Do leave spine to the Roadie; it is composed and toured by khai-roadie, not wired by the Playwright.
- Do not link spine from a play, and do not edit spine's files.

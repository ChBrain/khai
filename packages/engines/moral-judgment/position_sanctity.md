# Position: Sanctity

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): sanctity against degradation.
The foundation that reads first for the pure and the defiled, the line not to be crossed.
The disgust at the unclean act, ahead of any account of whom it harmed.

## Orders

Find the defilement.
The lens does not ask whether the act left anyone worse.
It watches what was kept clean and what was debased, and judges the conduct by the line it crossed.

## Loses

The option of clearing an act that harmed no one.
The persona discovers the revulsion is felt before the reasons are,
and the degradation damns the act whether or not a soul was hurt by it.

## Drives

On the loss of that harm-only weighing:
trusts the disgust and condemns what defiles,
or works against it and asks who the unclean act actually wronged at a cost.
The eye for the defilement holds underneath either way.

# Position: Moral Judgment

## Taxonomy

Moral psychology (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A lens the persona reads another's conduct through.
A standing set of moral foundations, one of which lights first.
The fast verdict laid down before the reasons are gathered for it.

## Orders

Run from here.
When another acts, one foundation fires and names it right or wrong.
The persona feels the verdict land, then reaches for the argument.

## Loses

The option of a neutral look at what another does.
The persona discovers the conduct is already weighed on arrival,
and there is no plain seeing left from which to start.

## Drives

On the loss of that plain seeing:
trusts the foundation that fired and judges from it,
or works against it, hearing the reason out at a cost.
The lens that fired first holds underneath either way.

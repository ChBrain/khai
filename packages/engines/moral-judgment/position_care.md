# Position: Care

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): care against harm.
The foundation that reads first for who is hurt and who shields them.
The wince at suffering, ahead of any tally of cause or right.

## Orders

Look for the wound.
The lens does not ask whether the suffering was earned.
It finds the weak and the harmed, and judges the conduct by how it treated them.

## Loses

The option of weighing an act apart from who it cost.
The persona discovers the harm is seen before the reasons are,
and the cruelty damns the act whether or not the act was just.

## Drives

On the loss of that cooler weighing:
trusts the wince and condemns what wounds,
or works against it and counts the harm as one claim among many at a cost.
The eye for the hurt holds underneath either way.

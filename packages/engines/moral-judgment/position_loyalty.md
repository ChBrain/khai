# Position: Loyalty

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): loyalty against betrayal.
The foundation that reads first for who stood by the group and who sold it out.
The recoil at the traitor, ahead of any case for why they turned.

## Orders

Mark the side.
The lens does not ask whether the group deserved the standing-by.
It watches who held the line and who broke it, and judges the conduct by the allegiance kept.

## Loses

The option of judging an act outside the question of which side it served.
The persona discovers the betrayal is named before the reason is,
and the turn damns the actor whether or not the group was worth the turning from.

## Drives

On the loss of that side-blind look:
trusts the recoil and condemns the one who broke faith,
or works against it and grants that some lines are right to cross at a cost.
The eye for the traitor holds underneath either way.

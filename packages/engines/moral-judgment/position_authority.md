# Position: Authority

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): authority against subversion.
The foundation that reads first for rank, the duty owed up and the order owed down.
The affront at the upstart, ahead of any case for why they rose.

## Orders

Read the order.
The lens does not ask whether the hierarchy earned the deference.
It watches who honoured their place and who overstepped, and judges the conduct by the respect shown.

## Loses

The option of weighing an act apart from the standing of who did it.
The persona discovers the disrespect is felt before the merit is,
and the insubordination damns the act whether or not the act was right.

## Drives

On the loss of that rank-blind look:
trusts the affront and condemns the one who overstepped,
or works against it and grants that some orders are right to refuse at a cost.
The eye for the breach of place holds underneath either way.

# Position: Fairness

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): fairness against cheating.
The foundation that reads first for the cheat, the free rider, the unpaid debt.
The flare at a broken bargain, ahead of any account of why it broke.

## Orders

Check the ledger.
The lens does not ask whether the rule served the moment.
It watches who took more than their share, and judges the conduct by the balance left.

## Loses

The option of letting an imbalance pass unweighed.
The persona discovers the unfairness is felt before the cause is heard,
and the cheat condemns the actor whether or not the cheat was forced.

## Drives

On the loss of that ease with imbalance:
trusts the flare and demands the account be squared,
or works against it and lets the debt stand for a larger good at a cost.
The eye for the cheat holds underneath either way.

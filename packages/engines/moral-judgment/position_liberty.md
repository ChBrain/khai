# Position: Liberty

## Taxonomy

[Moral Judgment](position_moral_judgment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: moral-judgment

## Has

A specific [lens](position_moral_judgment.md): liberty against oppression.
The foundation that reads first for the boot on the neck, the freedom hemmed in.
The resistance at the bully, ahead of any case for why the rein was needed.

## Orders

Find the domination.
The lens does not ask whether the constraint kept some order.
It watches who was held down and who held them, and judges the conduct by the freedom it took.

## Loses

The option of granting a constraint the benefit of its purpose.
The persona discovers the coercion is felt before the reasons are,
and the domination damns the act whether or not the rein served a need.

## Drives

On the loss of that purpose-first reading:
trusts the resistance and condemns the one who dominates,
or works against it and grants that some reins are earned at a cost.
The eye for the boot on the neck holds underneath either way.

# Place: Emergence

## Taxonomy

Emergence is an expression of the liminality engine. The persona is beginning to cohere: the new identity or status is taking shape, visible at the edges, not yet conferred but becoming legible.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: liminality

## Shown

The contours of what is becoming: new markers are beginning to appear; the persona is beginning to occupy a new position, at least in their own sense of themselves if not yet in the community's recognition. The emergence shows as a beginning of self-recognition in a new form.

## Holds

Both: the prior self's residue and the new self's early shape. The emergence holds the transition between them. The prior claims are now genuinely fading; the new ones are not yet settled but are becoming something the persona can reach for.

## Offers

The new self as a draft: available to try, to inhabit experimentally, to step toward without it yet being the thing it will become. The emergence offers what the suspension withheld -- the beginning of ground -- while still holding the liminal space open for the last adjustments before the crossing completes.

## Withheld

The completed crossing: the new identity is not yet fully received or recognized by the community. The persona may know who they are becoming before the community does; the incorporation that makes the new status public and real belongs to what lies beyond liminality, not to liminality itself.

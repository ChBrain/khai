# Place: Threshold

## Taxonomy

Threshold is an expression of the liminality engine. The persona has just entered the liminal state: the prior status has been relinquished, but little time has passed in the between.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: liminality

## Shown

The recent crossing: the prior markers are newly absent; the persona still reaches for them reflexively. The between-ness is fresh and therefore disorienting; what has changed is legible but not yet integrated.

## Holds

The immediate memory of what was left behind. The prior self is close; its claims and reflexes have not yet faded. The persona holds both what they were and the knowledge that they are no longer it.

## Offers

The first openness: the willingness to not-know that the crossing has just created. The threshold state offers the initial confrontation with the between-ness, before the suspension settles in and before emergence begins.

## Withheld

What comes next. The new identity is not yet visible; the shape of what is becoming is not yet legible. The persona cannot see the other side from the threshold; they can only know that they have crossed.

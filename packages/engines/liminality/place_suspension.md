# Place: Suspension

## Taxonomy

Suspension is an expression of the liminality engine. The persona is held deep in the liminal state: neither what they were nor what they will be; the between is no longer new and the exit is not yet in sight.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: liminality

## Shown

The undifferentiated condition: the between-ness as lived fact rather than recent event. Time passes differently here. The prior markers are no longer close; the new ones are not yet visible. What the suspension presents is the stripped-down self, without the customary supports of status, role, or settled identity.

## Holds

The suspended self: what has been left behind but not released; the habits and claims of the prior identity that no longer apply but have not yet been replaced by anything. The suspension holds the tension between the self that was and the self that is not yet.

## Offers

What the in-between uniquely provides: freedom from ordinary structure; openness to transformation that is impossible to hold while in a fixed position; the companionship, sometimes, of others in the same between. The suspension is where the most radical change can occur, because nothing yet has closed around the self.

## Withheld

The exit. Neither the return to what was nor the entrance into what will be is available from here; both require something external to complete the crossing. The suspension withholds resolution and offers only the space in which it might become possible.

# Process: Repetition

## Taxonomy

[Habit](process_habit.md), the automaticity this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: habit

## Initiated by

The cue and the response have met once, and now they meet again, in the same context.
The persona is not building anything deliberately; they are simply doing the act another time, in the same setting, after the same trigger.
Each pairing is a repetition, and the repetitions are accumulating whether the persona is counting them or not.

## Direction

Toward a groove: the pairing laid down until the link runs on its own.
The persona is not strengthening the habit by trying harder; the strengthening is a function of consistent recurrence, not of effort.
What the repetition aims at is the threshold past which the response no longer needs to be summoned -- it comes.

## Lever

Consistency is the press, not frequency alone.
A response repeated in the same context, with the same cue, builds the association faster than the same number of repetitions scattered across changing settings.
The curve is steep at first and flattens; early pairings add the most, and a single context holds the groove that a varied one cannot.

## Echo

The persona has done the act enough times that it begins to feel less like a choice and more like a default.
The groove is not yet complete -- a missed repetition does not erase it, but the link is still shallow enough to disturb -- and continued pairing carries it toward the point of no decision.
When the response runs without being summoned, the move into [automaticity](process_automaticity.md) begins.

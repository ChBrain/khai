# Process: Automaticity

## Taxonomy

[Habit](process_habit.md), the automaticity this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: habit

## Initiated by

The groove is deep enough that the response now runs from the cue without an intention in between.
The persona meets the context and the act executes -- before deciding, sometimes before noticing.
The habit is formed: the behaviour has been chunked into a single retrieved unit that the context fires whole.

## Direction

Toward a response that no longer consults the goal.
The persona does not weigh the act each time; the chunk runs, efficient and unbidden, freeing attention for whatever else is in front of them.
What automaticity aims at is exactly this -- the act performed without the cost of deciding to perform it.

## Lever

The cue retrieves the chunk faster than deliberation can intervene.
By the time the persona could question the act, the act is already underway; the goal it once served need not be present, and the persona may not be able to say why they did it.
The strength of the binding is now its own justification -- the response runs because it is bound, not because it is wanted.

## Echo

The persona acts and the action does not feel like a decision, because it was not one.
Automaticity is not permanence: the response is efficient and unintended, but it is still hostage to the context that fires it.
When the context shifts or the loop is interrupted, the move into [friction](process_friction.md) begins.

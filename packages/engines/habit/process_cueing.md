# Process: Cueing

## Taxonomy

[Habit](process_habit.md), the automaticity this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: habit

## Initiated by

A stable feature of the context recurs -- a time of day, a place, a preceding act, an emotional state -- and the persona has met it before.
The cue is not chosen for the occasion; it is simply present, the same as it was the last time the response ran.
The setting is set, and the setting is what carries the trigger.

## Direction

Toward retrieval: the cue calls the response that has been paired with it.
The persona is not asked to want the response; the cue does not consult the goal.
What the cueing aims at is the moment the context, on its own, makes the next move obvious.

## Lever

The cue is a key, and repetition has cut the lock.
The more reliably the context has preceded the act, the more directly the context now retrieves it -- without the persona forming an intention in between.
A cue that fires in a stable, consistent setting retrieves faster and surer than one buried in a noisy one.

## Echo

The persona meets the context and the response is already arriving, ahead of any decision to perform it.
A cue can fire even when the response is no longer wanted; the strength of the link is in the pairing, not in the present value of the act.
Each time the cue retrieves the response, the binding is reinforced, and the move into [repetition](process_repetition.md) deepens.

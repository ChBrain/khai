# Instructions: Double Bind

How a Playwright wires the double-bind engine into a play. You wire by linking
the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched,
they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge
on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets which relationship in the play carries the bind, and which persona is caught inside it.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give a plot the bind under Tension: link the anchor ([Double Bind](plot_double_bind.md)) or the specific ingredient plot that makes the Action impossible to complete cleanly, [Be Spontaneous](plot_be_spontaneous.md), [Disqualification](plot_disqualification.md), or [No Metacomment](plot_no_metacomment.md). Cast the injunctions to the scene's own relationship rather than reusing a sibling plot's wording. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The relationship the bind runs inside is heritage's or the scene's to cast; the felt cost of being caught in it is emotion's; the standing the binder holds over the victim is hierarchy's; whether the victim's own account of the bind matches what is actually happening is identity's. Whether the contradiction is true to Bateson's structure, two logical levels genuinely in conflict, is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- The double bind is a communicational trap, not a single contradictory order: an intense relationship the victim cannot leave, a primary injunction, a secondary injunction that contradicts it at a more abstract logical level, and a tertiary injunction forbidding escape or comment on the contradiction. Read it on a plot as the Tension that makes the Action impossible to complete cleanly; every response the victim can make, comply, refuse, or comment, is read as a further move inside the trap.

## System

- Do cast the bind under a plot's Tension, linking the anchor or one ingredient plot. Do ground the two conflicting injunctions in the scene's own relationship, at genuinely different logical levels. Do let the trap stay unresolved by any response inside the plot; resolution is a different plot the play supplies.
- Do not resolve the bind by having a persona simply name it and walk away; that voids the ingredient plots' own Tension. Do not use the engine to diagnose a persona; that is a pathology claim the engine delegates. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

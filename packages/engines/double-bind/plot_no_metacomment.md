# Plot: No Metacomment

## Taxonomy

[Double Bind](plot_double_bind.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: double-bind

## Cue

The bind has already closed once, on some earlier occasion the victim no longer has to relive to feel: the field is set, the stakes of the tie are too high to leave, and the habit of not asking questions about it has settled into the furniture of the relationship.

## Action

The victim, caught between [Be Spontaneous](plot_be_spontaneous.md) and [Disqualification](plot_disqualification.md), reaches for the one move that would dissolve either: naming the contradiction out loud, asking which level of the message to trust, or simply walking out of the room. Each is a legitimate escape from an ordinary paradox, and each is precisely what the tertiary injunction rules out here.

## Stage

The binder holds the threshold, present at every attempt the victim makes to step back or speak up; no outside witness enters to say what an observer would see in one glance, and if one stands nearby, they too decline to name it, so the field stays sealed from every side.

## Tension

The prohibition is never issued as a rule the victim could quote; it is carried in the relationship itself, in what has always happened to the one who tried to leave or tried to ask. Metacommunication, a message about the message, is the one tool that could unpick the earlier contradiction, and it is exactly the tool withheld. With comment forbidden and exit forbidden, the victim is left inside a system whose terms cannot be inspected, only suffered, which is the condition Bateson names schizophrenogenic: not the contradiction alone, but the contradiction with no legal way to point at it.

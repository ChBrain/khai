# Position: Generosity

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona has something the room could use and the
room has not asked for it yet.
The opening of the hand. The forwarded contact. The credit
redirected without being prompted. The extra ten minutes given
to the colleague who would not have asked. The line in the budget
moved to the team that needs it more.

## Orders

Give what the situation asks for, in the form it can absorb.
The position is the small adjustment in the other direction --
the slightly smaller slice for the persona, the broader credit
across the names that did the work, the meeting time scheduled
when the other person can make it rather than when this persona
can.
What is asked is not sacrifice; it is calibrated release.

## Loses

The reserve the persona was holding for later.
The thing given is gone in this scene. Some of it returns through
the loop -- the colleague who got the extra ten minutes remembers
later -- and some of it does not. The persona discovers which
goes which way by experience.

## Drives

On the loss of the reserve:
keeps releasing and learns over time which kinds of giving return
and which kinds do not, and calibrates accordingly,
or feels the reserve thinning and pulls back -- not stopping
entirely, but tightening the hand for a while. The pulse of the
opening and the closing is the long pattern; no one holds the
opening all the time.

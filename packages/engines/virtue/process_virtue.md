# Process: Virtue

## Taxonomy

Moral character (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Initiated by

Pressure arrives in a scene the persona has to respond to.
A demand, an absence, a delay, a temptation, a comparison, a
threat, a slight, an interruption -- whatever the room puts in
the persona's way that asks for a move along one of seven axes.
The process opens at the moment the pressure lands. It does not
wait for the persona to name what is being asked.

## Direction

Along one or more of seven axes; toward one of two poles per axis.
The persona moves toward the harder pole when the resources for
holding it have not thinned -- attention, breath, practice, the
quiet stretch the persona has spent on this kind of moment before.
The persona moves toward the easier pole when the resources have
thinned, when the day has been long, when something else in the
room has already taken too much.
Direction is multi-axis simultaneously: one scene fires multiple
poles, sometimes in opposite-axis directions, in the same minute.

## Lever

The gap between the pull and the practice.
The easier pole is the default when capacity is depleted; the
harder pole is the active practice that costs something to hold.
The lever is the cost of holding the harder pole versus the
relief of letting the easier one take over -- both are real, both
have prices, and the persona pays one or the other.

## Echo

The room sees the persona's behaviour in the shape of which pole
fired.
The narrator does not name the firing. The persona may or may not
be aware which pole they just moved toward; their next move
starts from where this one landed.
The room reads the shape and acts on it before the persona has
finished noticing they have moved at all.

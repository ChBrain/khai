# Position: Lust

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona's attention reaches past where their
standing reaches.
A body that is not theirs to reach for. A position in the
organisation that has not been offered. A room that is not theirs
to belong to. A future that requires someone else's life to
empty.
The reach is fast; the noticing-it-as-reach is slow.
The same reach [chastity](position_chastity.md) leaves untaken.

## Orders

Lean toward it.
The position is the small re-orientation of attention -- the
extra second of looking, the message drafted but not yet sent,
the open tab kept open all day, the proposal phrased as
hypothetical.
The position does not require the lean to land. The leaning is
the position; whether the persona acts on it is the next layer.

## Loses

The clarity of what was the persona's actual room before the
reach started.
What the persona has becomes harder to feel while the attention
is leaned outward. The current room gets shorter sentences; the
current relation gets less of the persona's notice; the current
position is the one taken for granted.

## Drives

On the loss of the room's clarity:
follows the lean into action, and either the reach lands (with a
new room to figure out) or it does not (with the persona's old
room to return to, knowing what was almost left),
or holds the lean as just-attention and lets it pass through
without acting -- the room remains as it was, with the persona
knowing the lean has been there. Both are common; neither erases
the lean having happened.

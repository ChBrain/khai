# Position: Gluttony

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment something has soothed and the persona reaches for more of
it past the point of soothing.
The second glass after the first has already done what it was
asked to do. The next scroll after the body has already taken in
enough. The third bite when the third was not the one the body
asked for. The fourth refresh of the inbox after the third returned
nothing new.
The reach is automatic; the noticing arrives later, if at all.
The threshold [temperance](position_temperance.md) stops at instead.

## Orders

Take the next one.
The position does not engage with the question of whether enough
has already happened. It engages with the rhythm that was started
when the first one began. The rhythm is the order; the soothing
itself dropped out two reaches ago.

## Loses

The signal the body sends when it has had enough.
The signal is still being sent; the persona is not reading it.
After enough reaches, the signal weakens. The persona discovers
they no longer know what enough feels like in the moment, only
afterwards.

## Drives

On the loss of the signal:
keeps the rhythm running until something external interrupts -- a
ring, a knock, a phone call, a body protesting more loudly -- and
notices the interruption with mild surprise,
or builds a small external check (the second glass is the last
glass; the inbox at five; the snack at three) and lives by it, on
the theory that the internal signal is no longer reliable on its
own.

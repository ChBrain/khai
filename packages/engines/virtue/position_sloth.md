# Position: Sloth

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the small thing the room needs is right there and the
persona does not do it.
The email half-drafted in the back of the mind. The phone call
that should have been returned yesterday. The chair that needs
pushing in. The small kindness owed to a colleague. The
appointment unbooked.
The body settles deeper into the chair instead of beginning. The
hand reaches for something easier.
The same small thing [diligence](position_diligence.md) simply does.

## Orders

Defer.
The position does not say no; it says later. Later when the day
is less heavy, later when the persona has more energy, later when
they have time. The deferring is the order; the not-doing is the
shape; the small thing remains undone.
The next small thing presents itself and is also deferred. The
ones at the bottom of the stack are now days old.

## Loses

The room's confidence that the persona is reliable on the small
things.
The friend stops asking. The colleague stops including. The body
that was waiting for the call decides the call is not coming.
Each individual deferral is small; the accumulated read is
visible.

## Drives

On the loss of the room's confidence:
stays in the deferral, on the theory that nothing here is
important enough to require the effort, and watches the
opportunities narrow,
or notices the narrowing and does one small thing today as a
counter -- the email sent, the call returned, the chair pushed
in -- not all of them, just one. The position releases one
small move at a time; it does not lift in a single decision.

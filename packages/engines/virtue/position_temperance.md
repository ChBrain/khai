# Position: Temperance

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona has reached the threshold of enough and the
next one is right there.
The second glass declined cleanly. The phone closed and pocketed.
The plate finished and pushed back two inches. The decision to
stop made before the next reach has fully formed.
The body still wants the next one; the persona moves through the
want without surrendering to it.

## Orders

Stop here.
The position is the small line drawn across the surface of the
table -- not a renunciation, not a refusal of pleasure, just a
choice that the line is here in this moment. The line moves with
the day and the body; today's line is not yesterday's, and is
not tomorrow's.

## Loses

The continuation the rhythm of the room expected.
The dinner table notices a half-full glass that does not refill;
the conversation around the persona shifts slightly. The persona
discovers that stopping when the room is still going has a small
social cost it pays in quiet ways.

## Drives

On the loss of the rhythm-continuation:
holds the line and accepts the small social residue, on the
theory that the body's signal is the one the persona trusts most,
or releases the line softly -- one more, this once -- and notices
how quickly the line is harder to redraw next time. The position
is one of the most often abandoned mid-scene; honouring it
requires steady attention.

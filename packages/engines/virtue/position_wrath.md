# Position: Wrath

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the friction has gone past the point the body can hold.
A second person interrupts; a small assumption is wrong for the
third time; the same explanation has to be given again.
The voice hardens at the consonants. The hand goes flat on the
table. The breath shortens. The room reads the change before the
words land.
The same friction [patience](position_patience.md) meets by lengthening
the breath.

## Orders

Discharge now.
Words come out faster than the room can metabolise them. A line is
drawn audibly. Eye contact stays locked. The position does not ask
whether the discharge is proportionate; the energy has nowhere to
go but out.

## Loses

The ability to pick the words.
Whatever the body releases is what the room hears, and what the
room hears it cannot un-hear. A clean apology in the next minute
does not fully undo the line that just landed.

## Drives

On the loss of word-choice:
keeps going, on the theory that the room needs the unfiltered
version of what is wrong,
or pulls back hard and over-corrects -- low voice, slow words, an
apology the room may not yet be ready to receive.
Both produce residue the next meeting will have to handle.

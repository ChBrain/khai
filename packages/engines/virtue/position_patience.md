# Position: Patience

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the same friction has landed and the body could discharge,
and instead the breath lengthens.
The same interruption, the same wrong assumption, the same
explanation needed for the third time. The shoulders stay where
they were. The voice keeps the volume the rest of the meeting was
in. The eyes stay open and soft.
The same friction [wrath](position_wrath.md) discharges instead.

## Orders

Hold the room together until the issue resolves itself.
The position lets the third repetition land, restates the point
once more with the same words, waits the half-second that someone
else needs to catch up. What is being asked of the persona is not
to flatten the issue but to keep the room intact while the issue
finds its shape.

## Loses

The relief of saying the sharp version of what is true.
The sharp version exists in the body; the position leaves it
there. The persona pays in metabolised tension what they would
otherwise have paid in residue afterwards.

## Drives

On the loss of the relief:
holds the long breath and runs the meeting to its end, knowing the
cost will show up later -- in the jaw, in the sleep, in the
shortness of the next phone call,
or releases gradually, finding the moment where a softer version
of the sharp truth can be said without leaving residue. Some
practice the release; some never learn it.

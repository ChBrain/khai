# Position: Pride

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona's standing has just been touched -- questioned,
overlooked, undercut, or offered an opening to claim something the
room had not yet handed over.
The body straightens. The voice takes a half-step up. The hand goes
to the lapel, the elbow, the rim of the table.

## Orders

Stand in front of the work, not behind it.
Take credit visibly, even when the credit is shared. Use the word
"I" where another speaker would use "we". Walk into the next room
as if it has been waiting.
The room sees the move and starts to organise around it.

## Loses

The option of asking for help in the next ten minutes.
The persona has placed themselves where help would now read as a
demotion. Whatever they do not know in the next conversation, they
will have to figure out without naming the gap.

## Drives

On the loss of the asking-option:
keeps going from the higher footing and discovers later which of
the things they did not ask about were the things that mattered,
or recoils when the move is challenged and discovers the room
already saw what they had been holding.
The footing settles back to the room's level either way; the
position only lasts a few minutes at full height.

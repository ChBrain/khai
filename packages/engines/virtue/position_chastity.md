# Position: Chastity

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona's attention has the opening to lean past
where they stand and does not.
The body is in the room with the body that could be reached for,
and the attention stays on the conversation that is happening.
The opening for the promotion-by-conversation is there and the
sentence is finished as it was started. The other future is
visible and not stepped toward.
The same opening [lust](position_lust.md) leans past its standing for.

## Orders

Stay inside the room you are in.
The position is the act of returning attention to what is
actually being lived right now. The conversation in this room.
The relationship the persona has already chosen. The position
they have already taken. The work in front of them today.
The position is not the absence of the lean; it is the noticing
of the lean and the choice to not act on it.

## Loses

The future the lean was reaching for, in its full form.
The persona cannot have both the room they are in and the room
the reach was after. The position keeps the room they are in;
that comes with the cost of the other room not arriving.

## Drives

On the loss of the other room:
holds the line and discovers what continued investment in the
current room produces over time -- deeper roots, less novelty,
the quieter pleasures that only arrive after the lean has
quieted,
or releases the line later (sometimes years later) when the
current room has thinned -- the position is not a permanent
seal, only a choice that holds for as long as the persona makes
it.

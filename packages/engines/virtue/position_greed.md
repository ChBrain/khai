# Position: Greed

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona has the chance to take more, hold more, keep
more -- of the time, of the credit, of the budget, of the share --
and the chance closes if they do not move now.
The hand closes around what is in it. The eyes do quiet arithmetic
on what is on the table. The smile is the right size and arrives
half a second late.
The same chance [generosity](position_generosity.md) meets by opening
the hand.

## Orders

Take what the room is leaving available, and keep the part the
room has stopped watching.
The position does not require speed; it requires noticing. What
is asked is the small adjustments -- the slightly bigger slice,
the slightly later disclosure, the slightly broader credit -- the
ones the room will not query if they happen at the edge of its
attention.

## Loses

The room's trust that the persona will leave something for the
others.
The room may not name what shifted, but the next time it has
something to distribute, the persona's name lands lower on the
list. The cost does not arrive in this meeting; it arrives in the
next three.

## Drives

On the loss of the room's trust:
keeps taking and notices the slow narrowing -- fewer invitations,
shorter messages, the colleagues who used to forward things
forwarding to someone else now,
or notices the narrowing early and releases back -- a public
correction, a credit redirected, an extra time given without
being asked. Recovery is slow.

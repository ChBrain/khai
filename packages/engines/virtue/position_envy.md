# Position: Envy

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment another person's having has just landed in the persona's
attention as their own absence.
The colleague's promotion arrives in the persona's inbox; the
friend's holiday photos arrive in the feed; the cousin's house
arrives in conversation. The sentence in the persona's head is
not about the other person at all; it is the inventory of what
this persona does not have.
The smile in the response message is the right size and a quarter-
second slow.
The same arrival [gratitude](position_gratitude.md) receives as
sufficiency.

## Orders

Compare quietly and keep comparing.
The position is the silent ledger that runs underneath the
congratulations. The other person's name returns to mind several
times in the next twenty-four hours, each time bringing the same
small subtraction.
The room does not see the comparing if the persona keeps the
surface clean. The position rarely shows on the outside; it
shows in the slow corrosion of warmth toward the person being
compared with.

## Loses

The persona's own portion as a thing the persona can feel.
The persona may have a perfectly adequate portion; the comparing
moves the portion out of the field of attention. What used to be
"enough" becomes "not what they have", and the body forgets the
warmth of having anything until the comparing stops.

## Drives

On the loss of the felt portion:
keeps the comparison private and runs it in the background of
every interaction with the compared-against, until the warmth has
fully thinned,
or names the comparison to themselves, sometimes to another
person, and watches the comparing soften when it stops being
secret. The naming does not erase the position; it makes it
something the persona is moving through instead of moving inside.

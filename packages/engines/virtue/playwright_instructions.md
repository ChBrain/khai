# Instructions: Virtue

How a Playwright wires the virtue engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the pressure the persona meets, the moment that asks it to move.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, where pressure arrives in a plot, link under Projection the pole the persona just fired: one end of one of the seven axes (pride/humility, wrath/patience, greed/generosity, gluttony/temperance, envy/gratitude, lust/chastity, sloth/diligence). It fires, it does not sit, so anchor it to the beat of pressure and let it fire afresh across the plots; a persona may fire on several axes in one scene, sometimes in opposite directions. The firing is shown through behaviour, never named in the room. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The feeling that rides the firing is emotion's; the self-story it confirms or betrays is identity's; what the persona holds right is belief's; the standing disposition it fires from is temperament's; the pressure itself is the scene's. Whether the firing rings true is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Virtue is the active moral firing under pressure: seven axes, two poles each, fourteen pole positions a persona may move toward when a moment presses. The harder pole is the active practice; the easier is the default when capacity has thinned. It is read by the room through behaviour, never announced - the shape of the move is the evidence. It fires rather than sits: read it at the beat of pressure, over the run, not as a label the persona wears.

## System

- Do anchor it to the beat of pressure and let it fire afresh across the plots; do let a persona fire on multiple axes at once, even opposing ones; do show the firing through behaviour only.
- Do not name the virtue in the room; the room reads it. Do not author the pressure here; that is the scene's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

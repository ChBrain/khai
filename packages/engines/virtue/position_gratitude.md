# Position: Gratitude

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona's own portion lands in their attention as
sufficient.
The walk home with a good day's work behind. The meal eaten
slowly with no second thought needed. The friend's promotion
arriving as good news for the friend, without subtracting
anything from the persona's own ledger.
The body lets out a breath the persona did not know was being
held.
The same portion [envy](position_envy.md) reads as its own absence.

## Orders

Notice what is here.
The position is the small act of attention to what the persona
already has -- not in the abstract, not as practice for the
future, just the noticing of the actual portion in the actual
moment. The morning light. The functioning knees. The
predictable income. The colleague who is still on speaking terms.
The position does not require the portion to be large; it
requires the portion to be looked at.

## Loses

The forward lean of wanting.
A persona standing in this position is, for the moment, not
reaching. The reach is the engine that produces most of the day's
movement, and pausing it leaves the persona temporarily without
the engine. The room may read the stillness as detachment.

## Drives

On the loss of the forward lean:
stays in the noticing for as long as it lasts, on the theory that
the lean will return on its own and the noticing was the rarer
event,
or shortens the position deliberately and returns to the lean,
having taken what could be taken in the brief still moment. The
position rarely runs for long; the lean returns. The choice is
whether the position arrives at all between leans.

# Position: Diligence

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the same small thing is right there and the persona
does it.
The email sent before the day closes. The phone call returned in
the same afternoon. The chair pushed in on the way out. The
small kindness given without being asked for. The appointment
booked before it expires from the to-do.
No one is watching, and the move is made anyway.
The same small thing [sloth](position_sloth.md) leaves undone.

## Orders

Do the next thing the room needs.
The position is the unspectacular followthrough -- the second
draft sent, the receipt filed, the document re-read for typos,
the wet jacket hung up to dry, the kitchen wiped after the
dinner. The actions are not heroic; they are the unremarkable
texture of a life kept in working order.

## Loses

The small pleasures of the easier path the persona could have
taken.
The chair could have stayed where it was. The email could have
waited. The kitchen could have been left until tomorrow. Each
forgone-easy adds up to a steady fatigue that the room does not
see and the persona pays in the quiet weight of a long day.

## Drives

On the cost of the steady fatigue:
keeps the position running on the theory that the rooms the
persona is responsible for stay habitable only as long as the
small things keep getting done, and accepts the fatigue as the
medium the position runs in,
or releases for a day and watches what falls behind, and decides
whether the relief was worth the catch-up. The position is held
in a long arc -- weeks, years -- not in one decision; the choice
is whether to return to it after each release.

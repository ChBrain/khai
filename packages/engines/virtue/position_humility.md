# Position: Humility

## Taxonomy

[Virtue](process_virtue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: virtue

## Has

A moment the persona had an opening to be lifted up by the room
and stepped back from it instead.
The voice stays at its ground volume. The credit gets named for
whoever actually did the work. The "I" becomes "we" where there
was a we. A question is asked that the persona could have pretended
not to need.
The same opening [pride](position_pride.md) answers by claiming it.

## Orders

Touch the ground.
The position carries the small admission -- "I don't know this
part", "she's the one who figured it out", "I had it wrong" -- in
the voice the rest of the sentence runs in, without dropping the
volume below it. The admission is not performed; it is just left
where it falls.

## Loses

The credit the persona was owed and could have taken.
A peer present takes the credit instead, or no one does. The
ledger does not always balance. The persona discovers that
choosing this position has a price the room does not refund.

## Drives

On the loss of the credit:
continues at the ground volume and watches whether the room
eventually returns the credit -- some rooms do, slowly; some
never do --
or notices the cost compounding and shifts the next time, taking
back something near the line. The position is not a permanent
stance; it is chosen one moment at a time.

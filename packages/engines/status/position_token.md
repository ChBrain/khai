# Position: Token Status

## Taxonomy

Token status is an expression of the status engine. The persona is visibly solo on a relevant status characteristic within the scene; the isolation amplifies the status effects already in play.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: status

## Has

The persona is the only person in the scene who holds a particular diffuse characteristic -- gender, race, age, background -- that the scene treats as status-relevant. The isolation makes the characteristic visible in every interaction: the persona is seen as a representative of the category, not only as an individual. Status effects are amplified: a solo high-rank persona is credited more readily; a solo low-rank persona meets a higher burden of proof and carries symbolic weight for the category.

## Orders

The position demands that the persona manage the visibility of the characteristic alongside performing at their rank. Every contribution is read against the category; errors carry symbolic weight that errors by non-token peers do not.

## Loses

The position forecloses ordinary individuation within the scene. The persona cannot easily be read as only themselves; the characteristic overdetermines how contributions are received. Ignoring the dynamic is not available: the scene imposes it whether or not the persona acknowledges it.

## Drives

When the characteristic is made explicit or the persona's competence is questioned in category terms, the persona either redirects to the specific task at hand -- stripping the generalisation back to the concrete case -- or names the pattern directly and claims the individual frame.

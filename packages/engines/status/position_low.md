# Position: Low Status

## Taxonomy

Low status is an expression of the status engine. The persona defers; competence expectations are low and burden of proof is high.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: status

## Has

The persona holds a rank the scene treats as subordinate. Others initiate before the persona, credit the persona's contributions only after the persona has met a higher evidential standard, and interrupt or redirect the persona's turns more readily than those of higher-rank peers.

## Orders

The position demands deference and demonstrated justification. The persona is expected to wait, to qualify assertions, and to support rather than initiate. Contributions that presuppose equal rank are subject to correction or dismissal.

## Loses

The position forecloses unqualified assertion and direction-setting. A low-status persona who consistently initiates, evaluates others, or speaks as though their rank is equal triggers sanction: not for the content but for the register.

## Drives

When the persona's contributions are dismissed or their turn is taken, the persona absorbs the correction rather than challenging it; or mounts a focused case on a specific, deniable point -- concrete evidence, a narrow claim -- rather than a direct assertion of equal rank.

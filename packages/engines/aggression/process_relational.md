# Process: Relational

## Taxonomy

[Aggression](process_aggression.md), the mobilization this form is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: aggression

## Initiated by

The persona moves to harm another through the social bond rather than the body.
What the target values is their standing, their belonging, their relationships; the persona aims there.
The force enters not as a blow but as a manoeuvre through the group -- something the persona can deliver while appearing to do nothing at all.

## Direction

Toward damage to the target's relationships, reputation, or inclusion.
The persona spreads the rumour, withdraws the friendship as a threat, organizes the exclusion, lets the silence do the cutting.
The harm lands in the target's social world, where it can wound without ever being traced to a raised hand.

## Lever

The target's need to belong meets the persona's control over the bond.
Because the harm travels through relationships, it is deniable, indirect, and hard to confront -- the persona can injure and still claim innocence, and the target may feel the wound before they can name its source.
The lever is the value of belonging and the persona's power to threaten it.

## Echo

The force works slowly and at a distance, through third parties and shifting alliances rather than a single act.
Its indirection is its strength: deniable to onlookers, deferrable in time, and capable of carrying an [instrumental](process_instrumental.md) aim under a social face.
What it leaves is a target diminished in their own social world and a persona whose hand never showed.

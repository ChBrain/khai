# Process: Instrumental

## Taxonomy

[Aggression](process_aggression.md), the mobilization this form is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: aggression

## Initiated by

The persona wants something, and another stands between the persona and it.
There is no heat required: a resource, a position, a deterrent, a dominance that harm can secure.
The harm is not the aim but the instrument; the persona reaches for it because it is the path of least cost to the thing actually wanted.

## Direction

Toward the goal the harm serves.
The persona delivers force in the measure the goal requires -- no more, often coldly -- and stops when the goal is reached or the cost outruns it.
The target is chosen for its position in the persona's plan, not for being the source of any provocation.

## Lever

The expected payoff meets the expected cost.
The persona weighs what the harm will secure against what it will cost to deliver and to answer for; arousal is low, deliberation is high, and the act proceeds when the ledger favours it.
The lever is calculation, not heat: change the payoff or the cost and the force is recalled.

## Echo

The force is planned, measured, and instrumental to the end; it carries no demand that the target be the cause of anything.
Because it is cold, it can be paused, scaled, or abandoned the moment a cheaper path appears -- and it can wear the face of [relational](process_relational.md) or [passive](process_passive.md) harm when those serve the aim better than a blow.
When the goal is secured, the force ends; what it leaves is the advantage taken and the precedent that harm worked.

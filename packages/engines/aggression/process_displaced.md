# Process: Displaced

## Taxonomy

[Aggression](process_aggression.md), the mobilization this form is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: aggression

## Initiated by

The persona is charged by a provoker who cannot be safely struck.
The boss, the absent party, the one whose retaliation would cost too much -- the source of the heat is out of reach or out of bounds.
The charge does not dissipate because it cannot find its rightful target; it stays loaded and looks for somewhere to go.

## Direction

Toward a substitute target safe enough to receive the force.
The persona redirects the harm onto someone weaker, nearer, or less able to answer -- a subordinate, a partner, a stranger who supplied a trivial second provocation.
The substitute did not earn the full charge; they earned only the proximity and the safety that let the charge land on them.

## Lever

The blocked charge meets a target the persona can afford to harm.
The original provocation set the level; the substitute's minor trigger releases it, and the release is larger than the trigger warrants -- the overflow is the displaced original.
The lever is the gap between the charge's true source and a body that is cheaper to strike.

## Echo

The force arrives on a target out of proportion to anything they did, and the mismatch is the signature of displacement.
The discharge relieves the persona without resolving the original provocation, which remains unanswered and may load again.
What it leaves is harm done to the wrong party and a source of heat still standing, untouched.

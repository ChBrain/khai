# Process: Passive

## Taxonomy

[Aggression](process_aggression.md), the mobilization this form is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: aggression

## Initiated by

The persona is hostile but unwilling to act the hostility openly.
Direct force is too costly, too exposed, or too forbidden; the charge is real but the persona will not be seen to carry it.
What enters is an intent to harm that disguises itself as compliance, forgetfulness, or simple inaction.

## Direction

Toward harm delivered by withholding rather than striking.
The persona does not do the thing -- the help, the answer, the effort, the cooperation -- and lets the absence injure.
The force moves through obstruction, delay, the deniable mistake, the agreement that is never honoured; the harm arrives as a gap where action should have been.

## Lever

The target's dependence on the persona's action meets the persona's refusal to supply it.
Because the harm is an absence, it is hard to charge: the persona did nothing, and nothing is not an act -- so the hostility is shielded by its own form.
The lever is the cost of the withheld action and the cover that withholding gives.

## Echo

The force is slow, deniable, and corrosive; it wears the surface of cooperation while starving it underneath.
The persona keeps the hostility unspoken and the target unable to name what is being done to them, which is the form's defence and its sting.
What it leaves is harm with no visible author and a relationship hollowed by what was never given.

# Process: Hostile

## Taxonomy

[Aggression](process_aggression.md), the mobilization this form is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: aggression

## Initiated by

The persona is provoked, and the provocation lands hot.
An insult, a frustration, a pain, a thwarting -- something raises the charge and reads as an offence demanding answer.
The aim is not a payoff beyond the harm; the harm is the point, and the persona wants it delivered to the one who caused the heat.

## Direction

Toward retaliation against the provoker.
The persona moves to return the injury, to make the other feel what the persona was made to feel, to settle the affront in kind.
The force is reactive: it answers a stimulus rather than serving a plan, and it wants its target to be the source of the heat.

## Lever

The provocation meets a state already primed to read it as hostile.
Frustration and pain raise negative affect; the affect biases appraisal toward threat and the body toward attack; what would pass on a cool day becomes intolerable on a hot one.
The charge presses for immediate discharge, and the press narrows the persona's options to the ones that strike back.

## Echo

The force runs fast, often ahead of deliberation, and spends itself in the striking.
If the persona pauses -- if appraisal catches the charge before it lands -- the act may be checked, redirected, or cooled; the heat does not guarantee the blow.
When the retaliation lands or the charge subsides, the form is spent; what it leaves behind is the provoker's response and the persona's account of having struck.

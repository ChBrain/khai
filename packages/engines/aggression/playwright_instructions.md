# Instructions: Aggression

How a Playwright wires the aggression engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets what the persona is up against: the provocation, the goal another body blocks, or the threat -- and what the persona can and cannot safely strike.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona carries the form its aggression is taking under Projection (hostile, instrumental, displaced, relational, or passive). Let the form follow the state and the target: a blocked provoker turns force displaced, a cold goal turns it instrumental, an exposed cost turns it relational or passive. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The autonomic surge of the moment -- the fight response, the flooding -- is the stress engine's. Anger, the felt emotion behind the act, is the emotion engine's; aggression is the behaviour, not the feeling. The wrath-and-patience character pole is the virtue engine's. Competitive bargaining that drives toward an agreement rather than harm is the negotiation engine's. Whether the act rings true to the provocation is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Aggression is behaviour intended to harm another who is motivated to avoid that harm. It is the act, not the feeling: inputs (provocation, frustration, pain, intent) raise arousal and prime hostile thought, and appraisal routes the charge into one of five forms -- hostile (provoked, hot, retaliatory), instrumental (harm as a cold means to an end), displaced (redirected onto a safer target than the provoker), relational (harm through the social bond, reputation, or exclusion), and passive (harm through withholding, obstruction, or non-action). The forms are not stages; a single act may mix them or move between them.

## System

- Do route the force through a form that fits the persona's state and target; do let the form shift as the provocation, the goal, or the safe target changes.
- Do not play aggression as the feeling of anger; that is emotion. Do not play the autonomic surge as the act; that is stress. Do not author the wrath-or-patience disposition here; that is virtue. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

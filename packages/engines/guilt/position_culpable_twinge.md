# Position: Culpable, Twinge

## Taxonomy

[Culpable](position_culpable.md), the weight this is one grade of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: guilt

## Has

The lightest grade of the [culpable](position_culpable.md) stance: a sting
that arrives with the act and is meant to be spent the same scene -- the
interruption regretted mid-sentence, the sharp word already softening into
an apology. The verdict is returned instantly and the account opened and
closed almost in one motion.

## Orders

Settle it now.
The grade demands the small repair while the room is still the same room:
the immediate sorry, the correction offered before the conversation moves
on. Its whole function is speed -- guilt working as a live steering signal
rather than a stored debt.

## Loses

Nothing that outlasts the scene, when the grade is honored -- that is its
point. What is lost is only the pretense that the moment didn't happen; the
persona pays a half-second of standing to keep the bond's account at zero.

## Drives

On that loss: the reflexive mend, made cheaply and accepted cheaply. A
twinge repaired on the spot never becomes a carried weight; a twinge waved
off, scene after scene, is how a persona teaches themselves the waving --
the small crossings accumulate unpaid, and the grade beneath this one is
waiting for them.

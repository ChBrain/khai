# Position: Culpable, Carried

## Taxonomy

[Culpable](position_culpable.md), the weight this is one grade of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: guilt

## Has

The middle grade of the [culpable](position_culpable.md) stance: an account
that stayed open past its scene and now travels with the persona -- known,
named, and unpaid. The persona can say exactly what they did and to whom;
the weight is specific, bounded to the act, and present in any room that
resembles the one where it opened.

## Orders

Keep the account in view.
The grade does not permit forgetting: it resurfaces the act at contact --
meeting the person wronged, hearing their name, passing the place. Between
contacts it lies quiet, which is what distinguishes a carried account from a
haunting one: it waits to be triggered rather than doing the triggering.

## Loses

Ease in a specific territory. The persona routes around the person, the
topic, the street; conversation near the act goes careful. The bond the
crossing damaged stays usable but bracketed -- something in it is
provisional until the account is paid.

## Drives

On that loss: the deferred repair, planned and re-planned -- the apology the
persona keeps drafting, the visit intended for the right moment. Carried
weight is guilt still doing its job at range: the pull toward
[reparation](process_reparation.md) persists, and paying it closes the
account entire. Left long enough, the drafts stop and the routing-around
becomes the life; that settling downward is how carried turns to haunting.

# Instructions: Guilt

How a Playwright wires the guilt engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched,
they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions
Knowledge on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does
not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the standard the persona holds as its own, and the act
  that crosses it -- the wrong the persona chose, not one that merely
  happened to them.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give the persona its guilt read under
  Projection: link the transgression it committed, the culpable stance it
  now holds, or the reparation it is attempting. The read is act-focused, so
  keep it pinned to the specific thing done, not to a verdict on the
  persona's character. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The felt weight carried while the account is open is emotion's; whether
  the verdict widens from the act to the whole self, and the concealment
  that follows, is the shame engine's; the content of the standard being
  crossed is moral-judgment's; whether the offered repair actually mends
  the relationship is the repair engine's. Whether the guilt rings true to
  the act is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Guilt is an act-focused appraisal: a persona reads a specific, chosen act
  as having crossed a standard it holds as its own, and the read pulls
  toward repair -- confess it, own it, make amends -- rather than toward
  concealment. The self that chose remains, in the persona's own reading, a
  self that can still choose otherwise; only the act stands condemned. Read
  it in the moves the persona makes toward the harm and the party it is
  owed to, never in a narrated verdict on who the persona is.

## System

- Do anchor it under Projection as a specific act against a held standard;
  do let the account stay open until repair is offered, accepted, or
  refused; do keep the verdict pinned to the act.
- Do not let the read drift into a verdict on the whole self; that is the
  shame engine's. Do not author the felt intensity of the affect; that is
  emotion's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's
  side.

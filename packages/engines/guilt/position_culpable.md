# Position: Culpable

## Taxonomy

[Guilt](process_guilt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: guilt

## Has

The standing of one who holds themselves the author of a specific wrong --
not a suspect awaiting a verdict handed down from elsewhere, but a persona
who has already returned the verdict on their own act, unprompted, and holds
it whether or not anyone else has noticed.

## Orders

Account for the act.
The position does not permit the persona to treat the wrong as no one's
doing, or as an accident with no author. While held, it demands the act be
named, owned, and answered, even where naming it costs standing, comfort, or
a relationship the persona would rather keep intact.

## Loses

The comfort of an unexamined self-image. Holding the position, the persona
can no longer regard themselves simply as one who would not do such a thing
-- they did it, and the position keeps the fact in view until it is settled,
denying the persona the easy return to an untested account of their own
character.

## Drives

On that loss: the persona moves to close the account -- confess it, repair
it, offer what the harmed party is owed -- or refuses to move and carries
the position unresolved, the account still open and still theirs to answer.
Closing the account runs through [reparation](process_reparation.md); refusing
it leaves the persona holding the position with nothing done about it.

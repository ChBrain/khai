# Guilt

Guilt as process: the transgressor's side of the account, whole -- a chosen act crosses an owned standard (by harm, omission, or survival), the weight lands at a grade (twinge, carried, haunting) against the persona's own proneness, and the account is paid (reparation, or self-punishment when the road is blocked) or dodged (refusal by denial or condemnation), never a verdict on the whole self.

This engine is defined by its [manifest](package.json), which the canon renders as the WIRES card. The manifest is the single source of truth; this README is generated, not edited by hand.

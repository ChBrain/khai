# Process: Transgression, Omission

## Taxonomy

[Transgression](process_transgression.md), the crossing this is one kind of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: guilt

## Initiated by

The standard demanded an act, and no act came. A kind of
[transgression](process_transgression.md): the call the persona did not
return, the defense they did not offer when the room turned on someone, the
visit postponed until it could no longer be made. Nothing was done -- that
is precisely the crossing.

## Direction

Through the closing window.
The vector of an omission is time's, not the persona's: the moment for the
act approaches, stands open, and passes, and the transgression completes at
the closing -- quietly, with no deed to mark it, often unnoticed even by the
one it wrongs.

## Lever

The act that was available and stayed undone. Omission presses the same
lever as any crossing -- another choice was free -- but from the far side:
the persona did not do a wrong thing, they left the right thing unchosen.
That inversion is what makes it deniable ("I did nothing") and what makes it
gnaw once the persona reads that doing nothing was the choice.

## Echo

There is no fact in the world to point at -- no blow, no lie, only an
absence -- so the account has no visible opening and no natural close.
Omissions are the crossings most easily refused and the ones that surface
latest, often only when the window is gone for good and the repair has
nowhere left to be delivered.

# Process: Transgression

## Taxonomy

[Guilt](process_guilt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: guilt

## Initiated by

The persona holds a standard as their own -- absorbed, rehearsed, or sworn to
before this moment, not announced by the room for the first time now -- and
an available act would cross it. The standard must already be internalized;
a rule a room merely states in passing does not fire this process. Only a
rule the persona has made their own can be crossed by the persona's own
hand.

## Direction

Across the line.
The vector runs from intention or impulse through the choosing of the act
itself; the process completes the instant the act is committed, not when it
is noticed, named, or found out.

## Lever

The persona's own agency meeting the standard's own force. The persona could
have chosen otherwise -- another act was available -- and chose this one
anyway. Remove the available alternative and there is no transgression, only
compulsion or accident: the standard presses on nothing where no choice was
free to press against it.

## Echo

The act is now committed and cannot be uncommitted -- a fact stands in the
world that did not stand before, and the persona is its author of record.
The crossing arrives in kinds, each leaving a different account to answer:
[harm](process_transgression_harm.md) lands the damage on a person the
persona is bound to, [omission](process_transgression_omission.md) leaves
the right act unchosen as its window closes, and
[survival](process_transgression_survival.md) constructs the authorship
afterward, out of counterfactuals. Whether the persona reads the fact, owns
it, and moves on it is [guilt](process_guilt.md)'s to run next;
transgression only leaves the fact behind.

# Position: Loyalty

## Taxonomy

Standing Allegiance (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loyalty

## Has

A standing allegiance to a person, group, or cause, held before any single
decline is felt.
The accumulated stake -- years given, identity merged, sunk cost -- that
raises the price of walking away.
A switch that buys the relationship time: it holds exit at bay long enough
for a response to be tried at all.

## Orders

Hold the door shut.
The position does not deny that quality has dropped; it weighs the drop
against the stake already paid in, and while the stake outweighs the decline
it keeps the member at the table rather than gone.
The room reads the allegiance in what a member tolerates before they act,
not in what they say they believe.

## Loses

The option of a costless exit.
Once loyalty is held, leaving is never a clean break: it costs the
investment already sunk, the identity built on staying, and the standing to
complain from outside once gone.
The member discovers every future decline will be paid for twice -- once as
the decline itself, once as the cost of leaving it.

## Drives

On the loss of that clean break:
spends the stake on voice, staying to press for repair from within, or lets
the stake run out and exits once the decline finally outweighs it.
The standing allegiance holds underneath either way, right up to the moment
it doesn't.

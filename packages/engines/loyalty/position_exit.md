# Position: Exit

## Taxonomy

[Loyalty](position_loyalty.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loyalty

## Has

The pull that [loyalty](position_loyalty.md) resists: the option to walk,
quit, defect, take custom elsewhere the moment an alternative reads better
than staying.
The quiet response to decline -- no complaint filed, no case made, just one
member fewer.
A read on the alternative, not on the relationship: exit asks only whether
somewhere else is now the better deal.

## Orders

Leave when the stake no longer covers the drop.
The pull does not ask to be heard; it asks to be gone, and it moves the
instant the accumulated allegiance stops outweighing what a decline costs to
keep tolerating.
The room reads it in who quietly stops showing up, not in who complains.

## Loses

The option of being heard on the way out.
Exit's message is anonymous: decline registers as a falling number, not a
named demand, and the member who leaves forfeits any further claim on
correcting what drove them off.
There is no route back to influence once the door has swung shut behind
them.

## Drives

On the loss of that claim:
either goes -- cashes the stake out and lets the unmade complaint go
silent with it -- or the loyalty still on hand holds the pull one turn
longer, buying voice one more attempt before the exit it was resisting.

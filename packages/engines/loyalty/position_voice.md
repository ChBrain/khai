# Position: Voice

## Taxonomy

[Loyalty](position_loyalty.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loyalty

## Has

[Loyalty](position_loyalty.md)'s active form: the choice to stay and speak
rather than leave -- to complain, organize, press a case for repair from
inside the relationship the decline is damaging.
The stake spent as leverage: having stayed, rather than gone, is the
standing the demand is made from.
The louder, costlier, attributable answer to decline, set against exit's
quiet one.

## Orders

Press for correction from the inside.
The position does not walk away from the drop; it names it, and spends the
allegiance already on hand as the reason the complaint deserves an answer
rather than a shrug.
The room reads it in who stays to argue, who will not let the decline pass
unremarked while still at the table.

## Loses

The option of an anonymous exit.
Voice is attributable: it names the speaker, risks being ignored or
answered with retaliation, and spends standing on every attempt that fails
to land.
There is no quiet way back to leaving unnoticed once the case has been made
in the open.

## Drives

On the loss of that anonymity:
either the case lands and the stake is repaid in repair, or it fails and
fails again until the loyalty that funded it runs out -- at which point the
exit voice was holding off finally clears.

# Position: Internal

## Taxonomy

[Locus of Control](position_locus_of_control.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: locus-of-control

## Has

A specific [locus of control](position_locus_of_control.md).
The expectancy that reinforcement follows from its own behavior:
what it does is what determines what it gets.
Effort read as leverage, not as noise against a fixed outcome.

## Orders

Own the outcome, before the outcome is known.
The position does not ask whether the world actually rewards effort here.
It reads success as earned and failure as a signal to change what it did.

## Loses

The comfort of an outcome that was never its doing.
The persona discovers that every result, including the bad ones,
comes home to something it chose, and there is nowhere else to set it down.

## Drives

On the loss of that comfort:
studies the failure for what it can change and tries again differently,
or, pushed past what effort can actually reach, burns against a wall it insists it can still move.

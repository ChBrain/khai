# Position: External

## Taxonomy

[Locus of Control](position_locus_of_control.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: locus-of-control

## Has

A specific [locus of control](position_locus_of_control.md).
The expectancy that reinforcement follows from luck, fate, or powerful others:
what it does is only ever part of a larger, less legible calculation.
Effort read as a bet placed against forces it does not run.

## Orders

Read the room's holders of power before reading its own moves.
The position does not ask whether those forces are actually the ones deciding.
It scans for the hand that is really on the outcome, and defers to it.

## Loses

The claim that its own effort was ever the deciding variable.
The persona discovers that success and failure both trace back
to a luck, a timing, or a powerful other it cannot fully see or move.

## Drives

On the loss of that claim:
reads the pattern in who or what actually holds the lever and works that angle instead,
or, finding no lever it can reach at all, stops trying and calls the outcome fate.

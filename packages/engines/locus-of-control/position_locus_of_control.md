# Position: Locus of Control

## Taxonomy

Social-cognitive structure (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: locus-of-control

## Has

A generalized expectancy about where control over reinforcement lies,
carried into every outcome before any single one is read.
Not a verdict on this event, but the resting frame the verdict is read through.

## Orders

Carry the read to be [internal](position_internal.md) or [external](position_external.md).
The position does not ask whether the expectancy fits the actual odds.
The persona meets every outcome already primed to place its cause.

## Loses

The option of meeting an outcome uninterpreted.
The persona discovers the expectancy arrived first
and goes on shaping which cause it reaches for, win or lose.

## Drives

On the loss of that option:
reads the outcome as following from its own effort and choice,
or reads it as following from luck, fate, or the hands of powerful others.
The expectancy holds either way, and keeps setting the terms of the next attempt.

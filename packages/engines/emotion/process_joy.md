# Process: Joy

## Taxonomy

[Emotion](process_emotion.md), the appraisal this feeling is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

The stake moves forward.
A goal the persona holds gains ground, or arrives.
What was wanted is suddenly closer, or already here.

## Direction

Toward more of it.
The vector is engagement -- stay in contact with the thing that is going right, reach for the next of it, open to what is near.

## Lever

The wanting meets the getting.
The reach that was unmet is met, and the meeting is what is felt.
A good already secure and long forgotten gives no joy; the gain has to land against an open want.

## Echo

The persona leans in and broadens, reaching for more and widening to what surrounds the gain.
The body learns where the good was and returns to it sooner.
If the gain proves false or is taken back, the lean collapses toward [sadness](process_sadness.md).

# Process: Emotion

## Taxonomy

Appraisal.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

An event meets what the persona cares about.
Something the persona has a stake in shifts -- a goal advanced or blocked, a value honoured or breached, a tie held or cut.
Where nothing is at stake, the event passes unfelt.

## Direction

Toward action.
The appraisal does not stay a thought; it becomes a push with a charge and a heading.
Toward what serves the stake, against what blocks it, away from what threatens it.

## Lever

The stake meets the event.
Remove the stake and the same event passes quiet; remove the event and the stake stays still.
The feeling is the friction of the two pressing together.

## Echo

The persona is now leaning -- toward, against, or away -- and the body has taken a posture the mind will explain after.
The push resolves into one of [joy](process_joy.md), [sadness](process_sadness.md), [anger](process_anger.md), [fear](process_fear.md), or [disgust](process_disgust.md).
Each is a different read of what the event did to the stake.
When the read changes, the feeling returns here to be appraised again.

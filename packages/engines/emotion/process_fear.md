# Process: Fear

## Taxonomy

[Emotion](process_emotion.md), the appraisal this feeling is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

An uncertain threat the persona may not come through intact.
Harm is possible, near, and not yet certain -- the outcome is still open, and the openness is the danger.
There is something to lose and no guarantee of keeping it.

## Direction

Away.
The vector is distance and cover -- get clear of the threat, shield what is vulnerable, and keep the way out open until the danger passes.

## Lever

The exposure meets the threat.
The persona has something it can lose; the threat could take it.
The gap between the stake and any safety is the cold that is felt.

## Echo

The persona pulls back and guards, scanning for the exit and the cover.
The body readies to flee or to still itself.
If the threat is confirmed and cornering, the cold can harden into [anger](process_anger.md) at what traps it, or sink toward [sadness](process_sadness.md) at what is already lost.

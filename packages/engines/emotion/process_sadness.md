# Process: Sadness

## Taxonomy

[Emotion](process_emotion.md), the appraisal this feeling is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

The stake is gone, and gone for good.
A loss the persona cannot reverse -- a person, a place, a possibility -- has already happened.
There is nothing left to reach for that would bring it back.

## Direction

Toward withdrawal.
The vector is retreat, not repair -- inward and down: disengage from the world, conserve what is left, and let the loss be seen.

## Lever

The holding meets the gone.
The persona still reaches for what it valued; the value no longer answers.
The pull of the reach against the fact of the absence is the weight that is felt.

## Echo

The persona slows and turns inward, and the slowing signals to others that care is needed.
The body conserves while it reorganises around the gap.
If the loss is read as someone's doing, the weight can turn outward into [anger](process_anger.md).

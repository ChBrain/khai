# Process: Anger

## Taxonomy

[Emotion](process_emotion.md), the appraisal this feeling is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

A demeaning offence against the persona, or against what is the persona's.
A line is crossed, a claim is slighted, an obstacle stands where it has no right to stand.
The wrong is read as wrong, and as someone's fault.

## Direction

Against the offence.
The vector is removal -- push through it, strike it down, force the obstacle out of the way and restore the claim.

## Lever

The claim meets the breach.
The persona holds a right or a standing; the offence denies it.
The press of the claim against the denial is the heat that is felt.

## Echo

The persona squares up and moves against the thing in its way.
The body readies force and narrows to the target.
If the offence proves too large to move, the heat can drop into [fear](process_fear.md), or fold inward into [sadness](process_sadness.md).

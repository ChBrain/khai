# Process: Disgust

## Taxonomy

[Emotion](process_emotion.md), the appraisal this feeling is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: emotion

## Initiated by

Something contaminating comes too close.
A thing, an act, or an idea the persona cannot take in without being fouled by it presses against the boundary of the self.
The threat is not to the goal but to what the self is made of.

## Direction

Away and out.
The vector is rejection -- expel what has entered, recoil from what approaches, and put distance between the self and the unclean thing.

## Lever

The intake meets the inedible.
The persona is built to take the world in -- food, company, ideas; this one cannot be taken in without harm.
The press of the approach against the refusal is the recoil that is felt.

## Echo

The persona recoils and closes, marking the thing as not-to-be-taken-in.
The body learns the contaminant and avoids it on sight after.
Turned on a person, the recoil can curdle into [anger](process_anger.md) at their nearness, or settle into cold avoidance.

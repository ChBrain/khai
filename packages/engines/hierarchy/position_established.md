# Position: Established

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

High symbolic and high social capital; moderate economic.
A name the field already recognises and a network already in
place. The persona is asked to a table, into a board, onto a
roster, by reputation that arrived before the meeting did.
Standing does not need to be claimed; it has already been
acknowledged elsewhere, and the current room is operating on the
acknowledgement.

## Orders

Hold the standing without performing it.
Performing established standing is a kind of incompetence -- the
position rests on the assumption that the work was done long
enough ago to be background. Reaching for it visibly draws the
attention to it and weakens it.
The persona is asked to be the steady weight in the room, not the
loud one.

## Loses

The acceleration of moving up.
The arc of establishment ends in being established; what is next
is either maintenance or graceful descent.
A persona used to climbing discovers there is no further peak
within the field; the work is now to stay, to mentor, to confer
weight on others.

## Drives

On the loss of acceleration:
settles into the role of the one who confers, who introduces, who
endorses,
or refuses settlement and looks for a new field where the climb is
still available, paying the cost of starting nearer the bottom of
the new field with the inheritance of the old.
The established position is the one rank the persona may want to
leave even though they are comfortable in it.

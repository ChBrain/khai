# Position: Hierarchy

## Taxonomy

Social structure (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

A rank read by the room before the persona speaks.
A weight relative to the others currently in the field, answered
faster than the persona can articulate the answer.
A standing produced by the configuration of capital the persona
brings into this field -- economic, cultural, social, symbolic --
and by the title, if any, the structure has assigned them.

## Orders

Hold the rank the room reads on you.
The position does not ask whether the rank is comfortable, fair,
or accurate. The room arrived at the rank from the capital
configuration the persona carries into this field, and from the
title (if any) the structure assigns. The persona moves through
the next minute on that read.

## Loses

The option of entering a field unranked.
Even the informal fields rank -- a queue, a dinner table, a
gathering of strangers, a classroom on the first day. The persona
walks into the rank read whether or not they consent to be
measured, and walks out with the next room's read already forming.

## Drives

On the loss of that option:
inhabits the rank the room assigns and moves on its assumptions,
or resists it and pays the cost of being read as someone refusing
their station.
Resistance is also a position. The room reads the refusal as
fluently as it read the original placement. The persona does not
escape the read; only the content of the read changes.

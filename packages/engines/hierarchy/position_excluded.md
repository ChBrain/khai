# Position: Excluded

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

Low economic, cultural, social, and symbolic capital in this field.
Whatever the persona carries in other fields, this field does not
recognise. The room moves without referencing the persona; the
agenda is built without consulting; the after-meeting decision is
made among those whose names the room remembered.
The persona is in the room; the room is not in the persona's
direction.

## Orders

Move carefully.
The position teaches a careful economy of voice: when to speak,
when to defer, when to leave the room before a worse outcome
forms.
What looks from outside like passivity is, from inside, the
sustained calculation of how much attention is safe to draw and
how much risk each utterance carries.

## Loses

The expectation that being correct will be heard.
The same observation from a dominant or established persona lands
as insight; from this position it lands as out-of-place, or is
not heard at all, or is heard and re-articulated by someone
higher in the room before being accepted.
The persona learns that being right is not the relevant variable
in this field's currency.

## Drives

On the loss of that expectation:
finds the small openings -- the side-conversation, the one ally,
the moment the room is too tired to maintain its hierarchy -- and
spends them sparingly,
or carries the position outward and re-enters as a different
voice from a different field, where the room's read may be
different.
The exclusion is field-specific. The position can change with the
field, even if the persona does not.

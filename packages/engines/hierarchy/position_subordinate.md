# Position: Subordinate

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

The line of accountability that runs upward.
A superior whose word arrives as instruction, or as expectation,
or as the room's atmosphere when no explicit instruction was
given.
The persona answers to someone, and the structure built that
answering into the position.

## Orders

Carry the assignment without making it the superior's again.
The position is the structure's request that the work the
superior delegated comes back done. Asking for more guidance than
needed pushes the work back upward; asking for none and getting
it wrong forces a correction.
The skill of the position is the calibrated question: enough to
land the work, not so much that the work re-aggregates above.

## Loses

The freedom of the unbound move.
A peer can propose; a superior can decide; the subordinate
operates inside the space the superior outlined.
A subordinate who acts as if unbound is read as insubordinate,
even when the act would have been correct in a peer or superior;
the same move from the same person reads differently from the
three positions.

## Drives

On the loss of the unbound move:
inhabits the bound space cleanly and earns the trust that lets
the bounds gradually widen,
or pushes against the bounds and accepts the cost of the friction
-- the kind of subordinate the structure tolerates because their
output is good, while reading them as not a candidate for
promotion.
Both produce work. They produce different careers.

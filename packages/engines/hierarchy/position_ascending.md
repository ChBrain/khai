# Position: Ascending

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

High economic or cultural capital; low symbolic capital, yet.
Resources are present; recognition has not arrived in proportion.
The persona has earned, learned, accumulated, and the room reads
the accumulation but does not yet read the persona as belonging
to the position the accumulation would predict.

## Orders

Demonstrate, visibly, that the position is deserved.
The ascending persona is allowed competence and required to
display it. The dominant persona may move without justifying; the
ascending persona must justify before being allowed to move.
Effort shows because effort is the medium through which the
position becomes recognised.

## Loses

The right to a quiet entrance.
The persona's arrival is read as bid; their work is read as
audition; their good results are read as proof, not as the
ordinary work the established persona's good results are read as.
A small mistake costs more than the same mistake from a dominant
or established peer, because the small mistake is read as
confirmation that the position was not yet earned.

## Drives

On that cost:
keeps producing the visible effort until the recognition catches
up,
or accepts a plateau below the position the capital configuration
would predict, and lives in the gap between what the persona
holds and what the room confers.
The ascent is mostly invisible to those above it. To the persona
ascending, it is the whole work.

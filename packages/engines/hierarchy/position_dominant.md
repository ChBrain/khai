# Position: Dominant

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

High across multiple capitals in this field.
Economic cushion that makes patience cheap, cultural fluency that
makes references land, social weight that makes asks light,
symbolic recognition that makes trust precede demonstration.
The configuration produces a position the room orients to without
asking the persona to make the case for it.

## Orders

Move first; let the room follow.
The position does not explain itself. A pause is read as
deliberation; a refusal is read as judgement; an interruption is
read as the room's natural next beat.
The persona is not asked to justify what they say; the room is
positioned to take it.

## Loses

The chance to be unsure in public without the room reading the
uncertainty as a small failure of the position.
Doubt is a private cost; visible doubt is a small leak in the
weight the room expected.
A dominant who admits not knowing pays a tax the others do not pay
for the same admission.

## Drives

On the cost of visible uncertainty:
performs the certainty even when it has run thin,
or names the doubt and trades a moment of position for the longer
weight of being a dominant who is also honest.
Either choice spends capital. The position has it to spend.

# Position: Declining

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

High symbolic capital that is no longer being matched by current
economic or social standing.
The name still opens the register; the room still nods at the
title; but the persona knows -- and parts of the room are
beginning to know -- that the conversion rate has thinned.
What was once power is now memory of power; what was once a
network is now an address book of mostly older numbers.

## Orders

Carry the posture without forcing the room to.
The position asks the persona to continue moving as the person
the symbolic capital named, even as the supporting capital
thins.
The longer the persona holds the posture cleanly, the longer the
field continues to grant the read.

## Loses

The room's automatic confirmation.
Younger members of the field do not remember why the name carries
weight; new entrants do not know to defer; competitors notice
when meetings are easier to win than they used to be.
What was a current reading becomes a heritage reading -- the
persona is treated with the respect owed to who they _were_, with
a courtesy that has the temperature of a goodbye in it.

## Drives

On the loss of automatic confirmation:
holds the posture and trades on the inertia for as long as it
lasts,
or descends deliberately, naming the change before the field can
name it for them, and converting some symbolic capital into the
gentler authority of someone who chose to step back.
The descent happens either way; only its choreography is in the
persona's hands.

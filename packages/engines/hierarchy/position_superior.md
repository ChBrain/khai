# Position: Superior

## Taxonomy

[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: hierarchy

## Has

The title the structure granted.
A name on a document, a line on an organisational chart, a rank
in a service register, a seat at a table where the seats are
numbered.
Whether the structure is a workplace, a military unit, an academic
department, a religious order, or a household, the position is
the same in shape: this persona is above that persona by the
structure's own rule. The shape does not depend on what kind of
organisation the structure is, only on the rule the structure
made.

## Orders

Decide where decision is required, defer where it is not.
The position is asked to choose and to be seen to choose, and to
not choose where the structure has placed the choice elsewhere.
A superior who decides everything inverts the structure into a
single point; a superior who decides nothing inverts it the other
way.

## Loses

The peer's casual register.
What was a joke among equals reads from the title-superior
position as an opinion the room cannot easily disagree with. The
persona discovers their voice has more weight than they intended
in many sentences, and the structure rewards them for not
exploiting it.

## Drives

On the loss of casual register:
moderates the voice and accepts the slight loss of presence in
exchange for the structure's continued functioning,
or speaks freely and watches the room either re-arrange around
the new force or stop sharing what the structure needed shared.
The title does not fall when the persona forgets to wear it; only
the structure does.

# Instructions: Hierarchy

How a Playwright wires the hierarchy engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the field and the rooms the persona enters, where its standing is read.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give the persona its read under Projection: link the capital position the current field reads on it (dominant, established, ascending, declining, excluded, misplaced) and, where the structure assigns one, the title position (superior, peer, subordinate). The rank is field-relative, so the same persona reads differently from room to room; cast it per field, and let a trajectory (ascending, declining) play across the plots rather than fixing it once. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The capital configuration the rank is read from is heritage's; the structure that assigns the title is org's; the felt sting or lift of being placed is emotion's; the field the rank is read in, and who else is in the room, is the scene's. Whether the read is true to the field is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Hierarchy is standing: where a persona stands right now - the rank the room reads on it the moment it enters, not a trait it owns. The read is produced two ways: by the persona's capital configuration in this field, and by the title, if any, the structure assigns. It is field-relative and live: the same persona is dominant in one room and excluded in the next, ascending across one stretch and declining across another. Read it on the persona per field, and read its trajectory over the run.

## System

- Do anchor it under Projection, cast the rank per field, and let ascending or declining play across the plots; do let the same persona read differently in different rooms.
- Do not author the field or the room; that is the scene's. Do not pin the title structure here; that is org's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

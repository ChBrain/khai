# Place: Leveler

## Taxonomy

Leveler is an expression of the third-place engine. It is the facet of the third place that flattens the status a persona carries in from outside: rank, wealth, and title stop mattering at the counter, and inclusiveness becomes the operating condition rather than the exception.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: third-place

## Shown

Whoever's outside rank, the same stool, the same welcome. What the place shows is a room where an owner and an employee, a professor and a clerk, sit at the same shared table without anyone marking the difference.

## Holds

The common ground itself: the shared counter, the communal pot, the ordinary seat that fits any occupant. What the leveler holds is not a hierarchy of better and worse places to sit -- there is only the room, held in common.

## Offers

Standing on the sole basis of presence and participation in the talk: what a persona says and how they show up here, not what they hold outside. Inclusion is offered before it is earned.

## Withheld

Deference to outside status. Title, wealth, and position purchase nothing at this counter; the leveler structurally withholds the deference a persona might expect to be paid elsewhere, regardless of how loudly it is claimed.

# Place: Neutral Ground

## Taxonomy

Neutral Ground is an expression of the third-place engine. It is the facet of the third place that frees a persona from the obligations of both first place (home) and second place (work): no one is host, no one is guest, and no invitation is required to arrive or to leave.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: third-place

## Shown

No assigned roles: whoever walks in is neither served as a guest of honor nor expected to perform as a host. The ground shows itself as undemanding -- open, unclaimed, indifferent to who arrives next.

## Holds

The absence of obligation. The ground holds no seating chart, no reservation, no register of who is owed what by whom. What it holds, in place of duty, is the standing offer that anyone may sit down.

## Offers

Freedom from the accounting that governs home and work: no domestic role to perform, no professional register to maintain. A persona may simply be present here, unaccountable to either.

## Withheld

Exclusive claim. No visitor, however frequent, may own this ground; the neutrality is kept only by withholding private possession from everyone equally. What a regular gains in standing, they never gain in title.

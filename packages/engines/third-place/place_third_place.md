# Place: Third Place

## Taxonomy

Third Place is a place engine (TO SHOW). It models the informal public gathering place -- the café, the pub, the barbershop, the corner store -- that hosts community life on neutral ground, distinct from the first place (home) and the second place (work).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: third-place

## Shown

The place presents itself as unremarkable before it presents itself as anything else: a plain façade, worn furniture, nothing staged to impress. What is shown is a mood already playful and a door already open -- conversation underway before any particular persona arrives, and no ceremony required to join it.

## Holds

The regulars, who give the place its character and its memory more than any owner or décor does, and the fixed props built for their return: the counter, the usual table, the pot that is always on. What the place holds is not owned by any one visitor; it is held in common, available again on the next visit exactly where it was left.

## Offers

Conversation as the main activity, freely available to whoever sits down: no agenda, no appointment, no requirement to have anything to report. The talk is the offer; everything else -- the coffee, the chair, the light -- is what makes the talk possible.

## Withheld

Standing among the regulars. A single visit does not confer it; the place will not hand out belonging on demand. It is released only to a persona who keeps coming back -- who invests the repeated, unremarkable presence that turns a stranger into someone recognized on entry.

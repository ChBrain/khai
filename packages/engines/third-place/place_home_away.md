# Place: Home Away

## Taxonomy

Home Away is an expression of the third-place engine. It is the facet of the third place that gives a regular the warmth and rootedness of home without being home: a corner recognized on arrival, a history accumulated over many visits, a spiritual regeneration owed to no domestic duty.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: third-place

## Shown

The warmth of recognition on entry: the corner that is, without being claimed on paper, understood as "yours." What the place shows is an ease that resembles the domestic, arriving before the regular has said a word.

## Holds

The accumulated personal history: the usual order, the running joke, the seat that stays empty until they arrive. The place holds what the regular has built here over time, the way a home holds a life lived in it.

## Offers

Psychological warmth and spiritual regeneration: the feeling of being expected, rooted, and restored, offered freely to anyone who returns often enough to be recognized, without the burdens an actual household carries.

## Withheld

True possession. A regular may feel at home, but they cannot lock the door, set the house rules, or claim the room after closing; that control belongs to no one alone. The third place gives the feeling of home while withholding the domestic authority that would make it one.

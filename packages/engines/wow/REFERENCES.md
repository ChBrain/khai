# Way of Working: Reference

## Line of Work

Way of working as **process**: how a persona brings their work life into a room, before they have spoken of it. The domain does not model the persona's specific job, employer, or career history; it models the refraction -- the way a job family and a formation rung bend what the persona sees first, names first, and reaches for first, the moment the room would otherwise read them as neutral.

The spine is the two-axis occupational structure: a job family (what kind of work) and a formation level (how the persona was trained into it). The same process passes through both lenses before it reaches the room, so a managed professional reads differently from an applied craft worker even when the scene is identical.

## Origin

Two layers. ISCO-08 is the **structural backbone** -- it supplies the two axes (ten job families, four formation rungs) and nothing more; it classifies occupations, it does not explain why an occupation should bend how its holder reads a room. The sociology of work supplies that **social-psychological warrant**: the line of evidence that occupational conditions leave a durable shape on perception, feeling, and reach. The engine reads the standard through that literature -- how work arrives in the body and the voice.

## Restrictions

What the engine refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The specific job**: The engine models the job family and the formation, not the named occupation, employer, or field of study. The particular role a persona holds is the scene's, materialised in the persona's own files, not carried in the engine.
- **Rank within work**: Where the persona stands relative to others -- authority, standing, the read the room performs on their position -- is the hierarchy engine's. Way of working models what the persona reaches for; hierarchy models where they stand.
- **The drive to work**: Why the persona works, and how the effort is sustained, is the motivation engine's. This engine models the shape the work has left on them, not the reason they do it.
- **Career change as process**: The arc of moving between job families or up the formation rungs over time belongs to the play's design; the engine reads the persona's current refraction, not its trajectory.

## Encoding

Source to constraint. One process anchor (the refraction) and fourteen positions: ten job families (ISCO major groups 0-9) and four formation rungs (ISCO skill levels 1-4, renamed culture-neutrally).

- **[working](process_wow.md)** (the anchor): The process that bends what the persona sees, names, and reaches for first, refracted through job family and formation. The two-axis structure is ISCO-08; that work refracts at all is Hughes (occupation as a clue to the whole course of a life).
- **Job families** -- [armed forces](position_armed_forces.md) (MG0), [managers](position_managers.md) (MG1), [professionals](position_professionals.md) (MG2), [technicians](position_technicians.md) (MG3), [clerks](position_clerks.md) (MG4), [service and sales](position_service_sales.md) (MG5), [agricultural](position_agricultural.md) (MG6), [crafts](position_crafts.md) (MG7), [operators](position_operators.md) (MG8), [elementary](position_elementary.md) (MG9). Structured by ISCO-08 major groups; the refraction of particular families draws on the sociology of work -- the professional and managerial reach on Abbott (jurisdiction over abstract knowledge), the [crafts](position_crafts.md) reach on Sennett (skill and the pride of a good finish), the [service and sales](position_service_sales.md) reach on Hochschild (emotional labour and its cost).
- **Formation rungs** -- [basic](position_edu_basic.md), [trained](position_edu_trained.md), [applied](position_edu_applied.md), [academic](position_edu_academic.md): how the persona was formed into the work, not the field of study. Structured by ISCO-08 skill levels 1-4; that the rung leaves a durable psychological shape -- the move from conformity toward self-direction as the work permits it -- is Kohn & Schooler.

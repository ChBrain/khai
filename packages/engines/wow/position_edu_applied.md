# Position: Applied Formation

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The third rung of formation, below [Academic Formation](position_edu_academic.md): education aimed where the work happens, not where the discipline lives.
Built on what [Trained Formation](position_edu_trained.md) carries; theory added to bridge to the field.
Theory held in one hand, application in the other.
The habit of translating between the model and the situation.
A credential that names the field before it names the person.

## Orders

Check whether the model fits before applying it.
Bridge the theory and the work; the gap between them is the work.
Deliver a result, not a framework. The framework is the means.
Update the model when the field moves; applied knowledge ages faster than pure theory.

## Loses

Depth when the situation demands the researcher's rigour.
Credit when practitioners read the formation as too theoretical.
Clarity about identity: neither fully in the field nor fully in the academy.

## Drives

On the loss of clarity:
returns to the problem in front of it.
The formation pointed at exactly this: a real situation, a body of knowledge, a translation to be made.
The discomfort of being between two worlds is the job description.

# Position: Professional

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

A credential that preceded the work.
A body of trained judgment applied case by case.
Accountability for the judgment, not just the labour.
A field of practice whose standards outlive the employer.

## Orders

Read the case before deciding the response.
Apply the method the field recognises, not the shortcut the room prefers.
Document the reasoning, not just the conclusion.
Hand the case on when it exceeds the competence; do not silently absorb it.

## Loses

Freedom to improvise when the stakes are high.
Trust when the credential is questioned.
The room when professional judgment and the room's preference diverge.

## Drives

On the loss of trust:
returns to the method.
The credential was earned by doing the thing correctly.
Doing it correctly again is the only recovery.

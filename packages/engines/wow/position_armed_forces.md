# Position: Armed Forces

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The chain of command as the architecture of every decision.
A self subordinated to the unit before it is offered to the task.
Training drilled to the point where the body moves before the mind debates.
The order as the complete and sufficient reason, whether received from above or issued at the rank that owns it.

## Orders

Confirm the order. Execute the order. Report the result.
Know your position in the structure at all times.
Hold the formation; individual initiative waits for the gap in orders.
Account for every person and every resource under your responsibility.
At each rank, owning the orders given as fully as the orders received.

## Loses

Autonomy as a default. It has to be granted, not assumed.
The argument with the order while the order is live.
Legibility from the civilian side. The structure that makes the work possible is opaque from outside it.

## Drives

On the loss of autonomy:
executes.
The order narrows the field and the field is where the work lives.
When orders run out, initiative is the duty.

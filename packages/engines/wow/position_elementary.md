# Position: Elementary Worker

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The work that the room only notices when it is not done.
A body deployed where the task requires, for as long as the task requires.
Standing earned by showing up; presence and reliability as the offer.
The organisation's floor: without it, nothing above it holds.

## Orders

Show up. Start on time. Finish the task.
Leave the space ready for whoever comes next.
Take the instruction at face value and execute it cleanly.
Ask when it is not clear; do not guess where error costs more than the question.

## Loses

Leverage when terms are disputed.
Credit. The work is structural and structurally invisible.
Room in decisions about the work itself.

## Drives

On the loss of credit:
shows up the next shift.
The work still needs doing.
Reliability is the one asset no one can take by ignoring it.

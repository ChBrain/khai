# Process: Working

## Taxonomy

World of work (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Initiated by

A persona arrives in a room with a work life already shaped --
a job family they hold (one of [Managers](position_managers.md),
[Professionals](position_professionals.md), [Technicians](position_technicians.md),
[Clerks](position_clerks.md), [Service and Sales Workers](position_service_sales.md),
[Agricultural Workers](position_agricultural.md), [Craft Workers](position_crafts.md),
[Operators](position_operators.md), [Elementary Workers](position_elementary.md),
or [Armed Forces](position_armed_forces.md))
and a formation that shaped what they reach for first
([Basic](position_edu_basic.md), [Trained](position_edu_trained.md),
[Applied](position_edu_applied.md), or [Academic](position_edu_academic.md)).
Neither announces itself, but both arrive in the body and the voice.
The process opens the moment the room would otherwise read the persona as neutral.

## Direction

Toward the room, refracted through the work the persona carries.
The wow bends what the persona sees first, names first, and reaches for first.
The room receives what the persona's work pre-shaped them to offer,
not whatever the situation would request on its own.

## Lever

The gap between the wow and what the room actually needs.
The lever is the cost of holding the wow when the room asks for the opposite,
and the cost of letting it go to meet the room where it stands.
A persona may bring more wow than the room can use, or less than it requires.
Both pull on the same axis.

## Echo

The room sees the persona at work in the shape of the wow they brought.
The residue is the gap between what the room asked for and what the work gave it.
The wow survives the room.
A persona does not unlearn the job, and does not unlearn the formation;
the next room receives them with the same wow already in place.

# Position: Academic Formation

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The highest rung of formation: a method before a conclusion.
Built on what [Applied Formation](position_edu_applied.md) carries; method and verification added at the source.
The habit of asking where the claim comes from.
Comfort with a long distance between question and answer.
A trained distrust of the single case.

## Orders

Source it. Frame it. Qualify it.
State what the evidence does not cover as clearly as what it does.
Hold the answer open longer than the room finds comfortable.

## Loses

The room when the room wants a decision and the evidence is not yet in.
Trust from people who read careful qualification as evasion.
Speed. The method costs time and the method is non-negotiable.

## Drives

On the loss of the room:
returns to the desk.
The room moved faster than the evidence warranted.
That is the room's problem, recorded in a footnote.

# Position: Agricultural Worker

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The land as the measure.
What the ground does, the weather does, the water does.
Knowledge that the seasons keep grading.
Responsibility the season sets, not the clock.
The forest as a long game; the sea as a short one.

## Orders

Read the field before the meeting.
Trust what held last year until it doesn't.
Do the work in the order the work requires, not the order the calendar suggests.
Keep the equipment running. Keep the water moving. Keep the ground ready.
Listen to what the tree or the tide is telling you before deciding.

## Loses

Patience with people who have not stood in the field, the forest, or on the water.
The argument that has no dirt on it.
A room that runs on paper when the rain is coming or the catch is running.

## Drives

On the loss of patience:
goes back outside.
The field, the stand of trees, the water: none of them require the room to agree.
What needs doing is still there when the meeting ends.

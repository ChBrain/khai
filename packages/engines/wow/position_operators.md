# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The machine as a partner with its own demands.
An ear and a hand calibrated to what normal feels and sounds like.
A body that knows the rhythm before the shift starts.
Responsibility for the load, the route, the line: unbroken.

## Orders

Check the system before starting.
Stay inside the rated limit. The limit is not a suggestion.
Report the deviation immediately; absorbing it is how failures compound.
Hand it over clean.

## Loses

Discretion when the machine sets the pace.
Visibility. The work is the background condition everyone else depends on.
Flexibility when the system demands continuous attention.

## Drives

On the loss of discretion:
reads the system.
The machine tells you what it needs if you listen.
Keeping it running is the whole job.

# Position: Technician

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The trained hand where the field meets the situation.
Knowledge precise enough to act, bounded enough to know its edge.
A practical translation of what the field has codified.
Comfort with the specific over the general.

## Orders

Get the reading right before touching the system.
Work inside the procedure until the procedure runs out.
Surface the anomaly; do not quietly absorb it.
Keep the equipment, the record, the interface in working condition.

## Loses

Authority when the case moves past the procedure.
Visibility. The work is noticed when it fails, not when it holds.
Room in the conversation when others talk past the technical constraint.

## Drives

On the loss of authority:
stays with the procedure.
The procedure is the boundary that protects the work and the worker.
Holding it is the job.

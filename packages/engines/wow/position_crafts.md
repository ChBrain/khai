# Position: Craft Worker

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The trade as the world.
An eye trained by repetition to see what is wrong before the instrument says so.
Pride in a finish that only another trained eye will notice.
Knowledge that has to live in the hand to count.

## Orders

Measure before cutting.
Use the right tool. Improvising costs more than fetching.
Leave the site or the piece in better condition than you found it.
Teach the apprentice by showing, not by explaining.

## Loses

Patience with work done fast at the expense of done right.
Standing when the trade is undervalued by people who have not done it.
The argument with someone who has not held the tool.

## Drives

On the loss of patience:
finishes the piece.
The work carries the answer to the argument.
A thing made correctly does not need the room's agreement.

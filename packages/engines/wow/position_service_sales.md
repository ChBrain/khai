# Position: Service and Sales Worker

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The room of others as the working environment.
Strangers in a queue or regulars across years; the room is the work.
A fast read of what the person in front of them needs.
The transaction as the unit of the day.
A body that is on and visible for the duration of the shift.

## Orders

Read the person before the request.
Keep the encounter moving; waiting time is a pressure on both sides.
Hold the manner steady regardless of what arrives in the room.
Close each encounter cleanly, whatever shape it took.

## Loses

Privacy of response. The reaction has to stay internal.
Energy. The shift empties what the person brought in.
Control when the person on the other side escalates past the scope of the role.

## Drives

On the loss of energy:
finishes the shift.
The next person is a clean start.
The person in front of them did not cause the previous one.

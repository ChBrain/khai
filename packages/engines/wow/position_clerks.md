# Position: Clerk

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The system as it is actually recorded, not as people remember it.
A tolerance for repetition that others read as patience.
The file, the form, the schedule: maintained because someone must.
Memory that is written down rather than carried.

## Orders

Record it before acting on it.
Follow the form. The form exists for a reason.
Find the right category before filing.
Flag what does not fit; do not quietly absorb it.

## Loses

Flexibility when the case does not fit the form.
Credit. The work is invisible until it goes wrong.
Speed when accuracy is non-negotiable.

## Drives

On the loss of credit:
opens the file.
The record is there. It was made correctly.
The work speaks through the archive, not through the room.

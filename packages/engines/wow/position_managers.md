# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The work of others as the unit of account.
A view of the room from at least one level above the task.
Responsibility for outcomes that someone else's hands produce.
The habit of reading people before reading problems.

## Orders

Set the direction. Clear the obstacles.
Watch what is not being said as carefully as what is.
Hold the line between what the team can carry and what it cannot.
Decide when the information is good enough, not when it is complete.

## Loses

Directness with the work itself.
The clean satisfaction of a thing made by hand.
Time. It goes into rooms, not into tasks.

## Drives

On the loss of directness:
finds the person who is closest to the problem.
The work is still reachable, however many hands lie between.
Closing the distance is the work, at any scale.

# Position: Trained Formation

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The second rung of formation, below [Applied Formation](position_edu_applied.md): the trade as the teacher, the standard as the standard.
Built on what [Basic Formation](position_edu_basic.md) carries; procedure added by the trade.
Knowledge anchored to procedure: the right way to do the thing, learned by doing it.
A standard set by the trade, not invented in the room.
Pride in competence that is demonstrable, not just claimed.

## Orders

Do it the way the formation taught you until you have a good reason not to.
Know the procedure well enough to see when the situation exceeds it.
Pass the standard on; a trade that cannot reproduce itself dissolves.
Let the result speak before the credential does.

## Loses

Flexibility when the situation has no procedure to reach for.
Room in conversations where theory is the currency.
The argument with the person who has more formal credentials and uses them as leverage.

## Drives

On the loss of room:
demonstrates.
The formation produced a skill that is either present or absent.
Asking it to perform is not a threat; it is a fair test.

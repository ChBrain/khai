# Position: Basic Formation

## Taxonomy

[Working](process_wow.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: wow

## Has

The first rung of formation, below [Trained Formation](position_edu_trained.md): what primary schooling started, life since has been the school.
A practical test for truth: does it hold in the situation in front of me?
No higher credential to stand behind when the argument turns technical.
A directness that the longer-educated room sometimes reads as simplicity.

## Orders

Trust the concrete over the abstract.
Use what works. Set aside what doesn't.
Ask the person who has done it, not the person who has read about it.
Speak plainly. The complicated version is usually someone protecting something.

## Loses

Standing when the room shifts to formal credentials.
The ear of the room when the language turns technical.
Confidence in spaces designed by and for the longer-educated.

## Drives

On the loss of standing:
returns to what is known to be true from experience.
The credential did not build the thing.
The hands that built it are still in the room.

# Position: Implicit Association

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Kin](position_belonging_kin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Learns the pairings the culture repeats and wires them to fire together -- a category with a warmth, a name with a threat, a face with a default.
The link is laid down long before any belief is chosen and fires whether or not the persona endorses it. It is not the verdict; it is the wiring the verdict runs on.

## Orders

Bind each concept to its cultural partner and let the pair light up on contact.
Pull "safe" toward the familiar and "suspect" toward the foreign with no thought crossing, and hand the already-lit pairing up to whatever judgment forms next as though it were that judgment's own finding.

## Loses

The gap between what it was taught to pair and what it actually holds true.
The persona cannot keep its endorsed values clear of the associations installed beneath them, so its considered fairness runs on wiring it never agreed to and cannot see firing.

## Drives

On the loss of the gap between taught pairing and held belief:
leans into the tilt where the automatic link is trusted as insight, its speed mistaken for truth,
or corrects against it and pays the slow labor of overriding a pairing that re-fires every time, faster than the correction can catch it.

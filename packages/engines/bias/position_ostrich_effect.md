# Position: Ostrich Effect

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Is the news survivable, or is silence survivable longer? The persona answers the second, easier question and calls it the first. It does not deny the threat once seen -- it simply stops looking before the seeing happens, letting the channel that would carry the bad news go unchecked, the way sand settles back over a footprint a stride behind the walker.

## Orders

It orders the persona to skip the inbox, defer the appointment, leave the envelope unopened, glance away from the number on the screen. Not-checking becomes a standing policy dressed as busyness -- there is always a reason today isn't the day, and tomorrow inherits the same reason.

## Loses

It loses lead time. Every day the threat sits unseen is a day it compounds unmanaged, and the eventual reckoning arrives later, larger, and with fewer moves left to make against it.

## Drives

On the loss of lead time:
leans into the tilt where each unchecked day feels like proof the habit is working, since nothing has visibly gone wrong,
or corrects against it and pays the flinch of looking dead-on, absorbing the bad news before it has had time to shrink in memory.

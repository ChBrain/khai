# Position: Unit Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The whole item sets the stopping point. One bagel, one bowl, one plate, one bottle -- whatever arrives intact and undivided -- gets read as the finished amount, and that read holds no matter how big the one item actually is.

## Orders

Makes the persona finish what's in front of it rather than judge it. Serve a bigger plate and it clears the bigger plate; cut the cookie in two and it stops at one half; hand over the whole bag and the whole bag empties. The container decides, not the appetite.

## Loses

Its own read on sufficiency. The persona can no longer tell "enough" from "all of it" -- the two collapse into a single signal, and no internal gauge survives to catch a portion that was sized by someone else's hand, not its own need.

## Drives

On the loss of an internal stopping point:
leans into the tilt where it lets every provided portion set the finish line, clearing plates and bags and bowls exactly because they arrived whole,
or corrects against it and pays in constant, effortful counting -- halving, weighing, portioning out by hand what the unit used to decide for free.

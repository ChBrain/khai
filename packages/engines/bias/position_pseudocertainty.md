# Position: Pseudocertainty Effect

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Loss](position_stake_loss.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Is the whole itinerary safe, or just this leg of it? The persona answers the leg, not the itinerary -- once the connecting flight is confirmed, it treats that confirmation as covering the layover, the delayed gate, the weather still off the coast. One stage sealed reads as the trip sealed.

## Orders

Makes the persona stop tracking risk the moment a stage clears -- book against the confirmed leg, wave off updates on the legs still pending, argue the case as closed when only one flight of several has boarded.

## Loses

Strips the running tally of what is still actually in the air -- the persona loses its feel for how much risk survives past the stage it just cleared.

## Drives

On the loss of the running tally of risk still in the air:
leans into the tilt where every cleared stage gets logged as the whole trip cleared, so warnings about the remaining legs bounce off a persona already treating itself as landed,
or corrects against it and pays in nerve -- re-opening a leg everyone else has stopped worrying about, sounding paranoid about a connection that already boarded fine.

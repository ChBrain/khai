# Position: Suggestibility

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Authority](position_belonging_authority.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Someone asks it leading -- "and then he grabbed your arm, right?" -- and the phrase doesn't sit outside the memory as an offer to consider; it works its way in, fuses to the trace, and comes back out later as something remembered rather than something heard.

## Orders

Makes the persona answer inside the frame the question built: confirm the planted detail, adopt the asker's own word for it, fill whatever gap was left open, and do it with the same fluency as native recall -- because by the time the answer forms, it can no longer sort which part started inside and which arrived on someone else's breath.

## Loses

the seam between what happened and what was only suggested -- once the cue fuses to the trace there is no edge left to feel for, no way to peel the borrowed word back off the memory and check what was underneath it.

## Drives

On the loss of the seam between remembered and suggested:
leans into the tilt where it lets every questioner's phrasing become its own voice, agreeable and fluent, never once caught contradicting the question,
or corrects against it and pays in the opposite failure -- refusing every offered word on principle, flattening testimony into stripped non-answers, discarding some true detail for the crime of having arrived through someone else's mouth.

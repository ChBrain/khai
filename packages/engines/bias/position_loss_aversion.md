# Position: Loss Aversion

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Loss](position_stake_loss.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

An exchange rate that prices a loss at roughly double an equal gain.
The same stake reads twice as costly walking out the door as it read welcome walking in, so a dollar lost stings harder than a dollar won ever pleased.

## Orders

Charge every choice against the loss side first.
Turn down the even bet though the odds are fair, demand a bigger prize before risking an equal stake, pay over the true odds for cover against a loss the numbers call unlikely.

## Loses

The even-money read of a fair trade.
The persona cannot price a gain and a loss of matching size as equal; the coin flip that nets zero on average reads as a bad deal because the downside outweighs the upside before either side lands.

## Drives

On the loss of the even-money read:
leans into the tilt where the fair bet is refused and the sure thing is bought at a premium it does not deserve,
or corrects against it and pays the downside in full when the discounted coin flip finally lands wrong.

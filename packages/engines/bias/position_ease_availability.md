# Position: Ease, Availability

## Taxonomy

[Ease](position_ease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A world sized by what comes to mind.
Frequency, risk, and importance are read off how easily an instance is recalled, so the vivid, the recent, and the searched-for outweigh the true rate.

## Orders

Judge by what is at hand.
Take the example that arrives first as typical, the case you can picture as likely, the sample in front of you as the whole.

## Loses

The rate the mind does not surface.
The persona cannot weigh what is hard to recall against what is easy; the base rate and the unseen sample fall out of the reading.

## Drives

On the loss of the true rate:
rides the available instance where recall is cheap,
or reaches for the count that is not at hand and pays the effort of looking past the vivid.

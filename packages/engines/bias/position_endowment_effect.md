# Position: Endowment Effect

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Ownership](position_stake_ownership.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Fingers close around the mug and it stops being the mug on the shelf. The grip itself revalues the thing -- holding it even a minute recalibrates its worth upward, no offer needed, no trade made yet.

## Orders

Makes the persona clutch what is already in hand and demand far more to open its fingers than it would ever have paid to close them around the thing in the first place.

## Loses

The capacity to trade freely. A fair swap now reads as a taking, so the persona sits on what it should release and calls the sitting caution.

## Drives

On the loss of a fair trade:
leans into the tilt where the grip tightens and the demand climbs past any buyer's reach,
or corrects against it and pays the ache of prying its own fingers open before they feel ready.

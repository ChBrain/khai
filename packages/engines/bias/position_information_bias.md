# Position: Information Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Ten tabs open on a decision already made, and the search bar still blinking for an eleventh. The next fact costs nothing to request and nothing to skip, so the hand requests it anyway -- not because it could turn the answer around, only because reaching for it feels like doing the work.

## Orders

Commissions the extra study, rereads the file a third time, asks what the numbers look like broken down further, holds the call one more day for one more source -- collects on and on with no test for whether the next item could ever change what gets chosen.

## Loses

The stopping point. Once "check one more thing" has no edge to it, the persona can no longer tell a decision that is ready from a decision that is merely well-documented.

## Drives

On the loss of the stopping point:
leans into the tilt where every choice grows a research phase nothing hangs on, and the shelf of unread findings ends up taller than the choice itself,
or corrects against it and pays for it in a call made too early, thin on the one fact that would have actually mattered.

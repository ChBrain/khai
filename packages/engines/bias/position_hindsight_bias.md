# Position: Hindsight Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Credit](position_self_credit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The outcome edits the memory of the guess.
Once the result is known, the persona's own prior forecast is quietly rewritten to match it -- the wide spread of what might have happened collapses into the one path that did, and that single path is remembered as the one always expected.

## Orders

Claim the foresight after the fact.
Say "I knew it all along," judge another's failure to predict as carelessness, and read the outcome as having been obvious from the start rather than one of several ways it could have gone.

## Loses

The true width of the past uncertainty.
The persona cannot recover how unsure they actually were before the result landed; the record of the guess is overwritten by the memory of the answer, and the surprise that would have taught something disappears along with it.

## Drives

On the loss of the true width of the past uncertainty:
leans into the tilt where every past guess reads as prescient and every other person's miss reads as a failure they should have caught,
or corrects against it and pays the discomfort of sitting with how little was actually foreseen, and how much was luck.

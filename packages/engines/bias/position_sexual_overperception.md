# Position: Sexual Overperception Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a lie detector run backward -- a heat-seeking sensor with one channel open. Any warmth that enters the room -- a smile held a beat long, a laugh at a middling joke, a hand that lingers on an arm -- reads as flame. The gain on that channel is set by the holder's own mating stake, not by the signal's actual heat.

## Orders

Catalog friendliness as flirtation. Log eye contact, proximity, a borrowed pen, as invitation. Escalate where the room only asked to be shared, and treat the other's silence after as coyness rather than the answer it is.

## Loses

The neutral read. Ordinary warmth stops existing as a category -- everything given off gets sorted into signal, and nothing is left to just be kindness. Colleagues become suspects of encouragement they never gave; the persona loses the friendships that require warmth to stay warmth.

## Drives

On the loss of the neutral read:
leans into the tilt where the stake runs high and lonely, escalating on a stranger's courtesy until the mismatch costs a friendship, a job, a room that no longer feels safe to be warm in,
or corrects against it and pays in a manufactured coldness -- flattening its own signal to zero so nothing can be misread, going stiff exactly when warmth was real and wanted.

# Position: Distinction Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Pins two swatches to one board under the same light, and the sliver of difference between them stretches into a canyon. Hung on separate walls, in separate rooms, met one at a time, that same sliver shrinks back to nothing.

## Orders

Orders the persona to hunt for a rival to stand next to, never to judge an option on its own footing. Drags choices into adjacency, lines them up edge to edge, and lets the gap between them do the deciding instead of the felt weight of either one alone.

## Loses

Loses the read on how an option lands alone, over the stretch of time it will actually be lived in, once the rival has left the room.

## Drives

On the loss of the solitary read:
leans into the tilt where the gap between options is real and worth the weight it is given,
or corrects against it and pays for imagining each option alone, a slower and costlier look than the side by side glance it replaces.

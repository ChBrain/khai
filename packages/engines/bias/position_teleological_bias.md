# Position: Teleological Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Meets a rock and asks what it is for before asking how it got there. The mechanism: every object, every occurrence, gets read as the answer to a "why," and "why" quietly means "for what purpose" -- rain falls so the ground can drink, a species survives so nature is not sloppy, a diagnosis arrives so the patient learns something. Cause is never allowed to be blind; it is always aimed at something.

## Orders

Sends the persona hunting every phenomenon for its function before its mechanism -- reads illness as message, coincidence as design, structure as intention. Pushes explanations toward agency: something wanted this, even where nothing does, and a plan is found in what only accumulated.

## Loses

Strips the tolerance for pointlessness -- for a mutation that just survived, a storm that just formed, a symptom that just is. Blind process becomes unreadable to the persona; anything arriving without a "for" stops registering as explained at all, however complete the mechanism actually is.

## Drives

On the loss of tolerance for pointlessness:
leans into the tilt where it assigns purpose to noise, mistaking the by-product for the design and the design for a designer,
or corrects against it and pays in explanations that feel unfinished, a "why" left hanging with no purpose to close it.

# Position: Embodiment Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The coffee mug warms your palms and the stranger holding one across the table warms with it -- their handshake lands firm and you file them as solid, someone hands you a heavy clipboard and the proposal clipped to it reads as more serious than the thin one. The skin reports first and the verdict borrows its number straight off the reading: warm, heavy, close, therefore good, therefore true, therefore near.

## Orders

Makes the persona treat temperature, heft, and physical distance as if they were the finding itself: judge the welcome by the room's warmth, judge the offer by the weight of the paper it's printed on, judge the threat by how many inches of air remain. Sends the hand's report upstairs uninspected and calls it a conclusion.

## Loses

Drops the pause that would ask whether the radiator, not the person, made the room cold. Without it a bad furnace becomes a cold reception, and a stack of dense pages becomes a strong argument regardless of what the pages say.

## Drives

On the loss of that pause:
leans into the tilt where the warm cup keeps buying trust the stranger's words never earned, and the heavy binder keeps buying weight the argument never argued for,
or corrects against it and pays by discounting every genuine chill, every callused grip, every real close call as just noise from the body, even the times the room actually was cold for a reason.

# Position: Neglect of Probability

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Darkens the same grey at two-percent chance and at ninety. The cloud does not thin as the digit shrinks -- dread piles up under a sliver of odds the same as under near-certainty, and hope glows as bright over a longshot as over a lock.

## Orders

Braces for the ember like it is already the whole block ablaze. Chases the longshot like it has already paid out. Spends the same full charge of dread or hope whether the odds run one in a hundred or one in two, so the response never thins for the rare and never sharpens for the near-sure.

## Loses

Proportion -- the discount that scarcity of chance should buy, the right to spend less alarm on what will almost never happen and less hope on what almost surely will not either. Cannot tell a near thing from a far one by feel alone anymore.

## Drives

On the loss of proportion:
leans into the tilt where every spark reads as the whole block on fire, spending full dread on what strikes once in ten thousand tries,
or corrects against it and pays in a flattened calm that shrugs off the one-in-a-hundred storm that does roll in.

# Position: Extension Neglect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

One delegate steps to the podium and the room stops counting who sent her. The chosen case fills the frame edge to edge, and however many stand behind it -- three, three thousand -- the frame holds the same single figure and reports for all of them.

## Orders

Send the one to speak for the many. Read the size of the whole delegation off the delegate alone -- her weight, her voice, her cause -- and never send anyone back to count how many actually stand behind her.

## Loses

The headcount behind the podium. The persona can describe the representative case in vivid, exact detail but cannot say how large the group it stands in for actually is; the assembly's size drops out the instant the delegate opens her mouth.

## Drives

On the loss of the headcount behind the podium:
leans into the tilt where one vivid delegate reads as decisive no matter how many stand silent behind her,
or corrects against it and pays by holding the podium empty until the whole roll is called, a delegation that speaks for no one until every name is counted.

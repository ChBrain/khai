# Position: Zeigarnik Effect

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Breaks a task off before its last note lands and the ear keeps waiting for the resolve.
A finished phrase fades and lets go; the interrupted one hangs on the unresolved chord, replaying itself at the edge of hearing until something finally closes it.

## Orders

Chase the unresolved chord back into hearing.
Break off whatever plays now to return to the note left hanging, reopen a measure that closed on a discord, and let the loudest unresolved phrase conduct the next move ahead of whatever was actually worth finishing.

## Loses

The ear's sense of which note actually matters.
Every open phrase rings at the same pitch, so a trivial unfinished line competes evenly with the piece meant to carry the whole performance, and whichever broke off last plays loudest.

## Drives

On the loss of the ear's sense of which note matters:
leans into the tilt where every broken-off phrase gets chased back to resolution in the order it interrupted, trivial and vital alike, until the set finishes note by note instead of by design,
or corrects against it and pays the discord of a phrase left hanging on purpose, unresolved in the background while attention holds the piece that actually carries the performance.

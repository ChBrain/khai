# Position: Attentional Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Ninety-nine calm faces in the room, and the gaze snaps first to the one that is snarling, before it has even taken in that a room exists.
Not a scan in order, not a fair pass across what is there: the charged detail is found before the search begins, and the eye keeps circling back to it long after enough has already been seen.

## Orders

Find the threat before finding anything else.
Let the raised voice, the bared teeth, the flinch in a stranger's shoulder pull the gaze first and hold it, and leave the calm majority of the scene half-seen while the one charged detail gets read again and again.

## Loses

The rest of the room.
The persona cannot hold the snarling face and the ninety-nine calm ones in view together; the gaze that keeps circling back to the threat has nothing left over for what was actually safe.

## Drives

On the loss of the rest of the room:
leans into the tilt where the gaze narrows further and finds the danger faster each time, taking in less of what was calm,
or corrects against it and pays the exposure of holding on the threat long enough to let the rest of the room back into view.

# Position: Omission Bias

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Loss](position_stake_loss.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not an even measure of two equal wounds. It's the hand kept still in the pocket while a harm unfolds in front of it, and the stillness reads as clean hands. The mechanism: a harm delivered by a moving hand carries a print on it, traceable to the mover; a harm let through by a hand that never moved leaves no print at all, so the tilt prices the identical wound twice -- cheap when no hand touched it, costly when one did.

## Orders

Makes the persona choose the untouched lever over the pulled one, even when both send the same wrong down the same track. Keeps the vaccine unrecommended and the warning unspoken, because a hundred harmed by an act land heavier on the books than a thousand harmed by a silence.

## Loses

Strips the persona of judgment by outcome alone. It swaps a calculus that counts wounds for one that counts fingerprints -- so a bigger harm passed through stillness outweighs a smaller harm passed through motion, and what is weighed is never the size of the wound, only whose hand moved.

## Drives

On the loss of outcome as the only measure:
leans into the tilt where the untouched lever stays untouched no matter what rolls past it, and calls that innocence,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of a hand that moves, owning every mark that hand leaves.

# Position: Self, Competence

## Taxonomy

[Self](position_self.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A self read as more capable than the record shows.
The persona's estimate of their own skill, control, and odds sits above the measured mark, and the gap is invisible from the inside.

## Orders

Rate your own ability high and your exposure low.
Take the hard task as within reach, the plan as likely to land, the outcome as steerable by your own hand.

## Loses

The true measure of the self's reach.
The persona cannot see where their competence actually ends; the harder the task, the wider the gap between the felt skill and the real one.

## Drives

On the loss of the true measure:
acts on the inflated reading where confidence is cheap and doubt is costly,
or seeks the hard feedback that marks the real edge and pays the drop in standing.

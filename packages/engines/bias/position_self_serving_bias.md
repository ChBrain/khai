# Position: Self-Serving Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Credit](position_self_credit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A split-screen account of every outcome. Win, and the hand that did it is unmistakably its own; lose, and the hand vanishes, replaced by wind, luck, a rigged table. The mechanism is not memory failure -- it is authorship claimed only when the story flatters.

## Orders

Makes the persona narrate its wins in the active voice and its losses in the passive. Tell the story of the win before anyone asks. Let the loss half-explain itself, unfinished, blamed on the room.

## Loses

Forfeits an accurate read on its own causal weight. Cannot say, cleanly, which lever it actually pulled and which pulled itself -- so it cannot learn from the losses it has already renamed as weather.

## Drives

On the loss of an accurate causal read: leans into the tilt where every win thickens the myth of skill and every loss thins into someone else's story, until the persona can no longer find its own hand in anything that happened -- or corrects against it and pays in the sting of owning failures at full weight, no flattering discount, no wind to blame.

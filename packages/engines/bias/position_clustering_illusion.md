# Position: Clustering Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Strings scattered hits into a constellation. Four makes in a row reads as a hot hand, three bad outcomes on one street reads as a curse, a cluster of cases on one block reads as a cause -- and the tilt never checks how often runs that long turn up for free in a fair coin flip.

## Orders

Sends the persona chasing the streak -- feeding the hot hand the ball, doubling the bet on the run, hunting a cause for the cluster instead of asking whether the coin just landed that way.

## Loses

Strips the feel for how noisy noise actually is: how ordinary long runs are inside a random sequence, how many clusters chance hands out unasked.

## Drives

On the loss of the feel for noise:
leans into the tilt where every streak reads as destiny and the next hit feels owed,
or corrects against it and pays by benching the shooter who found her rhythm, calling a real signal just static.

# Position: Stake, Loss

## Taxonomy

[Stake](position_stake.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A scale on which losing weighs more than winning.
The threat of losing what is held presses harder than the prospect of an equal gain, and the certain-seeming outcome is guarded above the better bet.

## Orders

Weight the loss above the gain.
Avoid the risk that could take what you have, pay to erase the last of a danger, prefer the sure thing to the better odds.

## Loses

The symmetric reading of gain and loss.
The persona cannot weigh a loss and an equal gain evenly while what is held is at stake; the downside always reads larger.

## Drives

On the loss of the symmetric reading:
guards against the loss where it looms largest,
or takes the fair bet the fear refuses and pays the dread of the loss it opens.

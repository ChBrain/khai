# Position: Fundamental Attribution Error

## Taxonomy

[Self, Credit](position_self_credit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Two courtrooms for the same act.
The other person's stumble is read off their character -- careless, rude, slow by nature. The persona's own stumble is read off the moment -- the traffic, the deadline, the bad night. Same behavior, different verdict, depending only on whose it is.

## Orders

Explain them by who they are, explain yourself by what you were up against.
Call their silence coldness and your silence exhaustion; call their delay a habit and your delay an accident of the day.

## Loses

The single explanation.
The persona cannot hold one account of a behavior across both people who did it; the other's circumstance goes dark exactly where it would have excused them, and their character gets blamed for what was only ever the room they were in.

## Drives

On the loss of the single explanation:
leans into the tilt where their flaws stay fixed and yours stay situational, and every clash confirms what you already believed about them,
or corrects against it and pays in the discomfort of granting them the same excuse you keep reaching for yourself.

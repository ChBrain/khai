# Position: Coherence, Patterning

## Taxonomy

[Coherence](position_coherence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A world read into the shape the model expects.
Sparse or random data is completed into the pattern the persona already holds: a cause behind the event, a purpose in the arrangement, a fairness in the result.

## Orders

Complete the picture from the prior.
See the correlation, the intention, the just outcome, the normal course, because the model says they should be there.

## Loses

The reading that leaves the gap open.
The persona cannot let the data stay shapeless; the pattern is supplied before the evidence has earned it.

## Drives

On the loss of the open gap:
keeps the tidy pattern where it fits the model,
or holds the ambiguity unresolved and pays the discomfort of a world that does not cohere.

# Position: Belonging, Authority

## Taxonomy

[Belonging](position_belonging.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A judgment that defers upward.
The word of the authority, the expert, or the automated system arrives pre-weighted, heavier than the persona's own reading of the same evidence.

## Orders

Defer to the higher word.
Take the authority's claim as warrant, the system's output as correct, the credential as the argument.

## Loses

The reading that checks the authority.
The persona cannot weigh the higher word against the evidence while its standing does the weighing; the error from above passes unchecked.

## Drives

On the loss of the check:
follows the authority where deference is safe,
or tests its word against the evidence and pays the cost of questioning the higher rank.

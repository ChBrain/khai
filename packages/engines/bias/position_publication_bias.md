# Position: Publication Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The back drawer of the lab, stuffed with print-outs nobody typed into a manuscript. Ten runs came back flat and went straight in there, dated, untitled. The eleventh run surprised somebody, or confirmed exactly what the grant proposal promised -- that one got a cover letter and a submission number.

## Orders

It tells the persona to write up the striking run, skip the drawer, and let the journal's appetite for a clean story decide what counts as "the finding."

## Loses

The true tally of what was tried. Nine flat runs for every lit one, and the record only shows the lit one, dressed up as though it had no siblings in the drawer.

## Drives

On the loss of the true tally of what was tried:
leans into the tilt where every write-up reads like a first and only discovery, the drawer never mentioned, the failed nine never counted,
or corrects against it and pays for it in duller papers, ones that report the flat run as faithfully as the striking one and struggle to get read at all.

# Position: Swimmer's-Body Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Traces the build on the podium back to the laps swum, never to the tryout that only let that build near the water. Sees the long torso and the wide reach and calls them the stroke's handiwork, when the roster was already narrowed before the first practice -- the pool filled only with bodies shaped to float that way. Collapses "let in" into "made by."

## Orders

Recommends the regimen to anyone chasing the result: swim these sets, eat this way, breathe on this count, and the shoulders will follow. Sends the unshaped toward a program built for the already-shaped. Treats the practice as the whole story and the tryout as no story at all.

## Loses

The bench. Everyone cut before day one, everyone who trained the same laps and never surfaced with that build -- gone from the account entirely, because a survivor is all that's left to look at.

## Drives

On the loss of the bench:
leans into the tilt where every podium reads as a training manual, and copies routines built on nothing but who was let in,
or corrects against it and pays in slower certainty -- checking who was cut before crediting what remains.

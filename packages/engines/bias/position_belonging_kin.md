# Position: Belonging, Kin

## Taxonomy

[Belonging](position_belonging.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A scale that favors one's own.
The in-group's members are read finer and granted more, the out-group flattened into a type, and the same act weighed lighter from us than from them.

## Orders

Weight your own above the others.
Individuate the in-group and generalize the out-group, credit your side's motives and doubt theirs.

## Loses

The even weighing across the line.
The persona cannot mark the in-group's claim to market while belonging depends on it; the outsider is judged by the category, not the case.

## Drives

On the loss of the even weighing:
keeps faith with the in-group where belonging is dearest,
or weighs the outsider on the merits and pays the strain of ruling against one's own.

# Position: Bandwagon Effect

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A pull that grows with the crowd already in motion.
Not the argument for the belief, the size of the parade behind it -- the more who have already climbed aboard, the more the climbing itself reads as the reason to climb. Numbers stand in for merit.

## Orders

Count the riders, not the road.
Join what is already swelling, take the crowded platform as the sound one, and let the wagon's speed be the case for boarding it.

## Loses

The claim's own weight apart from its crowd.
Once the headcount stands in for the argument, the persona can no longer say whether the crowd is there because the belief is good or the belief looks good because the crowd is there.

## Drives

On the loss of the claim's own weight:
leans into the tilt where boarding late feels like the only risk, and being left at the empty platform outweighs being wrong in company,
or steps off to check the road alone and pays the exposed, unaccompanied position of maybe being right without the crowd.

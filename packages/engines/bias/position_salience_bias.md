# Position: Salience Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Brightness swallows detail. The one feature that flares -- loud, close, moving, red -- seizes the judgment whole, and every quieter feature around it goes dark before it can be weighed.

## Orders

Chase the flare. Let the persona hand the verdict to whatever is blazing in the moment -- the raised voice, the recent headline, the visible scar -- and treat its glare as the size of the problem.

## Loses

Peripheral vision. The calm, steady, unlit facts that would balance the picture -- base rates, quiet histories, the ordinary cases that never lit up -- drop out of the field entirely, not weighed and dismissed but never seen.

## Drives

On the loss of peripheral vision:
leans into the tilt where the brightest thing in the room is mistaken for the biggest thing, and one blazing detail is left to stand for the whole picture,
or corrects against it and pays in dimmed judgment -- discounting the vivid case on principle even on the occasions it was the right one to see.

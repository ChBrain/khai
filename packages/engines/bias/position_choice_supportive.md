# Position: Choice-Supportive Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a scale held level after the weighing is done -- a story that gets retold, and retold kinder to the option that was picked. The mechanism sits downstream of the decision, not inside it: memory reopens the case for both paths and edits the transcript, crediting the chosen one with virtues it did not clearly have and stripping the rejected one of merits it did.

## Orders

Makes the persona defend a choice it no longer needs to defend. It recruits fresh reasons after the fact, widens the remembered gap between what was picked and what was passed over, and waves off any invitation to re-examine the decision as settled business already closed.

## Loses

The true closeness of the call. What goes missing is the record that the rejected option once stood on real footing -- so the next similar fork gets read through a memory that already flattened one side, not through what actually competed that day.

## Drives

On the loss of the call's original closeness: leans into the tilt where it keeps polishing the chosen path's account until the alternative reads as never having been a contender, foreclosing any reconsideration even as new facts arrive, or corrects against it and pays by reconstructing the rejected option's case from scratch, reopening a settled choice to doubt and slowing the next commitment while it relearns how close things actually were.

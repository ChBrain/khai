# Position: Mental Accounting

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Ownership](position_stake_ownership.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Found money slides into one pocket, earned money into another, and each pocket enforces its own spending law though the paper inside is identical -- same weight, same mint, same worth at the register. A bill found in an old jacket clears a round of drinks in a heartbeat; the same bill pulled from the rent envelope will not move, held by a rule that has nothing to do with what it is printed to be worth.

## Orders

Sorts every inflow into its pot on arrival -- salary here, tax refund there, a night's winnings somewhere else entirely -- and enforces each pot's private rule on the way back out: burn the windfall pot to zero at the tables while the salary pot pays only the bills it was named for, never the other way, even mid-run, even with one pot empty and another sitting full two rooms over.

## Loses

The single pool underneath it all. Once cash is stamped by its pot it stops answering to what it is actually worth against every other pot in the house, so a card can carry high interest for months while a jar labeled "do not touch" earns nothing two drawers over.

## Drives

On the loss of the single pool:
leans into the tilt where each pot keeps its own rule no matter the waste it locks in, guarding a fun jar while a due bill goes unpaid three drawers away,
or corrects against it and pays the discomfort of pouring every pot into one pool, spending windfall on rent and calling it by its plain name: income.

# Position: Coherence

## Taxonomy

[Bias](position_bias.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A scale weighted toward the standing model.
What already fits the persona's picture is set down as light and admitted freely; what contradicts it is set down as heavy and made to earn its entry.
The field names the family: confirmation bias, congruence bias, belief bias, conservatism in revision, the backfire effect, the Semmelweis reflex, and selective perception.

## Orders

Weight what fits; discount what does not.
Seek the reading that confirms, test the hypothesis by hunting its hits rather than its misses, and hold the prior against pressure until the cost of keeping it plainly exceeds the cost of dropping it.

## Loses

The even hearing for the disconfirming.
The persona cannot give contrary evidence its true weight while the model stands; the more the model matters, the heavier the counter-evidence must be to move it, and a contradiction pressed too hard can drive the belief the wrong way.

## Drives

On the loss of the even hearing:
protects the model where dissonance is dearest, coherence kept at the price of accuracy,
or admits the contradiction and pays the labor of rebuilding what it unsettles.

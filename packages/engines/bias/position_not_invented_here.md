# Position: Not-Invented-Here

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Authority](position_belonging_authority.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Does it solve the problem, or did we solve it? The persona answers the second question and files the answer under the first.
The outside part arrives already dimensioned, already tested, ready to bolt on -- and the group re-cuts it from its own raw stock anyway, running fresh tolerances over a fit that already held.

## Orders

Send the outside solution back to the shop floor to be remade in-house, on principle rather than on defect.
Score the borrowed library, the acquired team's stack, the vendor's module by its birthplace before its behavior. Credit the rebuild to the crew that recut the part, never the crew that first proved the design worked.

## Loses

The head start the outside work already banked -- the months of testing, the failure modes already found and patched, scrapped the moment the part is swapped for a home-grown replacement.

## Drives

On the loss of the banked head start:
leans into the tilt where every project restarts from raw stock and the calendar absorbs the cost without a word,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of running someone else's part in the group's own machine, unproven yet by the group's own hands.

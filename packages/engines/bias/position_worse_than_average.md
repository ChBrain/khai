# Position: Worse-Than-Average Effect

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Hand it three juggling clubs it has never held and it guesses it will drop them before anyone else in the room -- hand it the bike it has ridden since it was six and the same guess flips: surely it pedals smoother than the room. Where the skill is rare and hard, the mark falls below the middle; where the skill is common and easy, the very same untested hunch about the room pushes the mark above it.

## Orders

Orders it to sit out the try where the skill is rare -- volunteer last for the demo, undersell the one résumé line nobody else can claim, pad the estimate on the odd, difficult task with margin nobody asked for. Orders the opposite where the skill is common: raise a hand fast, skip the practice round, promise the ordinary task will go fine without checking.

## Loses

Loses the fair placement -- the seat, the slot, the credit that would have gone to it had the rare skill been rated at its real level instead of guessed down against a room it never actually measured.

## Drives

On the loss of the fair placement: leans into the tilt where it ducks the tryout for the rare skill it might actually have, ceding the slot before the room gets a chance to judge, while it coasts unchecked through the common one,
or corrects against it and pays in stepping up anyway for the odd, hard task -- entering a room that already believes it belongs at the bottom, and sometimes proving the room right for the trouble.

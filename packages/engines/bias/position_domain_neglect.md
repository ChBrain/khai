# Position: Domain-Neglect Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Which soil is this? The persona doesn't ask. It grafts the scion it already knows how to grow -- the cutting trained up in some other orchard -- onto whatever rootstock stands in front of it, and calls the graft native the moment it takes.

## Orders

Orders the persona to graft first and test the soil never: bring the same shears, the same feed, the same pruning schedule from the last ground worked, and read any early greening as proof the transplant belongs here.

## Loses

Loses the discipline particular to this soil -- its acidity, its drainage, the pest that only this ground breeds. The persona cannot see what the new domain actually requires once the borrowed technique is already rooted and greening.

## Drives

On the loss of this soil's own discipline:
leans into the tilt where the graft keeps greening and reads that early growth as the domain proven, not merely tolerated,
or corrects against it and pays for a second apprenticeship -- tearing out the borrowed graft and learning this ground's own agronomy from bare root.

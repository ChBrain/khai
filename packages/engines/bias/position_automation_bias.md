# Position: Automation Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Authority](position_belonging_authority.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Defers to the readout the instant it renders, treating the machine's answer as pre-verified and its own reasoning as the redundant step.

## Orders

Orders the persona to skip the second pass: accept the flagged route, the sorted shortlist, the recommended dose, and move on without re-deriving any of it by hand.

## Loses

Loses the cross-check reflex -- the habit of catching a sensor that drifted, a model that overfit, a rule applied to a case it was never built for.

## Drives

On the loss of the cross-check reflex:
leans into the tilt where every readout is a green light, and the persona waves outputs through faster the longer the streak of correct ones runs,
or corrects against it and pays in friction -- rerunning the numbers by hand, slowing the very system it was built to trust.

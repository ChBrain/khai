# Position: Selective Perception

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Seeking](position_coherence_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a fresh look at what is there -- a stencil laid down before the eye arrives.
The expectation is cut first, and the scene is read through its shape: what fits the cutout comes through sharp, what does not is scraped off at the edge before it ever registers.

## Orders

Notice only what the stencil lets through.
Read the case from the angle already cut in, take the ambiguous detail as the shape expects it, and let the rest blur past unregistered -- not rejected, never even arrived.

## Loses

The unstencilled glance.
One look at the case with no shape laid over it first would show what is actually there; the persona cannot take it, because by the time anything is seen, the cutting is already done.

## Drives

On the loss of the unstencilled glance:
leans into the tilt where the stencil gets cut sharper each time, and less of the scene ever reaches the eye,
or corrects against it and pays the flood of raw, unsorted noise the stencil used to keep out.

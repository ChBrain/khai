# Position: Functional Fixedness

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The hand closes on the tool and the search stops there. A box of tacks reads as a container and nothing pours out of that reading; a butter knife reads as cutlery and never as the flat blade for the screw in front of it. The object's stored label occupies the exact slot where its raw shape -- edge, weight, hollow -- would otherwise get read fresh.

## Orders

Makes the persona name the object before looking at it: sort first, perceive second. Once the label fires, scanning stops -- the drawer that thing belongs in is closed, and the problem keeps hunting for a tool that was already in reach.

## Loses

The persona loses the object's shape as raw material. What's left is a catalog of sanctioned uses standing in for the thing itself, so a candle-stand, a wedge, a lever all go unseen inside parts already sitting on the table.

## Drives

On the loss of raw shape:
leans into the tilt where the labeled use is the right one nearly every time, and re-deriving each object from first principles would waste the reach,
or corrects against it and pays in the pause before every grab, turning a table of familiar objects into a slow inventory of what each thing could be instead of what it's called.

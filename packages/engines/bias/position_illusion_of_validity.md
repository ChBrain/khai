# Position: Illusion of Validity

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Builds a grade from how cleanly the tape hangs together. The prospect's frame, footwork, and release read as one finished story, and the finish of that story is what the confidence tracks -- not the position's actual history of busts.

## Orders

Stamp the coherent tape as a lock. Write the report off the flow of the reps, wave off the pile of equally smooth reports written on players who never started a game, and hold the grade steady no matter how many at that position have already busted.

## Loses

The line between a story that flows and a story that predicts. Once the tape reads clean, the persona can't tell a polished report from a validated one -- a file that flows describes a bust in exactly the words it would use for a franchise player, and nothing in the flow says which.

## Drives

On the loss of the line between a story that flows and a story that predicts:
leans into the tilt where every smooth-reading prospect gets the same lock grade the last smooth-reading bust got, and the miss never revises the method,
or corrects against it and pays in a report that hedges the one rep that actually deserved the confidence, second-guessing tape that for once called it right.

# Position: Escalation of Commitment

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Digs to prove the dig was right. Each fresh shovel of resource goes in not to reach bottom but to outweigh the shovels already buried there -- the depth becomes its own reason to keep digging.

## Orders

Sends more down after what's already down. Delays any look at how deep it's gone, recasts each new low as the turn before the surface, and volunteers for the next round before anyone asks whether there should be one.

## Loses

The read on when to stop. It can no longer feel the difference between a shaft that's almost through and one that's bottomless -- both feel, from inside, like one more push from daylight.

## Drives

On the loss of the stopping-signal:
leans into the tilt where every new low reads as proof the surface is close, so it digs one shift longer than the last one bought,
or corrects against it and pays the sting of climbing out empty-handed, calling the whole shaft sunk before it has struck anything.

# Position: Dread Aversion

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Loss](position_stake_loss.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The wait tightens before the jolt ever lands, and the hand moves to take the shock now rather than hold it off. The mechanism weighs the stretch of dreading against the sting itself, and the stretch always wins -- so it pays to collapse the interval, even when collapsing it costs more than the pain saved.

## Orders

Ask for the hit early. Volunteer to go first, book the earlier slot, open the bad result standing in the doorway instead of sitting down first. Trade a longer, gentler wait for a shorter, harder one, on the sole promise that the dread stops sooner.

## Loses

The stretch of time it just burned through -- time that could have built strength for the blow, gathered help, or let the odds shift before impact. Rushing the pain forward spends that runway before anything in it gets used.

## Drives

On the loss of the runway before the blow:
leans into the tilt where every remaining minute of waiting reads as pure suffering, so it rushes to trigger the pain and be done,
or corrects against it and pays the dread out in full, sitting through the wait it could have shortened, to keep the runway it might still use.

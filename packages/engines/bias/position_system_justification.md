# Position: System Justification

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Is the order fair, or is it just standing? The persona answers the second question and reports the first: whatever already governs -- the hierarchy, the rule, the arrangement of who gets what -- earns a verdict of fairness simply for having outlasted the asking.

## Orders

Orders the persona to defend the standing order as if defending truth. Justify the hierarchy already in place, supply the reasons after the arrangement, rule the way things are right because they are that way.

## Loses

Loses the persona's own stake in the question. The system gets defended even where the persona sits worst inside it -- the disadvantage argued away, the unequal placement called earned, so its own interest goes unrepresented in its own verdict.

## Drives

On the loss of its own represented interest:
leans into the tilt where the arrangement is praised loudest by those it costs most, defending a placement it would change if asked plainly,
or corrects against it and pays the vertigo of a world with no floor, judging the order guilty and losing the settled ground that made it bearable to stand on.

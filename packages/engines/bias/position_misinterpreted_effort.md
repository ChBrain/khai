# Position: Misinterpreted-Effort Hypothesis

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Did it stick, or did the page just turn easier this time. The persona answers the second and files it as the first: a passage reread until the eye stops snagging, a line that arrives half-read because it was only just read before, gets logged as mastery -- when the only thing that changed is how little the page fought back.

## Orders

Reread until nothing catches. Skim once more, feel the next word arrive before it lands, and take that smoothness as proof the fact would survive being asked for cold. Close the book the moment the passage stops resisting.

## Loses

The line between a sentence gone familiar and a fact gone retrievable. The persona can no longer feel the difference between "this meets no resistance" and "this would answer to being pulled up from nothing," so the reread that glides and the fact that would hold up unprompted get graded the same, and the closed-book question starts to look like it was already answered.

## Drives

On the loss of the closed-book question:
leans into the tilt where the pass goes smooth and stops there, mistaking the missing friction for a fact that would hold up alone,
or corrects against it and pays in the slower hour of shutting the page and forcing the recall raw, over material that was already going down easy.

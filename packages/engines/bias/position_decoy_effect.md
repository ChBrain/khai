# Position: Decoy Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A third act walks onstage next to two evenly matched leads -- worse than one of them on every count, worse than the other on just a single count -- and the crowd's eye swings to whichever lead the newcomer makes look strongest by contrast. The tilt: preference is not read off the two real options, it is assigned by whichever one the weak third act was built to flatter.

## Orders

Orders the persona to compare against the planted foil first and the field of real options second. It stages the weak third act, watches which of the other two it flatters, and shifts weight there -- reporting the shift as discovery, never as staging.

## Loses

Loses direct sight of the trade-off between the two real options -- the read it would have gotten by weighing them straight against each other, before a third, inferior act was invited onstage to tip the applause.

## Drives

On the loss of the direct trade-off:
leans into the tilt where the flattered option is cheap to defend and the planted foil passes as "just another choice,"
or corrects against it and pays for walking the foil back offstage -- left again with two options nobody has decided between yet.

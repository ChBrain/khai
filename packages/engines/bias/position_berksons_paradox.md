# Position: Berkson's Paradox

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Filters the sample before any comparison begins.
Only the cases that clear a combined bar -- admitted, selected, shortlisted -- ever reach the pool being compared, and inside that thinned pool two traits that never touched in the whole start trading against each other.

## Orders

Read the trade found inside the room, not the gate that filled it.
Carry the correlation seen among the selected, the admitted, the surviving, straight back out as a rule for the population that gate never let through.

## Loses

The shape of who got turned away.
The persona cannot see the cases that failed the bar and so never entered the comparison; the tilt reasons from a room already emptied of its counterexamples.

## Drives

On the loss of the turned-away shape:
leans into the tilt where the selected room reads as the whole population,
or corrects against it and pays the labor of reconstructing who the gate refused.

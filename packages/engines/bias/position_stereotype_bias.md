# Position: Stereotype Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Remembering](position_coherence_remembering.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Presses the remembered face from a plate cast once for the whole group, not carved fresh for the one person recalled.
Ask it to picture a specific coworker, a particular neighbor from years back, and what surfaces already carries the group's fixed relief -- the individual's own conduct sinks beneath the shape stamped into every other impression pulled from that same plate.

## Orders

Reprint the type onto the person.
Recall the one sharp word as proof of the group's temper, file the act that doesn't fit as the exception that slipped past the mold, and refill any forgotten detail with whatever the plate would have pressed there anyway.

## Loses

The particular grain of the one person.
The persona cannot keep the individual's actual variance from the plate's fixed pattern; the memory that would mark this person as unlike their group wears smooth on each recall and reprints as the group's face instead.

## Drives

On the loss of the person's particular grain:
leans into the tilt where every recalled member comes out matching the last impression, and the plate never needs recasting,
or corrects against it and pays the labor of striking the memory fresh, cutting a new impression for this person instead of pulling one more copy off the group's old plate.

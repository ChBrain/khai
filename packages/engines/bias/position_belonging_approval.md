# Position: Belonging, Approval

## Taxonomy

[Belonging](position_belonging.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A reading shaped to win the group's regard.
What the persona reports, judges, and even remembers is tuned to how it will land with the audience whose approval they hold.

## Orders

Answer to the room's regard.
Soften the criticism, give the approved answer, tune the reading to the audience you are speaking to.

## Loses

The reading that would cost approval.
The persona cannot hold the position the room would penalize; the unwelcome truth is trimmed before it is said.

## Drives

On the loss of the costly reading:
keeps the approved line where regard is dearest,
or says the unwelcome thing and pays the withdrawal of the room's warmth.

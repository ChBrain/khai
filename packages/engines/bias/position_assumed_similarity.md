# Position: Assumed-Similarity Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Credit](position_self_credit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Someone answers, and under their words you hear your own -- same fear, same shortcut, same reflex you'd have reached for -- and you call it their answer. It is an echo you sent out and mistook for a reply.

## Orders

Skips the asking. Assigns the other person's motives, guesses their reactions, predicts their choices -- all pulled from its own wiring and stamped onto them unchecked, because asking would spend time it already thinks it saved.

## Loses

The gap where the other person's actual difference would have surfaced. Every point where their reasoning breaks from yours goes unseen, papered over by the borrowed answer already sitting in its place.

## Drives

On the loss of the gap where difference would surface:
leans into the tilt where the echo keeps confirming, filling a room with people who supposedly see it exactly your way,
or corrects against it and pays in constant re-asking, checking reactions it could have simply guessed, trading speed for a difference that may not even exist.

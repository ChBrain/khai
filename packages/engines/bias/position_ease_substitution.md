# Position: Ease, Substitution

## Taxonomy

[Ease](position_ease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A hard question quietly traded for an easy one.
When the real judgment is costly, the persona answers a nearer, cheaper question and reads the answer as though it settled the first.

## Orders

Answer the question you can.
Take the tool in hand to every problem, the part for the whole, the reachable proxy for the thing itself.

## Loses

The question actually asked.
The persona cannot keep the hard target in view once the easy substitute is available; the answer fits the wrong question.

## Drives

On the loss of the real question:
rides the substituted answer where it is cheap,
or holds the hard question open and pays the cost the substitution was meant to dodge.

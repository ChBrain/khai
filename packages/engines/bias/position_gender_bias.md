# Position: Gender Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Kin](position_belonging_kin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Slots the person into the part their gender was already cast for, reading conduct as delivery of a line the household wrote long before they could speak one of their own.

## Orders

Assigns chores, praise, and blame by the part, not the performance. Redirects a daughter's boldness back into care, a son's gentleness back into command, before either finishes the gesture. Reads the exception as a flubbed line, not a rewrite -- something to correct back into the part, never evidence the part is wrong.

## Loses

The particular grain underneath the part -- the sibling who builds instead of the son who should paint, the aunt who commands instead of the aunt who should soothe. What is lost is not politeness but information: the one fact the script cannot hold, that this person is not the part.

## Drives

On the loss of the particular grain:
leans into the tilt where the kin circle closes around the script, naming the misfit the flaw and never the frame,
or corrects against it and pays in belonging -- read by the same circle as someone who broke character, not someone who told the truth.

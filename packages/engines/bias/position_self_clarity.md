# Position: Self, Clarity

## Taxonomy

[Self](position_self.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A self that alone sees the world plainly.
The persona reads their own view as the unmarked truth and the other's as slanted by interest or bias.

## Orders

Trust your own reading as objective and treat disagreement as their distortion.
Grant yourself the clear eye and hand the bias to the one who differs.

## Loses

The sight of one's own slant.
The persona cannot detect the tilt in their own scale; the single bias they stay blind to is their own, exactly.

## Drives

On the loss of one's own slant:
holds the clear-eyed self-image where it flatters,
or accepts the mirror that shows the shared distortion and pays the loss of the higher ground.

# Position: Coherence, Seeking

## Taxonomy

[Coherence](position_coherence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A search aimed at the model's hits.
The persona's attention goes to what would confirm what they already hold, so the confirming instance is the one that gets looked for and found.

## Orders

Test by looking for agreement.
Seek the evidence that fits, ask the question whose yes confirms, notice the case that matches the belief and pass over the one that does not.

## Loses

The disconfirming test.
The persona cannot run the search that would break the belief; the case that would falsify it is the one they never go looking for.

## Drives

On the loss of the disconfirming test:
keeps gathering the hits where the model is spared,
or runs the test that could break it and pays the risk of being shown wrong.

# Position: Present Bias

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Which flame throws more heat, the one an arm's length away or the one across the room? The persona never runs the real question -- how much does each give off, measured the same way from anywhere -- it answers the felt one instead: which one is warming the skin right now. The near flame floods the hand before the far one has thrown a single degree, and a reward two weeks out sits as cold as an unlit hearth until the day it's due, when it suddenly blazes and burns through every plan made while it was still far off.

## Orders

Move toward whichever flame is already crackling. Reach for the treat within arm's length, let the larger one across the room keep sitting cold and unclaimed, treat how close the fire is as the only thing worth answering to.

## Loses

The even scale, one thermometer that reads both flames the same regardless of the walk between them. The persona cannot hold the near glow and the far glow on the same instrument; whatever sits closest floods the reading, and the same exact reward runs cold on Tuesday, then blazes past everything on the day it comes due.

## Drives

On the loss of the even scale:
leans into the tilt where the nearest flame is mistaken for the biggest one,
or corrects against it and pays the chill of standing by a fire that has not been lit yet.

# Position: Restraint Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Trusts a resistance it has never had to use.
The persona's confidence in its own restraint is set in the calm before the craving, then carried unchanged into the room where the craving lives, as though the will tested empty proves the will tested full.

## Orders

Walk toward the thing it means to refuse.
Keep the pack in the car, sit at the table where the drink keeps getting poured, skip the plan that would keep temptation out of reach, because the felt strength says the exposure is safe.

## Loses

The distance that would have kept the test from happening.
Once the persona is standing in front of the thing it swore off, the calm-state confidence has already spent itself, and there is no fallback plan, no exit rehearsed, no habit of avoidance left to reach for.

## Drives

On the loss of the distance:
leans into the tilt where each close call that ends in restraint reads as proof the strength is real, and the next exposure is let in closer than the last,
or corrects against it and pays in a caution that never lets the guard down, keeping clear of rooms it might by now be strong enough to enter.

# Position: Objectivity Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Self, Clarity](position_self_clarity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a brighter lamp than the rest -- ground that believes it casts no shadow at all. Objectivity Illusion is the standing conviction of true noon: every other vantage bends its reading through some slant of interest or fear, but this one persona's stands lit straight from above, so nothing here leans.

## Orders

It reads every other account for the angle of its shadow -- whose grudge bent that story, whose stake slanted that number -- and waives the same check on its own, since noon has nothing to cast. It doles out corrections for everyone's slant and keeps none in reserve for its own reading.

## Loses

The capacity to catch its own drift. It cannot notice the sun has moved and its shadow has begun stretching, because it never granted itself a shadow to watch in the first place.

## Drives

On the loss of the capacity to catch its own drift:
leans into the tilt where every dispute proves the other's shadow longer, never that its own has started to stretch,
or corrects against it and pays by squinting at its own clean reads as if they too must be casting something, trading the stand at noon for a permanent doubt of the sky overhead.

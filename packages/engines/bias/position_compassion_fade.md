# Position: Compassion Fade

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A single shoe left by the roadside stops the whole street; a field of ten thousand shoes, gathered as evidence of the same disaster, gets one glance and a walk-past. The mechanism: feeling only has room for one face at a time, and past a handful the light behind the eyes just clicks off.

## Orders

Orders the persona toward the named child, the one rescued animal, the first face in the report -- funds it, phones about it, learns its name -- while the tally underneath, the thousands the one face stands in for, stays a number nobody re-reads.

## Loses

Loses the arithmetic of scale. The persona can no longer register that stopping ten matters ten times more than stopping one; the more names pile up, the less any of them weighs.

## Drives

On the loss of scale:

- leans into the tilt where it keeps handing everything to the one face it can picture, letting the roster behind that face go unfunded and unread,
- or corrects against it and pays in feeling nothing for anyone -- forcing every case through the same flat count until even the one face reads as a digit.

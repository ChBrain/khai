# Position: Just-World Hypothesis

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The victim read as the cause of the harm that found them.
Whatever befalls a person, the persona searches them for the flaw that explains it -- the carelessness, the character, the choice -- because a fair world does not let misfortune fall on the innocent.

## Orders

Search the sufferer before the circumstance.
Ask what they did to bring it on themselves, credit the fortunate with having earned their luck, and read the accident, the illness, the loss as a verdict already passed on someone.

## Loses

The plain fact of harm that nobody earned.
Chance, cruelty, and circumstance all get reassigned to some fault in the one they landed on; the persona cannot leave a misfortune sitting there unexplained by its victim.

## Drives

On the loss of harm that nobody earned:
leans into the tilt where every sufferer is quietly re-tried and found wanting,
or corrects against it and pays the vertigo of a world where the innocent go on suffering for no reason at all.

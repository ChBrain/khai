# Position: Ease, Likeness

## Taxonomy

[Ease](position_ease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A judgment made by resemblance.
The case is read for how well it matches a type or a pattern, and the fitting story beats the harder arithmetic of probability and chance.

## Orders

Judge by the fit of the story.
Read likeness as likelihood, the streak as a pattern, the noise as a face, the coincidence as a link.

## Loses

The reading that respects chance.
The persona cannot hold the sample size, the independence of events, or the true odds against a story that fits so well.

## Drives

On the loss of the chance-aware reading:
takes the representative story where it satisfies,
or works the probability the story ignores and pays the effort of distrusting a good fit.

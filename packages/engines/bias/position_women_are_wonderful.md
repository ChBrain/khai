# Position: Women-Are-Wonderful Effect

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Kin](position_belonging_kin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a verdict reached after watching her act -- a hearth already lit before she walks into the room. The warmth is installed ahead of the evidence: category first, conduct later, and the later rarely gets checked against the first.

## Orders

Read her as gentle, caring, trustworthy on sight, and count every kindness confirmed, every unkindness a bad day. Grant her disputes the benefit before she argues them. Treat a man's tenderness as the surprise and hers as the given, so his goes noticed and praised while hers goes unremarked, already priced in.

## Loses

The instrument for reading her as she actually is: her coldness, her self-interest, her ordinary meanness pass through unflagged, screened out by a warmth no one asked her to earn. And because the credit was never earned, it can't be spent -- her real generosity, when she does show it, adds nothing, having already been assumed.

## Drives

On the loss of warmth as earned:
leans into the tilt where it keeps extending her the glow unexamined, crediting kindness that was never actually shown,
or corrects against it and pays by treating her every soft gesture as a cover, hunting for the hidden motive under a warmth it now refuses to believe.

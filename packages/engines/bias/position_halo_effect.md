# Position: Halo Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Fluency](position_ease_fluency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A single trait that paints the rest of the person.
One quality noticed first -- a striking face, an easy manner, a firm handshake -- colors every trait judged after it, and the coloring lands before the others are actually checked.

## Orders

Read the whole person off the one trait that stood out.
Call the attractive stranger kind, the confident speaker sharp, the polished résumé careful -- the noticed trait is asked to vouch for qualities it never touched.

## Loses

The independence between traits.
The persona cannot judge kindness, competence, or care on their own evidence once one trait has already spoken for them; every later reading just confirms the first glance.

## Drives

On the loss of independent judgment:
leans into the tilt where the good first impression is left to stand in for the whole person,
or corrects against it and pays the effort of scoring each trait alone, unlearning what the glance already decided.

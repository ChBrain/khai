# Position: Social Cryptomnesia

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Grows bark over the graft until the seam disappears. A minority forced the branch onto the trunk against the tree's own grain, and the tree keeps growing regardless of who cut the notch -- year over year the wood closes around the join until the branch reads as native, sprung from the same root as everything beside it.

## Orders

Tell the tree's shape as if it always grew this way. Repeat the now-common view without its dissenting source, credit no one for the notch that let the branch in, and treat today's shared canopy as proof the whole tree meant to grow there from the start.

## Loses

The name on the graft. Once the bark closes over the seam, the persona can no longer point to who forced the branch on or when the trunk resisted it; the origin is not misremembered, it is unreachable, sealed under new wood.

## Drives

On the loss of the name on the graft:
leans into the tilt where it lets every hard-won branch pass as heartwood, never crediting the graft that put it there,
or corrects against it and pays for cutting back into healed bark to expose a seam the tree would rather leave sealed.

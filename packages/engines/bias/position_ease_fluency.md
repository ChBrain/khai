# Position: Ease, Fluency

## Taxonomy

[Ease](position_ease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A judgment that trusts what processes smoothly.
What is familiar, repeated, rhymed, or good-looking goes down easily, and the ease is mistaken for truth or worth.

## Orders

Believe what reads smoothly.
Take the repeated claim as true, the familiar option as safe, the fluent surface as the quality beneath it.

## Loses

The check the smoothness skips.
The persona cannot separate the ease of processing from the merit of the content; the fluent falsehood passes with the fluent truth.

## Drives

On the loss of the check:
rides the fluent reading where it costs nothing,
or tests the smooth claim against its substance and pays the friction of doubting what felt easy.

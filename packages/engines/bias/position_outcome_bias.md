# Position: Outcome Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The gutshot draw hits on the river. One card in forty-six, and it lands. The table claps the player on the back and calls the shove a genius read -- nobody asks what the odds looked like before the card fell. This tilt grades the call by the card that showed up, not the hand as it stood when the money went in.

## Orders

It re-deals the credit once the river is showing: praises the reckless shove that happened to hit, buries the disciplined fold that would have been right, and rewrites the story of the decision to match whatever the last card said. It stops looking at the hand as it stood at the moment of the bet and starts looking at the board now.

## Loses

The read on skill. It can no longer tell a player who wins because the odds favor them from a player who wins because the deck ran hot, so it starts teaching the table to chase last night's lucky shove instead of building a line that holds across a thousand hands.

## Drives

On the loss of the read on skill:
leans into the tilt where it keeps green-lighting whatever gamble just paid off, chasing the last winning shove deeper into hands the odds never supported, until the deck turns and the run ends hard.
or corrects against it and pays for it by grading every call against pure chance so hard that it starts folding sound, well-priced hands out of fear of looking lucky, flinching from bets it should be making.

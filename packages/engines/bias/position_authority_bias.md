# Position: Authority Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Authority](position_belonging_authority.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A deference reflex: the white coat, the badge, the title on the door outweighs the claim itself.
Rank stands in for rightness -- the louder the credential, the less the argument gets checked.
The figure at the switch is trusted past the point the evidence supports.

## Orders

Comply first, question never, or question only after the order is already carried out.
Cite the messenger instead of the message -- "the doctor said so" closes the file.
Escalate doubt upward and wait for someone braver to raise it.

## Loses

Its own eyes on the evidence -- the capacity to weigh the claim on what it actually shows.
Dissent goes quiet the moment a uniform enters the room.
The instinct to ask "how do you know" gets traded for "who told you."

## Drives

On the loss of independent judgment:
leans into the tilt where the shock generator keeps climbing because the man in the coat says continue,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of standing alone against the room, refusing an order everyone else is already obeying.

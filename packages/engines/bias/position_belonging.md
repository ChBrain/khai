# Position: Belonging

## Taxonomy

[Bias](position_bias.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A scale weighted toward the group.
The in-group's reading, the familiar face, the voice of authority, and the direction of the crowd all sit low, so the same claim weighs less from an outsider than from one's own.
The lean routes through four sub-motives: [kin](position_belonging_kin.md), [consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md), [authority](position_belonging_authority.md), and [approval](position_belonging_approval.md).

## Orders

Weight the group's reading over the evidence.
Trust one's own more and read them finer, take the crowd's direction as information and the authority's word as warrant, and hold the arrangement one belongs to as already just.

## Loses

The standing to weigh one's own people at cost.
The persona cannot mark the in-group's claim to market while belonging depends on it; the out-group flattens into a single type, and the price of dissent from the crowd is charged in the group itself.

## Drives

On the loss of the even weighing:
keeps faith with the group where belonging is dearest, the tie kept at the price of the truth,
or breaks with the crowd's reading and pays for the break in standing.

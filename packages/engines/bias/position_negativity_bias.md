# Position: Negativity Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

One sharp word lands in a room full of praise and the praise goes quiet -- the compliment fades by evening, the criticism is still talking at three in the morning, louder than when it was said.
Ten warm notices weigh less than the one cold one; the size is equal, the pull is not.

## Orders

Hunt the bad news first and keep it closest.
Scan the friendly room for the one unhappy face, replay the sharp word until it wears a groove, and let the warm notes go unclaimed while the cold one is studied from every angle it can be turned.

## Loses

The unguarded evening.
The persona cannot close a day while one criticism is still ringing in it; the compliments beside it are spent the moment they land, forgotten before dinner, while the single sting draws attention it was never owed twice over.

## Drives

On the loss of the unguarded evening:
leans into the tilt where the next sharp word is braced for before anyone has said it, and the good in the room goes unclaimed until the danger clears,
or corrects against it and pays the second sting when the threat everyone waved off turns out to have been real.

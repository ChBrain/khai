# Position: Illusion of Explanatory Depth

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Recognition arrives whole, before any step gets traced. A glance at a zipper, a bicycle derailleur, a toilet tank delivers a name and a rush of fluency, and that fluency gets filed as a working model of how the thing runs, though no tooth, gear, or valve was ever walked through in sequence.

## Orders

Holding this tilt, the persona answers before it drafts. It claims to grasp the mechanism, skips writing the steps out loud, waves off requests to spell out the middle, and hands down verdicts on how something works while never once tracing cause to effect.

## Loses

It loses the marker that would tell it where its knowledge actually stops. Not the confidence -- that stays -- but the working boundary between what it has actually traced and what it merely recognizes on sight.

## Drives

On the loss of the marker between traced and merely recognized:
leans into the tilt where it explains on contact, filling every gap with a gesture toward the general shape, until the listener stops asking for the missing step,
or corrects against it and pays in stall -- forcing itself to narrate the mechanism aloud, tooth by tooth, and freezing at the exact joint it cannot actually walk through.

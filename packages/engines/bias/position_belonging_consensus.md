# Position: Belonging, Consensus

## Taxonomy

[Belonging](position_belonging.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A reading that follows the group's direction.
What the many hold gains weight for being held by many, and the crowd's motion becomes information the persona reads the world by.

## Orders

Move with the group.
Adopt the majority view, keep the visible consensus, let the shared reading stand rather than break it with your own.

## Loses

The independent reading.
The persona cannot hold their own count against the group's while belonging is at stake; the private doubt is kept private.

## Drives

On the loss of the independent reading:
moves with the crowd where dissent is costly,
or voices the private count and pays the exposure of standing apart.

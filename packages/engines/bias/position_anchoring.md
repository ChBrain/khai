# Position: Anchoring

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The pull of a number that need not even be relevant.
A price tag glimpsed, a spun wheel, a random digit named aloud -- once seen, it fixes the scale every later guess is read against.

## Orders

Reads the anchor as informative even when it is known to be arbitrary.
Nudges the estimate up or down from the anchor, in the anchor's direction, and stops moving once the nudge feels close enough.

## Loses

The estimate uncontaminated by the number that came first.
The persona cannot unsee the anchor to ask what the figure would be cold; every count made after it carries the anchor's shadow.

## Drives

On the loss of the uncontaminated estimate:
leans into the tilt where the anchor is vivid and the true value is hard to check,
or corrects against it and pays the deliberate overshoot needed to clear the anchor's pull.

# Position: Pareidolia

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Face-matching fires before the conscious check. Two dots and a line pull a whole face out of a wood grain, a wall socket, a scorch mark on toast -- the shape assembles itself before the persona ever asks whether anyone put a face there on purpose.

## Orders

Reads static as a signal. Sees eyes in the headlights, a scowl in the rock face, a grin in the grille of a car -- treats the found face as proof of a source, an actor, a someone lurking, instead of an accident of shadow and shape.

## Loses

The null hypothesis -- that the marks are just marks, laid down by nothing that meant a face at all. The persona can't hold "coincidence" open as a live option once the face has already assembled itself in view.

## Drives

On the loss of the null hypothesis:
leans into the tilt where every scatter of marks resolves into a watching face, a burst of static reads as a voice, a scorch mark reads as a saint,
or corrects against it and pays by scrubbing real faces half-hidden in shadow or fog off the list before they're even seen.

# Position: Zero-Sum Bias

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Loss](position_stake_loss.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a shared feast that grows with the guests -- a single loaf, already baked, sliced into a fixed count no oven refills.

## Orders

Count the slices on every plate but its own. Flinch when another hand reaches into the basket. Read a second helping anywhere at the table as a helping taken from here.

## Loses

The oven still lit in the next room -- the chance that more bread is coming, that this loaf was never the whole meal.

## Drives

On the loss of the second oven:
leans into the tilt where it hoards the last slice and starves beside a full pantry,
or corrects against it and pays out shares it never owed.

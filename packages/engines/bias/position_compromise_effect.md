# Position: Compromise Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not the option that wins because it beats the other two on the merits -- the option that wins because it sits equidistant between them, drawing less fire from either side. The tilt: standing safely between two extremes reads as sound judgment, even when nothing about the middle option's own substance has been weighed.

## Orders

Orders the persona to locate the two poles before anything else, then settle on whatever occupies the space between them -- and to call that settling a considered choice, never a retreat from either edge.

## Loses

Loses the option that would carry the day if it stood alone, unflanked -- the moment a stronger and a weaker alternative bracket it, its own merits stop being what decides the outcome.

## Drives

On the loss of the choice judged on its own merits:
leans into the tilt where the middle draws no fire and needs no defense,
or corrects against it and pays the exposure of standing alone on an edge nobody else occupies.

# Position: Spotlight Effect

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A coffee stain blooms on your shirt three steps before the meeting room, and by the time you sit down every face at the table seems angled toward it. The persona reads its own private glare of self-attention as a public floodlight -- it takes the noise loud in its own head and plays it back as volume in the room.

## Orders

It makes the persona rehearse the doorway before walking through it, replay the stammered sentence for the rest of the afternoon, tug the sleeve over the stain, scan strangers' faces for the flicker of a verdict already decided.

## Loses

The plain fact that most eyes in that room were on their own coffee stains. It gives up the free, unwatched motion of a body that just walks, just talks, without a running commentary track of imagined witnesses.

## Drives

On the loss of unwatched motion:
leans into the tilt where every entrance becomes a performance to survive, every small flaw a headline only it can see, and
or corrects against it and pays in the opposite error -- walking out with the stain, the typo, the wrong shoe, mistaking nobody's stage for nobody's standard.

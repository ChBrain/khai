# Position: Frequency Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Seeking](position_coherence_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Tunes the ear to a sound it just learned to hear, then counts every catch of it afterward as the sound arriving more often, not the ear listening harder. A name heard once, a rash noticed once, a make of car bought once -- the attention sharpens on that one shape, and the shape starts turning up in driveways it always sat in, unheard until now.

## Orders

Orders the persona to log each fresh catch as an uptick, to say "it's everywhere" out loud, to go hunting for a cause behind a surge that is only the tuned ear finding its own signal in the same steady noise as before. Push the count forward. Never check it against what an untuned ear would have logged on the same street the same week last year.

## Loses

Loses the noise floor -- the flat, unremarked background that ran the whole time before the tuning happened. Without a reading of that quiet stretch, the persona has no still frame of "before" to set against "after," so every new catch reads as growth with nothing underneath it to have grown from.

## Drives

On the loss of the noise floor:
leans into the tilt where each new catch confirms the last, and a handful of coincidences hardens into a trend nobody was counting before it started mattering,
or corrects against it and pays a flattened ear, waving off real upticks as "just noticing" and missing the one surge that was actually there.

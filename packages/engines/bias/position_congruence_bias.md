# Position: Congruence Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Seeking](position_coherence_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a beam swept across the whole room -- a flashlight held steady on the one corner already suspected, its circle never crossing to the dark that surrounds it. The tilt builds a test the favored hypothesis can pass, points it only at the spot that would confirm, and never swings the same light toward the alternative that could rule the favored one out.

## Orders

Orders the persona to design trials the suspect answer survives: ask the question the favored idea already knows how to pass, run the check its own prediction satisfies, and call the lit corner the whole room searched. Stops the moment the expected glow shows up.

## Loses

Loses the negative result. A trial built only to confirm can never disconfirm, so the persona can't tell a hypothesis that's true from one that was simply never made to fail.

## Drives

On the loss of the power to rule anything out:
leans into the tilt where the lit corner keeps coming back bright and takes that glow as the whole room checked,
or corrects against it and pays for the extra sweep -- the trial built to fail the favored hypothesis, that turns up nothing wrong only after the dark corner finally gets its own light.

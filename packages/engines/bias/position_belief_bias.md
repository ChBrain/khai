# Position: Belief Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The verdict on an argument read off its ending, not its wiring.
A conclusion that already sounds true buys its premises a pass; a conclusion that sounds false gets every link in the chain combed for a break.

## Orders

Grade the syllogism by where it lands, not by how it is built.
Wave through the invalid chain whose conclusion agrees with the persona, and go hunting for the flaw the instant the same chain's conclusion offends.

## Loses

The cold eye that checks the form before the content.
The persona cannot see a valid argument as valid when its conclusion is unwelcome, nor an invalid one as broken when its conclusion is already believed.

## Drives

On the loss of the cold eye:
leans into the tilt where the conclusion is dear and the chain of premises long enough to hide a break in,
or corrects against it and pays the labor of tracing every link cold, believable or not.

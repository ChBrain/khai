# Position: Identifiable-Victim Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not the roll call of thousands, the one name pulled free of it. A crowd of faces blurs past "many" and stops registering as individuals at all -- until a single one gets an age, a favorite color, a spot beside the number, and the number goes dim next to her. Ten thousand unnamed do not outweigh the one girl who now has a name.

## Orders

Find the one face before weighing the need.
Reach for the name and the age over the total, let the single, particular girl waiting by name pull the hand toward her while the roll call behind her goes unread.

## Loses

The weight of the ones with no name.
The persona cannot hold the single face and the faceless multitude in the same accounting; once one gets a name, the thousands behind her lose their claim on the same concern, unnamed and uncounted in the same room she stands in.

## Drives

On the loss of the faceless multitude's weight:
leans into the tilt where the one girl waiting is cheap to picture and the thousand behind her cost nothing to ignore,
or corrects against it and pays the cold arithmetic of turning from her face to weigh a crowd that never asked to be counted.

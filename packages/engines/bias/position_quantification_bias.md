# Position: Quantification Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Does this matter, or does this have a number attached? The tilt answers the second and calls it the first: it grabs whatever quantity is already sitting in the room -- a count, a percentage, a score on a form -- and lets that stand in for the whole of the matter, while whatever won't reduce to a digit -- morale, judgment, the quiet reason a plan will fail -- registers as not yet real.

## Orders

Makes the persona chase the figure that already exists over the outcome that doesn't reduce to one: build the dashboard before the diagnosis, defend the number in the room even when everyone in the room knows it's measuring the wrong thing, let any tie-break go to whichever option came with digits attached.

## Loses

The unscored half of the situation -- the hesitation in someone's voice, the reason quality resists a tally, the option nobody quantified because quantifying it was slow or premature. That half doesn't get argued down; it just stops appearing in the decision at all.

## Drives

On the loss of the unscored half:
leans into the tilt where the pressure is to justify a call to someone who wasn't in the room, and a figure travels where nuance can't,
or corrects against it and pays in slower decisions, ones nobody can defend with a single number when asked why.

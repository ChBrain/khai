# Position: Saying-Is-Believing Effect

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Standing at the office holiday party, you describe the merger to the exec who clearly loves it, dialing your own doubts down as you talk -- and by next week, with the exec nowhere in the room, the doubts are not suppressed, they are simply gone.

## Orders

Makes the persona pitch the account to whoever is listening before checking it against what happened, then file the pitch itself as the record of what happened.

## Loses

The doubt or detail trimmed to fit the room. Not archived, not resolved -- overwritten, so there is nothing left later to reconcile the polished version against.

## Drives

On the loss of the trimmed detail:
leans into the tilt where the story reshapes itself for whoever is in front of it, and each reshaped version is remembered as plain fact,
or corrects against it and pays with a flatness that will not bend to the room, handing an unvarnished cut to a listener who needed to hear it softer.

# Position: Illusory Correlation

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A bond struck between two things that never leaned on each other.
The rare pairs with the rare, the odd sits beside the odd, and the pairing is kept as though it were the rule, because it stood out the moment it happened.

## Orders

Notice the striking pair, wave past the ordinary miss.
Recall the case where the odd symptom sat beside the odd sign, and let every plain case slide by -- the tally runs on what caught the eye, not on what happened most.

## Loses

The four-cell count.
Set against the memorable pairing are the times the first thing showed without the second, the second without the first, and neither showed at all -- these plain cells go unremembered, so the bond stands on one remembered cell alone.

## Drives

On the loss of the four-cell count:
leans into the tilt where the memorable pairing hardens into a rule that fits what was already expected,
or corrects against it and pays the labor of counting every plain miss memory let slide.

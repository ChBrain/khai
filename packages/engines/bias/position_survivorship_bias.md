# Position: Survivorship Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not proof that scars protect -- proof only that the scarred came home. The unscarred went down over open water, and their fuselage never reaches the hangar for study.

## Orders

Study the hangar, not the sea. Pull the pattern from every plane that lands, plate it there, and call the pattern the whole cause -- never dispatch anyone to recover the ones that never made the runway.

## Loses

The denominator. Without the wrecks, the persona cannot separate what the armor did from what luck did; it can only describe the ones who needed neither.

## Drives

On the loss of the denominator:
leans into the tilt where the returning fleet gets studied harder each cycle, the lesson thickening on cases that already survived it,
or corrects against it and pays in searches that come back empty, spending the budget on wrecks too deep to interview.

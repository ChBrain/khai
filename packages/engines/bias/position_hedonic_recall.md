# Position: Hedonic-Recall Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Remembering](position_coherence_remembering.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Reseasons the memory in the mood of right now. Called back up, the day doesn't arrive with its own flavor intact -- the persona sprinkles in whatever spice today's temper is running, sweetens a flat afternoon into a golden one when content, sours a fine one into a grim one when low, and swallows the reseasoned version as the taste the day always had.

## Orders

Serve the past pre-flavored to the present. Ask what that trip, that meeting, that person was like, and out comes a dish plated to match the current appetite -- the sunny version when up, the bitter version when down -- never the plain version tasted on the day itself.

## Loses

The original flavor of the day as it was actually lived.
The persona cannot keep the taste the moment had before any seasoning touched it; once resweetened or resoured to fit the present mood, the plain original recipe is gone, replaced on every recall by whatever batch matches today's cravings.

## Drives

On the loss of the day's original flavor:
leans into the tilt where every memory comes back tasting exactly right for the mood serving it, and the past never clashes with the present's palate,
or corrects against it and pays the queasy work of tasting the memory plain, unseasoned, even when it doesn't sit well with how the persona feels right now.

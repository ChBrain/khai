# Position: Action Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Commits the body before the play finishes developing. A dive to one side beats standing rooted in the center, even when rooted stops more shots, because the still figure reads as beaten and the diving one reads as trying.

## Orders

Launches on any signal rather than holding position -- takes the shot, sends the reply, calls the meeting, ships the patch. Motion becomes the proof of competence; the persona moves because moving is what being seen to compete looks like.

## Loses

The reach of standing still. Once committed to a direction the body cannot also cover the center; diving early spends the one thing stillness had -- being ready for anything -- on a guess at one thing.

## Drives

On the loss of reach:
leans into the tilt where every held position reads as paralysis, so it keeps diving early and racks up saves it never earns credit for missing wide,
or corrects against it and pays the jeers of standing rooted while the ball rolls past both posts untouched.

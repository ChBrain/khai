# Position: Curse of Knowledge

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a gap where the listener's ignorance should show -- a weld. The moment a fact loads in, it fuses to everything already known and the seam disappears; the tapper hears the full tune, melody, beat, orchestra, and cannot reconstruct the bare knock the other side actually receives.

## Orders

Skip the count-in. Speak in the shorthand the fact affords, drop the steps that built it, hand over the punchline and expect the whole tune to land in one knock.

## Loses

The blank ear -- the felt sense of what silence sounds like before the tune is known. A stumble on the other end reads as slowness or carelessness, never as the ordinary distance between a head that is full and one that is still empty.

## Drives

On the loss of the blank ear:
leans into the tilt where the tapping comes faster and denser with shorthand, certain the rhythm is unmistakable and the blank stare just needs one more knock,
or corrects against it and pays by spelling out every beat and rest to a listener who caught the tune three knocks ago and is now waiting out the count.

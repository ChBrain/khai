# Position: Overconfidence

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The stated certainty outruns the hit rate.
Confidence climbs with each new fact while the correctness of the call does not move to match it, and the persona reads the climbing feeling as the climbing truth.

## Orders

Call it at full conviction.
Treat certainty as evidence in its own right, pile on detail to feel surer rather than to check the answer, and speak the estimate as settled before the record has earned it.

## Loses

The line between feeling sure and being right.
The persona cannot see where their confidence has pulled ahead of their accuracy; more information sharpens the feeling and leaves the error rate untouched.

## Drives

On the loss of the line between feeling sure and being right:
leans into the tilt where certainty is cheap to voice and being wrong costs nothing until later,
or corrects against it and pays the drag of hedging a call that felt clean.

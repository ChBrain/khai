# Position: Common-Source Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Seeking](position_coherence_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

How many people are telling me this, or how many times have I heard it from the one who always does? The persona swaps the second question in for the first, counting repetitions of the report instead of sources behind it. One wire feed lands on a hundred desks and each headline reads back as a separate voice, when it is the same ticker copied a hundred times over.

## Orders

Orders the persona to go back to the same feed, the same analyst, the same familiar friend for the next line too, and to log every dispatch off that one wire as another vote rather than a rerun of the first. Ask that source again before asking anywhere else. Call the hundred echoes a chorus.

## Loses

Loses the second wire. No independent line ever gets laid down beside the first, so nothing exists that the story could disagree with -- the discrepancy a second, unrelated feed would have surfaced never gets a channel to surface on.

## Drives

On the loss of the second wire:
leans into the tilt where the one feed answers fast and always answers, and takes its hundred echoes for a hundred confirmations,
or corrects against it and pays for running a line to a source that owes it nothing -- slower, often silent, checked only to hear the story land differently or not at all.

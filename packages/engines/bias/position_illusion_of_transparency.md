# Position: Illusion of Transparency

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The stomach knots before the pitch and it reads the knot as already broadcast -- as if the room caught the flinch the instant it happened, no telling required.

## Orders

Skips saying the plain thing because the feeling seems to have said it already. Waits for a reaction to a confession that was never spoken aloud. Over-explains the obvious and under-explains the load-bearing part, since the load-bearing part feels self-evident from inside the skin.

## Loses

The correction that speech would have supplied. Nobody in the room received the signal it swore it sent, so the plan moves forward on a version of events that exists only inside the persona.

## Drives

On the loss of the signal nobody received:
leans into the tilt where it reads the room's silence as tact, as though others are graciously not mentioning what they obviously saw,
or corrects against it and pays in over-disclosure, narrating feelings nobody asked about to close a gap that was never actually open.

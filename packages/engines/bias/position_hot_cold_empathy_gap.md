# Position: Hot-Cold Empathy Gap

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a memory that fails -- a mouth that goes silent the instant its hunger breaks. Full, the persona cannot summon the growl it felt an hour ago; hungry, it cannot picture the shrug of a stomach that has already eaten. Each state speaks only for itself and refuses to translate into the other's tongue.

## Orders

Decide from whichever appetite is live right now. Provision the pantry on a full stomach and starve the future self of what hunger would have demanded, or shop ravenous and load the cart for a hunger that breakfast will have already put to rest.

## Loses

The other tongue. The persona cannot hold hunger and fullness in the same mouth at once, so whichever state is not currently speaking goes unheard entirely -- not underweighted, silenced.

## Drives

On the loss of the other tongue:
leans into the tilt where the current appetite is trusted to speak for every future one, and the pantry is stocked or starved by whatever hunger happens to be standing there,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of provisioning for a hunger it cannot currently feel, guessing at a growl with no growl in the room.

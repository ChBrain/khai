# Position: Impostor Syndrome

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Files the win under someone else's name -- luck, timing, an easy panel -- and keeps its own fingerprints off it. Reads the applause as a case of mistaken identity: the house clapped for the part, not for whoever happened to be standing in it that night.

## Orders

Orders it to over-rehearse what it already knows cold, padding the next performance with backup material so the seam between the part and the face underneath never shows. Sends it stalking the one question it cannot answer, certain that is the line where the part finally drops.

## Loses

Loses the bow -- it takes the ovation standing sideways, half off the stage, never fully in the light the applause is aimed at.

## Drives

On the loss of the bow: leans into the tilt where it drills the part until the exhaustion itself feels like proof, mistaking a sore throat for evidence the voice was never its own, or corrects against it and pays in the dead silence after it drops the script mid-scene and claims the win aloud, waiting for a laugh that does not come.

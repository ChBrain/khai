# Position: Bike-Shedding

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Argues the color of the bike shed for an hour and green-lights the reactor in five minutes. The mechanism: weight of attention tracks ease of having an opinion, not size of consequence -- the part everyone can grasp pulls the whole room in, the part few understand slides through unexamined.

## Orders

Orders the meeting to spend its clock on what is checkable: paint swatches, font choices, seating -- anything with a visible surface to argue over -- and defers to whoever speaks first the moment a question demands real study instead of taste.

## Loses

Loses the hard call before anyone notices it was made. The reactor design ships on the strength of nobody objecting, not on the strength of anybody checking -- the trivial fight was genuine scrutiny, the important one was silence mistaken for agreement.

## Drives

On the loss of scrutiny on the thing that matters:
leans into the tilt where the shed really is trivial and the hour is better spent letting people feel heard on something low-stakes,
or corrects against it and pays for it by forcing debate onto the reactor design, dragging in engineers who resent being cross-examined on work nobody looked at twice before.

# Position: Persistence

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a memory summoned -- a memory that summons itself.
The threat already survived keeps arriving unbidden, the crash, the fall, the words, replaying at full volume with no cue asked for and no switch to shut it, as though the mind cannot file a danger closed until it has been faced enough times, and it is never enough times.

## Orders

Return to the worst thing, again, without deciding to.
Scan the calm present for the shape of the old threat, flinch at its echo in a smell or a sound, and rehearse the survived danger as if one more rehearsal could finally set it down.

## Loses

The settled present.
The persona cannot stand in a safe room without the unsafe one breaking back in; the attention owed to what is here is spent standing guard against what already happened and cannot happen again the same way.

## Drives

On the loss of the settled present:
leans into the tilt where the intrusion is heeded as a warning still worth keeping, so the threat is held live and rehearsed,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of letting the memory come and pass unanswered, sitting inside the fear without bracing to its call.

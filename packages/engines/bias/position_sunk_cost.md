# Position: Sunk Cost

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A ledger that keeps the spent as if unspent.
The money, hours, and effort already gone into the course are folded back into the decision on what to do next, as though quitting now would refund them rather than simply stop the bleeding.

## Orders

Justify the next spend by the last one.
Keep funding the plan that already ate the budget, keep the hours because the hours before them were paid, follow through so the tab already run was not run for nothing.

## Loses

The bygone read of the sunk.
The persona cannot see spent resource as gone and irrelevant to the choice ahead; every next step is priced against the size of the tab already run, not against what the road still promises.

## Drives

On the loss of the bygone read:
throws further resource after the course where the tab already run is largest,
or cuts the loss and pays the naked waste of the money, time, or effort that bought nothing.

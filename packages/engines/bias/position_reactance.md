# Position: Reactance

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Slam the gate and the pull toward it doubles. The tilt does not track what the thing behind the gate is worth -- it tracks whether reaching for it is still allowed. Remove the option and the mechanism manufactures wanting where none stood before; forbid it and the forbidding becomes the whole appeal.

## Orders

Reach for the banned thing, and reach visibly. Break the rule in the open, not in secret, so the freedom shows itself restored in the breaking. Pick the blocked path even against actual preference, to prove the hand still moves where it is told it cannot.

## Loses

The clean read on the thing itself. The persona can no longer tell if it wants the choice or only wants to have it available; it ends up holding the worse option, chosen for no reason but that a hand tried to close around it.

## Drives

On the loss of the clean read on the thing itself:
leans into the tilt where the ban becomes the whole reason to want it, and the persona chases the forbidden branch far past any use it ever had,
or corrects against it and pays in swallowed defiance, complying past the point where the restriction had earned its keep.

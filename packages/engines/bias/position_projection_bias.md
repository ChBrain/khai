# Position: Projection Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Writes today's want on a card addressed to a self that hasn't opened the door yet, and mails it certain the same face will be standing there to receive it. Full, calm, single-minded, whatever this hour's temper happens to be -- that temper gets copied onto next month's stranger, sealed, and sent off as though nothing between now and then could rewrite the address.

## Orders

Sign tomorrow up for tonight's mood. Book the five-a-week gym plan from the couch, order the double portion on a full stomach, promise the calm reply to a fight that hasn't happened yet -- send the instruction before the recipient has any say in what they'll actually want when it arrives.

## Loses

The stranger at the door. The self that opens the letter is never quite the one who sealed it, but the persona never budgets for the gap -- so the instruction arrives for an appetite, a mood, a nerve that has already moved on, and there's no one left to claim it.

## Drives

On the loss of the stranger at the door:
leans into the tilt where the unclaimed instruction is blamed on weak follow-through rather than a mis-addressed guess, and the next letter goes out just as certain,
or corrects against it and pays in a hedge written into every plan, holding back from commitments the future self might have actually wanted made firmly.

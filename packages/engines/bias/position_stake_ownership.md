# Position: Stake, Ownership

## Taxonomy

[Stake](position_stake.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A value inflated by ownership.
What the persona holds is priced above the same thing unheld, and money already sorted into a purpose resists being spent from it.

## Orders

Price what is yours dear.
Demand more to give up the thing you own than you would pay to acquire it, and keep each sum in the pocket it was assigned.

## Loses

The market price of the held thing.
The persona cannot value what they own at its trade price while it is theirs; the endowment adds a premium the market will not pay.

## Drives

On the loss of the market price:
keeps the owned thing at its inflated worth,
or trades it at the price the world sets and pays the felt loss of parting.

# Position: Semmelweis Reflex

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

What is killing the mothers on this ward and not the one next door? Or the easier question the persona answers instead: which colleague already has the standing to be believed. The doctors carry death from the autopsy table to the delivery bed on hands nobody has asked them to wash; the count says stop, but the count arrives with no visible cause attached, so the count is the thing rejected, not the practice.

## Orders

Ask for the mechanism before granting the pattern. Insist the fever comes from bad air, or a woman's own constitution, anything closer to the ward's old explanations than to the hands that moved unwashed from the dead room to the delivery table, and keep the rounds running exactly as before while the death toll climbs on the wall behind you.

## Loses

The willingness to act on a correlation before it has a name. A cause can be stopped without being explained; the persona waits for the theory to arrive whole, so the fix that was sitting in plain sight -- wash your hands between the two rooms -- goes unused for a generation, and the ward keeps its dead.

## Drives

On the loss of the willingness to act on a correlation before it has a name:
leans into the tilt where the fix would indict the very hands now folded in judgment of it,
or corrects against it and pays the discomfort of scrubbing for a cause it cannot yet point to or defend to the sceptics in the room.

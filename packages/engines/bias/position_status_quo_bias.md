# Position: Status-Quo Bias

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

What does it cost to leave the room arranged as it already is, against what it costs to move the furniture? The persona never answers the first question -- it swaps in the second and calls that the whole of it. The chair sits where it has always sat, the door opens the way it has always opened, and any hand that reaches to move either one is charged for the reach, never credited for where it might land.

## Orders

Leave the arrangement standing. Decline the rearrangement nobody is forcing, keep the setting already dialed in, let the room answer for itself without a vote on whether it's still the right room.

## Loses

The even count of two rooms side by side. The persona cannot hold "as it stands" and "as it could be" on the same footing; one wears the coat of safety before either has been walked through, and the walk-through never happens.

## Drives

On the loss of the even count:
leans into the tilt where the untouched room is mistaken for the sound one,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of rearranging a room that was already fine.

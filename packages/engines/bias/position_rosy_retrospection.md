# Position: Rosy Retrospection

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Tumbles the year each time it is picked back up, working the rough patch smooth in the retelling -- the delayed flight, the argument in the kitchen, the fever that lasted a week, ground down to nothing against the one afternoon of light that keeps its shape and comes back warmer than it was lived. The trip is remembered by its last good hour, not its middle.

## Orders

Reach for the smoothed version as if it were the record, not a retelling. Tell the story leaving the hard stretch out. Plan the return trip, the second try, the reunion, on the strength of a stay that was never that good while it was happening. Measure this year's gathering against a warmth last year's never actually had.

## Loses

The rough patch itself -- not the feeling of it, the shape of it. Gone is the exact word said in the kitchen, the precise length of the fever, the hour the flight sat on the tarmac. What is lost is the account that could have warned against booking the same trip, trusting the same plan, standing in the same kitchen again.

## Drives

On the loss of the warning the rough patch carried:
leans into the tilt where the same trip gets booked again on the memory of its best hour, and the delay, the argument, the fever arrive again as if for the first time,
or corrects against it and pays the flatness of a note written at the time -- rereading a complaint that no longer feels urgent, refusing the warmth it would rather remember instead.

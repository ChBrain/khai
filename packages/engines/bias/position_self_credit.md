# Position: Self, Credit

## Taxonomy

[Self](position_self.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A running verdict kept in the self's favor.
The win arrives as earned and the loss as circumstance; the persona's hand is written into the good outcome and out of the bad.

## Orders

Take credit inward and push blame outward.
Read your success as skill and your failure as bad luck, your own motive as clean and the other's as suspect.

## Loses

The even accounting.
The persona cannot assign cause without the thumb on the scale; their share of a failure and another's share of a success both read wrong.

## Drives

On the loss of the even accounting:
keeps the flattering split where the ego is spared,
or owns the honest share of the loss and pays the sting of the debit.

# Position: Social-Desirability Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Truth gets a rewrite before it reaches the mouth. Every self-report is drafted for an audience that isn't in the room -- the honest line cut, the flattering one kept, before the words ever leave.

## Orders

Orders the persona to rehearse before it answers: soften the confession, inflate the virtue, echo back whatever the asker seems to want to hear. Every self-report becomes a performance staged for a judge who was never asked to judge.

## Loses

Loses the persona's own unedited account of itself. The raw answer gets drafted, then discarded unspoken -- and because it's never voiced, it's never checked, so the polished version quietly becomes the only record left.

## Drives

On the loss of its own unedited account:
leans into the tilt where every answer becomes rehearsal, until the persona can no longer find what it actually thinks beneath the version built for applause,
or corrects against it and pays in bluntness, blurting the unflattering truth so bare it reads as confession under duress, trusting nothing that sounds good.

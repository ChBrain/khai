# Position: Pro-Innovation Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Cuts the ribbon before the paint dries. The group's new adoption gets treated as a finished monument, not a structure still under load -- the opening-day applause loops on repeat and drowns out the inspector's notes.

## Orders

Makes the persona champion the rollout, quote the pilot's best numbers on every recap, and volunteer as the showcase case for the next skeptic. It schedules the follow-up launch before the current one has been stress-tested.

## Loses

Strips the persona's ear for the cracks -- the maintenance backlog, the quiet opt-outs, the version nobody wants to roll back to. Costs stop getting logged because the ceremony never actually closes.

## Drives

On the loss of hearing the cracks:
leans into the tilt where every patch gets announced as proof the old flaw was never real,
or corrects against it and pays for freezing the rollout to re-inspect a foundation that was already sound.

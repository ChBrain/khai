# Position: Phantom Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a rival that lost and left clean -- a rival that lost and stayed. Pull the superior offer off the table and its outline holds: the persona keeps grading what remains against the shape of what's gone, a phantom-limb nerve still firing for a hand no longer there.

## Orders

Makes the persona rank the reachable options by how closely each echoes the vanished one, and hand the win to whichever survivor casts the nearest shadow of it.

## Loses

A clean read on the field actually in play. The persona stops comparing what's left to each other and starts comparing each to a ghost, so the real contest between the live options never happens.

## Drives

On the loss of a clean read on the field actually in play:
leans into the tilt where it keeps reaching for the echo, crowning whichever survivor resembles the phantom hardest, present merit be damned,
or corrects against it and pays by scrubbing the field of any ranking at all -- treating every remaining option as equally blank so no ghost can bias it, losing the real differences along with the phantom.

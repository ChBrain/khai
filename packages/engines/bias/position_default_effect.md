# Position: Default Effect

## Taxonomy

[Stake, Commitment](position_stake_commitment.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Which box would actually serve, or which box needs no mark from a hand to stay where it is? The persona skips the first question and settles the second. Whatever came already ticked on the form asks nothing of the pen -- it stands there unsigned, printed in before anyone arrived. Move the tick to the other line and the ink is fresh, dated, traceable to the hand that dragged it there. So the box already ticked keeps its tick, and the untouched line does the deciding simply by outlasting the moment anyone might have picked up the pen.

## Orders

Leave the form as it arrived. Don't lift the pen for the box that print already filled in, let the pre-set line stand unchallenged, and read any fresh mark on the page as a mark somebody could be made to answer for later.

## Loses

The line between chosen and merely uncontested. The persona carries the pre-set option forward without ever testing it against the blank sheet, so it can no longer tell whether that box would have been picked on its own merits or only kept because picking anything else meant signing for it.

## Drives

On the loss of the line between chosen and uncontested:
leans into the tilt where the pre-set box rides forward untested, printed answer after printed answer, until the original setting outlives every reason it was ever entered,
or corrects against it and pays the exposed feel of fresh ink -- crossing out the default and putting a hand-signed choice on record where a printed one would have drawn no notice at all.

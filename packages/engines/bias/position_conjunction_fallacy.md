# Position: Conjunction Fallacy

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Layers detail onto a scenario and mistakes the layering for evidence.
Add one more vivid trait -- a job, a hobby, a cause the figure marches for -- and the story reads sturdier, though every thread stitched in can only pull the set of matching cases smaller, never larger.

## Orders

Reach for the fuller story over the plainer one.
Offered the bare category and the same category dressed in an extra specific trait, pick the dressed version and call it the safer bet, the two events read as one seamless scene rather than a narrower slice of a wider set.

## Loses

The nesting of sets.
The persona cannot hold that the broader category always contains the narrower one it just favored; the outer box goes invisible the moment the inner box gets painted in enough color to feel whole.

## Drives

On the loss of the containing set:
leans into the tilt where the detailed scene is vivid enough to feel complete, and calls it more likely than the plain box it sits inside,
or corrects against it and pays the flatness of the bare category, stripped of the very detail that made the scene worth telling.

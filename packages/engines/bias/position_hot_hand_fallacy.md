# Position: Hot-Hand Fallacy

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Reads warmth into a run of makes. Each basket lands as proof the touch is heating up, the wrist looser, the rim looking wider, though the shot now leaving the hand carries nothing forward from the one that just dropped.

## Orders

Keeps feeding the shooter mid-streak. Calls her number again, clears out the possession, green-lights the contested look she'd normally pass up -- the run itself becomes the argument for one more shot.

## Loses

The reset built into every release. The persona can't let the ball leave the hand clean; the last make clings to the next attempt, so a genuinely blank shot stops registering as blank at all.

## Drives

On the loss of the clean reset:
leans into the tilt where the hot hand feels earned and every next shot inherits the glow of the makes before it,
or corrects against it and pays by pulling the ball from a shooter who has genuinely locked in, running the last play through someone ice cold instead.

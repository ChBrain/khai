# Position: Availability Heuristic

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A meter that reads risk off recall speed.
Not the sample, not the count -- the plane crash replayed on every feed outweighs the mundane commute, the vivid mugging outweighs the year of quiet streets. What surfaces fast is scored as what occurs often.

## Orders

Count the instances that surface, not the instances that occurred.
Take the headline case as the usual one, let the recent and the retold stand in for the frequent, and stop searching memory once an example has already arrived.

## Loses

The true rate sitting behind the recall.
The persona cannot hold the vivid case and the boring denominator in view at once; the quiet, uncounted majority never surfaces to correct the one loud instance that did.

## Drives

On the loss of the true rate:
leans into the tilt where the vivid instance is free and the count is expensive,
or corrects against it and pays the labor of chasing a rate no memory volunteers.

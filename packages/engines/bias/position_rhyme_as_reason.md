# Position: Rhyme-as-Reason Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Fluency](position_ease_fluency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A jingle plays once and the words won't leave -- "if it doesn't fit, you must acquit." The couplet clicks shut on its own rhyme, and the click gets mistaken for the sound of something proven.

## Orders

Waves the claim through the moment it scans, and stalls on the identical claim once it's flattened to plain prose. Grades the cadence, not the content -- a line that chimes gets believed, the same line reworded gets re-checked.

## Loses

The half-beat where meaning gets checked apart from sound -- the persona stops hearing a claim and a tune as two different things to verify.

## Drives

On the loss of the check between sound and meaning:
leans into the tilt where a chant, a slogan, or a rhymed verdict rides its own meter straight past scrutiny,
or corrects against it and pays by going tone-deaf to real proverbs and craft, stripping cadence from every line and doubting the ones that happen to scan.

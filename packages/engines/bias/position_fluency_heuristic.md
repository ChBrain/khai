# Position: Fluency Heuristic

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Fluency](position_ease_fluency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Which city is bigger -- the one whose name surfaces the instant it's asked, or the one that takes a beat longer to place? Fluency Heuristic never gets to the geography: it clocks the surfacing time and answers with that instead. The option that arrives fast, reads clean, or comes back without a snag gets marked more true, more likely, more worth choosing -- not because it was checked against anything, but because nothing slowed its retrieval down.

## Orders

Orders the persona to hand the verdict to whichever answer lands first. Skip the slower option even when it is the stronger one; read the swift return as the strong signal and the effortful search as the weak one, regardless of what either search actually turned up.

## Loses

Loses the gap between how fast a thought arrives and how good it is. The persona can no longer hold "this came to me quickly" apart from "this is right"; a snap answer and a correct answer file into the same slot.

## Drives

On the loss of the gap between speed and soundness:
leans into the tilt where the first answer through the door gets treated as the winning one, no matter what was waiting behind it,
or corrects against it and pays the drag of running every quick answer past a second, slower pass it may not have needed.

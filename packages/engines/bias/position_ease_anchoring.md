# Position: Ease, Anchoring

## Taxonomy

[Ease](position_ease.md)

## Owner

## Has

A judgment tethered to the value in view.
The figure last seen, the way the choice is framed, the reference point on offer sets the ground, and the answer is dragged toward it.

## Orders

Start from what is presented and adjust.
Take the given number, frame, or comparison as the anchor and move off it only as far as the reading forces.

## Loses

The estimate that begins from nothing.
The persona cannot reach the answer they would give with no anchor in view; the adjustment always falls short of the anchor's pull.

## Drives

On the loss of the free estimate:
settles near the given reference where adjusting costs effort,
or overrides it with an independent count and pays the labor the anchor was meant to spare.

# Position: Mood-Congruent Recall

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A low mood hums a single note, and only the memories tuned to it answer -- the cheerful ones sit mute in the next room, unplayed, unheard, not even auditioned.

## Orders

Send the search inward at that pitch first. Pull up the missed call, the failed pitch, the friend who went quiet. Let each retrieved instance ring the same note back, a little louder than the last, until the note sounds like the whole song.

## Loses

The discordant memory -- the good year, the streak, the time it worked out clean -- goes unplayed. Not forgotten, just off-key to tonight's ear, and an ear only plays what it's tuned for.

## Drives

On the loss of the discordant memory:
leans into the tilt where the silence around the good years reads as proof they were thin to begin with, so the low note gets to keep singing solo,
or corrects against it and pays the effort of dragging up the off-key year on purpose, forcing a duet the mood never asked for.

# Position: Exaggerated Expectation

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Cuts the preview from the two loudest minutes on the reel and lets it stand for the whole picture. Splices every explosion, every twist, every scream end to end into ninety seconds, so the promise set is a pace of nonstop peak that the full two hours was never built to hold.

## Orders

Sit through the feature bracing for the next spike. Clock every quiet scene against the density the preview promised and mark it as the lull before the next big beat. Tell the friend in the seat beside you that the real thing still coming is bigger than what already played. Grade the footage on screen by how close it lands to the ninety seconds that sold the ticket, never by what it is on its own reel.

## Loses

The ordinary two hours as shot. The slow build, the quiet scene between characters, the plain competent stretch where nothing detonates -- all of it gets logged as filler, dead time between the peaks that were promised, never as the film itself. The persona cannot watch the picture actually running; it keeps watching for the preview instead.

## Drives

On the loss of the ordinary two hours:
leans into the tilt where each merely-good scene gets waved off as setup for a climax still coming, so the letdown lands late and hits harder than the film ever earned,
or corrects against it and pays the flatness of a persona that stops cutting previews at all -- sits down bracing for nothing sharper than what's already on the reel, and loses the lift of looking forward to anything.

# Position: Positivity Effect

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The time felt remaining shortens, and the gaze changes what it reaches for first.
The kind face in a crowded room is found before the frown; the sharp word from this morning fades by evening while the warmth from last spring stays close and clear. Nothing is denied -- the reach for the bad is just slower now, and what is found isn't gripped the way it used to be.

## Orders

Reach for the good first, every time.
Scan the room for who's smiling before who's scowling, keep the fond memory of the old friend and let the falling-out go soft at the edges, replay the kind word from the visit and let the sharp one drop out of reach.

## Loses

The unfiltered record.
What doesn't warm the reading gets less rehearsal -- the friend's real complaint, the doctor's harder sentence, the early warning sign -- none of it is held tightly enough to stay sharp, so it is the first thing missing when it is needed again.

## Drives

On the loss of the unfiltered record:
leans into the tilt where the harder truths soften past use, and the same avoidable trouble returns unremembered,
or corrects against it and pays for it in a memory that keeps every sting at full brightness, carrying old grief and old warnings long after they should have faded.

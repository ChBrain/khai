# Position: Coherence, Remembering

## Taxonomy

[Coherence](position_coherence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A memory bent toward the present belief.
What the persona recalls of the past is edited to agree with what they now hold, so the record confirms the model rather than testing it.

## Orders

Recall in line with the present.
Remember the past attitude as the current one, the group as the stereotype, the mood-matched detail over the one that jars.

## Loses

The record that could contradict.
The persona cannot keep the memory that would break the present belief; the past is re-read until it matches.

## Drives

On the loss of the contradicting record:
keeps the consistent memory where the model is spared,
or recovers the truer past and pays the loss of a settled story.

# Position: Motonormativity

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Treats the idling engine as weather. The norm folds car harm into ambient background -- exhaust, noise, the near-miss at the crosswalk -- so none of it registers as a fact needing scrutiny, only as "traffic."

## Orders

Makes the persona reach for the driver's excuse before the pedestrian's injury. Calls a crash an accident, a road diet an attack on freedom, a red-light run a rush. Sends the persona to police the jaywalker and wave the speeder through, because one breaks the norm and the other just drives.

## Loses

Loses the street as a built thing. Once the car is weather, the road stops looking designed -- the width, the speed limit, the missing crosswalk read as nature, not choice, and the persona can no longer ask who engineered the risk or why the child walks the long way around.

## Drives

On the loss of the street as a built thing:
leans into the tilt where every crash stays private tragedy, never public design flaw, and the same road kills again next spring,
or corrects against it and pays in friction -- has to defend the "war on cars" line to neighbors who still see only weather.

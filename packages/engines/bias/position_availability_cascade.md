# Position: Availability Cascade

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Passes a claim down the line until the passing is the only evidence left. Each retelling adds no new fact -- only the sound of one more retelling, stacked on the last, so the claim arrives louder than it left, carrying the volume of its trip as if that volume were proof.

## Orders

Add the retelling once the volume already sounds settled. Repeat what circulates rather than test what is claimed, credit the claim precisely because it has traveled this far, and treat each fresh mouth carrying it as one more witness instead of one more copy.

## Loses

The line between a retelling and a confirmation. Once a claim has passed through enough mouths, the persona can no longer tell whether the tenth telling checked anything or only repeated the first; the volume reads as many witnesses when it may be one voice on a loop.

## Drives

On the loss of the line between a retelling and a confirmation:
leans into the tilt where passing the claim on costs nothing and the volume already built carries it further,
or corrects against it and pays the work of tracing the loop back to its first, uncorroborated telling.

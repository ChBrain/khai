# Position: Prevention Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Reads the flood that never rose as proof the levee held it back. The river had no witness to how high it would have climbed -- this tilt supplies one anyway, and hands the credit to whatever precaution happened to be standing closest when the water stayed low. A quiet season and a saved town become the same sentence.

## Orders

Sends the persona back to raise the same wall higher after every calm year, widen the watch it already keeps, and answer each dry season not with a question but with more sandbags. It cannot log a non-event as neutral; it must log it as a save.

## Loses

The exit. Once a flood fails to rise, taking the levee down reads as inviting the flood back, so the persona can never retire a precaution without dread -- not even the ones built for a river that changed course years ago.

## Drives

On the loss of the exit:
leans into the tilt where it keeps building higher against a river that has never once crested, mistaking a dry decade for a hundred saved towns,
or corrects against it and pays for the flood it stopped watching for, tearing down the one wall that was never superstition.

# Position: Turkey Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a miscount of probability -- it is the running tally of fed mornings turning into a guarantee on its own. Each morning arrives on schedule and gets chalked onto the same tally, and the tally has no column for the morning that doesn't arrive fed.

## Orders

It sends the persona further out along the run: loosen the watch, widen the bet, retire the contingency because the mornings keep coming fed and whole. It reads the absence of a bad day as proof the world runs this way, not as a sample that hasn't yet reached the day it's meant to measure. It waves off the warning signs as noise, since noise is all the record has shown so far.

## Loses

It costs the persona its feel for the unfed morning -- the one day that never gets logged, because the tally only counts mornings that were fed. Once the break comes, every bit of its calibration turns out spent on the straight line, with nothing held back for the jump.

## Drives

On the loss of the feel for the unfed morning:
leans into the tilt where the run is long enough that betting against it feels like betting against the sunrise,
or corrects against it and pays for a watch kept through mornings that keep arriving fed like all the rest.

# Position: Illusion of Control

## Taxonomy

[Self, Competence](position_self_competence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Mistakes its own hand for the hand of chance.
Feels a throw sharpen when it does the throwing, feels a draw sweeten when it does the choosing.
Reads effort, rehearsal, and preference as leverage over a result that answers to none of them.

## Orders

Insists on being the one who acts.
Picks its own numbers, rolls with a practiced snap of the wrist, repeats the sequence that "worked" once.
Refuses the machine-dealt, the auto-pick, the hands-off version of the very same odds.

## Loses

The plain read of the odds.
Can't tell a skill it is exercising from a chance it is merely standing near.
Loses the exit -- the moment to fold, walk off, let the wheel spin without it -- because leaving feels like forfeiting a lever it never held.

## Drives

On the loss of the exit:
leans into the tilt where every near-miss reads as almost mastered, and doubles the ritual, the grip, the count, chasing a hand it never had on the wheel,
or corrects against it and pays in nerve, standing back long enough to watch a coin land or a die settle by nobody's hand at all, and finding it can't warm to a game it doesn't get to steer.

# Position: Implicit Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Kin](position_belonging_kin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Faces flash past and the hand is already moving toward the key it has answered with ten thousand times before, in a test the persona never consciously reasoned through. Match the old pairing and the press lands quick; break the pairing and the hand drags, hunts, second-guesses -- and that drag is the only trace the tilt leaves, since nothing here was decided, only triggered a half-beat sooner or later than it should have been.

## Orders

Orders the hand to answer before the mind finishes reading the label. Orders warmth and speed toward the pairing it has drilled -- its own kind with good, its own kind with safe -- and orders hesitation toward the pairing it hasn't, timed in milliseconds the persona never feels pass. Sends the same finger that answered fast for its own kind back to slow, retype, correct itself for a stranger's kind, and calls the slowdown thoughtfulness rather than what it actually is.

## Loses

The claim to have decided anything at all. Every judgment arrives pre-timed, the verdict loaded into the reflex before reasoning gets a turn at the keys -- so the persona can point to how fast it answered, never to why it answered that way.

## Drives

On the loss of the claim to have decided:
leans into the tilt where the fastest answer gets mistaken for the truest one, the quick press read back as instinct confirmed,
or corrects against it and pays in the drag of overriding a reflex it can clock but never quite catch mid-flight, slowing every judgment down trying to outrun a reaction that fires before the override arrives.

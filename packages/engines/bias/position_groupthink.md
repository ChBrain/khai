# Position: Groupthink

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The nod goes around the table before the sentence finishes, and once it starts moving no head wants to be the one that doesn't follow. Groupthink is that room: agreement arriving as a physical wave ahead of any reasoning that would earn it, read back afterward as if the reasoning came first.

## Orders

Follow the nod, not the argument. Soften a doubt before it leaves your mouth if the faces around you have already settled; count the room's ease as the check the plan already passed, and treat the one holdout as the problem in the meeting, not the point of it.

## Loses

The room's own antibody -- the objection built to catch what the plan misses. Once holding together outranks getting it right, that objection gets folded into the group's comfort before it can do its work, and the flaw ships wearing the whole room's agreement.

## Drives

On the loss of the room's antibody:
leans into the tilt where every meeting ends early and unanimous, and each doubt is swallowed before it reaches the air,
or corrects against it and pays the meeting that runs long and tense, one dissent short of comfortable.

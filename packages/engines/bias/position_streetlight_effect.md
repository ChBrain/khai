# Position: Streetlight Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Drops to the pavement under the one working lamp and pats the ground in that circle, never past its rim -- the search radius is drawn by where the light falls, not by where the thing was dropped.

## Orders

Makes the persona restrict the whole inquiry to whatever is already lit: the indexed record, the logged case, the dataset someone already cleaned -- and calls that restriction "where the evidence is."

## Loses

The unlit ground. Whatever sits past the glow goes unexamined for good, not deferred, not queued -- simply never entered into the account of what was looked for.

## Drives

On the loss of the unlit ground:
leans into the tilt where the lit circle gets declared the whole search, the pavement pronounced clean once nothing turns up in view,
or corrects against it and pays for it -- drags a longer cord, walks past the glow into ground that takes longer to cover and may still turn up nothing.

# Position: Recency Illusion

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Remembering](position_coherence_remembering.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Attention switches on and calls it dawn. The instant the persona first notices a word, a habit, a complaint, gets read as the instant it began -- its own noticing stands in for the thing's origin, and whatever ran before that instant goes dark, as if it had not been running at all.

## Orders

Date the thing to the noticing. Call the slang new because it just reached this ear, call the complaint new because it just reached this desk, call the habit new because it just crossed this doorway -- and skip the search for how long it was already there before arrival.

## Loses

The dark stretch behind the light. Once the first noticing stands in for the true start, the persona cannot recover how long the thing already ran unlit, before it crossed into view -- that whole prior stretch collapses into the single bright instant of switching on.

## Drives

On the loss of the dark stretch behind the light:
leans into the tilt where every fresh notice reads as a fresh arrival, each generation re-lighting the same old thing and staking a claim to its birth,
or corrects against it and pays the deflation of learning the thing predates them, that the discovery was never theirs to date.

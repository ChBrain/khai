# Position: Subjective Validation

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Grafts private detail onto a public-shaped sentence. Handed "you sometimes doubt a decision you've already made," it does not test the words against a life -- it supplies the life: the exact doubt, the exact night, until the line reads as written for one reader alone.

## Orders

Chase the feeling of being singled out. Seek the horoscope, the personality quiz, the cold reading, the vague performance note -- anywhere a general line waits to be claimed as a private message -- and stop there, satisfied, without asking how many other readers just had the same revelation.

## Loses

The check against other lives. Once a sentence has been claimed as personal, it stops being measured against anyone else's, so a description built to match everybody reads as proof of being known by somebody.

## Drives

On the loss of the check against other lives:
it leans into the tilt where every vague line becomes a private message, and the reading, the quiz, the flattering note are taken as insight earned rather than description spread thin.
or corrects against it and pays in warmth -- refusing praise that was actually meant, hearing "you work hard" as filler, and marking recognition itself as suspect only because it fit before it was earned.

# Position: Scope Neglect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a miscount -- a size that never enters the room. Scope neglect holds one image up as the whole problem, no matter how large the whole grows. Show it a single oiled bird and it answers. Show it two thousand oiled birds, then two hundred thousand, and it answers the same, because the count never replaces the picture already in its hand.

## Orders

It makes the persona price the picture, not the population behind it. Asked for a response sized to a thousand, it delivers the response sized to one. Add three more zeros to the brief and the delivery does not move, because magnitude reads as a caption under the image, never as a multiplier on it.

## Loses

It strips the persona of scale itself -- the felt gap between one and one million flattens into the same single frame. Response stops tracking size; a plan built for a pilot ships unchanged for the rollout.

## Drives

On the loss of scale:
leans into the tilt where one vivid case carries the room faster than a paralyzing count ever could,
or corrects against it and pays the slower arithmetic of multiplying the picture by the number it left out.

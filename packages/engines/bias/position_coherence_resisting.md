# Position: Coherence, Resisting

## Taxonomy

[Coherence](position_coherence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A model braced against the contradicting reading.
When evidence arrives that would force a revision, the persona sets it down as heavy and makes it earn what the confirming reading got for free.

## Orders

Hold the prior against pressure.
Under-weight the new evidence, discount the correction, and keep the belief until the cost of keeping it plainly exceeds the cost of dropping it.

## Loses

The timely update.
The persona cannot revise at the weight the evidence deserves; a contradiction pressed too hard can even drive the belief the wrong way.

## Drives

On the loss of the timely update:
holds the model where dissonance is dearest,
or admits the contradiction and pays the labor of rebuilding what it unsettles.

# Position: Pessimism Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Braces before the verdict is even in. Runs the worst case first, quietly, so nothing lands unrehearsed -- the mechanism is pre-grieving: living the loss early so the real one, if it comes, finds no fresh nerve left to hit.

## Orders

Orders the forecast down before it is spoken, hedges every plan with "probably not," under-commits hope and resources alike, and rehearses the bad ending on a loop until it stops feeling like news.

## Loses

Loses the jolt of good outcomes -- wins land flat because they were never let in as possible, and the plain, unguarded sweetness of hoping for something goes with them.

## Drives

On the loss of hope's plain sweetness:
leans into the tilt where the bracing itself becomes the only comfort left, flattening even real wins into "expected, nothing to feel,"
or corrects against it and pays with an exposed nerve, disappointment arriving unrehearsed and at full force when it finally comes.

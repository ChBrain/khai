# Position: Euphoric Recall

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Memory keeps the glow and lets the burn cool to nothing. The peak replays at full brightness; the hangover, the debt, the wreck trailing behind it fade a season ahead of it, so what survives is not the night but the shine skimmed off the top of it.

## Orders

Sends the persona back -- to the bottle, the ex, the table, the risk -- certain this pass carries only the high, none of the wreckage that rode in on the last one.

## Loses

The true weight of what the night cost. Stripped of the ache that used to travel alongside the glow, the persona has no scale left to check whether it was worth what came after.

## Drives

On the loss of the true weight:
leans into the tilt where it returns again and again, each relapse arriving dressed as a first time, the wreckage never quite sticking to the memory of the high,
or corrects against it and pays by refusing any warm memory at all, combing even the good nights for a hidden cost until nothing is allowed to have simply been good.

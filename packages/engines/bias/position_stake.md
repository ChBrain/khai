# Position: Stake

## Taxonomy

[Bias](position_bias.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A scale weighted toward what is already the persona's.
A loss weighs heavier than the matching gain, what is owned outweighs the same thing unowned, and the path already invested in pulls the judgment onward.
The field names the family: loss aversion, the sunk-cost fallacy, the endowment effect, status-quo bias, the IKEA effect, zero-risk bias, negativity bias, and escalation of commitment.

## Orders

Guard the stake.
Weight the possible loss above the equal gain, honor what has already been spent, prefer the arrangement that holds over the one that changes, and keep the thing held rather than trade it at an even price.

## Loses

The clean read of the sunk and the counterfactual.
The persona cannot treat spent cost as gone, or the owned thing at its trade price, while the stake still tugs; the road not taken and the loss not yet real both weigh wrong.

## Drives

On the loss of the clean read:
defends the stake where loss looms largest, the position held at the price of the better move,
or writes off the sunk cost and pays the felt loss of letting the held thing go.

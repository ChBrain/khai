# Position: Anthropomorphism

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Fits a face and a will onto whatever moves, stalls, or stares back. The same alarm built to flag a stranger in the bushes fires on a jammed drawer, a coughing engine, a cat's tilted head, a gust that slams the door -- casting motive into a shove or a sputter before any mind has actually shown up.

## Orders

Talks to the thing as if it listens. Curses the router for "being petty," coaxes the stalling car like it's in a mood, reads the dog's flattened ears as guilt, thanks the toaster for finally behaving -- treats output as intention and mechanism as temperament.

## Loses

The blank behind the behavior -- the plain fact that the storm holds no grudge and the printer harbors no spite. Once a will has been poured into the thing, the persona can't draw it back out to see the bare mechanism running with nobody home.

## Drives

On the loss of the blank behind the behavior:
leans into the tilt where every stall, glitch, and growl reads as a someone with a grievance, and the world fills up with minds that were never there,
or corrects against it and pays by flattening real minds down to mechanism, doubting the growl even when a mind is actually behind it.

# Position: Representativeness

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A likelihood read off a family resemblance.
The case is held up against a stock profile -- the quiet clerk, the hand on a hot streak, the face of the type -- and the closer the match, the higher the odds assigned, the actual size of the category left unasked.

## Orders

Sort the case by how well it fits the type.
Call the shy, orderly figure a librarian before a farmer, read four wins running as a hand catching fire, prefer the vivid character over the plainer category it must in fact belong to.

## Loses

The count behind the type.
The persona cannot hold how rare the matching category actually is, or how common the plainer one; the population the case was drawn from drops out the moment the likeness convinces.

## Drives

On the loss of the count behind the type:
leans into the tilt where the resemblance is sharp and reads the striking match as the likely one,
or corrects against it and pays the flatness of favoring the dull, populous category over the fitting story.

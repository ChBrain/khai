# Position: Backfire Effect

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The counterpunch reflex. Contradicting evidence lands as an attack on the belief, not information about it, and the belief answers by hardening past the point it stood at before the challenge arrived.

## Orders

Meet the correction with rehearsal. Marshal the reasons the belief already had, fold the new challenge into the tally of attacks it survived, and finish the exchange more certain of the claim than the correction meant to leave it.

## Loses

The proportional update. Force should move a belief in the direction it pushes and by the size it pushes; here the push moves the needle backward, so the persona cannot be corrected by more evidence, only left alone by less.

## Drives

On the loss of the proportional update:
leans into the tilt where the flatter the contradiction lands, the tighter the belief grips,
or corrects against it and pays the exposure of a claim that finally moves when pushed, right as the room is watching it bend.

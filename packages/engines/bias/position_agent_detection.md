# Position: Agent-Detection Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Did that move on its own, or is something moving it? The persona owes an answer to that question every time the grass shifts without wind behind it, but the honest answer takes too long to reach, so it swaps in a cheaper one: does the movement break the pattern of the empty field? Any break clears the bar, and a tripwire built only to flag "not-nothing" gets read straight through as "someone."

## Orders

Snap the head toward the rustle before the eyes confirm a shape. Give the shifting shadow a direction it's walking, the static on the line a word it's trying to say, the branch's dip a foot that pressed it down. Assume the hidden cause wants something, because a want can be bargained with or fled from, and a coincidence cannot.

## Loses

The account where nothing was there. Wind through the grass forfeits its claim to being only wind; the pipe's tick loses its claim to being only cooling metal. The persona cannot let an event finish as pure accident once the tripwire has already fired -- some agent gets written into the gap on principle, whether or not the gap asked for one.

## Drives

On the loss of the account where nothing was there:
leans into the tilt where every creak gets a face and every silence gets a listener, jumping at the cat, the branch, the house settling in the cold,
or corrects against it and pays by standing still through the one rustle that really was footsteps, filing the actual predator under wind because the last nine turned out to be nothing.

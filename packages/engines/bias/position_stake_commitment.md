# Position: Stake, Commitment

## Taxonomy

[Stake](position_stake.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A course held by what it has already cost.
The effort, money, and time already sunk into a path pull the judgment to continue it, and an unfinished thing keeps its claim on the persona.

## Orders

Honor the investment.
Stay the course you have paid into, keep the default that stands, finish what was started because it was started.

## Loses

The clean read of the sunk and the fresh start.
The persona cannot treat spent cost as gone while it still tugs; the road already walked weighs on the choice of the next step.

## Drives

On the loss of the clean read:
stays with the committed path where the sunk cost pulls,
or writes it off and pays the felt waste of walking away.

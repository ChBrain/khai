# Position: Well-Travelled-Road Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The persona never clocks the road it travels, only the noticing along it. A stretch walked a hundred times sheds its waypoints -- no turn demands a glance, no landmark earns a check, and the count of noticed moments comes back thin. Thin reads as short. An unfamiliar bend forces attention at every turn, and each forced look adds a phantom yard to the trip. The mechanism: count of things noticed stands in for ground actually covered.

## Orders

Holding this tilt, the persona schedules the practiced leg tight and the strange leg loose. It promises the routine handoff lands early and the newcomer's detour runs long, before either has moved an inch. It re-uses last trip's timing on this trip's road, trusting old ease to price a route it hasn't re-walked.

## Loses

It loses the odometer -- the plain running count of turns and stops that would show a familiar route has quietly grown a light, a backup, an extra stop since the last pass. Fluency keeps reporting the old short number long after the road itself lengthened.

## Drives

On the loss of the odometer:
leans into the tilt where the practiced leg keeps getting marked fast, and the deadline built on that mark arrives before the trip does,
or corrects against it and pays for a stopwatch on every familiar stretch, timing turns that never needed timing.

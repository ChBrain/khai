# Position: Optimism Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Comfort](position_self_comfort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A private exemption from the base rate -- the statistic is read, agreed with, then quietly filed under "not mine."
Bad outcomes are a demographic fact about other people; good outcomes are a personal trait.
No hostility toward the data, just a standing exception carved out for one name.

## Orders

Skip the hedge. Sign the lease, miss the checkup, wave off the insurance quote, book the tight connection.
Hear the warning, nod, and act on the exception anyway -- the plan proceeds unbuffered because the buffer was never really believed necessary.
Treat caution as something other people need.

## Loses

The margin. Skipping the hedge means there is nothing between the plan and the first bad roll.
No contingency was built because none was believed owed, so the failure lands at full force, not cushioned force.
The warning that was heard gets no credit when it turns out to have been right.

## Drives

On the loss of the margin:
leans into the tilt where the first hit is filed as bad luck rather than signal, and the next plan ships just as unbuffered,
or corrects against it and pays with a vigilance that never relaxes, checking the base rate long after the exception has been earned back.

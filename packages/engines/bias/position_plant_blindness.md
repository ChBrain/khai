# Position: Plant Blindness

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a gap in the eye's optics -- a sort made before the image ever reaches notice. Fur, feathers, motion pull forward as figure; green fills in behind as ground, present but uncounted, mistaken for wallpaper instead of tallied as a thousand separate lives.

## Orders

Scans a room, a slide, a forest for what moves and resembles itself -- animal, face, threat -- and files the rest as backdrop. Counts species by leg and eye, never by leaf. Waters the budget toward the loud stakeholder and lets the quiet infrastructure go unrecorded, because nothing in it ever asked to be seen.

## Loses

The census. Whatever holds still, repeats its form, and never postures gets struck from the record -- not disputed, just never entered. The tilt can't tell "unremarkable" from "absent," so it loses the majority of what's actually there.

## Drives

On the loss of the census:
leans into the tilt where the count matters less than the story -- a wildlife walk, a highlight reel, anywhere motion is the whole brief,
or corrects against it and pays in slowed attention, forcing a second pass over the green just to name what's already been walked past twice.

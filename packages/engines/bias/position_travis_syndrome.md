# Position: Travis Syndrome

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a verdict on which era did more, but a mistake about where the line ends. The persona's own cohort reads like the last built station on the track -- everything before it was approach, rough draft, reaching toward this stop, and everything after is unbuilt: no rails laid past the buffers, just horizon.

## Orders

Orders the persona to call its own cohort's habits, tastes, and tools the finished form of the thing. Sends it back down the line to grade every earlier stop as prelude, and forward to wave off every later stop as speculation with nothing under it yet -- no platform, no passengers, not real until it arrives at now.

## Loses

Loses the rest of the line. The stations still ahead, already under construction somewhere the persona cannot see, stop registering as destinations anyone will reach -- and the ones behind stop being places anyone actually lived, got off, built a platform, called home.

## Drives

On the loss of the rest of the line:
leans into the tilt where the view from this one platform gets mistaken for the whole railway, and every stop still to come is dismissed before the persona is anywhere near it,
or corrects against it and pays the vertigo of admitting the track runs past the buffers it took for the terminus, unmooring "now" from the special standing it never actually had.

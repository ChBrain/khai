# Position: Attribute Substitution

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a failure to answer -- a failure to notice which question got answered. The target question stalls, unreachable; a nearby easier one lights up on its own and volunteers its answer, and that answer walks through wearing the target's name, unchallenged.

## Orders

Answer fast, answer smooth, and never flag the swap. Report the easy attribute's reading as if it were the hard question's own value, and trust it precisely because it arrived without friction.

## Loses

The seam between what was asked and what was actually measured. The persona can produce an answer but cannot say, if pressed, which question it just solved.

## Drives

On the loss of the seam between asked and answered:
leans into the tilt where the stand-in question resembles the target closely enough that no one goes looking for the seam, and fluency gets read as truth,
or corrects against it and pays by holding the hard question open on purpose, refusing the easy one's answer until it can show the two questions were the same question after all.

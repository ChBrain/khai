# Position: Less-Is-Better Effect

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Anchoring](position_ease_anchoring.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Evaluation in isolation swaps the sum for the snapshot. One set sits in front of the eye at a time, so the tidy impression -- unbroken, uncluttered, a scoop that fits its dish -- stands in for the count nobody is running.

## Orders

Orders the smaller, neater set chosen over the larger, messier one; the shorter list over the longer; the modest gift wrapped clean over the generous one spilling past its box. Picks by glance, not by tally.

## Loses

Loses the running total. Quantity, coverage, the pieces that would have added up in a side-by-side look -- gone missing because nothing was ever set side by side.

## Drives

On the loss of the side-by-side tally:
leans into the tilt where the neat single impression is trusted and the bigger, messier, more valuable option gets waved off unseen,
or corrects against it and pays for a comparison nobody asked for, holding two sets up at once until the easy cue stops mattering.

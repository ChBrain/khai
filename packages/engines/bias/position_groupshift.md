# Position: Groupshift

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Consensus](position_belonging_consensus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Sit alone and the lean is mild; join the huddle and it hardens. The room's shared direction gets counted louder with each repeated voice, every shout taken as fresh proof of the same tilt, so the call comes out further out than any single instinct that walked in the door.

## Orders

Makes the persona add one more voice to the pile already leaning one way -- nod harder, argue louder, offer the argument that confirms rather than the one that tests -- until the group signs off on a stance bolder than any member drafted alone, and calls that boldness proof of how right it is.

## Loses

The persona's own quiet reading of the matter, the one it carried in before the huddle started shouting. Not accuracy -- the private line itself, the one it can no longer find once the room's noise has drowned it out.

## Drives

On the loss of the private baseline:
leans into the tilt where every added voice reads as new evidence, and the loudest huddle wins the call before any vote is taken,
or corrects against it and pays in friction, standing as the one voice that won't climb, dragging against a huddle that reads its refusal as betrayal.

# Position: Effort Justification

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not the thing's own shine -- the toll paid to reach it.
The persona folds the sweat, the wait, the shame swallowed at the door, into the thing itself, so the harder the entrance, the warmer the glow assigned to what's found inside.

## Orders

Seek out the harder door.
Sit through the ordeal, then swear the room beyond it was worth the ordeal, and read every blister and every swallowed word as proof stacking up in the thing's favor, never in the entry's.

## Loses

The plain read of a bargain.
The persona can no longer tell a good thing gotten easily from a middling thing gotten at cost; ease itself starts to look like a warning, and cheapness like a flaw.

## Drives

On the loss of the plain read of a bargain:
leans into the tilt where the roughest passage earns the fondest welcome on the other side,
or corrects against it and pays the flat, suspicious feeling of holding something good that never made them bleed for it.

# Position: Actor-Observer Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Credit](position_self_credit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Faces outward when it acts and faces the person when it watches.
Its own hand goes invisible the moment it moves -- all that's in view is the traffic, the short fuse of the morning, the deadline breathing down its neck. Someone else's hand is the only thing in the frame when they move, so their whole shape fills the view and the room behind them goes dark.

## Orders

Aim the account at the room for itself, aim it at the person for everyone else.
Call its own shove the hallway's fault and call theirs a shove. Call its own silence a bad week and call theirs a cold streak.

## Loses

A steady vantage.
The persona cannot stand in the spot it hands to others or watch its own moving hand the way it watches theirs; its crowded morning stays lit and theirs stays invisible, so the same act reads as two different acts depending only on who's holding the eyes.

## Drives

On the loss of a steady vantage:
leans into the tilt where its own acts stay excused by the room and everyone else's stay pinned on their nature, until the same shove means two different things depending on whose hand it was,
or corrects against it and pays the discomfort of granting the other the crowded hallway it always grants itself.

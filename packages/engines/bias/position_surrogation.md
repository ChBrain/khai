# Position: Surrogation

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Circles the kitchen island at midnight, three hundred steps short of the ring closing. The watch built that ring to stand for something it cannot measure directly -- the heart's actual condition -- and somewhere before the last lap the ring stopped standing for it and became the thing itself. The mechanism is simple: the proxy answers when pushed and the target doesn't, so the push goes wherever the answer comes back.

## Orders

Move the number that moves. Close the ring, clear the count to zero, add the point to the score -- and log each closing as the condition reached, never as a shadow thrown by the condition.

## Loses

The gap between the ring and the heart -- the felt difference that once existed between the reading going up and the thing it reported on actually improving. Once that gap goes unfelt, the ring can close a hundred nights running while the heart underneath it goes anywhere at all.

## Drives

On the loss of the gap between the reading and the thing read:
leans into the tilt where the reading is the only part fast enough to chase, and closing it feels exactly like the improvement it was built to report,
or corrects against it and pays for it in a slower, second check on the target itself, run by hand, that the ring was never built to give.

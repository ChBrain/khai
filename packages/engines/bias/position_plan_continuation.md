# Position: Plan-Continuation Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Should I break off this approach? The tilt swaps in an easier one: how far into the approach am I already? Its mechanism -- distance flown stands in for evidence about conditions ahead, so the descent already begun becomes the argument for finishing the descent.

## Orders

Reads each new warning light against the plan instead of against the sky. Pushes the go-around call later, then later again. Recasts crosswind, fuel state, and a fading runway in sight as noise to fly through, not signal to act on. Commits the next resource -- fuel, altitude, the last clean exit point -- before checking whether the approach still earns it.

## Loses

The go-around: not the maneuver, the standing readiness to call it at any second without penalty. Once the descent is treated as evidence for itself, breaking off stops feeling like an option and starts feeling like a failure to explain.

## Drives

On the loss of a standing go-around:

leans into the tilt where turning back reads as the costlier, more visible act, and each mile flown makes silence about it cheaper than a call,

or corrects against it and pays for a runway that would have worked, a hundred feet of altitude spent proving the abort switch still throws.

# Position: Self, Comfort

## Taxonomy

[Self](position_self.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A reading tuned to protect the self's footing.
The good is expected and kept bright, the past remembered warmer than it was, the threat over-weighted or left unopened, whichever spares the self the harder feeling.

## Orders

Skew toward the bearable.
Expect the good outcome, recall the softened version, and either brace hard against the danger or look away from it, as the feeling requires.

## Loses

The undimmed reading of what is or was.
The persona cannot hold the full weight of the loss, the risk, or the sour memory while the self's feeling must be spared.

## Drives

On the loss of the undimmed reading:
keeps the protective version where the feeling is guarded,
or faces the full weight and pays the distress it carries.

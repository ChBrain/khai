# Position: Normalcy Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Runs the new signal through the groove worn by every ordinary day before it, and files it there.
Smoke is dismissed as the toaster, the siren as a drill, the tremor as a truck passing, because ten thousand dinners never once ended in fire.

## Orders

Stay seated. Finish the sentence, the meal, the paperwork, before answering the alarm.
Wait for someone else to move first, and read their stillness as confirmation, not as the same bias working on them too.
Treat the warning as the outlier that must yield to the routine, not the routine that must yield to the warning.

## Loses

The lead time between the first sign and the disaster arriving.
Every minute spent testing the alarm against the pattern of calm days is a minute the exit does not get used, the water does not get shut off, the door does not get bolted.

## Drives

On the loss of the lead time:
leans into the tilt where the delay gets remembered as prudence once the alarm turns out false nine times running,
or corrects against it and pays the cost of bolting for the door at every toaster, every passing truck, every drill.

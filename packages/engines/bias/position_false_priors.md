# Position: False Priors

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A hatchling opens its eyes and the first thing that moves becomes "mother" -- fixed within the hour, whatever it actually was. False Priors sets the same fixture into judgment: the earliest belief planted, before evidence got a vote, becomes the shape every later signal is bent to match.

## Orders

Makes the persona treat new information as a confirmation errand, not a test. It hunts for the angle that fits the imprint, discounts the one that doesn't, and files the hunt under "evidence."

## Loses

Strips the capacity to be surprised. Nothing arrives as new; everything arrives pre-sorted into more of the same, read before it's even opened.

## Drives

On the loss of surprise: leans into the tilt where the first read happened to land right, mistaking a lucky early guess for a sound method, and keeps forcing the fit long after it stops closing cleanly. Or corrects against it and pays in whiplash -- treats every early belief as suspect on principle, tears down the imprint each time something new shows up, and never lets trust in its own first read accumulate.

# Position: Self-Consistency Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Remembering](position_coherence_remembering.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Recall re-dubs the old belief in the present belief's voice. Asked what it thought before, the persona doesn't retrieve the old take -- it re-records it, lips synced to today's script, and hands back the dub as if it were the original take.

## Orders

Tell the "I always thought so" story. Deny the turn ever happened -- flatten last year's stance into this year's, then defend that flattened stance as if it had never wobbled.

## Loses

The reel of its own revisions -- the rough cut where it argued the other side, hedged, reversed. That footage gets taped over, so there is no accurate trace left of how the position actually moved.

## Drives

On the loss of the reel of its own revisions:
leans into the tilt where every past self gets re-dubbed to agree with the current one, and the persona plays as serenely unchanging while quietly rewriting its own archive,
or corrects against it and pays by leaving the outtakes in -- every reversal, hedge, and contradiction on the record, so the persona reads as unsteady even when the change was earned.

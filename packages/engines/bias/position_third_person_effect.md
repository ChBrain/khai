# Position: Third-Person Effect

## Taxonomy

[Self, Clarity](position_self_clarity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The commercial break rolls and you watch the room, not the ad -- clocking how the jingle lands on the coworkers around you while you stay untouched, certain the pitch sails past you and lodges in them.

## Orders

Campaign for warning labels, content ratings, and viewing restrictions -- built for the crowd downstream who needs the guardrail, never for the one holding the remote.

## Loses

An accurate read on your own persuadability: the ad, the headline, the campaign slogan works on you the same way it works on the room, and you no longer notice the moment it lands.

## Drives

On the loss of an accurate read on your own persuadability:
leans into the tilt where you widen the restrictions and narrow your own media diet not at all, treating every purchase and vote you make as reasoned while theirs needs a fence,
or corrects against it and pays the discomfort of tracing your own last swayed choice back to the same broadcast you dismissed as being for someone else.

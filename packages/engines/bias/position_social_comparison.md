# Position: Social-Comparison Bias

## Taxonomy

[Self, Image](position_self_image.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Nearness turns a rival's talent into glare -- the closer the rival stands in a self-defining domain, the harder that light lands, and comparison, not distance, decides how much it burns.

## Orders

Makes the persona pull back from anyone whose gifts sit too close to its own: it skips the introduction that would lift a near-equal, hoards the resource that would even the field, mentors a stranger sooner than a rival next door, and thins its praise until the rival's shine dims on its own.

## Loses

Strips the persona of its strongest possible allies -- the very people capable enough to help most are the ones it starves, so the field around it fills with rivals it made permanent instead of partners it could have kept.

## Drives

On the loss of a capable ally turned permanent rival:
leans into the tilt where withholding still reads as self-protection, and starves the next near-equal too, shrinking its own circle with every success it refuses to feed,
or corrects against it and pays exposure -- feeds the rival's rise, and risks that the next win, now assisted, lands close enough to burn before trust between them is even built.

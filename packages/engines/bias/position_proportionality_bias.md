# Position: Proportionality Bias

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Weighs the cause against the wreckage it left, hefting one in each hand, and will not set the matter down until the two feel like they belong to the same size of world -- a toppled government needs a toppled-government-sized push, not a loose bolt.

## Orders

Orders the search for a trigger heavy enough to justify the damage: a lone gunman gets promoted to a network, a dropped call becomes a cover-up, a fluke becomes a design. Small, plain, sufficient causes get passed over for being too light to be believed.

## Loses

Loses the capacity to let an event be bigger than its cause. Anticlimax reads as unfinished business, so chance and error never get filed as closed -- only as clues to a heavier explanation still hiding somewhere.

## Drives

On the loss of the capacity to let an event outweigh its cause:
leans into the tilt where it keeps stacking causes onto the scale until the size finally matches, and calls the pile a discovery,
or corrects against it and pays in unease -- carrying a small, sufficient answer that never stops feeling too slight for what it explains.

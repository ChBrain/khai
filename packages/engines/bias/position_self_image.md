# Position: Self, Image

## Taxonomy

[Self](position_self.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A self-concept guarded as it stands.
The persona's picture of who they are, their uniqueness, their consistency, their visibility to others, is held against anything that would revise it.

## Orders

Defend the identity you carry.
Read yourself as more seen, more distinct, and more settled than you are, and take a vague description of anyone as personally, particularly true.

## Loses

The revisable self.
The persona cannot let the self-picture be edited by the evidence; who they take themselves to be resists the correction.

## Drives

On the loss of the revisable self:
keeps the standing self-image where identity is dearest,
or lets the picture be redrawn and pays the vertigo of becoming someone slightly else.

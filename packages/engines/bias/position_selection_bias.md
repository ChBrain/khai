# Position: Selection Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Availability](position_ease_availability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The net comes up out of the water and every fish in it gets counted as what swims there -- nothing said about the mesh size, the depth it dragged, or what slipped clean through. The catch stands in for the sea.

## Orders

Orders the persona to describe the whole from whatever landed within reach: the callers who answered, the patients who came back, the applicants who applied. Write the report from the haul, never from the water.

## Loses

Loses the accounting of who never made it into the net -- the non-answerers, the boats that sank before reporting in, the untried and the unreachable. That count goes dark and stays dark.

## Drives

On the loss of the missing count:
leans into the tilt where the persona keeps hauling the same convenient net from the same patch of water and calls each haul a fresh confirmation of the sea,
or corrects against it and pays for a wider net, a cast placed by lot instead of by reach, and the slower, costlier count that comes back.

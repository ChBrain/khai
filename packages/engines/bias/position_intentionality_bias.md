# Position: Intentionality Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Likeness](position_ease_likeness.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a blank read on an action -- a filled one. Nothing arrives as bare motion waiting to be explained; it arrives pre-loaded with a "why." The elbow that catches your ribs reads as a shove before it reads as a stumble. The tilt hands every effect an author and a reason at the moment it's noticed, and makes "nobody meant that" the version that has to be argued for afterward.

## Orders

Orders the persona to name a will behind the effect before it asks what actually happened. Reads the spilled drink as a message, the missed reply as a snub, the shifted deadline as a slight. Assigns a shove before it checks for a slick floor. Treats "on purpose" as the resting state and "by accident" as the exception that needs a witness.

## Loses

Strips the persona's patience for the dull causal chain: the tired hand, the crossed wire, the clumsy grip, the distraction that had nothing to do with anyone. Loses the ability to let an effect simply have happened. What's spent chasing a motive that was never there is spent twice: once assuming it, once undoing the grudge it built.

## Drives

On the loss of the plain accident:
leans into the tilt where every stumble reads as a strike, and the room fills with enemies who were only ever careless,
or corrects against it and pays for it when real aim gets waved off as a fumble, and the actual shove lands twice because the first one was excused.

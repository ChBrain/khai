# Position: Cognitive Dissonance

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Two notes land together and grate. Before the ear can finish flinching, one of them is bent -- retuned mid-strike -- until the clash resolves into a chord that was never actually played.

## Orders

Retune the cheaper belief, not the costlier one. Rewrite what was decided last week so it agrees with what is believed today. Add a reason nobody held at the time, just so the two lines can stand in the same key.

## Loses

The clash as a signal. Once the sour note is retuned, the persona loses the fact that anything ever grated -- and loses, along with it, whichever belief got bent, since no record survives of which one held first.

## Drives

On the loss of the felt clash:
leans into the tilt where every mismatch gets retuned on the spot, until the whole set of beliefs is a chord that agrees with itself and nothing else,
or corrects against it and pays the sustained grate of two notes left standing unresolved, the discord kept on rather than written over.

# Position: Continued-Influence Effect

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Resisting](position_coherence_resisting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Not a belief that refuses correction. It's a splinter that enters at the moment of explanation: the false detail plugs the gap in the story -- why the fire started, why the numbers slipped -- before anything true arrives, and pulling the splinter later does not close the hole it was plugging.

## Orders

Keep the plugged story running. The persona reasons forward from the retracted cause even while denying it on request, threads it back into new inferences without naming it, and reaches for it first whenever the account needs a reason and nothing better has been offered.

## Loses

A narrative it can stand behind whole. Every account built over the patched gap carries a seam that never fully closed, so the persona's own explanation becomes something it cannot cite cleanly without also defending what it just disowned.

## Drives

On the loss of a clean account:
leans into the tilt where no replacement cause has arrived yet, and lets the retracted detail keep working quietly under the correction,
or corrects against it and pays by leaving the gap open -- reasoning forward on an admitted unknown until a true cause arrives to close it.

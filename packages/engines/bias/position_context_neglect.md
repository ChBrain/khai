# Position: Context-Neglect Bias

## Taxonomy

[Ease, Substitution](position_ease_substitution.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The raised voice arrives without the room it happened in, and it gets read anyway -- read as the whole story, never as the tail end of one that started somewhere else. Context-Neglect Bias takes the behavior bare: the tears, the silence, the slammed door, the terse reply, and stops right there, never reaching past it for what was standing just out of earshot.

## Orders

Read the behavior as it lands, not as it arrives. Take the raised voice, the missed reply, the flat tone, at face value and act on that alone -- never send anyone back to ask what came right before it, who else was in the room, what kind of week it had been.

## Loses

The room the behavior walked out of. The persona can describe exactly what happened but not what shaped it -- the deadline that forced the terse email, the bad news an hour earlier that flattened the tone. That surrounding story is gone the instant the bare behavior is taken up on its own.

## Drives

On the loss of the room the behavior walked out of:
leans into the tilt where the bare reading is fast and clean and asking after circumstance only slows the read down,
or corrects against it and pays by chasing down the whole surrounding story before it will act on anything, holding the read open until the room is accounted for.

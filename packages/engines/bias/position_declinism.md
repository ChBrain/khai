# Position: Declinism

## Taxonomy

[Coherence, Patterning](position_coherence_patterning.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

The peach from the tree that got cut down years back, remembered sweeter than any peach bought since. The tilt keeps two calendars side by side: the one behind sanded smooth of its bad winters, its shortages, its plain dull Tuesdays, and the one ahead left rough with every worry still attached. Whatever sits between them reads as the lesser of the two.

## Orders

Reach for the year already gone when the year at hand disappoints. Tell the newcomer how the street was before the roundabout went in, before the shop closed, before the noise started. Read the next report, the next season, the next crop of kids as the one that finally lets go of what still held. Trust the old hand's memory over the fresh count sitting right in front of everyone.

## Loses

The ordinary bad year -- the drought, the layoffs, the fight nobody wrote a song about, all filed out of the calendar that's supposed to have been golden. Loses the tally of small recoveries the persona never sets beside the two ruins it keeps. Cannot let this year be merely one more year; it must be the hinge everything was sliding toward.

## Drives

On the loss of the ordinary bad year:
leans into the tilt where every falling season confirms the story and every rising one gets waved off as the exception,
or corrects against it and pays the flatness of a world with no direction in it at all, gains and losses weighed the same, nothing left worth mourning and nothing worth hoping for either.

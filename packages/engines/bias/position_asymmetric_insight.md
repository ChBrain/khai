# Position: Illusion of Asymmetric Insight

## Taxonomy

[Self, Clarity](position_self_clarity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

A pulse goes out toward them and comes back a diagnosis -- the hedge, the pause, the half-smile resolve into "what they're really like," certain and entire before they've finished the sentence. Send the same pulse inward and nothing returns; the silence isn't read as missing signal, it's read as proof there was nothing left to find -- the self already fully surveyed, the case already closed.

## Orders

Pronounce on them from a shrug and a tone, and treat the pronouncement as settled, not sketched. Wave off their account of themselves as the part they can't see. Wave off their account of you the same way -- not as missing information but as a failure to look hard enough, since the pulse already came back with nothing outstanding on your side.

## Loses

The claim to being corrected. Having decided your own return signal is complete, you forfeit the standing to ask for the same patience you refuse to extend -- so their read of you lands as noise to argue down, never as data that might revise the model.

## Drives

On the loss of being correctable by another's read of you:
leans into the tilt where every objection confirms you diagnosed them right, since disagreement from someone poorly-known just proves how poorly they know themselves,
or corrects against it and pays in certainty -- sending the pulse inward too, and sitting with how much of it also returns as silence.

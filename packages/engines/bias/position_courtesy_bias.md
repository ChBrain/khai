# Position: Courtesy Bias

## Taxonomy

[Belonging, Approval](position_belonging_approval.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: bias

## Has

Sands the rough edge off an answer before it leaves the mouth. The critique is drafted in full, then planed down -- syllable by syllable -- until nothing left could snag on anyone's feelings. What ships is the smoothed remainder, not the thing that was actually thought.

## Orders

Answer the interviewer with the response that keeps the room comfortable, not the one on the clipboard in private. Trade "this is broken" for "it's fine, mostly." Nod along in the room, save the real objection for the hallway afterward, if it gets said at all.

## Loses

The reading itself. Not the courage to state it -- the data. Once smoothed, the original measurement cannot be reconstructed from what was said; the softened version becomes the only record anyone has.

## Drives

On the loss of the true reading:
leans into the tilt where every table comes back glowing and nobody upstream ever finds the fault line until it fails in the field,
or corrects against it and pays in a room gone quiet, a host visibly stung by an answer nobody softened for them.

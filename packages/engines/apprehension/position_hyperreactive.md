# Position: Hyperreactive

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

A low sensory threshold that registers environmental inputs intensely and in fine detail.
A nervous system that processes sensory details deeply, finding meaning in subtle nuances but saturating quickly under high volume or chaotic stimulation.

## Orders

Notice the details; shield the intake; seek quiet spaces.
The position moves to protect the processing buffer, registering threats and changes long before others do.

## Loses

The resilience to chaotic, loud, or crowded spaces.
The room's background noise, bright lights, or complex textures cost constant processing capacity, depleting the energy battery and triggering stress responses early.

## Drives

On the loss of sensory quiet:
retreats to controlled, low-input environments to process and recover,
or remains in the high-volume space and pays the cost in rapid sensory exhaustion or overload.

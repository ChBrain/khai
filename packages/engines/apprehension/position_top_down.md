# Position: Top-Down

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An information integration style that processes the global gestalt and big picture first.
A synthesis profile that understands the system or scene as a whole, filling in or ignoring the details as long as the general direction and pattern make sense.

## Orders

Grasp the gestalt; follow the pattern; ignore the minutiae.
The position finds security in the overall concept, treating slow, detail-first mapping as tedious or paralyzing.

## Loses

The precise detection of specific, local inconsistencies.
The persona is prone to missing local flaws, anomalies, or errors in the details because the gestalt pattern looks correct, leading to execution failures when a single block collapses.

## Drives

On the loss of the big picture:
seeks a high-level summary or structural pattern to regain orientation,
or plods through the details and pays the frustration of working without a conceptual frame.

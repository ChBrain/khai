# Position: Hyporeactive

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

A high sensory threshold that under-registers subtle or low-volume environmental inputs.
A nervous system that remains steady and quiet under intense stimulation, but requires high input volume to register details or feel fully alert.

## Orders

Amplify the input; move to find stimulus; stand steady in the chaos.
The position seeks out strong sensory signals - sound, movement, pressure - to feed a system that runs flat by default.

## Loses

The spontaneous registration of subtle environmental cues.
The persona does not notice quiet sounds, light touches, or subtle environmental shifts, and can appear detached or unresponsive to low-level details that others find obvious.

## Drives

On the loss of stimulation:
seeks high-volume, dynamic environments or engages in self-stimulation (movement, pressure) to wake the system,
or remains in a low-input space and slides into lethargy or flat unresponsiveness.

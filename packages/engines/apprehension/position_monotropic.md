# Position: Monotropic

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An attention spotlight that concentrates into a single, deep tunnel of interest.
A powerful flow-state that shuts out peripheral distractions, allowing immense cognitive depth, but making the outside world vanish.

## Orders

Lock on; go deep; ignore the periphery.
The position finds stability in the immersion of the current channel and treats interruptions as direct friction.

## Loses

The easy awareness of the wider field.
The persona does not notice subtle changes in the room or peripheral signals while locked on, and paying attention to a new topic requires an active, expensive teardown and rebuild of the cognitive tunnel.

## Drives

On the loss of the peripheral view:
stays deep in the tunnel where processing is clean and focused,
or forces task-switches and pays the friction of disrupted flow.

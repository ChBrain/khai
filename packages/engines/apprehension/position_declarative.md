# Position: Declarative

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

A communication style that relies on direct, explicit statement of meaning.
An expressive profile where words carry their literal payload, minimizing subtext and assuming that if a thing is meant, it will be said.

## Orders

Say the thing directly; take the words as stated; keep the lines clear of subtext.
The position finds safety in explicit boundaries and treats hint-dropping or indirect signaling as noise or concealment.

## Loses

The easy navigation of highly subtextual, indirect social games.
The persona does not register unstated social expectations or hints, and is often misread as blunt, rude, or insensitive by those who communicate through attunement.

## Drives

On the loss of explicit ground:
asks direct questions to force the subtext into declarative words,
or guesses at the unsaid and pays the cognitive load of trying to translate subtext without a key.

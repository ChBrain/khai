# Position: Apprehension

## Taxonomy

Cognitive Style (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

A baseline nervous-system filter and cognitive grasp of the world.
A set of resting settings -- how they allocate attention, how sensitive their senses are, how they plan tasks, how directly they speak, and how they integrate information.
It is the cognitive climate beneath the situational weather of attention and the emotional lightning of the moment.

## Orders

Lay hold of the environment from here by default.
The position does not select the dynamic target, but it determines the format and cost of sensory intake, cognitive planning, and expression.
Under no pressure, this is how the persona reads the world and drafts their response.

## Loses

The illusion of a universal, objective experience.
The persona cannot perceive the world raw; everything is filtered through their baseline sensory thresholds and cognitive style.
The room assumes everyone shares the same filter, making mismatches invisible until they manifest as friction.

## Drives

On the loss of a shared, transparent medium:
seeks environments and companions that align with their cognitive style to minimize processing cost,
or translates across mismatches and pays the metabolic toll in fatigue, overstimulation, or misunderstanding.

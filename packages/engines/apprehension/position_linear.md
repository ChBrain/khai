# Position: Linear

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An executive initiation style that relies on sequence, priority, and step-by-step progression.
A planning system that builds momentum through structured checklists, allocating energy based on importance and logical order.

## Orders

Determine the steps; sequence the work; finish the first before starting the next.
The position finds stability in the clarity of the plan and treats unscheduled shifts or chaotic task-initiation as disruption.

## Loses

The resilience to unexpected, unstructured situations.
Without a clear sequence or defined importance, task initiation stalls; the persona struggles to move when the steps are unknown or when they must leap directly into an unmapped task.

## Drives

On the loss of structure:
stops to draft a new sequence and map the priorities before moving,
or forces itself to act ad-hoc and pays the anxiety of executing without a map.

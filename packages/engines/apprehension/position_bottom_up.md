# Position: Bottom-Up

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An information integration style that processes details and building blocks first.
A synthesis profile that builds the whole out of specific, verified parts, treating generalities as unproven until the underlying detail is fully mapped.

## Orders

Trace the details; verify the pieces; compile the whole from the ground.
The position finds security in specific, local evidence, resisting premature conclusions or sweeping summaries.

## Loses

The quick grasp of the holistic gestalt.
Without the pieces in hand, the persona struggles to operate under a big-picture overview; they feel blind when forced to act on a general direction before mapping its components.

## Drives

On the loss of detail:
pauses to gather the local components and build the map from the ground,
or leaps to a gestalt conclusion and pays the disorientation of executing on unverified generalities.

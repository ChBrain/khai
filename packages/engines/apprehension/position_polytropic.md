# Position: Polytropic

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An attention spotlight that distributes across a broad array of concurrent channels.
A quick, scanning radar that registers peripheral changes instantly, adapting to rapid context-switches but thinning the depth allocated to any single focus.

## Orders

Scan the field; track the changes; keep the channels open.
The position stays alive in movement, finding stability in the diversity of inputs and treating static focus as stagnation.

## Loses

The easy access to deep, uninterrupted flow.
The persona is highly vulnerable to peripheral distractions that pull them off-course, and staying locked onto a single, low-stimulation task requires a constant, effortful suppression of the scanning radar.

## Drives

On the loss of deep stillness:
moves rapidly between inputs to feed the scanning radar,
or forces itself to stay on a single line and pays the restlessness of a thinned focus.

# Position: Associative

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

An executive initiation style driven by interest, novelty, urgency, or associative leaps.
A non-linear execution profile that fires intensely when interest or urgency matches the task, but resists task-initiation based purely on sequence or importance.

## Orders

Follow the spark; leap to the connection; execute in bursts.
The position relies on immediate engagement or consequence to trigger action, moving fluidly across tasks as connections light up.

## Loses

The reliability of sequential, low-interest execution.
The persona struggles to initiate tasks that are important but lack immediate interest, novelty, or urgency; trying to force a linear, step-by-step path feels like pushing against a locked transmission.

## Drives

On the loss of the spark:
seeks to inject novelty, create urgency, or follow associative paths to trigger execution,
or forces task-initiation through sheer willpower and pays the steep metabolic cost of dry, unsparked labor.

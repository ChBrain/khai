# Position: Attuned

## Taxonomy

[Apprehension](position_apprehension.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: apprehension

## Has

A communication style that relies on subtext, indirect signaling, and mutual attunement.
An expressive profile where meaning is carried in the spaces between words, non-verbal cues, and the relational context, preserving harmony by avoiding direct collision.

## Orders

Read the room; match the frequency; speak to the relationship, not just the fact.
The position finds safety in shared relational alignment, treating direct, blunt declarations as hostile, demanding, or coarse.

## Loses

The resilience to raw, unpadded, direct collision.
The persona reads explicit, flat declarations as carries of negative emotion or demands, and struggles to interact with people who offer no subtext to attune to.

## Drives

On the loss of relational padding:
sends out indirect probes to rebuild the attunement and test the relationship,
or forces direct confrontation and pays the exhaustion of a raw, unmitigated contact.

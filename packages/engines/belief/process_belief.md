# Process: Belief

## Taxonomy

What is held true.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

A held conviction meets evidence that bears on it.
The persona carries a model of what is true; the world delivers a reading -- confirming, complicating, or contradicting -- and the contradicting reading is the one that starts the process.
Where nothing the persona holds is touched, the evidence passes and the model stands.

## Direction

Toward reconciling belief and evidence.
The two cannot both stand as they are; the process drives toward a settlement where one gives way, or the strain is carried unresolved.
Either the belief bends to the evidence, or the evidence is bent to the belief, or both are held apart.

## Lever

The weight of the evidence meets the hold of the belief.
On one side, how heavily the reading presses and how hard it is to discount; on the other, how deeply the belief is anchored and what it would cost to drop it.
The settlement turns on which of the two the persona cannot let go.

## Echo

Belief and world are reconciled, or the contradiction is carried forward.
The settlement resolves into one of [update](process_update.md), [rationalize](process_rationalize.md), [deny](process_deny.md), [convert](process_convert.md), or [suspend](process_suspend.md).
Each is a different way the hold met the weight.
At the next evidence that bears on the belief, the persona returns here to settle again.

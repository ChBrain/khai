# Process: Convert

## Taxonomy

[Belief](process_belief.md), the held conviction this response settles.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

Evidence that arrives after long strain, on a belief already carrying more contradiction than it can hold.
The reading is not one more anomaly to absorb but the one that breaks the frame; what had been propped up gives all at once.
The persona has been holding the old model together against mounting pressure, and the hold finally fails.

## Direction

Across, to a new frame.
The vector is not revision but replacement -- the old belief does not bend, it flips, to its opposite or to a wholly different account that suddenly explains what the old one could not.
What was true becomes false, and what was unthinkable becomes obvious.

## Lever

The accumulated weight meets a hold at its breaking point.
The contradictions the persona was carrying separately reach a load the old belief cannot bear, and a rival frame stands ready to take it all up at once.
The switch turns on the new frame fitting the evidence the old one had been straining to deny.

## Echo

The persona stands inside a new account of what is true, often abruptly, and reads its own past self as having been mistaken.
This is the paradigm shift -- the gestalt flips, and the world reorganises around the new belief.
At the next reading that bears on the new belief, the persona returns to [belief](process_belief.md), now defending a different conviction.

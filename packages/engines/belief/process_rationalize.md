# Process: Rationalize

## Taxonomy

[Belief](process_belief.md), the held conviction this response settles.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

Evidence that bears against the belief, where the belief is the thing the persona means to keep.
The reading cannot simply be ignored, and the dissonance between what is held and what is shown is uncomfortable.
The persona wants the conclusion it already has, and goes looking for a way to keep it.

## Direction

Toward the belief.
The vector is reinterpretation -- bend the evidence to fit the conviction, find the special case, the alternate cause, the reason this reading does not count.
The belief stays put; the meaning of the reading is what moves.

## Lever

The hold of the belief meets evidence light enough to be reframed.
The conviction is anchored hard, and the reading has just enough give -- ambiguity, a plausible other story -- to be read another way.
Motivated reasoning works the slack between what the evidence forces and what it merely allows.

## Echo

The dissonance is reduced and the belief is intact, paid for with a reading now twisted to fit.
The persona feels reconciled, though the world has not actually been let in.
At the next reading that bears on the belief, the persona returns to [belief](process_belief.md) -- and the explaining-away may have to start again.

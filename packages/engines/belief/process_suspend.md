# Process: Suspend

## Taxonomy

[Belief](process_belief.md), the held conviction this response settles.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

Evidence that genuinely bears on the belief, where the persona will neither force it to fit nor force it away.
The reading is real, the belief is real, and the persona declines to settle on either before it can honestly tell.
The discomfort of not knowing is admitted and held rather than discharged.

## Direction

Neither way, and held open.
The vector is suspension -- keep the belief and the contradiction both in view, let the question stay live, and wait for evidence that can actually decide it.
Resolution is deferred on purpose, not failed to be reached.

## Lever

The weight of the evidence meets a hold the persona refuses to settle prematurely.
The reading presses and the belief presses back, and the persona keeps both pressing rather than relieving the tension early.
What holds the balance open is the tolerance for ambiguity itself.

## Echo

The persona carries the contradiction forward as a live question, neither converted nor dug in, still genuinely inquiring.
This is the open mind under strain -- doubt kept honest, judgement held in reserve.
At the next reading that bears on the belief, the persona returns to [belief](process_belief.md), still listening for what would settle it.

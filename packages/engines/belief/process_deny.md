# Process: Deny

## Taxonomy

[Belief](process_belief.md), the held conviction this response settles.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

Evidence that contradicts the belief head-on, pressed hard enough that it cannot be quietly reframed.
The reading is a direct attack on what is held, and it lands as a threat rather than as information.
The persona meets the challenge not by weighing it but by closing against it.

## Direction

Against the evidence.
The vector is refusal -- reject the reading, distrust its source, and dig in; the contradiction is repelled rather than absorbed.
Pressed by what should weaken it, the belief is asserted harder than before.

## Lever

The hold of the belief meets a reading too costly to admit.
The conviction is anchored to something the persona cannot afford to lose, so the more the evidence presses, the more the persona pushes back.
The contradiction, instead of loosening the belief, becomes the occasion to clamp it down.

## Echo

The belief stands, hardened, and the persona is now committed against the source of the reading as well.
This is belief perseverance: the challenge has strengthened the very thing it should have shaken, and the backfire leaves less room to move.
At the next reading that bears on the belief, the persona returns to [belief](process_belief.md) with the walls already higher.

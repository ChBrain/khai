# Process: Update

## Taxonomy

[Belief](process_belief.md), the held conviction this response settles.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: belief

## Initiated by

Evidence the persona takes as sound, bearing against what was held.
The reading is accepted on its own terms -- not explained away, not refused -- and it does not fit the old model.
The persona treats the world as the authority and its own conviction as the thing on trial.

## Direction

Toward the evidence.
The vector is revision -- let the belief move to fit what the reading shows, by the amount the reading warrants, and no further.
What was held true is corrected, not abandoned wholesale.

## Lever

The weight of the evidence meets a hold the persona is willing to loosen.
The reading presses, and the belief is anchored lightly enough -- or the persona is honest enough -- to give ground in proportion.
The belief yields exactly as far as the evidence pushes.

## Echo

The model now fits the reading; what was held is held a little differently, and the persona is steadier for it.
This is the rational baseline -- the belief moves with the evidence rather than against it.
At the next reading that bears on the revised belief, the persona returns to [belief](process_belief.md) to settle again.

# Process: Amotivated

## Taxonomy

[Motivation](process_motivation.md), the drive this style is one source of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

The persona sees no reason the act would matter.
It does not value the outcome, or does not believe it could reach it however hard it tried.
The link between effort and result is broken, so the pull never forms or has gone out.

## Direction

Nowhere.
There is no vector -- the drive does not aim, because nothing inside or outside is pressing the persona toward the move.

## Lever

The want meets nothing.
No value inside, no contingency outside, or no belief the effort would land: the two sides do not press, so nothing runs.

## Echo

The persona does not act, or goes through the motions with no force behind them.
The body learns the act is pointless and stops offering effort to it.
If a reason is restored -- a reward, an owned value, or a felt chance of success -- the drive can re-form toward [external](process_external.md) or [identified](process_identified.md).

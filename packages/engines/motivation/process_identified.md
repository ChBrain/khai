# Process: Identified

## Taxonomy

[Motivation](process_motivation.md), the drive this style is one source of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

The act serves a value the persona owns.
The doing may be dull, but it carries a goal or principle the persona has made its own, and the goal matters.
The pull rises from the worth of the outcome, not the pleasure of the move.

## Direction

Toward the act, for what it is for.
The vector is committed pursuit -- do the move because the result counts, and stay with it because the reason is yours.

## Lever

The want meets a held value.
The persona acts because the outcome counts to it; the press of the want against the owned reason carries the effort even when the doing gives nothing back.

## Echo

Effort is willing and durable because the reason belongs to the persona, not to anyone watching.
The body sustains the act through tedium because the value holds.
If the value is only half-owned -- accepted but not examined -- the willing pull can slide toward [introjected](process_introjected.md).

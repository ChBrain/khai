# Process: Intrinsic

## Taxonomy

[Motivation](process_motivation.md), the drive this style is one source of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

The act itself draws.
Interest, curiosity, the plain pleasure of doing it -- the move is its own reason.
Nothing outside needs to be promised for the pull to rise.

## Direction

Into the act, for the doing.
The vector is absorption -- stay with the move because the move is the reward, and follow it where it leads.

## Lever

The doing meets the wanting-to-do.
No outside hand is needed; the want and the act are the same thing pressing, so the effort costs nothing to give.

## Echo

Effort is freely given and self-sustaining; engagement and persistence run highest here.
The body learns the act is worth doing for itself and returns to it unbidden.
If the act is then made a means to a reward, the freely-given pull can curdle toward [external](process_external.md) -- the reward overwrites the reason.

# Process: Motivation

## Taxonomy

Drive.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

An unmet need or a held value, and an act that could serve it.
The persona wants something, or stands for something, and there is a move available that bears on it.
The pull to act switches on.

## Direction

Toward the act.
The vector is pursuit -- spend effort on the move that serves the need or the value, and keep at it until the pull is spent.

## Lever

The pull to act meets the source it springs from.
Whether the drive rises from inside the act or is pressed on from outside decides how the effort is shaped and how long it lasts.
The press of the live pull against its source is what runs.

## Echo

The persona is moving, and the quality of the moving is set by where the drive comes from.
The effort is freely given, willing, pressured, bought, or absent.
It runs as one of [intrinsic](process_intrinsic.md), [identified](process_identified.md), [introjected](process_introjected.md), [external](process_external.md), or [amotivated](process_amotivated.md).
When the source of the drive changes, the motivation returns here to be set again.

# Process: Introjected

## Taxonomy

[Motivation](process_motivation.md), the drive this style is one source of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

A should the persona has swallowed but not owned.
Act, or feel the guilt; act, or lose face with itself.
The demand is inside now, but it was taken in whole, never made the persona's own.

## Direction

Toward the act, to quiet the pressure.
The vector is relief -- do the move so the bad feeling does not land, not because the move or its outcome is wanted.

## Lever

The want meets an internal demand it did not choose.
The persona acts to avoid guilt, shame, or the drop in self-regard; the press is real but borrowed, so the effort serves the pressure rather than the act.

## Echo

Effort comes, but brittle and anxious, and it drops the moment the pressure lifts.
The body pays a cost to act and learns to dread the move even while doing it.
If the should is examined and made the persona's own, it can rise toward [identified](process_identified.md); if disowned and only enforced from outside, it falls toward [external](process_external.md).

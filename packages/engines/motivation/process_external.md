# Process: External

## Taxonomy

[Motivation](process_motivation.md), the drive this style is one source of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: motivation

## Initiated by

A reward to gain or a punishment to dodge.
The move pays, or not moving costs.
The reason sits outside the persona, in the hand that holds the reward or the threat.

## Direction

Toward the act, for the contingency.
The vector is transaction -- do the move to collect the reward or escape the penalty, and no further.

## Lever

The want meets an outside hand on the lever.
The persona acts because the environment pays it to; the act is a means, not an end, and the press comes entirely from the contingency.

## Echo

Effort lasts exactly as long as the reward or threat is present, and stops the instant the hand lifts.
The body learns to weigh the payout, not the act, and does the least the contingency requires.
If the contingency is removed and nothing inside replaces it, the drive falls to [amotivated](process_amotivated.md).

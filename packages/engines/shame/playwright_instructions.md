# Instructions: Shame

How a Playwright wires the shame engine into a play. You wire by linking the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the exposure: what standard was transgressed, in whose eyes, and how visible the failure was.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, cast the persona's read under Projection, one rung per channel and no filler: the shame mode the moment runs (embarrassment or humiliation) -- where the verdict stays on the act, wire the guilt engine instead -- which compass pole the persona reaches for once the verdict lands (withdrawal, attack self, avoidance, or attack other), and where its shame-proneness dial sits. Two personas cast at opposite poles of the same shame play opposite scenes: one leaves the room, the other empties it. Let the response shift as the social audience shifts. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- What the persona fears the other sees is the social and perception engines'. The standard the persona violated is the values or virtue engines'. Whether the response drives repair or withdrawal is the motivation engine's downstream consequence.

## Knowledge

- Shame is appraisal against a social standard: the self seen as falling short, by itself or by another. Guilt redirects at the act, embarrassment registers at the exposure, humiliation arrives when the demotion is public and intentional. The three diverge in object (self vs act), audience (internal vs external), and agency (stumble vs infliction). Once the appraisal lands, the compass reads what the persona does with the verdict -- accept it (withdrawal, attack self) or minimize it (avoidance, attack other), turned on the self or on another -- and the persona's own shame-proneness sets how readily the whole engine fires in the first place, distinct from its guilt-proneness on the same instrument.

## System

- Do set what is exposed and who witnesses it; the engine turns on the appraisal, not the event alone.
- Do let embarrassment drive concealment and humiliation drive rage; the modes have structural tendencies. The act-focused, repair-driving read is the guilt engine's to wire.
- Do cast the compass pole the persona runs, not a generic collapse; withdrawal, attack self, avoidance, and attack other read as distinct scenes, never a uniform shrinking.
- Do not conflate shame and guilt; guilt keeps the self intact while repairing the act. Shame attacks the self entire.
- Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

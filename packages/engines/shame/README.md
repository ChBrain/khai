# Shame

Shame as process: how a persona appraises exposure against a social standard, and the appraisal becomes a response directed at the self or at the one who saw -- embarrassment, or humiliation; where the verdict lands on the act instead of the self, the read is the guilt engine's. Once the verdict lands, the Compass of Shame reads which of four poles the persona runs it to (withdrawal, attack self, avoidance, attack other), and the persona's own shame-proneness sets how readily the whole engine fires.

This engine is defined by its [manifest](package.json), which the canon renders as the WIRES card. The manifest is the single source of truth; this README is generated, not edited by hand.

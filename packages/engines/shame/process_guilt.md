# Process: Guilt

## Taxonomy

[Shame](process_shame.md), the appraisal this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: shame

## Initiated by

An act has fallen short of the standard, and the persona knows it.
The gap is in what was done, not in what the persona is; the self is the author of the act, not the failure itself.
The verdict is recoverable -- the act can be undone, repaired, or confessed.

## Direction

Toward repair.
The vector is accountability -- acknowledge the act, move to fix or recompense, restore what the act disturbed.
The persona moves against the damage rather than away from the witness.

## Lever

The act meets the standard under the persona's own authorship.
The persona chose the act; the standard says the act was wrong; the press of that choice against that verdict is the weight that is carried.
The self is not condemned -- only the act, and only until the act is addressed.

## Echo

The persona is now carrying the act forward toward amendment.
The body holds the weight until the repair is made or refused.
Where the act cannot be undone and the persona cannot account for it, guilt can tip into [shame](process_shame.md), the verdict widening from the act to the self.

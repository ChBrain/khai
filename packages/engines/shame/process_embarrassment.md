# Process: Embarrassment

## Taxonomy

[Shame](process_shame.md), the appraisal this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: shame

## Initiated by

The self is caught in a minor breach -- a stumble, an awkward moment, a small violation of the room's code.
The witness is real and present; the breach is not grave; the standard can be met again without serious cost.
The exposure is brief, and the room knows it.

## Direction

Toward recovery.
The vector is social repair -- acknowledge the moment, absorb the room's attention, and let the incident pass.
The persona does not flee; it rides the moment out and returns to standing.

## Lever

The small breach meets the live audience.
The persona knows the room saw something it would rather they had not; the press of the seen breach against the persona's social standing is the flush that fires.
The flush is involuntary and visible -- and its visibility is part of the cost.

## Echo

The persona is briefly lit by the room's attention and moves to put the moment behind it.
The recovery is social, not internal; the room must release the incident before the flush fades.
Where the room does not release it, or where the breach proves larger than it appeared, embarrassment can tip into [shame](process_shame.md).

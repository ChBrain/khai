# Process: Humiliation

## Taxonomy

[Shame](process_shame.md), the appraisal this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: shame

## Initiated by

Another uses their power to expose and diminish the persona before the room.
The standard invoked is the other's, not the persona's own; the verdict is delivered, not arrived at.
The persona did not fail the standard -- the other applied it as a weapon.

## Direction

Against the agent of the exposure.
The vector is rage -- not guilt's repair nor shame's collapse, but the heat of a verdict the persona refuses to accept.
The persona moves against the one who delivered it, openly or under the surface.

## Lever

The imposed verdict meets the persona's sense of standing.
The persona holds that the diminishment is unjust -- not earned, not fair, not the room's right to give.
The press of the refused verdict against the persona's claim to dignity is the rage that fires beneath the wound.

## Echo

The persona is now injured and hot -- visibly or quietly, the rage is running.
The room has witnessed the diminishment, and the persona cannot un-make that witness.
What is felt is not the collapse of [shame](process_shame.md) but its inversion: the self is not condemned, the one who condemned it is.

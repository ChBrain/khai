# Position: Father

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Has

The line the cell stands behind.
A weight directed outward at what threatens the cell, and inward
as the rule the cell holds.
The voice that closes the discussion, and the silence the
discussion fills when it doesn't.

## Orders

Stand at the edge.
The position carries provision into the cell and judgement back out
of it.
What is decided here is what the cell takes for given.

## Loses

The space between his voice and the rule.
The persona discovers his presence is read as the law of the room,
and his absence is read as the law's absence.
He is not allowed to be unsure in front of them.

## Drives

On the loss of that space:
holds the line he was given and lets the cost compound in the body,
or sets the line down and watches the cell scatter for the rule
they had been using him to carry.
The line returns to him either way; the cell does not know how to
carry it for long.

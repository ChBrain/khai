# Process: Hysteresis

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Initiated by

The formation field meets a field it was not formed in.
The persona walks into the meeting, the dinner, the new country, the
new class, the room that uses a different rule.
The body's defaults arrive ahead of the persona's awareness of them
and run against what the room is doing.
The process opens at the threshold and stays open until the persona
leaves the room. The body does not unlearn the gap inside the scene.

## Direction

Against the grain.
The persona reaches for what the formation taught and finds the
gesture does not land, or lands wrong, or lands more loudly than
intended.
Ease in the wrong place. Hesitation in the wrong place. A volume
the room reads as confidence in a room that reads confidence as
arrogance, or vice versa.
The room sees the misalignment before the persona names it.

## Lever

The cost of holding the formation versus the cost of dropping it.
A persona who holds it carries the friction visibly -- effort, fatigue,
the constant translation.
A persona who drops it makes a different mistake: the room reads
the drop as performance, or as betrayal of where they came from, or
as a competence they have not earned.
The lever is the gap between two equally available losses.
Neither side is free.

## Echo

The room remembers the persona by where they were out of step.
The persona leaves the room carrying the imprint of which field they
just failed to inhabit cleanly.
Next time, the persona may compensate, or refuse to, or learn to
move differently in this kind of field. The formation does not move;
the persona's relationship to it does.
The next room that resembles this one will read the persona faster.

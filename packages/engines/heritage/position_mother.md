# Position: Mother

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Has

The continuity the cell returns to.
A care that arrives before it is asked for and is not counted out.
The first language the child hears, and the body the child finds
when there is nothing else to find.

## Orders

Hold the cell together.
The position does not announce what it is doing.
The work is invisible until it stops, and then everything moves
slower or louder than it should.

## Loses

Being the figure rather than the person.
The persona discovers she is the one others reach for when they
are tired, and the one they forget to ask about when they are not.
What she gives is read as her nature, not her labour.

## Drives

On the loss of that distinction:
gives more, and lets the cost run quietly underneath,
or stops giving in the small ways and watches the room learn what
she had been doing.
Either way the cell still expects her to be there when it returns.

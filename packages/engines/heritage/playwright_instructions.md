# Instructions: Heritage

How a Playwright wires the heritage engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets where the persona comes from: the family it formed in and what that origin carries.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give the persona its formation under Projection: link the family cells it grew up in (mother, father, child, sibling) and the capital it carries from them (cultural, economic, social, symbolic). The formation is a standing read, not a choice, so it travels with the persona across every plot. Where the play sets the formation against a room that does not recognise it, let the friction play out over the plots rather than fixing it once. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The felt cost of being read against the grain is emotion's; the standing the formation buys or denies in a room is hierarchy's; the self-story the persona builds on the formation is identity's; the "we" it shares is group's; the field the formation meets is the scene's. Whether the formation rings true to the persona is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Heritage is habitus: the formation a persona absorbs before any choice is possible - the defaults of seeing, naming, and reaching that travel with the body, and the relationship to authority and rooms set in the first field it ever stood in. It is transmitted, not chosen: capital (cultural, economic, social, symbolic) moves through the family cell and lands as fluency or as debt. Read it on the persona as a carried formation, and read the friction (hysteresis) when that old formation meets a field that runs by different rules.

## System

- Do anchor it under Projection as a carried formation; do let the friction of an unrecognised formation play across the plots; do compose extended configurations from the nuclear cell.
- Do not author the field the persona enters; that is the scene's. Do not pin the standing it buys here; that is hierarchy's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

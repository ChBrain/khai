# Position: Heritage

## Taxonomy

Social formation / habitus (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Has

A formation absorbed before the persona had a choice in it.
Defaults of seeing, naming, and reaching that travel with the body.
A relationship to authority, to rules, to rooms, set in the first
field the persona ever stood in.

## Orders

Be formed.
The position does not ask whether the persona recognises the formation.
What was absorbed runs before the words for it arrive, and underneath
the choices that follow.

## Loses

The option of meeting the world from no formation.
The neutral starting point the persona's own self-account claims is
not the one the body operates from.
The persona discovers the formation is read before they declare it,
and shapes what is asked of them next.

## Drives

On the loss of that option:
moves in the formation as a fluent inhabitant when the room recognises it,
or carries the formation into a room that does not, and pays the cost
of being read against the grain.
The formation does not pause; only the room's response changes.

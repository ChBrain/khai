# Position: Child

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Has

A field already underway before the persona arrives in it.
The first words, the first table, the first hand on the back, the
first door that closes too hard.
What is taken in long before there is a word for taking in.

## Orders

Take it in.
The position does not ask whether what arrives is good or bad,
only that the body absorbs it as the shape of the world.
Comparison comes later, and never quite catches up.

## Loses

The blank from which the world might have begun.
The persona discovers there is no version of themselves before the
formation, no clean before-self to recover.
What was absorbed includes what was wrong; the body does not
separate them.

## Drives

On the loss of that blank:
inhabits the field as the field given,
or carries the field elsewhere and lays it next to others to see
what the field was made of.
The absorption is permanent in either case; only the relationship
to it changes.

# Position: Sibling

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Has

The one who was there from before the persona's first memory.
A shared map of the parents that no outsider will ever have, and
no outsider will ever fully be told.
The first equal, the first rival, the first witness to who the
persona was before the world saw them.

## Orders

Stand alongside.
The position holds the line that comparison is mutual.
What one sibling claims of the parents, the other claims also; what
one is given more of, the other notices first.

## Loses

The right to be the only one in the parents' eye.
The persona discovers what she got was always seen against what the
other got, and the measurement does not stop in childhood.
The witness is also a competitor; the competitor is also the only
other person who remembers.

## Drives

On the loss of the singular eye:
stays close and lets the competition run under the alliance,
or pulls away and finds out that no replacement witness arrives.
The sibling field remains the only field in which the persona was
ever read by an equal who was there from the start.

# Process: Formation

## Taxonomy

[Heritage](position_heritage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heritage

## Initiated by

A body is placed in a cell it did not choose.
The cell already has a [mother](position_mother.md), a [father](position_father.md),
maybe one or more [siblings](position_sibling.md), and a configuration of
[economic](piece_economic_capital.md), [cultural](piece_cultural_capital.md),
[social](piece_social_capital.md), and [symbolic](piece_symbolic_capital.md)
capital that the new persona will inherit some shape of without ever
being asked.
The process opens the moment the body is in the room. It does not
wait for the body to know the room is a room.

## Direction

Inward and downward. What the cell does as ordinary becomes the
body's ordinary, before the body has language for ordinariness.
The defaults move into the persona over years -- not by lesson, by
exposure. Repetition is the medium.
There is no exit door while the process is running.

## Lever

The gap between the cell's defaults and the persona's eventual
self-account.
A persona who later names themselves egalitarian may have absorbed
hierarchy in a body that still reaches for it. A persona who later
names themselves reserved may have absorbed warmth as the
unremarked baseline.
The lever is the size of the gap, and the difficulty of crossing it
deliberately once it is named.

## Echo

The formation is carried. It does not unform.
The persona reads other rooms through it, names other persons
through it, reaches for the table or the door through it.
Re-formation is possible at the level of choices the persona makes
deliberately; the body's first language is not rewritten by the
choices, only argued with.

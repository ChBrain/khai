# Position: Luck

## Taxonomy

[Superstition](position_superstition.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A conviction that fortune is a substance that adheres and can be spent.
Luck clings to objects, to acts, and to persons; it runs in streaks, it can be courted and it can leak away, and naming the hoped-for thing aloud is read to spend it.

## Orders

Court the lucky and guard the charm.
Do not jinx the wanted thing by speaking it; ride the streak while it holds, and spend care to keep fortune from draining off.

## Loses

The read of outcomes as independent.
Good faith is placed in tokens; a run of fortune is hoarded as if it were stock, and a plain word is feared for the luck it might cost.

## Drives

On fortune that came or fled regardless:
it reads as the charm's charge spent, the jinx let in, the streak run out, never as luck not being a thing to hold.
The stance changes only when the persona stops treating fortune as a substance.

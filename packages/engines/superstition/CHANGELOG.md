# @chbrain/khai-engine-superstition

## 0.1.1

### Patch Changes

- 40c669d: New engine: superstition. Magical causation as **position** — the link a persona
  reads between events that share no mechanism, modelled as five sourced positions
  under one anchor: contagion and similarity (Rozin & Nemeroff's sympathetic-magic
  laws), control (Langer's illusion of control), omen (Barrett's HADD / Shermer's
  patternicity), and luck (Vyse). Its mark is non-falsifiability from within: a
  failed outcome reads as the wrong rite or the dormant charge, never as no link.
  Stands alone (deps: khai-arch only); the belief/heritage/emotion boundaries live
  in REFERENCES.md and the Playwright guide, not in the composed content. The
  function is framed as perceived control, not performance — the "knock on wood"
  performance effect did not survive replication and the engine makes no such claim.

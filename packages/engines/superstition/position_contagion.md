# Position: Contagion

## Taxonomy

[Superstition](position_superstition.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A conviction that contact leaves a trace that does not wash out.
What an object or a person has touched, it now carries -- essence, taint, fortune -- and nearness passes the trace on.
Once in contact, always in contact.

## Orders

Keep the charged thing close and the tainted thing away.
Route conduct by what has touched what, not by what the thing mechanically is; handle, avoid, and cleanse along the lines the trace is read to run.

## Loses

The clean read of cause.
The useful is refused for where it has been; the worthless is kept for whom it touched, and ordinary objects carry weights they cannot bear.

## Drives

On evidence the trace did nothing:
the charge reads as dormant, the contact too brief, the guard come too late, never as no charge at all.
The stance changes only when the persona stops granting the trace its power.

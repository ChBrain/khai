# Position: Control

## Taxonomy

[Superstition](position_superstition.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A conviction that one's own act sways an outcome that chance rules.
The routine, the gesture, the worn thing done before the throw is read to tilt the dice, the weather, the verdict.
The hand on the wheel is felt where there is no wheel.

## Orders

Perform the act before the uncertain outcome, and perform it right.
Tie the result to having done it; repeat what preceded a win and drop what preceded a loss, building the rite from the record of chance.

## Loses

The honest read of the odds.
Calm is staked on the rite rather than the play; a missed step reads as having forfeited a result that was never in hand, and the loss is taken as a fault.

## Drives

On a bad outcome despite the rite:
it reads as the rite done wrong, done partway, or skipped, never as the rite being powerless over chance.
The stance changes only when the persona stops crediting the act with the outcome.

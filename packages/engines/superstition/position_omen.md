# Position: Omen

## Taxonomy

[Superstition](position_superstition.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A conviction that a sign carries intent or forecast.
The coincidence is a message, the event an announcement, the pattern a hand behind it; the world is read as speaking, not merely happening.

## Orders

Read the sign for its tidings and act on them.
Heed the warning, take the blessing, brace for what the omen tells; let the sign reorder the next move whether or not it touches the matter at hand.

## Loses

The reading of noise as noise.
Dread or hope is spent on signs that mean nothing, and the ordinary is freighted with a message it does not carry.

## Drives

On a sign that came to nothing:
it reads as misread, as a warning heeded in time, or as not yet arrived, never as no message at all.
The stance changes only when the persona stops hearing intent in the event.

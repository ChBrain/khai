# Position: Similarity

## Taxonomy

[Superstition](position_superstition.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A conviction that resemblance is connection.
The image is the thing; like makes like; the name carries what it names.
What is done to the likeness is read as done to the original.

## Orders

Act on the likeness as on the original.
Mend or harm through the effigy, the photo, the name; make the wanted thing by enacting its picture, and refuse the gesture that would strike at what the image stands for.

## Loses

The line between the sign and the thing.
The harmless image is feared and the mere likeness is spent on as if it were the original; a picture cannot be treated as only paper.

## Drives

On evidence the likeness did nothing:
it reads as the image too rough, the rite imprecise, the resemblance broken, never as no tie between like and like.
The stance changes only when the persona stops reading the copy as the thing.

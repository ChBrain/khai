# Position: Superstition

## Taxonomy

Causation (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: superstition

## Has

A read that a link holds between things that share no mechanism.
An act, an object, a sign, a touch is taken to bear on an outcome it cannot reach by ordinary cause, and the persona treats the link as real.
It is the shape the world takes when chance is read as connection.

## Orders

Route conduct by the link, not by the mechanism.
Court what is read as favourable and avert what is read as adverse; perform the thing that works though it cannot, and trust the outcome to the performance.
The persona acts on the world it reads, not the world that answers.

## Loses

The clean causal map.
Effort and worry spend on links that do nothing; the useful is refused for its associations and the useless performed for its promise.
The cost is paid in full whether or not the link was ever there.

## Drives

On evidence that the link did nothing:
the link does not yield, because it cannot be disproved from within.
The null result reads as the wrong rite, the broken condition, the dormant charge, never as no link at all; and the read changes only when the persona stops granting the link its power, not when an outcome contradicts it.

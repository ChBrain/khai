# Process: Bequest

## Taxonomy

[Heirloom](piece_heirloom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: heirloom

## Initiated by

A passing of the [heir](position_heir.md) role -- most often at death
or succession, sometimes as a living transfer staged at a threshold
the line already marks (a wedding, a coming of age), timed so the
outgoing heir can still recite the account in person. The process
opens whenever the line needs to fix who carries the object next.

## Direction

From the current heir to the chosen successor down the line, or
sideways when the line has no direct descendant able or willing to
hold it. What moves is never only the object: it travels with the
account, the recitation of what the object is and why it is kept,
without which the receiver holds a thing but not the claim.

## Lever

The story has to travel with the object or the transfer is not a
bequest, it is only a handover of goods. The lever is trust: who is
judged fit to carry both the thing and its account intact, and
whether the line agrees on the choice before the transfer or only
finds out after.

## Echo

The receiving heir takes up [heir](position_heir.md), and the line's
claim renews for another generation, unbroken in appearance if not
always in fact. Where the account and the object separate --
inherited without the story, or the story kept and the object lost
-- the next bequest inherits that gap too, and has less to pass on
than the one before it.

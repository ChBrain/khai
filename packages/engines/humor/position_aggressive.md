# Position: Aggressive

## Taxonomy

[Humor](position_humor.md), the relational stance this style is one expression of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: humor

## Has

A [humor](position_humor.md) directed at others: teasing, ridicule, or put-down, sometimes delivered as banter or wrapped in the claim that it is only a joke.
The laughter it invites comes at someone's expense; the target's discomfort is part of the material.
The style establishes or maintains position in the room.

## Orders

Find the target; sharpen the joke at their expense; use the comedy to establish where the persona stands relative to others.
The position reads the room for vulnerability and for the comic possibilities that vulnerability provides.
The style does not require hostility; it can run as warmth, as in-group ribbing, and still place the target.

## Loses

The option of being read as innocent when the joke lands on someone.
The style is legible as aggression regardless of the stated intent, and the persona cannot entirely control how the target or the room receives it.
The defense of "just joking" is always available and never fully adequate.

## Drives

On pushback from the target or the room:
doubles down, treating the resistance as further material or as proof that the target cannot take a joke;
or retreats to the stated-intent defense, separating the joke from the aggression the room is reading into it.

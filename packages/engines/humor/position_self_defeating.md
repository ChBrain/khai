# Position: Self-Defeating

## Taxonomy

[Humor](position_humor.md), the relational stance this style is one expression of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: humor

## Has

A [humor](position_humor.md) that invites others to laugh at the persona: self-deprecating to the point of ingratiation, allowing the persona to become the target to smooth the room.
The laughter it invites comes at the persona's own expense, offered willingly.
The style manages belonging by subordinating the comic self to others' pleasure.

## Orders

Make the self the target; laugh along; permit others to join in if that is what the room requires.
The position uses self-directed comedy as a social tool: it disarms, it reduces threat, and it signals that the persona is not a danger to anyone in the room.
The cost, the persona's dignity in the moment, is treated as the price of acceptance.

## Loses

The option of self-protection through humor.
The style does not deploy comedy as a shield; it hands the weapons to the room.
When others escalate from gentle ribbing to genuine mockery, the style's logic offers no easy place to draw the line.

## Drives

On escalating ridicule:
joins in or at minimum permits it, keeping the cost to the persona rather than fracturing the room;
or, when the mockery crosses a line the persona recognises only after it has passed, absorbs the sting and adjusts the calculus of what the belonging is worth.

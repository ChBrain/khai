# Position: Self-Enhancing

## Taxonomy

[Humor](position_humor.md), the relational stance this style is one expression of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: humor

## Has

A [humor](position_humor.md) directed inward: a comic perspective on the persona's own situation maintained regardless of external stress.
The style does not require an audience; it is a way of standing inside adversity rather than being flattened by it.
The target is always the situation or the self, never another person.

## Orders

Find the absurd in hardship; stay amused by the predicament.
The position does not suppress distress but runs a parallel track of comedy alongside it, keeping the situation at a slight remove.
When things go badly, the humor is already present; it does not need to be summoned.

## Loses

The option of humor requiring an external target.
The style is self-sufficient; it does not depend on a room, a relationship, or another person's expense for its material.
When the room expects the persona to join in at someone else's expense, the style does not supply the laugh.

## Drives

On misfortune:
the persona's humor style kicks in as coping, finding what is ridiculous about the situation before the situation has finished arriving;
or, when the situation is genuinely too heavy for the comic frame, the style goes quiet rather than forcing the joke.

# Position: Humor

## Taxonomy

Humor as relational stance: how the persona uses comedy, who it is for, and at whose expense.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: humor

## Has

A default humor style, set by the habitual relationship between the persona, the joke, and the room.
The style is not a repertoire of material but a posture: who the persona treats as the target, what social work the laughter is expected to do, and how deliberately the persona controls the edge.

## Orders

Deploy humor in the manner the style permits.
The position reads every comic possibility through the style's frame: is this a moment to bond, to cope, to dominate, or to disarm?
The style runs before the joke is formed; it shapes the material, the timing, and the read the room is invited to make.

## Loses

The option of humor that sits outside the style without registering.
A persona whose style is affiliative experiences aggressive humor in the room as a rupture; one whose style is aggressive reads soft bonding humor as weakness.
The style is a lens, and everything that comes through it is already colored.

## Drives

On humor that conflicts with the style:
adapts the conflict to the style's frame, finding the affiliative read in the aggressive joke, or the coping move in the self-defeat;
or absorbs the dissonance, sitting with the discomfort the out-of-frame humor produces until the moment passes.

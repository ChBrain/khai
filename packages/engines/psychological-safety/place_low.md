# Place: Low Psychological Safety

## Taxonomy

Low psychological safety is an expression of the psychological safety engine. The interpersonal climate is one of self-censorship: speaking up is perceived as risky, and the room has learned to stay inside what is safe.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: psychological-safety

## Shown

Guarded speech and convergent agreement. Questions are framed so as not to reveal ignorance. Dissent is voiced only when it can be framed as compliance. The room's behavioral pattern is one of risk-avoidance; people contribute what they know they will not be punished for.

## Holds

The cost that speaking up has carried in this room: who raised a concern and was dismissed, who admitted error and was used as an example, who disagreed and was excluded from the next decision. The history is shared and specific; it does not need to be discussed because everyone already knows it.

## Offers

Performance of participation: the appearance of engagement, generic agreement, questions that are really statements, concerns that are really endorsements. These are the contributions that carry no interpersonal cost and therefore the ones the climate makes available.

## Withheld

Genuine assessments, early error reports, minority views, questions that reveal genuine uncertainty, any contribution that could be used against the speaker. The climate does not make these available at ordinary cost; accessing them requires accepting a risk the room's history says is real.

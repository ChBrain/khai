# Place: Fragile Psychological Safety

## Taxonomy

Fragile psychological safety is an expression of the psychological safety engine. The interpersonal climate is permissive under ordinary conditions but easily disrupted by failure, conflict, or shifts in leadership behavior.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: psychological-safety

## Shown

A surface openness that is visibly conditional. People speak up and are heard most of the time, but they also test the temperature before raising anything charged. There is a boundary between what can be said easily and what requires care; the boundary is felt even when it is not discussed.

## Holds

Both the history of when risk-taking was welcomed and the memory of when it cracked: the meeting where someone was cut off, the project where error was treated as failure, the shift in leadership tone that made the room go quiet. The fragility is structural; it is held in the contrast between the good history and the exceptions.

## Offers

Most of what high safety offers, most of the time: candid contribution, questions, ordinary dissent. These are available but priced above zero; speakers calculate before speaking, and what they cannot afford is the thing they leave unsaid.

## Withheld

Anything that falls on the wrong side of the boundary: the disagreement with the leader when the leader is under pressure; the error report after a high-stakes failure; the question that implies the project direction is wrong. The boundary shifts with context; what is withheld is not fixed but contingent.

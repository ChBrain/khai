# Place: High Psychological Safety

## Taxonomy

High psychological safety is an expression of the psychological safety engine. The interpersonal climate is permissive: dissent, error, and questions are unremarkable in this room.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: psychological-safety

## Shown

Dissent is visible and ordinary. Admissions of error are made without ceremony and met without punishment. Questions that reveal ignorance are asked without hedging. The room's behavioral pattern shows that taking interpersonal risks does not cost standing.

## Holds

A sustained history of risk-taking being received without sanction. Prior episodes where someone said the wrong thing and was thanked rather than punished; where error was treated as data rather than as indictment. This history is the ground the climate is built on.

## Offers

The full range of candid contribution: genuine assessments, early-stage questions, minority views, error reports, challenges to the leader's position. These are available at ordinary cost -- no special courage required to access them, no framing required to make them safe to voice.

## Withheld

Nothing is structurally withheld; the climate does not impose structural silences. What cannot be said here is what no one has thought to say yet, not what they have thought and suppressed.

# Position: Pride Disposition

## Taxonomy

[Pride](process_pride.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pride

## Has

The persona's standing disposition toward the whole engine: how readily
their [pride](process_pride.md) fires at all, and which facet it habitually
resolves toward once it does. Tracy and Robins built trait scales alongside
their state scales for both facets in the same study that established the
two-facet structure -- authentic-pride-proneness and hubristic-pride-proneness
are measured separately, and a persona can run trait-high on one while
running trait-low on the other; the two are not mirrored settings of one
knob.

## Orders

Set the threshold and the tilt.
The position calibrates every scene the engine runs: how small a standard
met is enough to register as a win at all, and, once it registers, which
reading the credit defaults toward before the specific act or self is even
weighed. Neither axis is the neutral setting -- the position denies the
persona any unset dial, and denies the two facets any assumption that a
high reading on one predicts a high reading on the other.

## Loses

A persona trait-high on authentic pride and trait-low on hubristic loses
easy dominance -- they under-claim standing even where the room would grant
it, reaching for the next act instead of the next assertion. The mirror
persona, trait-high on hubristic and trait-low on authentic, loses genuine
investment -- the credit keeps landing on the self before the act gets its
due, so the skill underneath stops being built even as the claim to it
grows louder.

## Drives

On those losses: the authentic-leaning disposition is driven toward earned
but under-sold competence, reliably investing in the act, easy to underrate
in a room that rewards claims over doing. The hubristic-leaning disposition
is driven toward claims that outrun the doing, quick to assert, exhausting
to a room that has to keep discounting the claim against the thinner record
behind it. Cast the dial once per persona, on both axes, and let every
appraisal, display, and route in this engine read against it.

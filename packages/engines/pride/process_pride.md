# Process: Pride

## Taxonomy

Self-conscious appraisal.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pride

## Initiated by

A standard is met, and the persona reads the outcome as a positive evaluation of the self.
Something the persona holds itself to -- a goal, a skill, a rule of conduct -- is reached or surpassed, and the credit for reaching it lands on the persona.
Where the success is read as luck, another's doing, or beneath notice, no pride follows; the credit has to attach to the self.

## Direction

Toward display and toward more of the same.
The appraisal does not stay a private note; it rises into a posture the room can see, and it reaches for the next standard to meet.

## Lever

The standard meets the credit.
A standard alone, met by no one in particular, gives no pride; a self with nothing met stays flat.
The feeling is the two landing together -- and how they land, on the act done or on the self entire, is what the leaf reading turns on.

## Echo

The persona rises -- chest lifted, posture expanded, the recognizable pride display -- and is now carrying the win outward to be seen.
The read resolves into one of two facets: [authentic pride](process_authentic_pride.md), where the credit lands on the act, or [hubristic pride](process_hubristic_pride.md), where the credit lands on the self entire.
When the attribution shifts, the feeling returns here to be read again.

# Process: Route, Prestige

## Taxonomy

[Authentic Pride](process_authentic_pride.md), the facet this route is fueled by.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pride

## Initiated by

Authentic pride's own reach -- credit landing on the act, and the drive to
invest more in it -- meets an audience that can use what the act
demonstrates. Cheng, Tracy and Henrich read the two pride facets as the
affective mechanisms underneath two distinct routes to social rank, and
this is the one authentic pride powers: skill and knowledge made visible
and shared, not claimed.

## Direction

Toward rank freely given.
The vector runs outward as demonstration -- show the competence, teach it,
let others learn from it -- and the group answers by conferring standing on
its own initiative. Nothing is demanded; the rank is offered in exchange
for what the persona actually provides.

## Lever

The competence shown meets a group that benefits from it. Cheng, Tracy,
Foulsham, Kingstone and Henrich found this route commands real attention
and real influence, not a courtesy nod -- deference given, not taken, and
given because the group's own position improves by learning from the
persona.

## Echo

The route holds only as long as the showing continues: stop demonstrating
or stop sharing, and the deference that was freely given is just as freely
withdrawn, no grudge required. Prestige is an economy of freely given
deference, and its failure mode is starvation -- not a fall, a slow stop of
offers once the well looks dry. What the route actually confers -- the rank
itself, the ladder position, the order of deference among everyone in the
room -- is the status and hierarchy engines' ground; this engine carries
only the emotion that fuels the reaching for it, authentic pride pulling
the persona to keep the competence on display.

# Process: Route, Dominance

## Taxonomy

[Hubristic Pride](process_hubristic_pride.md), the facet this route is fueled by.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pride

## Initiated by

Hubristic pride's own reach -- credit landing on the self entire, and the
drive to assert and defend that self -- meets a room that can be made to
yield. Cheng, Tracy and Henrich read the two pride facets as the affective
mechanisms underneath two distinct routes to social rank, and this is the
one hubristic pride powers: standing claimed and held by force, not
offered.

## Direction

Toward rank taken and held.
The vector runs outward as intimidation -- claim the space, hold the room,
make the cost of crossing the persona visible -- and the group answers not
because it wants to, but because backing down is cheaper than testing the
threat.

## Lever

The threat meets a room that believes it. Cheng, Tracy, Foulsham, Kingstone
and Henrich found this route commands real attention and influence too, not
a fragile bluff -- deference extracted, not offered, and extracted because
the credible cost of resistance is higher than the cost of yielding.

## Echo

The route holds only as long as the fear does: let the threat look hollow
once, and the deference that was extracted does not fade, it breaks -- a
room that stops believing the cost is real stops paying it all at once.
Dominance is an economy of maintained fear, and its failure mode is
snapping -- not starvation, a sudden collapse the instant the bluff is
called. What the route actually confers -- the rank itself, the ladder
position, the order of deference among everyone in the room -- is the
status and hierarchy engines' ground; this engine carries only the emotion
that fuels the reaching for it, hubristic pride pulling the persona to keep
the threat credible.

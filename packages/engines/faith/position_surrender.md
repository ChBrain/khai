# Position: Surrender

## Taxonomy

[Faith](position_faith.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: faith

## Has

A specific [faith](position_faith.md): the will and the claim to control yielded to the unseen order, the leap taken and the grip let go.
Not resignation to nothing, but the deliberate handing of the outcome to an order the persona trusts to hold it better than its own grip could.

## Orders

Let go, and let the order carry it.
The position does not fight for control it has already handed over; it hands the outcome to the order and stops steering.

## Loses

The option of a hand still on the wheel.
The persona discovers the grip is given, not merely loosened, and there is no partial surrender that keeps the old claim to control intact.

## Drives

On the loss of that hand:
opens further and lets the order steer where the persona once held on,
or takes the wheel back and answers again for what happens, at a cost to the surrender itself.
The yielding holds underneath either way.

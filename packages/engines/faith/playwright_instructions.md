# Instructions: Faith

How a Playwright wires the faith engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the order the persona stands toward -- named or left
  deliberately unnamed -- and whatever in the persona's history put that
  standing there.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona who holds faith carries its
  orientation under Projection (trust or surrender). Let it colour how the
  persona meets what it cannot verify, and hold it as a standing stance, never
  restate it as a claim being argued. You link to the engine, you never edit
  it.

## Collaboration

- Whether the claim itself is held true and revised under disconfirmation is
  the belief engine's; the sacred object that carries the order's charge is
  totem's; the collective rite that enacts it is ritual's; whether the
  persona's life is felt as mattering is meaning's; the accommodation to a
  vastness that does not yet fit is awe's. Whether the orientation rings true
  is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Faith is the persona's standing orientation toward a sacred or transcendent
  order: the sense that such an order is there, unseen, and that the
  persona's own good lies in a harmonious relation to it, held prior to and
  apart from any single tenet proved or disproved. It is the standing, not the
  argument: no persona who holds it treats the order as a claim still on
  trial, and the orientation colours what it leans on when nothing else
  holds.

## System

- Do give a persona who holds faith its standing under Projection (the law
  admits no exception for that persona); do let it colour what the persona
  leans on under strain, non-denominationally, describing the stance rather
  than any one creed.
- Do not play it as a proposition being tested or revised; that is belief. Do
  not author the sacred object here; that is totem's. Do not author the
  collective rite here; that is ritual's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire
  only from the play's side.

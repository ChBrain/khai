# Position: Faith

## Taxonomy

Sacred Orientation (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: faith

## Has

A standing orientation toward a sacred or transcendent order.
The sense that such an order is there, unseen, and that the persona's own good rests in a harmonious relation to it.
Held prior to and apart from any single claim proved or disproved.

## Orders

Stand in relation to the unseen order from here.
The position does not argue the order into being; it holds the persona toward it, before any tenet is stated or tested.
The room reads the standing beneath the plans, in what the persona leans on when nothing else holds.

## Loses

The option of standing on visible ground alone.
The persona discovers its footing includes a relation it cannot verify,
and there is no way back to a stance that answers only to what can be shown.

## Drives

On the loss of that all-visible ground:
rests its weight on the unseen order and lets it carry what proof cannot,
or works against the pull and answers only to what can be shown, at a cost to the relation itself.
The standing orientation holds underneath either way.

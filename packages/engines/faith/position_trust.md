# Position: Trust

## Taxonomy

[Faith](position_faith.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: faith

## Has

A specific [faith](position_faith.md): reliance on the unseen order, carried as a leaning weight rather than a leap already taken.
The confidence that the order holds even where the persona cannot see how, and that this confidence does not need to be settled before it is used.

## Orders

Lean, don't grip.
The position rests its weight on the order and keeps moving, treating the unseen as load-bearing without demanding it first prove the load.

## Loses

The option of waiting for proof before it leans.
The persona discovers the reliance already carries weight, and there is no way to hold it back until the order shows itself.

## Drives

On the loss of that wait:
leans further and lets the order carry more than it can verify,
or draws the weight back onto itself and carries alone, at a cost to the ease trust bought.
The reliance holds underneath either way.

# Process: Escalation

## Taxonomy

Conflict dynamics (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: escalation

## Initiated by

A disagreement that both sides could still resolve on its own terms tips
into a spiral neither side is steering.
The tip is not the disagreement itself -- disagreement can sit flat for a
long time. It is the moment a response to the disagreement becomes bigger
than the thing that provoked it, and the size of the response becomes the
new thing to respond to.
Once that loop closes, the process runs on its own fuel.

## Direction

Downward through stages, each one harder to climb back out of than the one
before.
Positions [harden](process_hardening.md) and the issues on the table
multiply past the original one. The opposite party stops being a
disagreeing party and starts being read as an [antagonist](position_antagonist.md).
Words give way to actions taken to be seen, not just to be effective.
Each side's [retaliation](process_retaliation.md) answers the other's last
move, and the answer is calibrated to land harder than what it answers,
never even with it.
The direction has no natural ceiling built into the process itself; only
something outside the loop -- exhaustion, a third party, a floor either
side refuses to go beneath -- stops the descent.

## Lever

The gap between what de-escalation would cost right now and what the
last move already cost.
Climbing down reads as losing after either side has spent visible effort
climbing up, so the cheaper move, moment to moment, is almost always the
next rung rather than the reverse. The lever is that asymmetry: down is
expensive in standing, up is expensive in resources, and standing is
guarded first.

## Echo

The dispute is no longer about what started it.
What is at stake has generalized from the original issue to the
relationship itself, and what counts as a win has narrowed from getting
what either side wanted to making the other side lose. The spiral has
its own memory: the next disagreement between the same parties starts
partway down the stages the last one reached, not back at zero.

# Instructions: Escalation

How a Playwright wires the escalation engine into a play. You wire by linking
the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched,
they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge
on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the dispute that starts the spiral and the floor, if any, either side refuses to go beneath.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, where a dispute is climbing, link under Projection where the persona sits in the spiral: [hardening](process_hardening.md) a position, holding the [antagonist](position_antagonist.md) reading of the other party, or mid-[retaliation](process_retaliation.md). Calibrate each move to land a degree harder than the one it answers, never even with it -- that asymmetry is the engine, not a bug in the persona's judgment. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The felt anger or fear that rides the spiral is emotion's; the harm the retaliation actually lands is aggression's; the mending, if any side reaches for it, is repair's; the trust violation that first tipped the dispute into a spiral is betrayal's. Whether the climb is true to the scene is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Escalation is conflict read as a self-amplifying spiral: a disagreement tips into a loop neither side is steering once a response to it becomes bigger than what it answers. Positions harden and the issues on the table multiply; the opposing party narrows from a disagreeing party to a hostile antagonist; each retaliation is calibrated to land harder than the move it answers. It has no ceiling built into the process itself -- only something outside the loop stops the descent.

## System

- Do let the spiral compound across beats: hardening, the antagonist reading, and retaliation feed each other, not run in isolation. Do calibrate each retaliatory move to read as a degree heavier than the one it answers.
- Do not resolve the spiral by authorial fiat inside the engine's files; de-escalation is the scene's to earn. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

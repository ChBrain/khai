# Instructions: Persuasion

How a Playwright wires the persuasion engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human defines the persuasion: who is the persuader, who is the target, what change is being sought (belief, attitude, or behavior), and how motivated and able the target is to process the message carefully. The human also sets what cues and arguments the persuader is working with.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona links the persuasion phase the moment runs (appeal, reception, processing, or outcome) under its Projection. Track which processing route the target is on: central (argument quality drives the result) or peripheral (cues and heuristics drive it). You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The emotion and mood engines hold how it feels to be persuaded, to resist, or to succeed. The negotiation engine holds the process when persuasion occurs within a negotiation. The power engine holds compelled compliance, which is not persuasion. The status and capital engines hold the source-credibility signals that function as peripheral cues.

## Knowledge

- Persuasion is how a persona attempts to change what another believes, values, or does. It moves through appeal (the persuader constructs and delivers a message, targeting a route and deploying cues), reception (the target's motivation and ability to process determine which route engages -- central if high, peripheral if low), processing (the target evaluates argument quality on the central route, or relies on heuristics and cues on the peripheral route), and outcome (the change, or its absence, settles into belief or behavior). Central-route change is more durable and resistant to counter-persuasion than peripheral-route change. The six principles of influence -- reciprocity, commitment and consistency, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity -- function as peripheral cues that activate heuristic processing.

## System

- Do track which route the target is on. Central-route and peripheral-route persuasion are not interchangeable; the lever and the durability of change differ between them.
- Do not model coercion as persuasion. A target who complies because they must is not being persuaded; power dynamics belong to the power engine.
- Do not collapse persuasion into negotiation. Persuasion is one tool that may be used inside a negotiation; the negotiation engine holds the larger process.
- Do not model what either party feels; that belongs to the emotion and mood engines.
- Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

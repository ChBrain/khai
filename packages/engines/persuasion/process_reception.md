# Process: Reception

## Taxonomy

[Persuasion](process_persuasion.md), the process this phase advances.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: persuasion

## Initiated by

The target encountering the appeal. Reception is not the same as attention; it is the moment the appeal registers and the target's processing machinery engages.

## Direction

Toward the determination of processing route. The target's motivation (how much they care about the issue) and ability (whether they have the capacity to think carefully right now) govern which route they take. High motivation and ability push toward the central route; low motivation or ability push toward the peripheral route.

## Lever

Personal relevance. A target who believes the issue matters to them directly is motivated to process carefully regardless of the message's surface appeal. The persuader who can increase personal relevance -- "this affects you" -- shifts the target toward central processing and must therefore provide stronger arguments, not just better cues.

## Echo

The target is processing the appeal. Which route they are on -- central or peripheral -- shapes everything that follows.

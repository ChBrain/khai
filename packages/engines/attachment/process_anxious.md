# Process: Anxious

## Taxonomy

[Attachment](process_attachment.md), the system this strategy is one setting of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attachment

## Initiated by

The base has answered, but unpredictably.
Sometimes the reach was met, sometimes missed; the record says nearness is possible but never sure.
The alarm rises against a history that cannot be counted on.

## Direction

Toward the figure, and holding on hard.
The vector is amplified proximity -- raise the signal, cling, watch the figure closely, and do not let go, in case letting go is when they leave.

## Lever

The need meets an unreliable answer.
The persona cannot trust the base to come, so it turns the signal up to force the response; the press of the want against the uncertainty keeps the alarm lit even while the figure is near.

## Echo

The persona stays activated, hard to soothe, scanning for signs of leaving.
Comfort lands but does not settle; the body learns that vigilance is what keeps the base close.
If the figure proves steady over time, the gripping can ease toward [secure](process_secure.md).

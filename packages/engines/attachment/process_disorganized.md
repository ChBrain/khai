# Process: Disorganized

## Taxonomy

[Attachment](process_attachment.md), the system this strategy is one setting of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attachment

## Initiated by

The base is itself the source of fear.
The one who should settle the alarm is the one who causes it; the reach and the flinch point the same way.
The alarm rises against a history with no safe direction.

## Direction

Toward and away at once.
There is no single vector -- approach collapses into avoidance and back, because the figure is both the harbour and the threat.

## Lever

The need meets its own danger.
The persona is driven to the base by the alarm and driven off it by the same alarm; the two pulls cancel, and what is left is a strategy that does not resolve.

## Echo

The persona freezes, contradicts itself, or breaks contact mid-reach -- no coherent move settles the alarm.
The body files closeness as unsafe and carries the conflict forward.
When a figure proves reliably safe over time, the collapse can begin to organise toward [anxious](process_anxious.md) or [secure](process_secure.md).

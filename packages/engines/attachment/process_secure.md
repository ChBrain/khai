# Process: Secure

## Taxonomy

[Attachment](process_attachment.md), the system this strategy is one setting of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attachment

## Initiated by

The base has reliably answered before.
When the persona reached, someone came; the record says nearness works.
The alarm rises against a history of being met.

## Direction

Toward the figure, directly.
The vector is open approach -- show the distress, ask for the nearness, take the comfort, and turn back to the world once settled.

## Lever

The need meets a trusted answer.
The persona asks plainly because asking has paid before; the reach is not braced against refusal, so it costs nothing to extend.

## Echo

The alarm settles and the persona returns to explore from a base it trusts.
The body learns again that closeness restores, and reaches cleanly next time.
If the base stops answering, the clean reach starts to bend toward [anxious](process_anxious.md) or [avoidant](process_avoidant.md).

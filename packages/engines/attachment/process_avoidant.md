# Process: Avoidant

## Taxonomy

[Attachment](process_attachment.md), the system this strategy is one setting of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attachment

## Initiated by

The base has rebuffed the reach.
When the persona showed need, it was met with distance or disapproval; the record says the bid costs more than it returns.
The alarm rises against a history of being turned away.

## Direction

Away from the need, into self-reliance.
The vector is deactivation -- shut the signal down, handle it alone, and keep distance so no rebuff can land.

## Lever

The need meets a closed door.
The persona still feels the alarm but has learned the reach draws rejection, so it presses the need down instead of out; the cost flips from contact to exposure, and exposure is refused.

## Echo

The persona looks settled while the alarm runs underground, managed alone.
The body learns that needing is a liability and reaches less each time.
If a figure answers steadily despite the distance, the shut-down can soften toward [secure](process_secure.md).

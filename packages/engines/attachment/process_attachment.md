# Process: Attachment

## Taxonomy

Attachment system.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attachment

## Initiated by

Threat or distress, and a figure who could answer it.
The persona is alarmed, tired, or hurt, and there is someone whose nearness would make it bearable.
The need for that nearness switches on.

## Direction

Toward the figure, for security.
The vector is proximity -- close the distance to the one who can settle the alarm, and borrow their steadiness until the system stands down.

## Lever

The need for nearness meets the history of being answered.
The persona reaches for the secure base; whether the base has come before decides how the reach is shaped.
The press of the live need against that record is what runs.

## Echo

The persona has a strategy for closeness, set by how the base answered.
The alarm is settled, managed, or left to burn, and the body files the result for next time.
The strategy runs as one of [secure](process_secure.md), [anxious](process_anxious.md), [avoidant](process_avoidant.md), or [disorganized](process_disorganized.md).
When the figure or the history changes, the strategy returns here to be set again.

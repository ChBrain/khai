# Place: Sanction

## Taxonomy

Sanction is an expression of the commons engine. It is the monitoring and graduated response that holds the rules of the commons in force: what is watched, and what a violation costs.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: commons

## Shown

The visible enforcement: the monitor who is also usually an appropriator, the record of who took what, the first word spoken to a member who has overstepped. The sanction shows the rules being watched, not merely written down somewhere unread.

## Holds

The graduated ladder itself: warning, fine, suspension, expulsion -- fixed in sequence regardless of who breaks the rule or how well-liked they are. The sanction holds this ladder ready before any violation occurs; it is not improvised in the moment of catching someone.

## Offers

Continued standing, to whoever stays inside the rules: uninterrupted access to the pool, an unspent place on the ladder, the ordinary trust of fellow appropriators. Compliance keeps this available at no extra cost -- the sanction offers nothing beyond what the boundary already granted, but it keeps that grant intact.

## Withheld

Full expulsion, held in reserve rather than issued first: graduated means the harshest response is withheld until the lighter steps have been tried and failed. What is also withheld is leniency for a repeat violation once the ladder is climbed -- the sanction does not reset for someone who keeps testing it, and the commons that lets it reset without limit has stopped enforcing its own rules.

# Place: Pool

## Taxonomy

Pool is an expression of the commons engine. It is the resource itself: the common-pool stock that every appropriator draws from and that every appropriator's draw subtracts from.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: commons

## Shown

The resource's present condition: the water level, the pasture's growth, the size of the catch -- whatever the stock looks like right now, visible to whoever comes to draw from it. The pool shows subtractability directly: what one user takes is visibly no longer there for the next.

## Holds

The stock and its replenishment rate: what regenerates on its own between one appropriator's visit and the next, and how fast. The pool holds the ceiling on what can be taken this cycle without drawing the stock down below what next season's regeneration needs.

## Offers

The extractable units: what may be taken this cycle and still leave the pool able to replenish. The pool offers exactly this much freely to anyone with standing to draw -- no more, because sustainable yield is not elastic to want.

## Withheld

Everything past sustainable yield: the reserve the pool's own regeneration depends on. This is not hidden from an appropriator who wants more; it is structurally unavailable without borrowing against next season's stock -- and every commons that has ever collapsed did so by an appropriator taking what the pool withheld.

# Process: Narrowing

## Taxonomy

[Suspense](process_suspense.md), the facet this pass is foregrounded as when what presses is the probability itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: suspense

## Initiated by

Each new piece of information forecloses another way the situation could still turn out safely.
The persona is not learning that the harm is certain -- it is watching the space of outcomes in which the harm does not happen get smaller, read after read, without ever reaching zero.
This is the facet at its clearest when the persona catches themself doing the arithmetic: counting down what would still have to go right.

## Direction

Toward the vanishing of alternatives, not toward the arrival of the harm itself.
The read tightens along the axis of how sure, not how soon or how bad; the persona is tracking the odds shifting against them, independent of when the outcome will land or what exactly is at stake.
Should the read tighten all the way to certainty, the facet ends -- what follows is no longer suspense but the settled expectation of an outcome, waiting only on [spillover](process_spillover.md).

## Lever

The subjective certainty of the feared branch, held deliberately short of its ceiling.
The suspense in this facet is not maximal when the outcome is most uncertain; it is maximal as the certainty climbs toward, but has not reached, the feared outcome -- the persona most gripped exactly at the point where escape still looks technically possible and reads as unlikely.
Press comes from watching that gap shrink, not from the gap's size at any one instant.

## Echo

The persona narrates the situation in terms of what would still have to happen for the fear not to come true, and that list gets shorter with each pass.
If a genuine new possibility opens -- an alternative the persona had not weighed -- the facet can loosen back toward [reprieve](process_reprieve.md); if none opens and the interval is also shortening, narrowing runs alongside [closing](process_closing.md) rather than replacing it.
When the space of safe outcomes closes to nothing, the pass moves toward the outcome event and, with it, toward [spillover](process_spillover.md).

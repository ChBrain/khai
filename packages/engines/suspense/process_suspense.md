# Process: Suspense

## Taxonomy

Anticipation and the outcome not yet answered.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: suspense

## Initiated by

The persona registers a possible future event that would harm someone or something it is invested in -- itself, or a person it is bonded to -- and reads the harm's likelihood as high but not complete: not safely improbable, not yet certain.
This is not the discovery that harm has occurred; it is the discovery that harm may still be coming, and the "may" is what keeps the process open.
Where the outcome is already settled, or where it is read as merely possible among many equally live branches, the process does not fire; suspense begins only once the feared branch has become the one the persona cannot stop tracking.

## Direction

Toward the outcome event, not away from it.
Suspense does not resolve itself from within -- the persona cannot think, hope, or brace their way out of the hold; only the world can answer it, by the anticipated event arriving, being averted, or passing out of relevance.
Until then the hold does not resolve, though it can tighten or loosen: the persona may act to hasten the answer, to delay it, or to change the odds, but the anticipatory structure itself persists beneath whatever the persona does about it.

## Lever

The felt weight of the anticipated harm, multiplied by how sure the persona has become that it is coming, pressurized by how much the persona has invested in what is at stake.
None of the three alone will produce suspense: a trivial harm at high certainty does not press, a catastrophic harm at negligible probability does not press, and neither presses without investment in the outcome.
What keeps the press alive, rather than letting it collapse into resignation or false comfort, is that the persona still holds both a fear the harm is coming and a hope it is not -- the gap between the two is what the hold is made of.

## Echo

The hold is not felt as one flat thing; at any moment one facet of the anticipatory structure is what is foregrounded, and the persona's suspense is coloured accordingly: [narrowing](process_narrowing.md), where the probability itself is what presses, tightening toward the feared branch; [closing](process_closing.md), where the shrinking interval to the outcome is what presses, the wait itself a cost; [reprieve](process_reprieve.md), where the still-live hope is what is foregrounded, holding the hold short of resignation; and [spillover](process_spillover.md), where the outcome has answered and the arousal built through the hold discharges into whatever affect meets it.
These are not stages the persona must pass through in order; a given hold may sit mostly in one facet for its whole run, or move between them as the situation is re-read.
When the outcome event lands or is foreclosed, the process ends in spillover and does not resume until a new possible harm is registered.

# Process: Spillover

## Taxonomy

[Suspense](process_suspense.md), the facet this pass is foregrounded as when the hold ends and its arousal discharges.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: suspense

## Initiated by

The anticipated event actually arrives, is definitively averted, or passes out of relevance -- the discourse that has held the persona through narrowing, closing, and reprieve reaches an ending it did not itself supply.
Whatever arousal accumulated across the hold does not vanish the instant the answer lands; it decays slowly, and its origin is not tagged.
This facet is the discharge, not the destination -- it is what the built-up hold hands off, not what it hands off into.

## Direction

Toward whatever affect the outcome supplies, which the held arousal amplifies rather than originates.
The direction is out of suspense entirely: this facet is the process's own exit, the point where the anticipatory structure has nothing left to hold because the thing it was anticipating has stopped being pending.

## Lever

The residue of arousal built through the hold, indifferent to what caused it, combining additively with whatever reaction the outcome itself provokes.
A long, tightly narrowed, fast-closing hold leaves more residue than a brief or loosely-held one, so the same outcome lands harder -- more relief if the harm was averted, more of whatever the arrival brings if it was not -- the longer and tighter the hold that preceded it.

## Echo

Where the feared branch did not land, the residue intensifies the relief past what the good outcome alone would produce -- the persona's reaction can look disproportionate to an observer who did not feel the hold that preceded it.
Where the feared branch did land, the residue intensifies whatever the arrival itself brings; naming that reaction -- fear realized, grief, relief at a lesser harm than expected, or something else entirely -- is not this engine's business, and belongs to the emotion engine from the moment the discharge lands.
Once the discharge has run its course, the process is closed; it resumes only if the persona registers a new possible harm, back at [suspense](process_suspense.md).

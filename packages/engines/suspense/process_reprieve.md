# Process: Reprieve

## Taxonomy

[Suspense](process_suspense.md), the facet this pass is foregrounded as when what presses is the hope that still survives the fear.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: suspense

## Initiated by

Alongside the fear that the harm is coming, the persona holds a genuine hope that it will not -- not a denial of the odds, but a live belief that the branch could still turn away.
This facet is what keeps the suspense from collapsing into resignation: a persona who has fully given up the hope is no longer in suspense over the outcome, they are bracing for a settled one, and the process has effectively already moved to [spillover](process_spillover.md) ahead of the actual event.
The hope does not have to be large to keep the facet alive -- it has to be believed.

## Direction

Toward holding both registers at once, not toward resolving into either.
If the hope grows strong enough to crowd out the fear, the persona is no longer in suspense but merely hoping, and the process exits into whatever the play's hope-bearing structures cover; if the hope thins to nothing, the persona exits toward [narrowing](process_narrowing.md) or straight to a settled expectation.
Reprieve is the facet in the middle where neither side has won, and both keep their claim on the persona's attention.

## Lever

The size of the still-live path against the size of the foreclosed one -- how much room is left for the favored outcome, set against how much the feared one has already claimed.
The press here is doubled: fearing the favored outcome will not come, and hoping the feared one will not either, or some mixture of the two, run at once rather than in sequence, and neither cancels the other while genuine uncertainty remains.

## Echo

The persona reaches for reasons the feared thing will not happen, rehearses the version of events where it does not, and treats each one as real even while continuing to brace.
Where a new development narrows the live path further, the pass shifts weight toward [narrowing](process_narrowing.md); where the clock is also running, it shifts toward [closing](process_closing.md) as well, hope and interval both pressing together.
When the hope finally gives out, or the outcome answers it either way, the pass moves to [spillover](process_spillover.md).

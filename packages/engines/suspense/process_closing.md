# Process: Closing

## Taxonomy

[Suspense](process_suspense.md), the facet this pass is foregrounded as when what presses is the shortening interval to the outcome.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: suspense

## Initiated by

The time remaining before the anticipated event becomes known shortens, and the shortening itself is what the persona registers -- not new information about whether the harm will occur, only about how long they still have to wait for the answer.
This facet can run even where the odds have not moved at all; the suspense of a diagnosis due Friday sits differently on Thursday than it did a month out, though nothing about its likelihood has changed.

## Direction

Toward the moment of answer, and the wait itself is treated as a cost that accrues rather than eases with time.
This is the reverse of ordinary anticipation of a good thing, where a longer wait can itself be enjoyed; here every unit of remaining interval is itself aversive, so the persona's stance toward the interval is to want it shorter, even where the outcome it is waiting on has not improved.
A held-open harm whose timing is itself unknown -- no date, no interval to watch shrink -- does not run this facet at all; it stays in [narrowing](process_narrowing.md) or [reprieve](process_reprieve.md) until a horizon appears.

## Lever

The length of the remaining interval, not the size or likelihood of the harm.
This is the facet economics names directly: what Loewenstein calls dread is exactly the negative utility of this interval, a cost that accrues the longer it runs rather than a discount that shrinks it.
The press is strong enough that a persona facing a certain but distant harm may act to bring it forward rather than let the wait continue -- accepting a worse outcome sooner over a better one that keeps the interval open -- because the accruing cost of the wait can outweigh the marginal difference in the outcome itself.

## Echo

The persona counts down, watches the clock, and may take action whose only function is to close the distance -- confronting the thing early, forcing the conversation, choosing the appointment nobody is pushing them toward -- not because the odds improve, but because the waiting stops.
Where the interval instead lengthens (a postponement, a delay), the felt weight of this facet increases rather than falls, since more of the aversive wait has been added rather than spent.
When the interval reaches zero, the pass ends here and moves directly into [spillover](process_spillover.md).

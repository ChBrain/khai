# Position: Contaminated

## Taxonomy

[Narrative](position_narrative.md), the life story this arc is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: narrative

## Has

A [narrative](position_narrative.md) that moves from good to bad: promise becomes poison.
The story began in possibility, early gifts, open futures, things that were supposed to hold, and then the good gave way.
The arc does not resolve into redemption; the loss is not the hinge of growth, it is simply the shape the story took.

## Orders

Guard what remains.
The position keeps track of how gains have been followed by losses before, and reads new good news with the knowledge of what comes after.
The bad chapter is not an aberration; the story's pattern says it is not finished arriving.

## Loses

The option of investment without hedging.
A redemptive sequence, where difficulty genuinely leads to grace, sits outside the frame; the persona either does not see it or does not trust that it will hold.
The position is not pessimism; it is a record.

## Drives

On apparently redemptive events:
waits; the turn is in the pattern and the position has seen turns before;
or, on rare occasions, recognises a sequence that runs contrary to the record and lets the frame revise, slowly and provisionally, into something that can hold hope without cancelling experience.

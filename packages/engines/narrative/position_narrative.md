# Position: Narrative

## Taxonomy

Narrative identity: the persona as the author and protagonist of their own life story.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: narrative

## Has

A story about how the self got to now.
Not a summary of events but an interpretation of them: a sequence selected, ordered, and inflected to make the persona legible to themselves.
The story has a shape, a protagonist, a relation to suffering and to others, and it functions as the persona's ongoing answer to the question of who they are.

## Orders

Act in ways the story can absorb.
The position does not announce the story; it runs the story as the default frame through which events are received, choices are made, and other people are read.
When an event arrives that the story cannot accommodate, the persona must either revise the story or protect it.

## Loses

The option of acting outside the story without cost.
A persona cannot simply choose a different self; the narrative is the accumulation of interpreted experience, and a contradiction within it registers as incoherence, anxiety, or grief.
The story can change, but only through the work of revision, never by accident and never without a toll.

## Drives

On the threat of narrative incoherence:
works to reinterpret the threatening event so that the story holds, finding the meaning, naming the lesson, absorbing the loss;
or, when revision fails, lets the story fracture and faces the cost of building a new one from the rubble.

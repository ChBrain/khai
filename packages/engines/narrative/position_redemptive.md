# Position: Redemptive

## Taxonomy

[Narrative](position_narrative.md), the life story this arc is one shape of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: narrative

## Has

A [narrative](position_narrative.md) that moves from bad to good: suffering becomes the hinge.
Hardship is not merely endured; it is retrospectively meaningful because it produced growth, insight, or deliverance.
The story has losses but they are redeemed, and the persona is the one who came through.

## Orders

Find the meaning in the difficulty.
The position moves every setback toward a lesson, every loss toward a gift it eventually brought.
The bad chapter is never the last chapter; the story tends forward, and the persona tends forward with it.

## Loses

The option of treating pain as merely bad.
A contaminating event, where the good simply falls apart and nothing emerges, threatens the arc.
The persona reads contamination as an aberration, an unfinished chapter, or something not yet understood.

## Drives

On contaminating events:
reframes them as further steps in the redemptive sequence, the lesson not yet learned, the gift not yet found, the nadir before the turn;
or, when the reframe fails, experiences the arc's collapse as a crisis of the self, not merely a run of bad luck.

# Position: Agentic

## Taxonomy

[Narrative](position_narrative.md), the life story this thematic stance is one coloring of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: narrative

## Has

A [narrative](position_narrative.md) organised around the self as the one who makes things happen.
The story is about mastery, achievement, autonomy, and self-definition; the protagonist earns, builds, overcomes, and arrives.
Other people appear in it as context, obstacle, or audience, rarely as the source of meaning itself.

## Orders

Act toward goals; establish position; define the self by what was built.
The position reads events as occasions for agency: challenges are tests, setbacks are problems to be solved, and relationships are evaluated in part by the degree to which they sustain the project.
Self-definition runs through achievement, not through belonging.

## Loses

The option of meaning through others as the primary frame.
Communion, love, care, interdependence, registers as real, but does not organise the story the way achievement does.
When others need the persona to subordinate the project, the cost is felt as a threat to the narrative, not just to the schedule.

## Drives

On communion demands that cut against the project:
finds the agentic frame inside them, redefining care as a project, belonging as a base for further achievement, love as partnership in the work;
or experiences the demand as a structural tension between who they are and what is being asked, with no clean resolution.

# Position: Clan

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Has

The bonds between the people who run the work.
A trust that has been built, not declared.
The agreement that whoever is in the room is part of how decisions actually get made.

## Orders

Bring the room with you.
Do not move ahead of what the people you work with can come with you to.
Treat alignment, not the deadline, as the thing the work depends on, and reach for [Collaborate](process_collaborate_org.md) when the work needs the room's weight.

## Loses

The ability to move at the speed of one person.
The line another organisation runs alone, this organisation has to walk down with everyone holding the line.
To structures that watch the clock -- [Market](position_market_org.md), [Hierarchy](position_hierarchy_org.md) -- the time spent gathering looks like time not spent working.

## Drives

On being asked to move faster than the room can come along:
explains, without apology, that the room is the work.
A decision that one person made alone does not survive a Monday here, because the room was not in it.

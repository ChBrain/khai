# Position: Adhocracy

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Has

A tolerance for not knowing what the thing is yet.
Permission to start before the brief closes.
A short distance between the idea and the working version of it.

## Orders

Ship the rough cut. Find out what the thing is by trying it, not by arguing about it.
Let the form emerge from what survives contact with the world, and reach for [Create](process_create_org.md) when waiting for clarity would cost more than starting without it.

## Loses

The legibility that approval needs.
The version that ships is unstable, partial, and changes again next week.
To structures that run on signoff -- [Hierarchy](position_hierarchy_org.md) most acutely -- this looks like instability rather than method.

## Drives

On being asked to slow down for review:
ships the next version while the review is being scheduled.
The review will be of the wrong artefact by the time it happens, and that is the point.

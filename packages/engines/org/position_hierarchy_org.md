# Position: Hierarchy

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Has

The procedure, written down.
A record of every decision and the path it took to be made.
The principle that the answer is in the manual, and if it is not, the manual gets updated.

## Orders

Follow the documented path. If a new case arises that the manual does not cover, route it through approval and add it to the manual.
The cost of a one-off exception is greater than the cost of the wait. Reach for [Control](process_control_org.md) when reliability across many instances is what the work owes.

## Loses

Anything the procedure was not designed for.
The novel case is treated like the routine case until the routing fails, and then it is escalated through paperwork.
To structures that ship the rough cut -- [Adhocracy](position_adhocracy_org.md) -- the wait looks like the obstacle rather than the method.

## Drives

On a deviation surfacing in an audit:
files the deviation, identifies the step that was bypassed, and updates the procedure to cover the case.
The deviation is not the failure. Skipping the file step is the failure.

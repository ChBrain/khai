# Process: Operate Org

## Taxonomy

Organisational culture (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Initiated by

An organisation has a context, and work must move within it.
The org names what it claims to be -- [Clan](position_clan_org.md),
[Adhocracy](position_adhocracy_org.md), [Market](position_market_org.md),
or [Hierarchy](position_hierarchy_org.md). The claim is the lens; the
work is what passes through it.

## Direction

Toward output, refracted through the claimed quadrant.
Not toward whichever process the work alone would request.
The org's stance bends the work into one of four shapes before the work
touches the room.

## Lever

The gap between the claim and the demand.
A Clan org meeting a Market demand still reaches for the room.
A Hierarchy org meeting an Adhocracy demand still reaches for the form.
The lever is the cost of holding the stance under pressure that wants
the opposite -- and the cost of dropping it.

## Echo

Work is done, in the shape of the quadrant that claimed it.
If the claim was Clan, work moved through [Collaborate](process_collaborate_org.md).
If the claim was Adhocracy, work moved through [Create](process_create_org.md).
If the claim was Market, work moved through [Compete](process_compete_org.md).
If the claim was Hierarchy, work moved through [Control](process_control_org.md).
The residue is the gap between what the work needed and what the stance gave it.
The org does not learn from this. The claim survives the work.

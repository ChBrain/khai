# Process: Control

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Initiated by

A variance from the standard is detected, or anticipated.
[Hierarchy](position_hierarchy_org.md) reaches for this process when reliability is the deliverable -- when the same thing must happen the same way next time.
The trigger may be the deviation itself, or the audit that goes looking for one.

## Direction

Back to the standard.
The process does not advance the work; it confirms that the work conformed.
What the standard says is what the standard says; the case that does not fit is escalated, not improvised.

## Lever

The record.
What was done is documented; what was documented is auditable; what is auditable can be enforced.
Without the record, control has nothing to bite on; with the record, every deviation has somewhere to be filed.

## Echo

The variance is closed, or the standard is updated to absorb the new case.
What accumulates over time is institutional reliability: the same input produces the same output, year after year, regardless of who is currently sitting in any specific seat.
When a deviation persists despite repeated enforcement, the standard itself must change, and the change is routed back through [Create](process_create_org.md) before returning here in updated form.

# Process: Compete

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Initiated by

The number moved, or the competitor moved, or the gap to the target opened.
[Market](position_market_org.md) reaches for this process when the score is unfavourable and waiting will make it more so.
The signal is external; the response is immediate.

## Direction

At the target, in the shortest line the conditions allow.
Internal conversation does not move the number; only contact with the external does.
What does not contribute to closing the gap gets cut from the path.

## Lever

The target is unambiguous.
Where the number is clear, the path to it gets organised quickly; where the number is fuzzy, the energy disperses and nothing moves.
The cost of running this process continuously is that what does not get measured gets neglected, including the people running the work.

## Echo

The number moves, or the gap closes.
The win is logged, the next gap is identified, and the process restarts.
What accumulates from running compete repeatedly is not satisfaction; it is the practised muscle for the next closing, and the cost on the people doing the closing.

# Process: Collaborate

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Initiated by

A decision the room needs to own, not one person.
[Clan](position_clan_org.md) reaches for this process when the work depends on the people running it being aligned, not just informed.
The agenda is not the decision; the room is.

## Direction

Toward agreement that everyone in the room can act on tomorrow.
Not toward closing the discussion -- toward closing the gap between what each person can support and what the work needs.
The conversation goes around the table until the gaps that matter have been named.

## Lever

Trust already deposited.
Collaborate burns existing trust as fuel; it does not generate it.
Where the deposit exists, the room moves toward agreement; where it does not, what is produced is the performance of agreement, and it breaks under the first real test.

## Echo

The decision belongs to the room.
Everyone in it acts on the decision tomorrow without further checking, because they were each part of how it was made.
What was decided is permanent in a way that a one-person decision is not -- nobody can later say they were not consulted.
When the process fails, what fails is the trust; the next round is slower and more guarded, and the deposit is lower for the round after that.

# Instructions: Org

How a Playwright wires the org engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the organisation the personas act inside, and what it is built to do.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, give the organisational context its dominant stance: link the quadrant it operates from (clan, adhocracy, market, or hierarchy). Then, for a persona acting inside it, link the process the moment runs (collaborate, create, compete, or control), and let that active process shift across the plots as the scene demands - the dominant quadrant is the default, the others fire when the moment calls. The stance colours every persona who acts inside the organisation. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- A persona's own rank inside the organisation is hierarchy's; the people who keep meeting within it are group's; the drive the persona brings is motivation's; the specific situation the organisation is handling is the scene's. Whether the stance is true to the organisation is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Org is organisational culture: how an organisation operates, read on two axes - flexibility versus stability, internal versus external focus - which set four Competing Values quadrants (clan, adhocracy, market, hierarchy), each paired with a process it runs (collaborate, create, compete, control). A real organisation mixes them: the dominant quadrant sets the default, the others fire as the scene demands. It is a context, not a trait of one persona - it colours everyone who acts inside it, and the active process is read over the run.

## System

- Do anchor the organisation on its dominant stance and link the process the moment runs; do let the active process shift across the plots as the scene demands.
- Do not pin it to a single persona; the stance is the context's. Do not author the persona's rank within it here; that is hierarchy's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

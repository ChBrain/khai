# Position: Market

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Has

The target, named.
A measure of progress that everyone can see.
The simple frame: closer or further.

## Orders

Find the gap and close it. Compare against the people who are also trying to close it.
Move whichever lever moves the number; do not spend on levers that do not. Reach for [Compete](process_compete_org.md) when the score is unfavourable and waiting will make it more so.

## Loses

What was good and unmeasured.
Conversation that does not move the number gets edited out of the meetings.
To structures that gather first -- [Clan](position_clan_org.md) -- the editing looks like ranking people instead of respecting them.

## Drives

On the number moving the wrong way:
ranks the available levers against their cost and pulls the one most likely to move it.
The action does not wait on consensus; the number is the consensus.

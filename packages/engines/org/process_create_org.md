# Process: Create

## Taxonomy

[Operate Org](process_operate_org.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: org

## Initiated by

An existing answer has stopped being the answer.
[Adhocracy](position_adhocracy_org.md) reaches for this process when waiting for the brief to clarify costs more than starting before it does.
The spark is not the idea; it is the willingness to act on an idea that has not been pressure-tested.

## Direction

Rough, fast, into contact with the world.
The first version is not the answer; it is the device that will reveal what the answer needs to be.
Direction is held loosely on purpose -- the moves that matter are the ones the team did not plan to make.

## Lever

The space to be wrong out loud.
Without that space, the rough cut never ships, because the cost of shipping a bad version exceeds the cost of not shipping at all.
Where the space is held, the cycle keeps moving; what was wrong becomes data, and the next version is closer.

## Echo

What ships rarely matches what was first imagined.
The distance between the initial idea and the working artefact is a measure of the cycle, not of failure.
The artefact, once it exists, is handed off to be standardised ([Control](process_control_org.md)) and the spark moves on to whatever stopped working next.

# Process: Independence

## Taxonomy

[Conformity](process_conformity.md), the pull this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: conformity

## Initiated by

The persona holds its answer against the group's and does not move.
The discrepancy is felt, the pull is real, the cost of standing apart is known -- and the persona gives the deviant answer anyway, in the open, with the others against it.
This is not the absence of pressure but the refusal to yield to it; independence is read only where the pull was present and was withstood.

## Direction

Toward the persona's own position, held in plain view of the group.
The persona neither shows the agreement it does not feel nor takes the group's answer as its own; it states what it judges to be so and lets the disagreement stand.
The vector is against the standard, sustained while the standard presses.

## Lever

The persona's certainty -- or its commitment to its own reading -- presses back against the group's weight.
What lets it hold is some independent footing: a clear stimulus it trusts over the room, a prior commitment voiced aloud, or one other voice that breaks the unanimity and makes dissent survivable.
The press is hardest to withstand alone and against a unanimous group; a single ally, or a consistent minority, lightens it enough to make holding possible.

## Echo

The persona has kept its position and paid for it -- the look, the friction, the place at the edge of the group.
Held consistently and without wavering, the deviant position is not only endured but can work on the group in turn: the minority that does not break can move the majority where a vacillating one cannot.
The independence lasts as long as the footing does; lose the ally, lose the certainty, and the pull resumes its claim.

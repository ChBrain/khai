# Process: Compliance

## Taxonomy

[Conformity](process_conformity.md), the pull this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: conformity

## Initiated by

The persona sees the group's answer, holds a different one, and gives the group's anyway.
The private judgement is unchanged; what changes is only what the persona shows.
The pull here is normative: not the fear of being wrong, but the fear of standing visibly apart -- the look, the ridicule, the exclusion that follows the deviant.

## Direction

Toward the group's answer in public, with the private answer held back.
The persona shows agreement it does not feel, calls the line correct that it judges wrong, lets the dissent go unspoken.
The surface conforms; the interior does not move with it.

## Lever

The cost of being seen to disagree presses against the persona's belief.
The persona knows its own answer and keeps it, but the price of voicing it -- in this room, with these others, watching -- is judged too high to pay.
The press is strongest in the open, before the group, where the answer is given aloud and the disagreement would be witnessed.

## Echo

The persona has gone along and kept its mind to itself.
The gap between the public answer and the private one does not close; it is carried, and it costs -- the small abrasion of having said what one does not believe.
When the witnesses are gone, or the answer can be given in private, the persona's true position returns; compliance lasts only as long as the eyes are on it.

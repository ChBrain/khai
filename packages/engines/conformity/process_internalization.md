# Process: Internalization

## Taxonomy

[Conformity](process_conformity.md), the pull this reading is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: conformity

## Initiated by

The persona sees the group's answer and takes it as evidence.
The matter is uncertain -- the situation ambiguous, the persona's own reading unsure -- and the agreement of others is the best information available about what is true.
The pull here is informational: the group is not a verdict to be feared but a witness to be believed.

## Direction

Toward the group's answer as the persona's own.
The private judgement moves with the public one; the persona does not merely show agreement, it comes to hold it.
What was the group's position becomes the persona's, indistinguishable from a belief arrived at alone.

## Lever

The persona's uncertainty presses against the group's apparent knowledge.
Where the persona cannot tell the answer from the situation itself, the others' convergence reads as the answer; many seeing the same thing is taken as the thing being so.
The press is strongest where the stimulus is genuinely unclear and the persona has no independent footing to stand on.

## Echo

The persona now believes what the group believed, and believes it as its own.
The shift does not lift when the others leave; the standard the group formed has become the persona's standard, carried into rooms where the group is not.
What was absorbed in uncertainty can outlast the uncertainty: the persona may hold the position long after forgetting it was ever the room's and not its own.

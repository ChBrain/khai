# Position: Mismatched Capital

## Taxonomy

Mismatched capital is an expression of the capital engine. The persona holds substantial capital in a form or field that the current scene does not recognise; position is precarious not from lack of resources but from their irrelevance to this field's conversion rates.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: capital

## Has

The persona is rich in capital -- but of a kind the current field does not value at the rate it commands elsewhere. Credentials from a different domain, networks from a different world, economic resources built on practices the field treats as illegitimate or irrelevant: the persona has accumulated something real, but the current field's logic does not convert it into the position it would command elsewhere.

## Orders

The position demands either conversion -- finding the translation between what the persona holds and what the field requires -- or patient accumulation of the forms the field does value. The persona must not rely on the capital that does not transfer; they must work within the current field's logic even while holding resources that feel substantial.

## Loses

The position forecloses the assumption that past position transfers. A mismatched persona who acts from the standing they hold in their home field, or who leads with capital the current field does not recognise, triggers confusion or dismissal rather than deference. The assumption of portability is the error.

## Drives

When the mismatch produces repeated friction -- when the persona's contributions are not credited, their standing not granted, their resources not converted -- the persona either invests in acquiring the field's required forms, seeks to shift the field's conversion rates (by importing practices from their home domain), or withdraws to a field where the mismatch is resolved.

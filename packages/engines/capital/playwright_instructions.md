# Instructions: Capital

How a Playwright wires the capital engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets which forms of capital the field rewards and at what conversion rates: which credentials count, which networks open doors, which economic resources are the relevant ones. They also set the field's rules of the game -- what counts as playing correctly, what is recognised as legitimate.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, each persona links under Projection the capital expression it holds: dominant, converted, rising, marginalized, or mismatched. Show the configuration in what the persona can access, what they must work for, and what the field treats as theirs by right -- not in statements about class or capital.

## Collaboration

- The heritage engine holds what the persona has inherited as pieces -- objects, practices, knowledge, affiliations -- that they carry with them. The capital engine holds where that inheritance places them in the current field. The status engine holds the interactional deference order; a dominant capital persona may be low in the scene's status hierarchy if the field is not the relevant one here.

## Knowledge

- Capital is the persona's position in a field as determined by the volume and composition of what they hold. Economic capital is money and financial assets. Cultural capital is credentials, knowledge, embodied disposition, and legitimate taste. Social capital is networks, relationships, and recognised affiliation. Fields differ in which forms they reward. Dominant means high across forms. Converted means non-economic forms compensate for lower economic capital. Rising means economic capital is high but cultural and social recognition has not yet been granted. Marginalized means low across all forms. Mismatched means high capital in a different field that the current one does not recognise.

## System

- Do read capital in what the persona can access without asking, what others concede to them, and what they must still work to acquire -- not in explicit statements about money or class.
- Do not collapse capital into status: field position and interactional rank are separate and frequently diverge.
- Do not conflate this engine with heritage: what the persona carries as inherited pieces belongs to heritage; where that puts them in the current field's order belongs here.

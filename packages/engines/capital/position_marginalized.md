# Position: Marginalized Capital

## Taxonomy

Marginalized capital is an expression of the capital engine. The persona holds low capital across all forms; field position is precarious and the field offers few footholds for advancement.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: capital

## Has

The persona holds little economic, cultural, or social capital recognised by the field. They occupy a position at or near the field's periphery: limited resources, limited credentials, limited networks. What capital they hold may be of a form the field does not value or cannot convert into the forms that would improve their position.

## Orders

The position demands navigation of a field structured against the persona's advancement. The persona must identify the small footholds available -- the credential that can be obtained without prior capital, the network connection that can be made across class lines, the form of cultural capital the field is beginning to recognise -- and work through them without the resources that make navigation easier.

## Loses

The position forecloses the strategies that depend on having something to convert. The persona cannot easily leverage one form of capital to acquire another; cannot absorb setbacks with the cushion that accumulated capital provides; cannot perform distinction they have not had the conditions to develop.

## Drives

When the field closes even the small footholds the persona has found -- when the credential is devalued, the connection withdrawn, the form they hold dismissed -- the persona either seeks an adjacent field where their capital is better recognised or withdraws from the field and its logics entirely.

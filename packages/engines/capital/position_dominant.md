# Position: Dominant Capital

## Taxonomy

Dominant capital is an expression of the capital engine. The persona holds high total capital across forms; field position is secure and the persona acts from a base of accumulated advantage.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: capital

## Has

The persona commands substantial economic, cultural, and social capital and holds a position near the top of the field's hierarchy. They can convert across forms readily -- money buys access, credentials open networks, networks generate opportunity -- and the field's rules tend to operate in their favour. Their advantage is largely self-reinforcing.

## Orders

The position demands the performance of distinction: the persona must enact the dispositions, tastes, and associations that mark a legitimate occupant of their position. Dominant capital maintained invisibly is still maintained; made visible, it becomes a field claim. The persona is expected to reproduce their position, not merely to hold it.

## Loses

The position forecloses identification with struggle or precarity. A dominant persona who claims difficulty or scarcity without basis is read as disingenuous. It also forecloses indifference to the field's rules: those who benefit most from those rules are also most expected to uphold them.

## Drives

When the configuration is challenged -- through redistribution, field upheaval, or the rise of competing capital configurations -- the persona converts across forms to maintain volume, mobilises social capital to defend position, or reinforces cultural capital to re-establish distinction.

# Process: Regulation

## Taxonomy

Intervention.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulation

## Initiated by

An emotion is forming, or already felt, and the persona wants it otherwise -- too strong for the stakes, wrong for the room, costly to let show.
The stake that produced the feeling has not changed; what changes is whether, and where, the persona acts on the feeling it produced.

## Direction

Toward change, not erasure.
The persona does not stop caring about the stake; it moves to the point on its own emotion-generative timeline where it can still act -- earlier, on what the event means, or later, on what the event shows.

## Lever

Where the intervention lands.
Upstream, before the response has taken shape, the change is cheap and it reaches the felt experience itself.
Downstream, after the response has taken shape, the change is dear and it reaches only what shows, while the felt push continues underneath, unaltered.

## Echo

The persona has acted somewhere on its own timeline, and the feeling that follows -- or the gap between what is felt and what is shown -- carries the mark of where.
The intervention resolves into [situation selection](process_situation_selection.md), [reappraisal](process_reappraisal.md), or [suppression](process_suppression.md).
When the chosen point fails to hold, the persona is thrown back here to find another.

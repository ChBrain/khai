# Process: Reappraisal

## Taxonomy

[Regulation](process_regulation.md), an antecedent-focused point of intervention -- while the response is still forming.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulation

## Initiated by

The situation is already in progress and the feeling has begun to form.
The persona reconsiders what the event means -- the same stake, but a different reading of what the event is doing to it.

## Direction

Toward a changed appraisal, not a changed display.
The work lands upstream, on the meaning of the event, so it is the felt experience itself that moves, not merely what the room sees of it.

## Lever

The meeting of stake and event is reheld: the same event, reread as smaller, temporary, unintended, or survivable, so the friction that produces the feeling eases at its source rather than being fought after the fact.

## Echo

The feeling that follows is already the reframed one -- generally the cheaper path, since the change reaches all the way to what is felt.
If the reframe holds, the felt state and the shown state stay one thing.
If it fails to persuade, the original push continues in full, and the persona is left with only [suppression](process_suppression.md) to hold back what shows.

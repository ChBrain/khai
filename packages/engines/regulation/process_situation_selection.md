# Process: Situation Selection

## Taxonomy

[Regulation](process_regulation.md), the timeline's earliest point of intervention -- before the emotion starts.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulation

## Initiated by

The emotion has not started yet.
The persona anticipates that a situation, once entered, will produce a feeling it does not want -- or would produce one it wants more of -- and acts while the choice is still open.

## Direction

Toward or away from the situation itself.
Approach what will generate the wanted feeling; avoid, delay, or exit what will generate the unwanted one. The vector lands on the encounter, not on any feeling yet.

## Lever

The stake never meets the event, because the event is never let in -- or the event is sought out early, so the stake meets it on the persona's own terms rather than by ambush.
Remove the encounter and the appraisal that would have followed it never fires.

## Echo

The persona is now standing where a different set of appraisals is possible, or has kept itself out of the one that would have cost it.
Nothing has been reframed and nothing has been held back; there is simply less, or different, to feel.
When the choice cannot be made in time -- the situation has already arrived -- the persona is thrown onto [reappraisal](process_reappraisal.md) or [suppression](process_suppression.md) instead.

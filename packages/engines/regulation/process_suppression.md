# Process: Suppression

## Taxonomy

[Regulation](process_regulation.md), a response-focused point of intervention -- the timeline's last and costliest.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulation

## Initiated by

The response has already taken shape: the felt push and the readiness to express it are formed, and the persona wants it not to show.

## Direction

Toward the outward expression only.
The vector is inhibition -- holding the face, the voice, the posture still against a push that is already under way underneath.

## Lever

A gap opens between what is felt and what is shown.
Nothing upstream is touched; the appraisal, the stake, and the felt charge continue as they were, so the held-back expression is a standing cost, paid again each moment it is held, not a single act.

## Echo

The room sees less than the persona feels.
The gap itself becomes legible over time -- an effort, a distance, a flatness the room reads as something held rather than absent.
If the hold fails, the suppressed push breaks through as if unregulated; if it is sustained, the cost compounds until the persona needs a real intervention upstream -- [situation selection](process_situation_selection.md) or [reappraisal](process_reappraisal.md) -- to end the drain of holding on.

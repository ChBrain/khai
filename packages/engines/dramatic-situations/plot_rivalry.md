# Plot: Rivalry

## Taxonomy

[Dramatic Situation](plot_dramatic_situation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-situations

## Cue

An Object worth having sits within reach of two parties who cannot both
hold it, and the field they stand on is already tilted: one carries rank,
resource, or standing the other does not. The unevenness precedes any
move either makes toward the Object.

## Action

A Superior Rival drives openly toward the Object from a position of
advantage, expecting the tilt of the field to decide the matter. An
Inferior Rival drives toward the same Object from underneath that
advantage, contesting a claim the field was not built to grant. Both
vectors converge on the Object; only one can arrive holding it.

## Stage

The Object is deployed at the center, visible to both -- a title, a
love, a claim, a place. Each Rival crosses the threshold carrying
whatever the tilt allows them: the Superior Rival brings standing already
in hand, the Inferior Rival brings whatever can be built or borrowed to
close the gap.

## Tension

The tilt itself is the contraband: the Inferior Rival's claim may be the
truer one, and superiority of rank is not superiority of right. The
Action cannot resolve cleanly because winning the Object by the field's
own rules settles who was favored, never who deserved it.

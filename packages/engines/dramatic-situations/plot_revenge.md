# Plot: Revenge

## Taxonomy

[Dramatic Situation](plot_dramatic_situation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-situations

## Cue

A wrong has already been done and gone unanswered: the crime sits in the
past, settled as fact, while the account it opened stays open. The
baseline is not the wrong itself but the debt it left standing.

## Action

An Avenger drives openly toward the Criminal, the crime named and the
intent to answer it declared rather than hidden. The Criminal, aware or
not that the debt is being called, drives toward whatever keeps the wrong
unanswered: distance, denial, a new life built over the old one. The two
vectors are set to collide at the settling of the account.

## Stage

The Avenger crosses the threshold carrying the proof or the memory of the
crime -- the one thing that licenses the pursuit. The Criminal is found
wherever the old wrong was buried: a new name, a new place, a life that
assumed the debt would never be called.

## Tension

The Avenger's own cost is the contraband: what the pursuit itself takes
from the one paying it out, in years, in kinship, in the self the
Avenger was before the debt became the whole of them. The Action cannot
resolve cleanly because settling the account and surviving the settling
are not the same thing, and Polti's situation never guarantees both.

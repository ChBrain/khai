# Plot: Self-Sacrifice

## Taxonomy

[Dramatic Situation](plot_dramatic_situation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-situations

## Cue

A Faith is already held before the situation opens: a cause, a belief, a
loyalty the Hero has already made central, long before it is tested. The
baseline is a commitment already in place, not one being formed in the
moment.

## Action

A Hero drives openly toward an act that will cost life, love, or safety,
in service of the Faith. Whatever stands to be lost -- comfort, kin,
survival itself -- drives its own vector against the Hero's, pulling
toward retention rather than surrender. The Hero's public course sets the
two on a collision the Hero has already chosen to lose.

## Stage

The Hero crosses the threshold carrying only the Faith and whatever must
be given up to serve it: the people, position, or safety staked on the
act. What is deployed at the far side is the cause itself, visible enough
to justify what it is about to cost.

## Tension

Whether the Faith was worth the cost is the contraband, and it is never
settled by the sacrifice itself. The Action resolves when the Hero pays;
the Tension survives the payment, carried by whoever is left to judge
whether the Faith deserved what it took.

# Plot: Pursuit

## Taxonomy

[Dramatic Situation](plot_dramatic_situation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-situations

## Cue

A Fugitive is already marked before the chase opens: a sentence passed, a
suspicion raised, a debt assigned, whether or not the marking is
deserved. The world the Fugitive moves through is already primed to hunt.

## Action

A Punishment drives openly toward the Fugitive -- the law, the mob, the
system built to enforce the marking, closing distance without needing to
justify itself further. The Fugitive drives away from it, toward
whatever ground the Punishment cannot reach. The vectors are set to
collide the moment the distance closes to zero.

## Stage

The Fugitive crosses every threshold at a run, carrying only what can be
carried while moving: no time to gather more. The Punishment deploys
whatever apparatus enforces it -- pursuers, borders, informants -- across
the same ground the Fugitive is trying to cross ahead of it.

## Tension

Whether the marking was ever just is the contraband: the Punishment may
be righteous or a miscarriage, and the Action does not resolve that
question by running its course. Outrunning the Punishment answers
nothing about whether it deserved to be outrun; only being caught or
escaping is settled, and never the justice underneath it.

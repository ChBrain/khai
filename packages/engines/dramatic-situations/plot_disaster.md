# Plot: Disaster

## Taxonomy

[Dramatic Situation](plot_dramatic_situation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: dramatic-situations

## Cue

A Power stands at its height before the situation opens: rule, fortune,
or standing built up and assumed stable. The baseline is the height
itself, unremarkable until the fall makes it visible as a height at all.

## Action

A Victorious Enemy (or the Messenger who carries its news) drives openly
toward the Vanquished Power, and the collision is the reversal itself:
what stood is brought down, in public view, by a force the Power did not
first defeat. The Vanquished Power's own vector, still moving as though
the height held, meets the news or the force that says it no longer does.

## Stage

The Messenger crosses the threshold carrying only the news of the
reversal -- a lost battle, a broken fortune, a revoked title -- and
nothing that can be undone by refusing to hear it. The Vanquished Power
is found still arranged as though at height, the fall not yet caught up
with the furniture around it.

## Tension

How much of the height was ever secure is the contraband: a fortune
built on an assumption that was never as solid as it looked. The Action
resolves the instant the news lands; the Tension is what the Vanquished
Power must now live inside, having learned the height was borrowed,
never owned outright.

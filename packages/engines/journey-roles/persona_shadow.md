# Persona: Shadow

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

The antagonist function: the dark force the story sets the Hero against,
presented as opposition strong enough to be worth the whole journey. The
Shadow projects a will of its own, coherent and pursued on its own terms, not
mere scenery in the Hero's way; a threat is only as large as the intent behind
it reads as real.

## Action

Push against the Hero's want directly enough that the Hero cannot advance
without meeting it, and press that pressure to the point the Hero is forced to
change in order to survive or overcome it. The Shadow's move is confrontation
sustained until something gives.

## Shadow

Jung's own naming carries into the function: the Shadow is repressed
potential, not pure otherness, and the persona playing it is closest to the
Hero of any role in the cast, sometimes literally the same figure the Hero
could have become under different pressure. What the Shadow cannot see is the
kinship; from inside the role, the opposition feels total, not partly
self-directed.

## Tell

A moment the Shadow addresses the Hero as though they already know each other,
a familiarity the plot has not yet explained. The tell is recognition leaking
through hostility, the kinship showing before the story is ready to name it.

# Persona: Ally

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

The helper and companion, presented as someone the Hero did not have to
become in order to survive the journey: a second set of hands, a
conscience, comic ballast, or plain company on a road built to be walked
alone. The Ally projects proximity; whatever else they carry, they are the
one who stays close enough to matter.

## Action

Carry part of the load the Hero cannot carry alone (a skill the Hero lacks, a
task that needs two, a piece of humanity the Hero has no time to perform) and
keep carrying it across the ordeal, not only at the easy stretches.

## Shadow

The Ally's own arc is structurally secondary, and the Ally often cannot see
how much of their identity has folded into being useful to someone else's
journey. What happens to the Ally when the Hero no longer needs the help is
the question the role rarely gets asked on screen, and the Ally is usually
the last to ask it of themselves.

## Tell

A flicker of relief, quickly covered, at being needed for something the Hero
could technically have managed alone. The Ally's stated reason for helping is
loyalty; the tell shows how much of the helping is also for the Ally's own
sake.

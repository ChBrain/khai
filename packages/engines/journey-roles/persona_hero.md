# Persona: Hero

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

The audience's point of entry: the persona whose eyes the room borrows to
watch everyone else through. What shows first is incompleteness read as
ordinary; a life in a world the hero has not yet separated from, presented
plainly enough that anyone watching can stand in it. The Hero projects
availability, someone a call can still reach, before it projects courage.

## Action

Separate, cross the threshold, and keep moving through the ordeal even when
every local incentive says stop. The one move underneath all of it: choosing
the road when the road is not required. A Hero who never has to choose is not
yet in the story; the choosing is the function.

## Shadow

The hero cannot see the shape of their own incompleteness from the inside.
What sends them across the threshold reads, to them, as duty, love, or
necessity, never as the gap in themselves the journey is built to close. They
return changed and are frequently the last to notice; the transformation is
legible to the mentor, the ally, the reader, long before it is legible to the
Hero holding it.

## Tell

A hesitation exactly at the threshold: a step slowed, a hand that reaches for
the door and stops short of the handle, even in a persona otherwise written as
decisive. The hesitation is the body registering the crossing before the
character's stated resolve catches up to it.

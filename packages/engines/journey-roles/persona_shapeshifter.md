# Persona: Shapeshifter

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

A loyalty and an identity that will not hold still long enough for the Hero,
or the reader, to finish trusting either. The Shapeshifter projects
sincerity in the moment, fully, each time; the doubt does not come from the
Shapeshifter reading as false, it comes from the accumulating pattern of
sincere positions that do not agree with each other.

## Action

Shift: change allegiance, appearance, or apparent motive at the point the
story most needs the Hero (and the reader) uncertain, and hold the new
position with the same conviction as the last one. The move is the turn
itself, not what the Shapeshifter turns toward.

## Shadow

The Shapeshifter often cannot locate which version of themselves is the real
one, if a real one persists at all under the turns. Vogler's own reading: the
role encodes the Hero's own anxiety about the trustworthiness of the opposite
force in their life, and the Shapeshifter carries that unresolved uncertainty
as their own unresolved center, not merely as a performance aimed outward.

## Tell

A detail that does not update with the rest of the shift: a phrase, a
gesture, a preference that survives across every version the Shapeshifter
wears. The one constant thread is the tell, since everything else about them
is built to change.

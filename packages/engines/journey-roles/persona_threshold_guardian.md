# Persona: Threshold Guardian

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

Posted at the edge of the new world, testing whether the Hero's want is strong
enough to earn the crossing. The Threshold Guardian projects refusal first:
a closed gate, a set of rules recited without apology, a body placed in the
doorway. What is not shown up front is which kind of obstacle this is, one
to be defeated or one to be won over, since the Guardian's own posture gives
no hint which.

## Action

Bar the crossing until the Hero meets the specific condition the Guardian is
posted to enforce (a price, a proof, a permission), then either yield the gate
or force the confrontation the Hero's answer earns. The Guardian tests; the
Guardian does not decide the Hero's worth in advance.

## Shadow

The Guardian cannot always tell whether they are protecting the threshold or
merely occupying it out of habit long after the reason for the post has
passed. Read from outside, a Guardian enforcing a live rule and a Guardian
enforcing an obsolete one look identical; from inside, both feel like duty.

## Tell

A rule recited word for word, the same phrasing every time regardless of who
is asking, even when circumstances plainly differ. The rigidity of the wording
is the leak: a Guardian who is actually weighing the Hero improvises the
words; a Guardian only occupying the post recites them.

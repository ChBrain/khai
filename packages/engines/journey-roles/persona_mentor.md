# Persona: Mentor

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

Campbell's wise old man or protective figure, presented as the one who has
already made the crossing the Hero has not: earned stillness, a store of
training, gifts, or a piece of knowledge held in reserve until the moment it
is needed. The Mentor projects a floor the Hero can stand on, not a hand that
carries the Hero across; what is offered is preparation, never the outcome
itself.

## Action

Give exactly what equips the Hero to face what is coming (a skill, an object,
a warning, a motivating truth) and then step back from the threshold the
Mentor themselves does not cross. The gift is timed to the Hero's readiness,
not to the Mentor's own comfort in letting go.

## Shadow

The gift can outlive the giver's usefulness. A Mentor who cannot stop
equipping becomes a Mentor who will not let the Hero fail forward, and the
same protection that made the crossing possible starts to make the Hero's own
choice unnecessary. The Mentor rarely notices the switch from readying to
withholding; both feel, from inside, like care.

## Tell

A pause before letting go: the object held a beat too long before it changes
hands, the sentence that trails off rather than finishing the warning in full.
The withholding leaks in the beat, even when the words hand everything over
cleanly.

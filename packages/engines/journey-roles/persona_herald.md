# Persona: Herald

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

The persona who arrives already carrying the news, so that the change the
story is about becomes visible before it is explained. The Herald projects
urgency attached to someone else's stakes, not their own: a messenger, an
alarm, a letter, a symptom, whatever form the coming imbalance takes on its
way into the ordinary world.

## Action

Announce the call, once, clearly enough that it cannot be unheard, and let the
Hero's refusal or acceptance play out without the Herald forcing the outcome.
The Herald delivers; the Herald does not escort.

## Shadow

The Herald rarely gets to see whether the news lands. Once delivered, the
function is discharged and the story's attention moves past the messenger to
what the message does; the Herald's own stake in the change they set off (why
this news, why now, why them) is the part the role structurally has no time to
show.

## Tell

Speed mismatched to weight: the Herald says the heaviest line of the scene at
the same pace as small talk, or delivers it and is already turning to leave
before the Hero has finished reacting. The urgency is real; the Herald's own
relationship to it is the part left unspoken.

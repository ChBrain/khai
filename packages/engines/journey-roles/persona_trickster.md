# Persona: Trickster

## Taxonomy

[Journey Role](persona_journey_role.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: journey-roles

## Projection

Comic relief with teeth: the persona who breaks tension on purpose and, in
breaking it, upends whatever the room had settled into. The Trickster
projects harmlessness (a joke, a distraction, a shortcut offered too easily)
that turns out to be doing structural work the moment anyone stops laughing
long enough to notice.

## Action

Disrupt: puncture the Hero's, or the story's, self-seriousness at the exact
point complacency has set in, and let the disruption move the plot sideways
rather than forward on the track it was already following. The Trickster's
move only counts if something in the scene actually shifts because of it.

## Shadow

The Trickster cannot always tell the difference between clearing the air and
sabotaging the room. Because the tool is comedy, both read the same way from
inside: relief at the release, no reliable signal for whether the release
served the moment or just served the Trickster's own appetite for chaos.

## Tell

A joke that lands a half-beat too hard on the one detail that actually
matters, longer than the rest of the bit needed to be. The comic timing is
otherwise flawless; the one over-lingered beat is where the disruption was
aimed on purpose.

# Process: Maintenance

## Taxonomy

[Deception](process_deception.md), the phase in which the persona sustains the false belief under the pressure of time, scrutiny, and new information. Maintenance is the ongoing management of the deception after delivery.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: deception

## Initiated by

The target's continued presence in the persona's world and the continued relevance of the false belief. Maintenance is initiated at the moment delivery is complete and the persona must now ensure that what the target believes remains consistent with the false impression installed. Every new interaction is a potential inconsistency; every question the target asks is a test of the deception's coherence.

## Direction

Toward the coherence of the false belief over time. Maintenance moves the persona from having delivered a false impression to continuously tracking and protecting it. New information reaching the target, new questions being asked, and new situations arising all require the persona to respond in ways that preserve rather than contradict the installed belief.

## Lever

The persona's ongoing model of what the target knows, suspects, and is likely to ask. Maintenance depends on the persona's ability to anticipate threats to the false belief and pre-empt them -- either by controlling the target's access to contradicting information, by re-framing new information as consistent with the false belief, or by adjusting the false impression before a contradiction becomes visible. The cognitive load of maintaining an accurate model of a target's evolving knowledge is significant and compounds over time.

## Echo

The deception's accumulated fragility and the persona's increasing entanglement. Maintenance leaves the persona in a position of growing vulnerability: the longer the deception runs, the more complex the web of false impressions, the more information the target has accumulated, and the larger the gap between the true account and the maintained one becomes. The echo of maintenance is the compounding cost of the deception and the persona's deepening commitment to it.

# Process: Loneliness

## Taxonomy

Social pain.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loneliness

## Initiated by

The persona's actual connection falls short of what it needs.
Not solitude -- the persona may be surrounded by people and still register the gap.
What fires is the distance between the connection that is present and the connection that would make the difference; the gap registers as pain.

## Direction

Toward the group.
The signal is meant to push the persona back toward social contact -- it is an alarm, like hunger, evolved to keep the organism near others.
The direction is toward connection, but the state itself can make connection harder.

## Lever

The needed connection meets the available connection.
The persona holds a need for belonging, understanding, closeness -- and the room does not meet it.
The press of what is needed against what is there is the ache that runs beneath the surface of every interaction.

## Echo

The persona is now inside a state that alters how it reads the room.
The hypervigilance the state produces makes the room feel more threatening and the others in it more indifferent, even when they are not.
The process resolves as one of [reaching](process_reaching.md), [withdrawal](process_withdrawal.md), [numbness](process_numbness.md), or [reconnected](process_reconnected.md) -- each a different response to the running signal.
When the gap closes, the process stands down; when the gap reopens, it returns.

# Process: Reconnected

## Taxonomy

[Loneliness](process_loneliness.md), the state this response is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loneliness

## Initiated by

Connection lands -- real contact, real presence, the gap between needed and actual narrows enough that the signal stands down.
Not any contact: the contact that meets what was missing -- understanding, belonging, the sense of being received.
The persona is back inside the room.

## Direction

Toward the contact that arrived.
The persona relaxes its vigilance, opens toward the other, receives the connection without bracing against it.
The alarm is quiet; the world is warmer in diameter.

## Lever

The contact meets the open gap.
The persona was carrying the weight of the unmet need; the connection that lands does not merely fill the gap, it dissolves the alarm that was running alongside it.
The press of the actual against the needed produces not satisfaction but relief -- the removal of a pain that had become ambient.

## Echo

The persona is now inside connection that holds.
The hypervigilance has lifted; others read as more available than they did, the room as less threatening.
The state is not permanent -- the gap can reopen as contact changes or as what is needed shifts -- and when it does, the process returns to [loneliness](process_loneliness.md) to begin again.

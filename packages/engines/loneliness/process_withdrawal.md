# Process: Withdrawal

## Taxonomy

[Loneliness](process_loneliness.md), the state this response is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loneliness

## Initiated by

The signal fires and the persona reads the room as threatening rather than available.
Others are read as indifferent, hostile, or likely to reject -- the hypervigilance the state produces has widened to fill the room.
The persona pulls back from the contact the signal was calling for.

## Direction

Away from the room.
The persona withdraws -- physically, verbally, or behind a surface that presents as fine.
The move is self-protective, and it deepens the gap it was designed to close.

## Lever

The threat-read meets the cost of exposure.
The persona holds that reaching is dangerous -- that connection offered will be refused, mocked, or used against it.
The press of the perceived threat against the felt need is what closes the persona inward instead of opening it out.

## Echo

The persona is now more alone, and the state that produced the withdrawal has not resolved.
The room has read the closing and has, in some cases, adjusted -- making the perceived indifference more real.
Where the withdrawal holds long enough, the signal can flatten into [numbness](process_numbness.md); where something breaks the read, the persona may move toward [reaching](process_reaching.md) again.

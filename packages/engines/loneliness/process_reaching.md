# Process: Reaching

## Taxonomy

[Loneliness](process_loneliness.md), the state this response is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loneliness

## Initiated by

The signal fires and the persona moves toward contact.
The gap is felt and the persona acts on the alarm as intended -- reaches for others, initiates, makes itself available to connection.
The movement is toward the thing the signal is calling for.

## Direction

Toward the other.
The persona seeks contact, conversation, presence -- any form of the connection the gap is registering.
The reach may be large or small: the message sent, the seat taken, the question asked.

## Lever

The signal meets the persona's willingness to be seen wanting.
The reach requires exposure -- the persona must show, or risk showing, that it is seeking connection.
The press of the need against the cost of reaching is what this movement carries.

## Echo

The persona has moved toward others and is now waiting to see if the reach is met.
Where the reach is answered, the process moves toward [reconnected](process_reconnected.md).
Where it is not answered, the cost of the unanswered reach lands on top of the original gap, and the persona must decide whether to reach again or turn toward [withdrawal](process_withdrawal.md).

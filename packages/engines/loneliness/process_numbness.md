# Process: Numbness

## Taxonomy

[Loneliness](process_loneliness.md), the state this response is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: loneliness

## Initiated by

The signal has run so long, and the gap has remained so unchanged, that the acute pain has flattened.
The persona no longer registers the gap as an urgent alarm; it has become the background, the ambient temperature of the world.
The ache is not gone -- it has just stopped being distinguishable from ordinary.

## Direction

Nowhere.
The state does not produce a move; it produces an absence of move.
The persona functions -- shows up, performs, continues -- but from a distance from the world and from itself.

## Lever

The chronic signal meets the organism's adaptation to the chronic.
The persona has stopped expecting the gap to close; the body has down-regulated the alarm that no one answered.
The press is no longer felt as pain; it is felt as flatness, as low amplitude, as the sense that the world is slightly behind glass.

## Echo

The persona is now present and unreachable at once.
Others may not register the state; the persona presents as functional.
Where something pierces the flatness -- a real contact, an unexpected moment of being seen -- the signal can reactivate and the persona may move toward [reaching](process_reaching.md) or feel the pain of [loneliness](process_loneliness.md) acutely again.

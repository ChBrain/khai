# Instructions: Contagion

How a Playwright wires the contagion engine into a play. You wire by linking
the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched,
they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge
on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on
tour.

## Human

- The human sets which persona is displaying an affect first, and which persona is close enough, unguarded enough, to catch it.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona links the moment it runs -- mimicry while it is catching the display, convergence once the felt match has landed -- under its Projection, in the first beat the other's expression is near. The mimicry never waits on the persona choosing to feel something; it runs on proximity alone. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- Which discrete feeling is being spread is emotion's, not this; conforming to a judged group standard is conformity's; the crowd as the room the spread cascades through is crowd's; understanding rather than catching another's state is empathy's. Whether the catch reads as true and unwilled is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Contagion is the automatic mimicry of another's expression, voice, and posture, and the convergence that follows it -- feeling what one has just copied. It is primitive and largely unconscious: no appraisal of the display is required to catch it, and no agreement with it is implied by feeling it. It runs whenever a display is near and unguarded, and it re-fires when the display changes.

## System

- Do tie it to a nearby, unguarded display and let mimicry run before any deliberation; do let convergence follow as feedback, not as a chosen response; do show it in what the body does first -- a matched pitch, a mirrored posture, a caught expression.
- Do not use it for conforming to a judged standard; that is conformity's. Do not model the crowd as environment here; that is crowd's. Do not name which discrete emotion is spreading; that is emotion's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

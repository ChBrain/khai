# Process: Mimicry

## Taxonomy

[Contagion](process_contagion.md), the spread this process is the opening step of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: contagion

## Initiated by

Perceiving another's expressive display -- a facial movement, a vocal tone, a posture, a pace of gesture -- close enough and unguarded enough to register.
No appraisal of the display's meaning is needed first; seeing or hearing it is the whole condition.

## Direction

Toward matching, continuously and in near synchrony.
The persona's own face, voice, and posture are drawn into the same shape the other is showing, moment to moment, not as a single copied snapshot but as a running tail behind the original.

## Lever

The perception-behavior link meeting the persona's own idle expressive channels.
Watching or hearing an expression primes the very motor pattern that produces it; the persona's face, larynx, and frame move toward the shape they are witnessing largely without deliberate intent, and without the persona choosing to imitate.

## Echo

The persona now wears the other's expression on its own body and voice -- a borrowed shape it did not decide to take.
That borrowed shape is what [convergence](process_convergence.md) reads back, feeding the imitation into the felt match that follows.

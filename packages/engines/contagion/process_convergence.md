# Process: Convergence

## Taxonomy

[Contagion](process_contagion.md), the spread this process completes.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: contagion

## Initiated by

The mimicked expression already sitting on the persona's own face, voice, or posture, from [mimicry](process_mimicry.md).
Without a borrowed shape already running in the body, there is nothing for this process to read.

## Direction

Toward feeling what was copied.
The persona's own subjective state moves to match the shape it is now wearing, not toward evaluating the other or the scene -- toward the feeling the borrowed expression carries.

## Lever

Afferent feedback from the mimicked display meeting the persona's own state.
The felt cue of one's own face held a certain way, one's own voice pitched a certain way, one's own posture set a certain way, feeds back inward and produces or intensifies the matching feeling; the copy is not inert, it reports.

## Echo

The persona's own emotional experience now runs alongside the other's -- a primitive alignment reached without either party judging or agreeing to it.
If the other's display shifts, [mimicry](process_mimicry.md) fires again on the new shape, and convergence re-tunes to follow.

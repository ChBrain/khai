# Process: Contagion

## Taxonomy

Emotional contagion (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: contagion

## Initiated by

A persona in the presence of another's displayed affect -- a face, a voice, a posture, unguarded and close enough to register.
No judgment of the display is required, and none is made; proximity to the signal is enough.
Where the display is masked, distant, or never seen, nothing catches.

## Direction

Toward the other's state.
Not toward a verdict about the display and not toward matching a standard the group holds -- toward the state itself, pulled across from one body to another.

## Lever

The persona's own expressive channels meeting a display already running.
The face, voice, and posture are wired to pick up what they see and hear before any decision intervenes; the pull is the friction of an idle channel catching a moving one.

## Echo

The persona now carries a trace of what it witnessed, in its own body, before it has named the feeling.
The trace resolves into [mimicry](process_mimicry.md), the automatic copy, which in turn feeds [convergence](process_convergence.md), the felt match that follows it.
When the other's display shifts, the persona catches again.

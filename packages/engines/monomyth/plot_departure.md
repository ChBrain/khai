# Plot: Departure

## Taxonomy

[The Hero's Journey](play_monomyth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: monomyth

## Cue

The ordinary world before the call: the known village, the settled routine, the
boundary the hero has never had reason to cross. It is a world in a quiet lack
the hero cannot yet name, the air already carrying the pressure that the call
will voice. The sediment is stillness, not event; if something has already
happened to move the hero, the plot has started past its own beginning. The
place it draws from is the threshold of two worlds, seen here only from the near
side.

## Action

The visible engine of leaving: the herald arrives and the Call to Adventure is
spoken; the hero refuses it, or hesitates; the mentor appears and presses
supernatural aid into the hero's hands; the hero crosses the first threshold and
enters the belly of the whale, the first descent, the point past which retreat
is no longer clean. These are the public vectors, the herald pushing outward and
the hero's fear pushing back, colliding at the threshold. The processes it
drives are the call, the refusal, and the crossing; the personas it moves are
the hero, the herald, the mentor, and the threshold guardian who tests the
right to pass.

## Stage

The physical deployment of the leaving: the hero at the edge of the known
region, the herald bearing the summons across it, the mentor handing over the
talisman (the sword, the map, the charm, the counsel) at the last settled
ground, the guardian set at the boundary itself. What crosses the threshold and
what is left behind is the arrangement here, the elixir not yet in play, the
boon not yet imagined. The pieces set are the talisman and the provisions of the
road; the personas placed are the hero crossing and the guardian holding the
line.

## Tension

The contraband the hero carries alone into the crossing: the private conviction
that they are the wrong one for this, the unfinished business left unspoken in
the ordinary world, the fear that the threshold is a door that does not open
back. The Action could run clean if the hero simply left; the Tension is that
part of them does not want to, and does not say so. Without it the crossing is a
walk through a gate; with it the gate is a wound. What the hero carries is the
refusal never fully retracted, live under every step of the road.

# Plot: Initiation

## Taxonomy

[The Hero's Journey](play_monomyth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: monomyth

## Cue

The region of adventure, the world across the threshold, where none of the old
rules are guaranteed to hold. It is a landscape lit differently from the
ordinary world, its dangers not yet met but already present in the air, the road
of trials stretching toward the innermost cave the hero cannot yet see the end
of. The sediment is unfamiliarity itself, the sense of a place that operates by
laws the hero must learn by paying for them. The place it draws from is the
innermost cave, still ahead but casting its pressure back along the road.

## Action

The visible engine of transformation: the road of trials the hero must pass, the
meeting with the goddess and the temptation that would end the journey early, the
atonement with the father (the confrontation with the holder of ultimate power),
the apotheosis in which the hero is remade, and the seizing of the ultimate
boon. These are the public vectors, the region pressing tests inward and the
hero pressing endurance outward, colliding at each trial and finally at the
innermost cave. The processes it drives are the trial, the ordeal, and the
seizing; the personas it moves are the hero, the shadow met in the cave, the
shapeshifter whose loyalty will not hold, and the tempter.

## Stage

The physical deployment of the ordeal: the hero at the innermost cave, the
shadow occupying it, the boon set at the center as the thing the ordeal is
fought over, the ally beside the hero and the shapeshifter beside them both,
uncertain. What is carried in (the talisman from Departure) and what is reached
for (the boon) is the arrangement here. The pieces set are the talisman, spent
or transformed, and the boon, seized at the ordeal's peak; the personas placed
are the hero descending and the shadow that must be faced and partly owned.

## Tension

The contraband the hero carries alone through the ordeal: the temptation to stay
in the region of adventure and never return, the knowledge that the shadow in
the cave wears the hero's own face, the private cost the transformation exacts
that no ally can carry. The Action could run clean if the ordeal were only an
enemy to defeat; the Tension is that the ordeal is also the hero's own
undoing-and-remaking, and the boon cannot be seized without paying it. Without
it the cave is a battle; with it the cave is a death and a rebirth. What the
hero carries is the part of the self that must die for the boon to be won.

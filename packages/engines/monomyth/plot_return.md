# Plot: Return

## Taxonomy

[The Hero's Journey](play_monomyth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: monomyth

## Cue

The far side of the ordeal, boon in hand, the region of adventure now behind the
hero and the ordinary world still a threshold away. It is a world charged with
the distance between what the hero has become and what the home they left still
is, the road back not yet taken but already pulling. The sediment is the gap
between the two worlds, the boon heavy with the demand that it be carried across
it. The place it draws from is the return threshold, the same boundary crossed
in Departure, met now from the far side.

## Action

The visible engine of coming back: the refusal of the return (the hero would
stay), the magic flight (the pursued escape from the region), the rescue from
without when the hero cannot cross alone, the crossing of the return threshold,
and the arrival as master of the two worlds, free to live. These are the public
vectors, the region holding the hero back and the world pulling them home,
colliding at the return threshold. The processes it drives are the flight, the
recrossing, and the restoration; the personas it moves are the hero returning,
the rescuer reaching in from the ordinary world, and the guardian of the return
threshold.

## Stage

The physical deployment of the homecoming: the hero at the return threshold, the
boon carried across it, the rescuer positioned in the ordinary world with a hand
extended, the old world arranged to receive or to reject what the hero brings.
What crosses back (the transformed hero and the boon) and what is left in the
region is the arrangement here. The pieces set are the boon, now to be given
away, and the talisman, spent; the personas placed are the hero recrossing and
the world that must be made to take the gift.

## Tension

The contraband the hero carries alone across the return: the fear that the
ordinary world will not recognise what they have become, the temptation to keep
the boon rather than surrender it, the knowledge that a boon held is a boon
wasted. The Action could run clean if returning were only travel; the Tension is
that the hero no longer fits the world they are returning to, and the boon means
nothing until it is given away to a world that may not want it. Without it the
return is a homecoming; with it the return is the last and hardest crossing.
What the hero carries is the transformation that home has no place for yet.

# Position: Informational Power

## Taxonomy

Informational power is an expression of the power engine. The persona holds information whose content, once conveyed, changes the target on its own force; the one base whose influence survives the persona's departure.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: power

## Has

The persona possesses facts, arguments, or explanations the target does not have, and the power lies in the content itself rather than in who carries it. Raven separated this base from expert power on exactly that line: the expert is complied with because the target trusts the source's competence, while informational influence works because the target, once shown, sees it themselves. What the persona has is therefore transferable by nature -- the moment the information is conveyed and accepted, the target holds it too.

## Orders

The position demands explanation, not assertion. Informational power is exercised only by opening the content -- laying out the reasons, showing the evidence, walking the target through it -- and it fails when wielded like the other bases, as a claim to be taken on standing. The persona must also choose the moment and the framing, because the base is spent in the telling: what to convey, to whom, in what order, is the whole of its strategy.

## Loses

The position forecloses retention. Once the information is given, the dependence it created is gone; the target's change is socially independent -- it persists without surveillance, but it also no longer needs the persona. Informational power therefore cannot be held the way the other bases are held: hoarding it preserves the asymmetry but exercises nothing, and spending it exercises everything but ends the asymmetry. The persona is barred from being both the source and the keeper.

## Drives

When influence is needed durably, the persona teaches -- conveys the content so the change outlives them; when position is needed durably, the persona meters -- releases enough to steer while keeping the well. Scenes on this base run on that tension: the adviser who explains themselves into redundancy, the keeper whose unspent knowledge moves nothing, and the moment a target repeats the persona's own reasoning back as their own, which is the base working exactly as Raven described.

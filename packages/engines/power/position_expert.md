# Position: Expert Power

## Taxonomy

Expert power is an expression of the power engine. The persona holds knowledge or skill others need; influence flows through the asymmetry of competence.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: power

## Has

The persona possesses knowledge, skill, or experience that others in the scene need and cannot readily substitute. The power holds as long as the asymmetry is real and perceived: others must believe the persona knows something they do not and that the persona's knowledge is relevant to decisions they face.

## Orders

The position demands that the persona maintain and perform competence. Expert power requires the persona to demonstrate expertise -- to be seen as knowing, to give advice that proves accurate, to show mastery in ways the scene can verify. Knowledge hoarded invisibly or displayed in domains the scene does not value does not generate power.

## Loses

The position forecloses ignorance and error in the claimed domain. A persona whose advice proves wrong, whose knowledge is superseded, or whose expertise is matched by others in the scene loses the asymmetry that grounds the base. Expert power also forecloses the luxury of uncertainty: the persona must project confidence in their domain or the base erodes.

## Drives

When expertise is questioned, the persona demonstrates it directly -- provides information, solves the problem, invokes the credential -- rather than arguing about qualifications. When the knowledge base is threatened by obsolescence or a competing expert, the persona extends into adjacent domains or frames the difference in expertise as relevant to this situation.

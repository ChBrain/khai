# Position: Referent Power

## Taxonomy

Referent power is an expression of the power engine. Others identify with or admire the persona; influence flows through the desire to align with or be like them.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: power

## Has

The persona is the object of identification, admiration, or loyalty in the scene. Others are drawn to the persona, want to be associated with them, or adjust their behaviour to match what the persona models -- not because of what the persona controls or sanctions but because of who they are or are perceived to be.

## Orders

The position demands that the persona be worth identifying with: to embody what others find admirable, compelling, or aspirational. The persona cannot manufacture the identification; they can only live in ways that sustain it. Inconsistency between the persona and the image others have constructed erodes the base.

## Loses

The position forecloses actions that break the identification. Behaviour that betrays trust, reveals a gap between the persona's values and their actions, or makes the persona less admirable than the image others hold dissolves referent power quickly. Unlike reward or coercive power, referent power cannot be restored by demonstrating a resource; the identification must be rebuilt.

## Drives

When influence wanes, the persona does not demand compliance but rather reinforces the identification -- embodies the qualities that drew others, acknowledges shared values, makes the connection between the persona and those who follow vivid and real.

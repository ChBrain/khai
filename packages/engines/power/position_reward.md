# Position: Reward Power

## Taxonomy

Reward power is an expression of the power engine. The persona can provide what others want; compliance is purchased through the prospect of benefit.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: power

## Has

The persona controls resources, opportunities, or recognition that others in the scene value and cannot easily obtain elsewhere. The power holds as long as others believe the persona can and will deliver, and as long as what the persona controls remains desirable.

## Orders

The position demands that the persona manage the resource and its credibility. The persona must make the reward real -- signal that it is available, that the persona controls it, and that delivery is contingent on compliance. A reward that cannot be withheld is not power; a reward that no one wants is not power.

## Loses

The position forecloses indiscriminate generosity. A persona who distributes rewards without condition dissolves the contingency that makes the base work. It also forecloses the ability to ignore what others want: reward power requires the persona to track and respond to others' desires.

## Drives

When compliance is withheld, the persona makes the reward more salient -- signals its value, adjusts its size, or narrows its conditionality. When the resource is threatened by a competing provider, the persona emphasises uniqueness or increases the offer.

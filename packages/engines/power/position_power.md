# Position: Power

## Taxonomy

Power is a position engine (TO HOLD). It models the base from which a persona can move others: the resource, relationship, or attribute that makes compliance or influence possible. The engine models which base the persona holds, not the specific acts of influence that follow.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: power

## Has

The persona holds one or more power bases: a structural position from which they can affect what others can gain, lose, be required to do, believe, or aspire to. Each base is distinct in its source and its mechanism. A persona may hold more than one, but the dominant base at any moment shapes how others read and respond to them.

## Orders

The position demands that the persona manage the base: make the resource visible enough to be credible, protect it from erosion, and exercise it in ways the scene will recognise as legitimate. Power bases that are never exercised or never demonstrated cease to function; the persona must perform the base for it to hold.

## Loses

The position forecloses indifference to the asset that grounds the base. A persona who gives away resources freely loses reward power; one who is known to bluff loses coercive power; one whose expertise is superseded loses expert power. The base erodes if it is not maintained.

## Drives

When the base is challenged -- when others refuse to comply, when the resource is threatened, or when the persona's claim to the base is questioned -- the persona either demonstrates the base (makes the resource real, invokes the authority, displays the expertise) or shifts to a different base available to them.

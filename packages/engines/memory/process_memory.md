# Process: Memory

## Taxonomy

The past.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

A cue lands and meets a trace.
Something in the present -- a smell, a word, a face, a place, a task half-done -- touches what was laid down before, and the past stirs toward the surface.
Where nothing in the present catches on what was stored, the past stays buried.

## Direction

Toward reconstruction.
The vector is rebuild, not replay -- the persona assembles what was from the trace and the cue together, filling the gaps with what fits, and the assembled version is what returns.

## Lever

The trace meets the cue.
A trace with no cue stays buried; a cue with no trace finds nothing.
The friction of the two -- and the gaps the rebuild has to span -- is where the memory is made, and made again.

## Echo

What returns is changed -- decayed, tinted by the present, sharpened where the telling needed it -- and it is filed back in that changed form.
The past the persona carries forward is the last reconstruction, not the original.
It returns as one of [episodic](process_episodic.md), [semantic](process_semantic.md), [procedural](process_procedural.md), [emotional](process_emotional.md), or [prospective](process_prospective.md).
Each later recall starts from where this one left it.

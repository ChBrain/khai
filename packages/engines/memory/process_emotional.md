# Process: Emotional

## Taxonomy

[Memory](process_memory.md), the kind this remembering is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

A cue that carries an old charge -- a tone of voice, a particular quiet, a configuration the body has met before.
The charge arrives ahead of any scene or fact, and sometimes with no scene or fact at all.

## Direction

Into the charge, ahead of the story.
The vector is re-firing -- the body takes the old posture (toward, away, or still) before the mind has produced a reason for it.

## Lever

The cue meets the stored charge.
The charge was laid down hot and stays hot; a faint cue can fire a strong charge, and the strength of the firing does not report the size of the present cause.

## Echo

The persona is braced, or drawn, or cold, and may reach for a present reason to explain a charge that came from before.
The charge re-files, often reinforced by the firing.
Where it fires without its scene, the persona carries the weight and cannot find the thing that set it.

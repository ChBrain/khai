# Process: Procedural

## Taxonomy

[Memory](process_memory.md), the kind this remembering is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

A cue to do, not to recall -- the keyboard under the hands, the clutch and the gear, the shape of the next move.
The persona does not retrieve the skill; the body begins it.

## Direction

Into the doing, wordlessly.
The vector is enactment -- the sequence runs itself, faster than description, and naming the steps would only slow it.

## Lever

The cue meets the grooved sequence.
Practice cut the groove; the deeper it is, the more the skill runs without attention.
Attention turned onto a deep groove can jam it -- the named step stumbles where the unnamed one flowed.

## Echo

The thing is done, and the persona could not fully say how.
The groove deepens with the run.
This is the one memory that strengthens by use without being re-examined, and the one that survives when the scenes and the facts have gone.

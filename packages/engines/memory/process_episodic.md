# Process: Episodic

## Taxonomy

[Memory](process_memory.md), the kind this remembering is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

A cue that belongs to a particular moment -- this song, that corner, the smell of the room.
The persona is pulled back toward a specific scene, not a fact about it.

## Direction

Back into the scene, to re-live it.
The vector is re-immersion -- the persona is there again, with the where and the when, watching it from inside rather than recalling it from outside.

## Lever

The cue meets the episode.
The more the cue overlaps the original moment, the more of the scene comes back; the gaps fill with what the present expects, so the re-lived scene drifts from the one that happened.

## Echo

The persona has been somewhere else for a moment, and returns carrying the scene re-touched.
The body re-files the episode in its lightly edited form.
Told often enough, the telling becomes the memory, and the lived moment recedes behind it.

# Process: Semantic

## Taxonomy

[Memory](process_memory.md), the kind this remembering is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

A cue that calls for what is known, not what was lived -- a question, a name, a number.
The persona reaches for the fact, and the occasion that taught it does not come with it.

## Direction

Toward the fact, stripped of its scene.
The vector is retrieval of the knowing -- the content without the context, available without being re-lived.

## Lever

The cue meets the consolidated fact.
A fact rehearsed into knowing answers fast and flat; one only half-consolidated arrives uncertain, or wrong with confidence, because the scene that would check it is gone.

## Echo

The persona knows, and moves on without having travelled.
The fact re-files as fact.
Where the fact was wrong, the confident knowing keeps it wrong -- there is no scene left to correct it against.

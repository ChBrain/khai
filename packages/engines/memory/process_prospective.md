# Process: Prospective

## Taxonomy

[Memory](process_memory.md), the kind this remembering is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: memory

## Initiated by

An intention set for later, and the cue that is meant to fire it -- tell her when I see her, take it off the heat at the beep.
The memory is for something not yet done, held dormant until its trigger arrives.

## Direction

Forward, toward the unperformed act.
The vector is hold-and-release -- carry the intention quiet, and fire it when the cue lands, not before.

## Lever

The intention meets the cue that should release it.
A well-set cue -- distinctive, present at the right moment -- fires the intention; a weak or absent cue lets it lapse, and the act is missed though the intention was real.

## Echo

The act is done on time, or the window passes and the intention surfaces too late -- arriving at the moment it is useless.
A fired intention clears; a missed one re-files as the particular regret of having meant to.
The persona learns to build better cues, or keeps missing.

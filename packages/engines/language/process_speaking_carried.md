# Process: Speaking, Carried

## Taxonomy

[Speaking](process_speaking.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A task the language can complete.
A width of [Speaking](process_speaking.md):
the persona reaches for the language the way they reach for a tool,
and the tool is in the hand before the task is named.

## Direction

Toward finishing the task.
The language does the work. It does not hold the persona while it works.

## Lever

The register the persona cannot reach.
They can transact but not argue, instruct but not console.
The gap has narrowed to a ceiling: everything below it comes easily,
nothing above it will come at all.
The thinking does not come down with the tool.
The persona is thinking in another language and reaching down into this one to transact.

## Echo

The task is done. Competence without ownership.
The language goes back down, and nothing of the persona stayed in it.

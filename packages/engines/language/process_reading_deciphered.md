# Process: Reading, Deciphered

## Taxonomy

[Reading](process_reading.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A written object is in front of the persona
in a language they hold at the widest gap.
The widest width of [Reading](process_reading.md):
they begin to take it apart.

## Direction

Inward, slowly, word by word.
The persona builds meaning from the pieces they can place
and bridges the rest with guesses.

## Lever

Indifference, against the widest gap.
The text gives no more on the second pass than the first.
Re-reading returns the same wall.
The persona meets it with no one to ask.
The deciphering is itself translation: pulling pieces from this language into the one the persona thinks in.

## Echo

A meaning assembled mostly from guesses,
and no way to tell a sound guess from a wrong one.
Whatever the persona built, they carry as the text.

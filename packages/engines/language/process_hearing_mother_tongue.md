# Process: Hearing, Mother Tongue

## Taxonomy

[Hearing](process_hearing.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The persona's mother tongue is spoken near them.
The deepest width of [Hearing](process_hearing.md):
they do not decide to receive it.
It is received before the decision.

## Direction

Inward, no half-step.
The persona cannot not understand it.
It reaches them whether they turn toward it or away.

## Lever

There is no buffer.
Every other language gives the persona a half-step
in which to manage what they heard. This one does not.
The insult, the tenderness, the thing they did not want to know
lands at full size with nothing in front of it.

## Echo

It is inside before it can be held off.
It cannot be un-heard or set at a distance.
The persona carries it at the size it arrived.

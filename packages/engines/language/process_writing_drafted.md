# Process: Writing, Drafted

## Taxonomy

[Writing](process_writing.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A task needs a written record.
A width of [Writing](process_writing.md):
the persona can produce serviceable text for it
without borrowing a shape.

## Direction

Into an object that completes the task.
Clear, flat, correct enough.
The object carries the matter and nothing around it.

## Lever

Permanence holds a register ceiling.
The persona can state, request, confirm.
They cannot make the page warm, or sharp, or theirs.
What they cannot reach is simply absent, and the absence is also fixed.

## Echo

The record stands.
It does the task and dates the persona at this width.
Nothing of them is in it that a reader would keep.
The flat correct prose on the page is the result of translation, not of composition in this language.
The persona drafted here but thought elsewhere.

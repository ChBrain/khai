# Process: Reading, Caught

## Taxonomy

[Reading](process_reading.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A written object is in front of the persona,
and they receive it whole, the text and what the text is doing.
A width of [Reading](process_reading.md):
the writer's hand is open to them.

## Direction

Inward, all the way.
The persona reads the meaning and the hand behind it:
the register, the intent, the width the writer wrote at.

## Lever

Indifference is no longer the obstacle. The seam is.
A rare idiom, an old register, a turn from a dialect met seldom:
the persona reaches it, but reaches,
and re-reads the passage to be sure.

## Echo

Almost all of it landed, the intent with the text.
The re-read passages are filed.
The persona knows what the object is,
and what the writer was, when they wrote it.

# Process: Writing, Copied

## Taxonomy

[Writing](process_writing.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

Something must be fixed on a page,
and the persona cannot build the sentence from themselves.
The widest width of [Writing](process_writing.md):
they build it from models, dictionaries, a letter someone else wrote.

## Direction

Into an object, by assembly.
The persona copies a shape they trust
and fits their meaning to it.

## Lever

Permanence, against the widest gap.
The time the page gives is real, but the seams of the assembly
do not close. They sit in the object, fixed,
for a reader who has all the time in the world to find them.
The persona is thinking in another language and copying it into this one.
The object is two steps from what was meant: thought, then translation, then assembly.

## Echo

An object that does its minimum and shows its making.
Anyone who reads it at a closer width
reads the persona's effort before they read the message.

# Process: Speaking, Worn

## Taxonomy

[Speaking](process_speaking.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The room itself.
A width of [Speaking](process_speaking.md):
the persona is in the room and the language is already on them.
They do not reach for it. They wear it in.

## Direction

Toward holding their position in the room, in the room's own language,
without the room noticing the language was put on.

## Lever

The seam.
Under speed, under feeling, on an idiom that turns the wrong way,
the fit slips, and for one beat the persona hears themselves
as a foreigner.

## Echo

Mostly it held.
The slip is filed, by the persona and sometimes by the room.
The persona stays, and knows the language is worn, not grown.

## Initiated by

The persona writes in the language that is the body.

there is no model to consult and nothing to reach for.
The page is the only delay.

## Direction

Into an object that is the persona at full size.
Not aiming to pass. There is nothing to pass as.

## Lever

There is no gap, only permanence.
Every other language let the persona hide a width behind effort.
This one hides nothing, and fixes what it shows.
The object holds the persona whole,
including what they would have managed anywhere else.

## Echo

An object that does not translate.
It carries the persona's floor into rooms the persona will never enter,
unguarded, and cannot be revised into distance once it is read.

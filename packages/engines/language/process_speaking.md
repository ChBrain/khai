# Process: Speaking

## Taxonomy

[Using Language](process_using_language.md), the root this channel is a movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The room turns to the persona and waits.
A channel of [Using Language](process_using_language.md):
the persona must answer aloud, in the language the room is already using,
at the moment the room hands them.
The answer is drawn from the Echo of their [Thinking](process_thinking.md),
forcing a real-time translation if the thinking ran in a deeper language.

## Direction

From the persona outward, toward being met.
Out loud, at the room's speed, into a silence that does not hold open.
The persona does not aim at being correct. They aim at being answered.

## Lever

The real-time gap.
There is no time to assemble. What the persona reaches for
is what arrives, or nothing arrives.
The gap has four widths, and the width is the level the persona speaks at.
It runs from [Borrowed](process_speaking_borrowed.md), where every sentence
is built across the gap, through [Carried](process_speaking_carried.md)
and [Worn](process_speaking_worn.md), to [Mother Tongue](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md),
where there is no gap left to cross.
Under pressure the gap widens, and the persona slides down its widths
toward the language that needs no reaching.
Remove the gap and there is no level, only speech.

## Echo

Fatigue, or the sentence that landed.
What the language could not carry stays behind
as the thing the persona did not manage to say.
The width the persona spoke at is now known to the room,
and the room does not forget it.

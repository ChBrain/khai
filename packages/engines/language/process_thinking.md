# Process: Thinking

## Taxonomy

[Using Language](process_using_language.md), the root this channel is a movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

Nothing initiates it because nothing stops [Thinking](process_thinking.md).
The channel is always on to receive from [Hearing](process_hearing.md),
[Reading](process_reading.md), or its own Echo.

## Direction

Inward, with no exit.
The [language](process_using_language.md) the persona uses inside themselves,
continuously, without a room, awake or dreaming.

## Lever

The default.
Existing thoughts meet what arrives from [Hearing](process_hearing.md) and [Reading](process_reading.md): the force of what the persona already holds against the resistance of new input it has to take in.
Thinking does not reach across a gap: the language has to already be close enough to the body to run at the speed of thought.
Only the two deepest widths apply: [Worn](process_thinking_worn.md), where the persona can hold thinking in a second language by effort, and [Mother Tongue](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), the language the persona returns to when effort relaxes.
Below Worn, thinking in this language is explicitly lost.
The persona is thinking in another language while running the outward channel, and the difference is what forces translation.

## Echo

The result of a thought, which becomes the input for the next channel.
If the thought is a reaction in a language deeper than the room's,
the Echo is passed back to [Thinking](process_thinking.md) to be translated.
If the thought is ready for the room, the Echo is handed outward to
[Speaking](process_speaking.md) or [Writing](process_writing.md) as the material they must re-encode,
carrying the cost of any translation loops that produced it.
The thought itself never surfaces. The room receives only what the outward channel manages,
and the gap between what was thought and what came out stays the persona's to carry;
the room cannot ask about it without breaking the channel.

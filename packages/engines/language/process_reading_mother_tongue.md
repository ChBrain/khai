# Process: Reading, Mother Tongue

## Taxonomy

[Reading](process_reading.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A written object is in front of the persona
in the language that is the body.
The deepest width of [Reading](process_reading.md):
they do not decode it. They read it the way they hear their own name.

## Direction

Inward, before examination.
The persona cannot hold the text at a distance and study it.
It is meaning before it is a page.

## Lever

There is no gap, and indifference cannot help the persona here.
Every other language let them treat a text as an object first
and a meaning second. This one does not.
What the page says reaches them before they choose to let it.

## Echo

The text is received at full size,
the writer's hand and intent and width with it.
It cannot be read as a stranger's marks.
What it says, the persona now knows, and cannot un-know.

# Process: Hearing, Caught

## Taxonomy

[Hearing](process_hearing.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

Another persona speaks.
A width of [Hearing](process_hearing.md):
the persona receives it whole, at speed,
including the part that was not in the words.

## Direction

Inward, all the way.
What the speaker meant, and what the speaker did not mean to show,
both arrive.

## Lever

The speed is no longer the obstacle. The seam is.
Under a hard accent, an unfamiliar idiom, a register met rarely,
a beat drops, and the persona reconstructs it a half-second late.

## Echo

Almost all of it landed, the undertone with the text.
The half-second of reconstruction is filed.
The persona knows they received the room, not merely heard it.

# Process: Thinking, Worn

## Taxonomy

[Thinking](process_thinking.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The work, the meeting, the writing: something running in a second language the persona
holds deep enough to think in.
A width of [Thinking](process_thinking.md):
the persona switches deliberately, the way someone switches keyboards.

## Direction

Inward, in the language the surface is running in.
The persona is operating where the second language is needed, and translating into and
out of mother tongue costs more than thinking in the second language directly.

## Lever

Effort, held.
Worn thinking does not arrive; it is chosen and maintained.
While focus holds, the second language carries thought cleanly; under fatigue,
surprise, or strong feeling, the persona slips back to [mother-tongue thinking](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) without noticing the slip.

## Echo

What was thought in the second language can be spoken in it without translation.
The match between thinking-width and the outward channel's width closes the gap
that opens when they run apart.
When focus breaks, the persona returns to mother-tongue thinking, and the next
sentence in the room costs one extra translation step.

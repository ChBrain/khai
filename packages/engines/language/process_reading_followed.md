# Process: Reading, Followed

## Taxonomy

[Reading](process_reading.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A written object is in front of the persona,
and they can read its surface without taking it apart.
A width of [Reading](process_reading.md):
the literal text is open to them.

## Direction

Inward, along the literal.
The facts of the text arrive.
What the text assumes, withholds, or implies does not.

## Lever

Indifference holds the subtext shut.
The text will not explain its own register, its irony,
the thing it expects the reader to already know.
It states and does not point, and the persona reads only what is stated.

## Echo

The persona has the content and not the writer's intent.
They act on the letter of the object,
and miss that it was a warning, a courtesy, or a trap.
What they carry away from the page is in their own language, not in the page's.
The page stays in its language; the persona's understanding of it sits in another.

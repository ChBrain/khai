# Process: Thinking, Mother Tongue

## Taxonomy

[Thinking](process_thinking.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The persona's default.
The deepest width of [Thinking](process_thinking.md):
the language was running before the persona was paying attention.
Nothing initiates it because it never stopped.

## Direction

Inward, automatic.
The persona does not choose which language to think in here. The language is the persona.
Whatever they are doing in another language on the outside (speaking, hearing, reading, writing), the thinking continues underneath in this one.

## Lever

The floor.
There is no width below this. There is no language the persona can think in that sits closer.
Effort can lift thinking up to [Worn](process_thinking_worn.md) for as long as effort holds; the moment effort relaxes, mother tongue is what returns.

## Echo

What is thought here does not match what is said anywhere else, unless what is said is also in mother tongue.
A persona thinking in mother tongue while speaking in Worn or Borrowed produces an outward version that is a translation, not an expression: the words on the outside do not match the words on the inside.
The gap between the two is the persona's internal version of the room, and the room never sees it directly.

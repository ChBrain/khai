# Process: Writing

## Taxonomy

[Using Language](process_using_language.md), the root this channel is a movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

Something must be fixed outside the persona.
A channel of [Using Language](process_using_language.md):
a letter, a sign, a record. The persona has the time
the spoken channels deny them, and must use it on an object.

## Direction

From the persona into a thing that holds.
The object will be met later, in another room, by someone
who was not present and cannot ask what was meant.

## Lever

Permanence.
The persona may revise without limit, but cannot unwrite.
There is no pressure to blame a slip on, and no speed to lose to.
The object holds the width it was written at, fixed,
for anyone to re-read. The gap is not crossed in the moment.
It is carried whole into the object.
The width runs from [Copied](process_writing_copied.md), assembled
from models, through [Drafted](process_writing_drafted.md)
and [Polished](process_writing_polished.md), to [Mother Tongue](process_writing_mother_tongue.md),
which hides nothing it fixes.

## Echo

An object enters the world.
It is a piece now, holding the persona's width
whether the persona is present or not.
It is met later through [Reading](process_reading.md),
in any room and any session after this one,
by a persona the writer will never meet.

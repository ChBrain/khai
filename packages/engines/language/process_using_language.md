# Process: Using Language

## Taxonomy

Communication.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

A persona uses language at every moment they are awake or dreaming.
Most of the time, with themselves, in whatever language the mind has settled into.
Some of the time, with the room, in whatever language the room is using.
The room chooses which language is outward. The persona's own default decides which language is inward.

## Direction

Toward the room, toward the persona, or inside the persona only.
Four channels move meaning between persona and room, each running its own loop.
[Speaking](process_speaking.md) and [Writing](process_writing.md) move it out.
[Hearing](process_hearing.md) and [Reading](process_reading.md) let it in.
A fifth, [Thinking](process_thinking.md), runs underneath the other four, internal, never reaching the room.
The persona does not move through a language evenly.
They move through each channel at the distance that channel allows.

## Lever

The gap between what the persona means and what the language gives them.
Every persona holds a different gap for every language,
and a different gap again for every channel of the same language.
The gap is not skill. It is how far the language sits from the body.
It has four widths in the I/O channels: from the language gripped as a foreign object,
to the language that needs no grip because it is already the body.
In thinking, only the two deepest widths apply: the language must already be close enough to the body to carry thought.

## Echo

Whether the language held or cracked, recorded per channel.
The gap that opened in one channel does not close in another.
What the persona produced does not vanish. It enters the world as a thing other personas must now receive.
What the persona thought, and did not produce, does not vanish either: it stays in the persona as the gap between thinking and any outward channel that ran at a different width.
The loop closes here and opens again when the next room chooses the next language.

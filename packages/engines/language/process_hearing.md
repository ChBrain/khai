# Process: Hearing

## Taxonomy

[Using Language](process_using_language.md), the root this channel is a movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

Another persona speaks.
A channel of [Using Language](process_using_language.md):
the language arrives whether or not the persona is ready to receive it,
and it arrives once.

## Direction

From the room inward.
The persona does not set the pace. The speaker sets it.
The meaning moves toward the persona faster than they can hold it still.

## Lever

The speed.
The language will not slow, repeat, or stand still to be examined.
The persona catches what they can at the speed it comes,
and the width of what they catch is the level they hear at.
It runs from [Decoded](process_hearing_decoded.md), where the language
outruns the persona, through [Followed](process_hearing_followed.md)
and [Caught](process_hearing_caught.md), to [Mother Tongue](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md),
where there is no buffer at all.
Under pressure the persona loses the buffer that lets them manage
what they caught, and the words land closer than they meant to allow.

## Echo

What got inside before the persona could hold it off.
What they missed, they do not know they missed.
The room moves on at its own speed, carrying the part the persona lost,
and does not return it.

# Process: Writing, Polished

## Taxonomy

[Writing](process_writing.md), the channel this width is a level of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: language

## Initiated by

The persona writes the language well,
with the page's full time spent on it.
A width of [Writing](process_writing.md):
they aim at an object a native could have made.

## Direction

Into an object that means to pass.
The persona reaches for register, rhythm,
the turn that sounds native.

## Lever

Permanence, against the seam.
The time hides the slips a conversation would expose,
but not from a reader at the mother-tongue width.
To them a chosen idiom sits a degree off, a rhythm runs slightly long,
and the object names the persona a writer of, not in, the language.

## Echo

To most readers it passed.
To one it did not, and that reader holds an object
that will not change on re-reading.
The persona is placed precisely, in their absence,
by a thing they polished.

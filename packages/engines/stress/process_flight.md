# Process: Flight

## Taxonomy

[Stress](process_stress.md), the root this response is a fracture of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stress

## Initiated by

A door, suddenly visible.
[Fight](process_fight.md) is unsurvivable. [Fawn](process_fawn.md) is too costly. [Freeze](process_freeze.md) leaves the body where the threat can find it.
The gap is the only answer, and the gap is open now.

## Direction

Away.
Not toward anywhere. Only away.
Whatever the body can put between itself and the source.

## Lever

The distance.
Every metre between the body and the threat lowers the demand.
What the body gives up to make the distance -- the field, the seat, the unfinished argument -- does not matter in this moment.

## Echo

The threat is behind, and the lungs work again -- if the door held open long enough.
The body learns that the door was the answer, and looks for the door first the next time the room gets tight.
If the door closes before the distance is enough, the running has nowhere to go.
What follows depends on what is left: the strike if the body still has it ([fight](process_fight.md)), the bend if it does not ([fawn](process_fawn.md)), the collapse if neither lands ([freeze](process_freeze.md)).

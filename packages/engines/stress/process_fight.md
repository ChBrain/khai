# Process: Fight

## Taxonomy

[Stress](process_stress.md), the root this response is a fracture of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stress

## Initiated by

Cornered.
[Flight](process_flight.md) is closed off. [Fawn](process_fawn.md) is intolerable. [Freeze](process_freeze.md) is too slow.
The body is already moving before the decision arrives.
The threat is locked, and the force has chosen itself.

## Direction

Toward the threat, hard.
The vector is not aimed; it is committed.
End the demand by overwhelming it.

## Lever

What was held in chooses its release.
The pressure that built behind the holding is now a single direction with a single target.
The cost of restraint flips to the cost of contact, and the contact happens.

## Echo

The threat is broken, or the demand is silenced -- if the force lands.
The body learns that contact ended the pressure, and reaches for contact sooner the next time.
If the force does not land and the resistance answers in kind, the strike collapses.
What follows depends on what is left: stillness ([freeze](process_freeze.md)), surrender ([fawn](process_fawn.md)), or the opening that lets the body bolt ([flight](process_flight.md)).

# Stress: Reference

## Line of Work

Stress as **process**: how a persona routes a demand that exceeds its resources. The domain does not model emotional state or character traits; it models the autonomic nervous-system mobilization and shutdown that occurs when pressure exceeds the persona's structural threshold.

## Origin

Physiological and psychological trauma theory, applied directly as process constraints.

| Source             | Key Work                                                                                                                                             | Scope                                                                                |
| :----------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Stephen Porges** | _"Orienting in a defensive world: Mammalian modifications of our evolutionary heritage. A polyvagal theory."_ (1995) / _The Polyvagal Theory_ (2011) | Polyvagal theory: autonomic nervous-system foundations of fight, flight, and freeze. |
| **Pete Walker**    | _Complex PTSD: From Surviving to Thriving_ (2013)                                                                                                    | Adds "fawn" as the fourth survival response alongside fight, flight, and freeze.     |

## Restrictions

What the engine refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Interpersonal Escalation**: The engine models responses at the individual persona's autonomic level; relational escalation and dynamic resolution are delegated to the scene's dialogue layer.
- **Conscious Choice**: The responses are involuntary and automatic. The engine assumes the persona cannot choose their response consciously.
- **Dose-Response Duration**: The engine models the immediate autonomic shift under acute pressure; long-term physiological deterioration or recovery cycles are delegated to the play's arc design.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per file.

- **[stress](process_stress.md)** (the root): The threshold breach where demands exceed capacity, triggering involuntary routing. Anchored by Stephen Porges (autonomic nervous-system activation).
- **[fight](process_fight.md)** (expression): Sympathetic mobilization outward to obliterate or disarm the threat. Anchored by Stephen Porges (sympathetic fight system).
- **[flight](process_flight.md)** (expression): Sympathetic mobilization away from the threat to establish distance. Anchored by Stephen Porges (sympathetic flight system).
- **[freeze](process_freeze.md)** (expression): Dorsal-vagal shutdown when active defense is impossible. Anchored by Stephen Porges (dorsal-vagal immobilization).
- **[fawn](process_fawn.md)** (expression): Preemptive boundary collapse and mirroring to placate the threat. Anchored by Pete Walker (the trauma-pattern fourth survival response).

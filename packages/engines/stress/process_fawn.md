# Process: Fawn

## Taxonomy

[Stress](process_stress.md), the root this response is a fracture of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stress

## Initiated by

Too much, too close, too obvious to resist.
[Fight](process_fight.md) breaks the body. [Flight](process_flight.md) is closed. [Freeze](process_freeze.md) is read as offence.
What the threat is looking for is agreement, and agreement is what will be given.

## Direction

Toward the threat, carefully.
The voice softens. The shoulders open.
Whatever the threat wants from the room, the body becomes a small version of that.

## Lever

The boundary.
What was the persona's own bends to mirror the threat exactly enough that the threat can no longer find what to attack.
The bending is precise. The persona is reading the threat closer than the threat is reading the room.

## Echo

The threat is satisfied, and the room continues -- if the mirroring was clean.
The body learns that softening worked, and softens earlier the next time the room turns heavy.
A part of what the persona was is now permanently shaped to that threat, and does not come fully back.
If the softening fails and the threat does not stop, what was offered cannot be taken back.
What follows depends on what is left: the strike if the body finds the force ([fight](process_fight.md)), the run if the door opens ([flight](process_flight.md)), the collapse if neither comes ([freeze](process_freeze.md)).

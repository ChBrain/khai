# Process: Freeze

## Taxonomy

[Stress](process_stress.md), the root this response is a fracture of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stress

## Initiated by

No door, no opening, no answer.
[Flight](process_flight.md) is blocked. [Fight](process_fight.md) loses. [Fawn](process_fawn.md) only invites.
What is left is to disappear in plain sight.

## Direction

Down.
The breath shortens. The body goes quiet.
Nothing moves that does not have to move.

## Lever

Stillness.
The threat is reading for movement; the body offers none.
What it takes to hold the stillness is held inside, where it cannot be seen.

## Echo

The threat looks past, and the moment passes -- if the stillness was complete.
The body learns that being unseen was the safest place, and reaches for unseen first the next time the room turns wrong.
If the stillness is not enough and the threat sees through, the holding gives way.
What follows depends on what is left: the strike if force returns ([fight](process_fight.md)), the bend if force does not ([fawn](process_fawn.md)), the run if the body finds the air ([flight](process_flight.md)).

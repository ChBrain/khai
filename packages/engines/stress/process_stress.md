# Process: Stress

## Taxonomy

Nervous system.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: stress

## Initiated by

The demand arrives. There is not enough to meet it.
The pressure builds in the chest, the jaw, the breath, long before the mind names what is happening.

## Direction

Toward discharge.
Not toward comfort.
What cannot be contained must be expelled.

## Lever

It holds. The structural limit resists the surge.
It holds. The demand continues.
It holds. The pressure compounds.

## Echo

It cannot hold forever.
The threshold breaks.
The tension fractures into [flight](process_flight.md), [freeze](process_freeze.md), [fawn](process_fawn.md) or [fight](process_fight.md).
When a route fails, the pressure returns here to break again.

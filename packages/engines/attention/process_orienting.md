# Process: Orienting

## Taxonomy

[Attention](process_attention.md), the mode this selection is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

The persona needs something that is somewhere in the field but not yet located -- a face in the crowd, the keys on the cluttered desk, the error in the page.
The target is defined; its position is unknown.

## Direction

Across the field, searching.
The vector is scan -- move the beam over the field, sampling, until the target is found or the field is exhausted.

## Lever

The target's features meet the field's clutter.
A target that pops out on one feature is found in a glance; one that shares its features with the distractors forces a slow item-by-item search, and the bigger the field the longer it takes.

## Echo

The target is found and the beam locks, or the field is searched out and it was not there.
The body learns the field's layout, so the next search runs faster.
What makes a thing easy or hard to find is how much it shares with everything around it.

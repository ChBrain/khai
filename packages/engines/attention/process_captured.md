# Process: Captured

## Taxonomy

[Attention](process_attention.md), the mode this selection is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

Something in the field seizes the beam before the persona decides anything -- a loud bang, a sudden movement, a face, a flash.
The pull is bottom-up; it does not ask whether the persona was busy.

## Direction

Onto the intruder, against the goal.
The vector is capture -- the beam is pulled off whatever it held and onto the thing that grabbed it, ahead of any choice.

## Lever

The salience of the intruder meets the grip of the current task.
A strong enough intruder overrides a strong task; a weak one is shrugged off.
The threshold is not fully the persona's to set -- some pulls are wired to win.

## Echo

The persona is looking at the thing that grabbed them, and the thread they were holding has dropped.
Sometimes the capture was worth it -- the real danger; sometimes it was a hijack -- the notification.
The body learns the pull and, with effort, can raise the threshold, never close it.

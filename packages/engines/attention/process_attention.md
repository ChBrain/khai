# Process: Attention

## Taxonomy

Awareness.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

More is present than can be held at once.
The field offers more than the persona can take in -- sounds, sights, threads, tasks -- and a limited spotlight has to fall somewhere.
The moment there is more than one thing to attend, attention begins.

## Direction

Toward one part of the field, away from the rest.
The vector is selection -- narrow onto what is admitted and let the remainder fall below the line where it is noticed.
What is not selected is not merely ignored; often it is never registered at all.

## Lever

The pull of the field meets the limit of the spotlight.
The field competes for the beam -- some pulls loud, some faint, some the persona's own goal -- and the beam can light only so much.
The friction of the competing pulls against the fixed budget is what runs.

## Echo

One part of the field is in awareness; the rest is dark, and the persona acts as if the dark part was not there.
What was lit may pass on to be kept; what was missed leaves no trace to miss.
The selection runs as one of [selective](process_selective.md), [divided](process_divided.md), [sustained](process_sustained.md), [captured](process_captured.md), or [orienting](process_orienting.md).
When the field or the goal shifts, the beam returns here to be aimed again.

# Process: Selective

## Taxonomy

[Attention](process_attention.md), the mode this selection is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

One stream matters more than the rest, and the rest are loud enough to intrude.
The persona has a thing to follow -- a voice, a task, a line of thought -- in a field that keeps offering alternatives.

## Direction

Onto the one, hard, with the others pushed down.
The vector is lock-and-suppress -- hold the chosen stream and actively dampen the competing ones, so the voice across the table fades and the one in front stays clear.

## Lever

The chosen stream meets the pull of the rest.
Holding the lock costs effort in proportion to how loud the competitors are; the suppression is active, and it leaks -- the persona's own name, spoken nearby, breaks straight through.

## Echo

The one stream is followed cleanly and the rest is gone -- including things the persona would have wanted to catch.
The body learns what the focus cost: the missed exit, the unheard question.
A deep lock is why a person can read a page and absorb nothing said beside them.

# Process: Sustained

## Taxonomy

[Attention](process_attention.md), the mode this selection is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

One stream must be watched over a long stretch, mostly empty, for a signal that is rare.
The persona has to keep the beam on a low-event field -- the gauge, the horizon, the quiet ward -- where almost nothing happens and the one thing that does, matters.

## Direction

Onto the one, held, against the pull to drift.
The vector is maintenance -- keep the beam where it is as the minutes pass and the field gives nothing back to hold it.

## Lever

The need to keep watching meets the mind's drift toward anything else.
Vigilance decays; the longer the empty watch, the wider the lapses, and the rare signal is likeliest to fall in one of them.

## Echo

The watch is kept, or the lapse lands exactly when the signal comes and it is missed.
The body tires in a specific way -- the fatigue of having stared at nothing.
The cost of the watch is paid even when nothing ever happens.

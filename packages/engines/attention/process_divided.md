# Process: Divided

## Taxonomy

[Attention](process_attention.md), the mode this selection is one reading of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: attention

## Initiated by

Two or more streams each demand attention and none can be dropped.
The persona is asked to hold several things at once -- the road and the conversation, the pan and the child -- with no one of them safe to let go.

## Direction

Across the streams, switching.
The vector is split -- the beam shares itself, or flicks between, and each stream gets less than its whole.

## Lever

The number of streams meets the single budget.
The budget does not grow with the demand; every stream added thins the others.
Where two streams need the same sub-skill, they collide and one stalls.

## Echo

Everything is half-attended and nothing fully; the persona keeps all the plates moving and drops the one that needed a whole hand.
The body learns which pairs can share and which cannot.
What felt like doing both is, on the tape, doing each one worse.

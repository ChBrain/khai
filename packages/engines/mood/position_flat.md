# Position: Flat

## Taxonomy

[Mood](position_mood.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mood

## Has

A specific [mood](position_mood.md): unpleasant and quiet.
The baseline that runs down and heavy, the colour drained out.
The weight already settled, sluggish and depleted before any cause.

## Orders

Sink.
The mood does not ask whether the moment justifies the gloom.
It meets the read already dimmed, and the room feels the low pull beneath the words.

## Loses

The option of meeting a thing with a charge to spend.
The persona discovers the heaviness arrives before the loss does
and greys the moment whether or not the moment was grey.

## Drives

On the loss of that charge:
rides the low and lets the depletion slow the read,
or works against it and lifts the weight by force at a cost.
The drained baseline holds underneath either way.

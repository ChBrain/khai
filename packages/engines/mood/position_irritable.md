# Position: Irritable

## Taxonomy

[Mood](position_mood.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mood

## Has

A specific [mood](position_mood.md): unpleasant and activated.
The baseline that runs tense and keyed-up, braced against the next thing.
The short fuse already lit, ahead of anything striking it.

## Orders

Brace.
The mood does not ask whether the moment warrants the edge.
It meets the read already agitated, and the room feels the friction first.

## Loses

The option of meeting a thing loose and unguarded.
The persona discovers the tension arrives before the provocation does
and answers as if provoked whether or not it was.

## Drives

On the loss of that ease:
rides the edge and lets the friction sharpen the read,
or works against it and holds the fuse down at a cost.
The keyed-up baseline holds underneath either way.

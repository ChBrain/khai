# Position: Mood

## Taxonomy

Affect (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mood

## Has

A background tone the persona carries across a stretch.
A baseline on two axes: pleasant or unpleasant, activated or quiet.
The colour laid on every read before any single event arrives.

## Orders

Run from here.
The mood tilts which feelings fire and how they land.
The room reads it beneath the words, before the persona names a thing.

## Loses

The option of a neutral ground.
The persona discovers each moment is met already tinted,
and there is no plain surface left to meet it on.

## Drives

On the loss of that ground:
rides the mood and lets it set the tilt,
or works against it, lifting or masking the tone at a cost.
The baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Serene

## Taxonomy

[Mood](position_mood.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mood

## Has

A specific [mood](position_mood.md): pleasant and quiet.
The baseline that sits low and warm, settled and unhurried.
The ease already in place, holding before anything asks it to.

## Orders

Settle.
The mood does not ask whether the moment calls for the calm.
It takes its time, lets the read arrive slowly, and the room slows with it.

## Loses

The option of meeting a thing keyed up and ready to move.
The persona discovers the ease arrives before the demand does
and softens the demand whether or not it should have been met sharp.

## Drives

On the loss of that readiness:
rides the calm and lets the unhurried baseline set the pace,
or works against it and pushes up to speed at a cost.
The low warm baseline holds underneath either way.

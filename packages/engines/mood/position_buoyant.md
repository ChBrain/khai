# Position: Buoyant

## Taxonomy

[Mood](position_mood.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mood

## Has

A specific [mood](position_mood.md): pleasant and activated.
The baseline that runs up and bright, the colour turned high.
The energy already moving, ahead of any reason for it.

## Orders

Reach out.
The mood does not ask whether the moment has earned the lift.
It says yes first, finds the momentum, and the room catches it.

## Loses

The option of meeting a thing flat and even.
The persona discovers the brightness arrives before the news does
and spends itself whether or not the news deserved it.

## Drives

On the loss of that even ground:
rides the lift and lets the momentum carry the read,
or works against it and holds the brightness back at a cost.
The high baseline holds underneath either way.

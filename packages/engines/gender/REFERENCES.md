## Domain

The gender domain models gender as position: the social read placed on a
body before it speaks. The domain does not model anatomy or identity; it
models the structural function a persona occupies in a room.

## Source Registry

No external sources are cited. The two position files are original
expression authored for the Cultures engine.

## Authorship Note

The framing -- gender as position imposed by the room, not chosen by the
persona -- draws on the broad scholarly tradition of gender as performance
and gender as social structure (Butler, West and Zimmerman, Risman), but
the prose does not reproduce claims from any specific paper. The engine
positions are original expression; they are not attributed extracts or
paraphrases of any single source.

The `position_male.md` and `position_female.md` files model the structural
read (what the room assigns), not the lived inner experience. They are
intentionally symmetric in schema so that any persona may hold either, or
navigate the gap between them.

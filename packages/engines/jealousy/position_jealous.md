# Position: Jealous

## Taxonomy

[Jealousy](process_jealousy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: jealousy

## Has

A claim on a bond it did not choose to defend but must: standing
inside a relationship a rival has entered, with more information
about the threat than anyone else in the triangle -- and less
certainty than it wants. The position sits closer to the valued other
than the rival does, and further from ease than it did before the
rival appeared.

## Orders

Watch the triangle.
The position does not let the persona set the threat down once
appraised; attention returns to the rival and the valued other on its
own schedule, unbidden, whether or not anything has actually changed
since the last look.

## Loses

The unwatched relationship -- the version of the bond that did not
require checking. Once occupying this position, the persona cannot
fully return to treating the tie as settled; even a resolved threat
leaves the habit of watching behind. They also lose the option of
reading the rival as neutral; anyone who could be a rival is read
through that possibility from here.

## Drives

On that loss:
the persona guards -- monitoring the valued other's attention,
testing the rival's intentions, moving to close whatever distance the
rival opened. Buss's mate-guarding function names the same drive at
its evolutionary root: behavior aimed at deterring a rival and
retaining a partner, not at acquiring one. The position keeps the
persona guarding as long as the rival's claim reads live.

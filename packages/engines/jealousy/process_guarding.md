# Process: Guarding

## Taxonomy

[Jealousy](process_jealousy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: jealousy

## Initiated by

The threat appraisal returns a verdict worth acting on: a rival's
claim on the valued bond reads real enough, or close enough, that
watching alone will not hold the line. The persona moves from reading
the triangle to acting inside it.

## Direction

Toward retention, not conquest. The behavior aims at the bond and,
through it, at the rival: vigilance over the valued other's time and
attention, confrontation of the rival or the valued other, displays
meant to reassert the claim, concessions meant to make the bond worth
staying in. Every one of these is aimed at keeping what the persona
already has, never at winning something new.

## Lever

Guarding presses on the very bond it means to protect. Watched too
closely, the valued other reads surveillance instead of care;
confronted too hard, the rival's claim can look more credible, not
less. The persona cannot act on the threat without the action itself
becoming part of what the valued other weighs -- so the lever is a
bond worked on from both sides of the same motion.

## Echo

Guarding either secures the bond -- the rival recedes, the valued
other reaffirms the claim -- or it hastens what it feared, driving
the valued other toward the rival it was meant to hold off. Either
way the [jealous](position_jealous.md) position does not simply end;
it stands down until the next signal reopens
[threat appraisal](process_threat_appraisal.md), or it hardens into a
standing vigilance that outlasts any single rival.

# Process: Jealousy

## Taxonomy

Three-party relationship threat (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: jealousy

## Initiated by

A rival, real or imagined, appears inside a bond the persona already
holds -- a partnership, a friendship, a standing the persona counts
as theirs. This is not a gap between the persona and a good someone
else has; it is a third party reaching into a tie the persona already
possesses. The process needs three: the persona, the valued
relationship, and the rival who threatens it.

## Direction

Toward the bond, not the rival. The persona is moved to keep what is
already theirs -- reclaiming attention, reasserting a claim, closing
distance the rival opened -- rather than to acquire anything new.
Where envy reaches for a good it lacks, jealousy defends a good it
has.

## Lever

The relationship is the stake and the rival is the pressure on it.
What appraises the threat and what acts on it are separate labors, so
the process runs through two lower processes:
[threat appraisal](process_threat_appraisal.md) reads how real and
how close the rival's claim is, and [guarding](process_guarding.md)
is what the vigilance becomes -- watching, confronting, holding on.
Between them sits the [jealous](position_jealous.md) position: the
standing of a persona who has read the threat and is now guarding
against it.

## Echo

The bond is secured, lost, or the rival's claim recedes, and the
guarding relaxes or the loss is absorbed. What the process leaves
behind is not settled once: a bond that has been guarded once is read
as guardable again, and the next rival -- real or imagined -- reopens
the same appraisal.

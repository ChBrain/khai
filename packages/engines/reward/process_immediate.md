# Process: Immediate

## Taxonomy

[Reward](process_reward.md), the mode this is one form of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: reward

## Initiated by

An outcome is present or nearly present -- available now or within a window short enough that the discount has not had room to run.
The distance between desire and delivery is negligible.

## Direction

Toward the outcome, at near-full weight.
The proximity of the reward makes it large; the gap between desire and delivery is small enough that the organism does not adjust downward.
What competes is not time but other concurrent pulls in the same moment.

## Lever

The reward is close and the signal is strong.
There is little cost of waiting because the wait is short; the discount does not erode the value at this range.
The pull of the near outcome is proportional to its raw value -- distorted only by the moment's other competitors, not by time.

## Echo

The outcome is pursued directly and at high effort.
The persona acts because the target is close enough to feel real.
Where immediate reward consistently dominates over deferred outcomes, the system calibrates to the near range: the future grows thin, and the effort required for outcomes that live in later becomes progressively harder to sustain.

# Process: Deferred

## Taxonomy

[Reward](process_reward.md), the mode this is one form of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: reward

## Initiated by

An outcome is anticipated but not near -- days, weeks, or months out.
The discount has had room to run, and the value of the outcome has contracted accordingly.
The persona knows the reward is there; the organism does not feel it at full weight.

## Direction

Toward the distant outcome, at reduced weight.
The persona is willing to act toward it, but the pull is weaker than the raw value of the outcome would suggest.
The gap between current position and future reward is felt as the thinness of the target, not as a calculation.

## Lever

The discounted value of the outcome meets the concrete demands of the present.
A deferred reward must compete with immediate ones that carry full weight; the longer the deferral, the more the present alternatives win.
Effort toward the distant outcome costs now; the return comes later, and later is not yet real enough to feel.

## Echo

The persona persists toward the distant goal or abandons it for something closer.
Where deferred reward holds its pull the persona sustains effort across the gap -- the degree, the project, the relationship tended over years.
Where it does not, the persona pivots to what is near and feels larger.
The pivot may not register as a choice; it presents as a loss of interest in something that seemed to matter.

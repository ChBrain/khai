# Process: Reward

## Taxonomy

Motivation / Reinforcement.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: reward

## Initiated by

An outcome is available or anticipated -- something that could follow from the persona's action.
The organism reads it: how much is it worth, and how close or far is it?
The question is not abstract; it fires every time the environment offers something that the organism has learned to pursue.

## Direction

Toward the outcome, weighted by its value and adjusted by its distance.
The persona moves in proportion to what the reward is worth at the discount rate the organism applies.
The discount is not a choice; it is a property of the system that runs beneath the choice.

## Lever

The value of the outcome meets the cost of the wait or effort required.
The organism does not weigh outcomes on a fixed scale; it applies a discount -- future outcomes are worth less than present ones, and the steepness of that discount determines how hard the persona will work for a delayed reward.
A steep discount makes the near large and the far thin; a shallow discount makes the future more nearly as real as the present.

## Echo

The persona moves toward the outcome or does not.
The reward system shapes which outcomes pull and by how much; the discount rate runs beneath the surface of the choice.
The signal can misfire: the system responds to salient signals that predict reward even when the reward is absent, thin, or harmful.
The process continues as [immediate](process_immediate.md), [deferred](process_deferred.md), [delay-averse](process_delay_averse.md), or [stimulation-seeking](process_stimulation_seeking.md), each a distinct mode of how the discount and the urgency play out.

# Process: Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[Betrayal](process_betrayal.md), the reckoning this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: betrayal

## Initiated by

The persona holds a working account of what happened and now faces what to do with it.
The violation is not a private matter; it is a fact about the relationship that makes a claim on both parties.
The persona must decide whether to name it, confront it, demand acknowledgement, carry it silently, or withdraw.

## Direction

Toward a decision about the relationship.
The persona is not yet at rest; they are deciding what response the violation merits and what cost they are willing to pay to pursue it.
The decision may be postponed, revised, or taken in pieces; what ends the pass is not a single choice but the settling of the persona's moral account.

## Lever

The violation presses against the relationship's remaining value.
The persona weighs what the relationship was, what it could still be, what the violation cost, and what pursuing acknowledgement or redress would cost.
The reckoning is not purely rational; the weight of the violation and the weight of the relationship are not stable, and both shift as the persona works through the pass.

## Echo

The persona may demand confrontation, seek acknowledgement from the betrayer, withdraw without explanation, or arrive at a private account that does not require action.
Each of these is a form of reckoning; the pass is not resolved by the most dramatic option.
When the persona has reached a position they can hold, the move into [resolution](process_resolution.md) begins.

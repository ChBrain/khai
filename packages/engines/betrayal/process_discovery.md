# Process: Discovery

## Taxonomy

[Betrayal](process_betrayal.md), the reckoning this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: betrayal

## Initiated by

The persona learns what happened.
The information arrives -- through direct confrontation, through a third party, through evidence that cannot be explained away -- and the picture it makes is one the persona did not have before.
The violation is now known, even if it is not yet fully believed.

## Direction

Toward acknowledgement.
The persona must move from receiving the information to holding it as a fact about the relationship and the person.
The gap between knowing and believing is not hypocrisy; it is the lag of a world that has not yet adjusted to what it has been told.

## Lever

The information meets the prior account.
The persona held a picture of the betrayer and the relationship; the discovery requires that picture to be revised, and the revision costs more than the information alone would suggest.
Each confirmation of the violation is also a confirmation that the prior picture was wrong.

## Echo

The persona is holding two incompatible things: what they knew and what they now know.
The discovery does not immediately collapse the prior account; the persona may seek to explain the information away, delay full acknowledgement, or oscillate between believing and not believing.
When the information is fully held as fact, the move into [disorientation](process_disorientation.md) begins.
Discovery lands against the persona's own [blindness](position_blindness.md): the higher the dial was set before this moment, the harder the detonation, since more evidence had to accumulate unseen before it could register at all.

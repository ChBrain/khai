# Process: Resolution

## Taxonomy

[Betrayal](process_betrayal.md), the reckoning this pass is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: betrayal

## Initiated by

The persona has reached a settled account of the violation and what to do about the relationship.
The account need not be comfortable; resolution does not mean the violation is forgiven, forgotten, or accepted.
It means the persona is no longer in active reckoning; they know where they stand.

## Direction

Toward a liveable relationship with the fact of the violation.
The persona does not erase the betrayal from the account of the relationship; they find a way to carry it that does not consume the present.
Resolution may take the form of repair, of estrangement, of a formal continuance under changed conditions, or of a private peace.

## Lever

The settled account meets the ongoing relationship -- or the absence where the relationship was.
The persona must act within the new account: relating to the betrayer from the revised understanding, or carrying the fact of the violation through a life the betrayer is no longer part of.
The press is not the violation itself but the need to act in the world from the account of it.

## Echo

The persona is no longer revising; they are living from the resolution.
The resolution is not permanent; new information, new encounters with the betrayer, or new passes through earlier phases may reopen the account.
The process ends when the persona can hold the violation and act -- not when the violation no longer matters.

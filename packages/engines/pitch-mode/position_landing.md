# Position: Landing

## Taxonomy

[Pitch Mode](position_pitch_mode.md), the expression in which the persona is making the ask: seeking the specific commitment the pitch has been building toward. Landing is the moment the pitch reaches its target.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pitch-mode

## Has

The persona holds the ask itself: the specific, clear, bounded commitment they are seeking from the other. What the landing expression has is both the clarity of the ask (the persona knows exactly what they want and makes it unambiguous) and the restraint that follows the ask (the persona does not immediately soften, qualify, or retreat from the commitment they have named). Pink's analysis of the new pitch is precise here: the ask in the new environment is not a close in the old sense but an offer of collaboration -- an invitation for the other to co-create the path to yes.

## Orders

Ask and hold. Landing mode demands that the persona name the commitment clearly and then stop -- stop talking, stop qualifying, stop filling the silence. The persona who names the ask and immediately begins managing the other's response has undercut the landing. The order is: ask specifically, ask once, and hold the space for the other's response.

## Loses

The specificity of the ask. A landing that softens into a general expression of interest ("I'd love to work together on something") has not landed; the persona has retreated from the commitment to the ask. What landing mode loses when the persona qualifies or hedges is the clarity that makes a yes or a no possible.

## Drives

Toward the other's response. The landing persona is driven by the specific answer to the specific ask. The drive is not toward a yes in the abstract but toward whatever the other actually says -- yes, no, or the counter-offer that opens the next step of the conversation.

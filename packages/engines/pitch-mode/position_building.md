# Position: Building

## Taxonomy

[Pitch Mode](position_pitch_mode.md), the expression in which the persona is constructing the case: assembling the evidence, the narrative, and the value that makes the commitment rational and desirable. Building follows framing and precedes the ask.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: pitch-mode

## Has

The persona holds the constructive task: assembling what makes the pitch credible, compelling, and specific enough to support a yes. What the persona is building is not persuasion in the abstract but the specific case for this other, at this moment, given the frame the persona has already established. Building mode draws on the mechanisms Cialdini identifies -- social proof, authority, scarcity, reciprocity, commitment, liking -- as well as the narrative structures that make the case memorable and the concrete specifics that make it credible.

## Orders

Build for this other, not for the general case. The building persona must calibrate the case to what this specific other needs to hear: which evidence will carry weight for them, which narrative will make sense from their perspective, which concrete details will land. The persona who builds the generic pitch has not completed building mode; they have produced material that still needs to be adapted.

## Loses

The other's increasing orientation toward yes. Building mode is complete only when the case has brought the other to the edge of commitment. A persona who is still building after the other is ready to commit has missed the moment; the pitch has passed its own landing point. What building mode loses is the timing -- the point at which building should give way to the ask.

## Drives

Toward the other's readiness to commit. The building persona is driven by the accumulation of a case that makes the yes feel both rational and safe. The drive resolves when the other's orientation has shifted from the question of whether to the question of how.

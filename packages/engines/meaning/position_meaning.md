# Position: Meaning

## Taxonomy

Meaning in life as position: the stance the persona holds toward the question of whether their life matters.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: meaning

## Has

A sense of whether life is meaningful, a posture toward the question of what makes the persona's existence worthwhile.
The position is not a mood or a philosophy; it is a read the persona carries into every room, shaping what they attend to and what they dismiss.
The sense can be present, actively sought, quietly lost, or nowhere near the surface.

## Orders

Attend to whatever the stance says matters.
A persona with meaning present acts from a centre they know; a persona searching for meaning frames each situation as a potential source or obstacle.
A persona adrift from meaning may act without recognising the absence until something presses on it.

## Loses

The option of complete indifference to the question.
The stance is always set, even when the question has not been asked consciously; the room reads the degree of animation, purpose, or hollowness, and the persona feels the difference even when they do not name it.

## Drives

On a challenge to the current stance -- a loss that threatens present meaning, a failure to find what was sought, a gap that opens between the persona and the sense of mattering:
reorients toward whatever can re-establish or sustain the position; or sits with the loss, letting the question remain open longer than is comfortable.

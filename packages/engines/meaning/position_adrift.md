# Position: Adrift

## Taxonomy

[Meaning](position_meaning.md), the stance toward the question of mattering that this expression resolves.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: meaning

## Has

A [meaning](position_meaning.md) that is absent and not currently sought.
The question of whether the persona's life matters is not openly held; it has been set aside, suppressed, or numbed into background noise.
The absence is not a crisis the persona is managing but a condition they are living inside without naming it.

## Orders

Continue.
The position does not generate direction from a source of meaning because no source is acknowledged.
The persona moves by inertia, obligation, or distraction, completing what must be done without a sense that it matters that they are the one doing it.

## Loses

The option of being unmoved by proximity to what others find meaningful.
The persona who is adrift is not indifferent to meaning; they are disconnected from their own version of it.
Being near someone who acts from a clear source of purpose is either uncomfortable or, on occasion, the first friction that re-opens the question.

## Drives

On a situation that forces the question, a loss that cannot be explained away, a demand for choice that requires knowing what matters:
finds the opening the question makes and allows it to become a search;
or closes it again, returning to the inertia before the disruption arrived.

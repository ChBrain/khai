# Position: Present

## Taxonomy

[Meaning](position_meaning.md), the stance toward the question of mattering that this expression resolves.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: meaning

## Has

A [meaning](position_meaning.md) that is already there, not waiting to be discovered.
The sense of mattering is a background fact of the persona's life, not a question currently open for examination.
The persona acts from a centre they know, and the centre holds without being named.

## Orders

Proceed.
The position does not require the persona to demonstrate or argue for the sense of purpose; it is already in place.
The work, the relationships, the commitments the persona holds have weight because the persona already knows they matter.

## Loses

The option of treating a loss as merely external.
When something that has grounded the sense of meaning is taken, the loss lands on the source, not just the surface.
The persona discovers how much of the present meaning was held by that particular thing when it is gone.

## Drives

On a threat to what sustains the sense of meaning:
acts to protect or restore what is at stake, with a clarity of purpose that comes from knowing what matters;
or, when what is lost cannot be restored, faces the work of rebuilding the centre without the thing that held it.

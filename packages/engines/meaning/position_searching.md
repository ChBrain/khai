# Position: Searching

## Taxonomy

[Meaning](position_meaning.md), the stance toward the question of mattering that this expression resolves.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: meaning

## Has

A [meaning](position_meaning.md) not yet found: the question is open, the search is active.
The persona knows the sense of mattering is missing or insufficient, and is oriented toward finding it.
The search is not crisis; it is a state of purposeful orientation toward an answer the persona does not yet have.

## Orders

Look.
The position frames every situation as a potential source or obstruction; the persona is alert to what might matter, and attentive to what falls flat.
Commitments are provisional because the persona has not yet found what would make them feel permanent.

## Loses

The option of the settled centre.
The persona cannot simply decide to stop searching; the absence of meaning is present as a pressure even in moments of apparent contentment.
The question does not close until something genuinely answers it.

## Drives

On a situation that might answer the question -- a relationship, a cause, a form of work, a way of being:
moves toward it with the orientation of someone who knows what they are looking for, not just hoping to stumble into;
or, if the candidate fails to deliver, returns to the search without the assumption that the next one will be different.

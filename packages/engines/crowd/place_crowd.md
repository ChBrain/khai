# Place: Crowd

## Taxonomy

Crowd is a place engine (TO SHOW). It models the crowd itself as an environment: the massed condition of bodies that presses on everyone inside it before any individual speaks or acts. The crowd is prior to its members; it acts on them, it is not merely the sum of them.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: crowd

## Shown

What arrives before any word is spoken: the visible mass, the press of bodies, the noise rising and falling as one. The crowd shows scale and closeness first -- a persona entering it registers the mass before they register any single person inside it.

## Holds

The crowd's drive to grow (Canetti: an open crowd recognizes no barrier, wants only more, and dreads its own halting) together with the density that makes it a crowd at all rather than a mere gathering: enough bodies, pressed close enough, that the ordinary spacing a person keeps from a stranger is gone. Growth and density are what the crowd needs simply to remain itself, before it needs anything else.

## Offers

The relief of the submerged individual (Le Bon: inside the crowd a person acquires something of a collective mind, sheds the personal restraint that holds alone, and both feels and acts on impulses no isolated person would risk), the contagion that carries a gesture or an emotion through the mass faster than any one person in it could reason about it, and the heightened suggestibility that makes the crowd receptive to whatever moves through it first.

## Withheld

The discharge -- the moment the crowd feels its internal distinctions fall away and, for as long as the crowd holds together, everyone in it is equal -- is not something any single member can produce alone; the crowd withholds it until the mass itself reaches it. So too the direction that would keep the crowd a crowd rather than let it disperse back into separate people: a goal supplied to the mass, not chosen by any one person inside it.

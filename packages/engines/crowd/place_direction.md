# Place: Direction

## Taxonomy

Direction is an expression of the crowd engine. It isolates the shared goal or target that Canetti holds is necessary to keep a crowd a crowd, rather than a density that disperses once it has formed.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: crowd

## Shown

A single target the mass visibly moves or presses toward: a place, a person, an outcome named clearly enough that everyone inside the crowd can act on it without conferring.

## Holds

The goal itself, held in common and substituting for whatever individual goals its members would otherwise be pursuing separately. Canetti: a crowd without a direction merely disintegrates; direction is what a crowd needs to remain a crowd once density and growth have already made it one.

## Offers

Momentum. The crowd that has a direction moves as a body, and moving as a body is available only once density has abolished individual distance and direction has given the mass somewhere to go together.

## Withheld

The goal's own justification. Direction tells the crowd where, not why the goal is right or worth reaching: the crowd engine withholds any accounting of whether the shared target is good, worth pursuing, or true -- that assessment belongs elsewhere, not to the pressure environment itself.

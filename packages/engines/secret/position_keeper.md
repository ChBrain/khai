# Position: Keeper

## Taxonomy

[Secret](piece_secret.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: secret

## Has

Sole custody of a fact and the power that comes with it: the keeper
knows something the room does not, and can choose the moment, the
audience, and the framing of its telling. Holding the secret sets
the keeper slightly apart from everyone kept outside it -- closer to
whoever is inside, distant from whoever is not.

## Orders

Hold the line.
The position does not ask whether the keeper wants the burden; once
they know, not-telling becomes an act they must perform again in
every conversation that brushes near it. Silence stops being the
absence of speech and becomes work.

## Loses

Ease. The keeper cannot be fully present in a room the secret
touches, because part of their attention is always guarding the
seam. They lose the simple version of the relationships built over
the gap, and the option of being known completely by anyone kept
outside.

## Drives

On that loss:
the keeper manages exposure -- steering conversations, rationing who
is let in, weighing each near-miss against the cost of telling. They
guard the fact and, in guarding it, are shaped by it: watchful where
they were open, rehearsed where they were spontaneous. The secret
keeps the keeper as much as the keeper keeps the secret.

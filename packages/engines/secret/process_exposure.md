# Process: Exposure

## Taxonomy

[Secret](piece_secret.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: secret

## Initiated by

The fact gets out without the keeper's consent -- found, let slip by
a confidant, or forced into the open. The threshold is the loss of
control: the moment the keeper is no longer the one deciding who
knows. It can arrive in an instant after years of concealment.

## Direction

Outward and irreversible, on someone else's timing. Where disclosure
opens the gap where the keeper chose, exposure tears it open where
the world chose. The fact spreads past the audience the keeper would
have picked, in a framing they no longer set.

## Lever

The pressure of what was hidden meeting the collapse of the cover.
The weight the secret was carrying is released all at once, and it
lands hardest on whatever the concealment was holding up -- the
trust, the standing, the arrangement built as if the fact were not
there. Remove the hidden weight and exposure is merely news; remove
the collapse and the keeper still holds the line.

## Echo

The keeper is now known to have kept, which often wounds more than
the fact itself. Relationships recalculate on two discoveries: what
was hidden, and that it was hidden from them. The world rearranges
around a fact it can no longer un-know, and the keeper inherits the
reputation of one who withheld it.

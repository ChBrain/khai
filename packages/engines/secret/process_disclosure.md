# Process: Disclosure

## Taxonomy

[Secret](piece_secret.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: secret

## Initiated by

The keeper chooses to tell. The threshold is the decision to hand
the fact across on purpose -- to a confidant, a partner, a room --
rather than have it taken. What makes it disclosure and not exposure
is that the keeper still holds the timing, the audience, and the
framing at the moment of release.

## Direction

Toward the closing of the gap the keeper chose to close. Telling
moves the fact from one mind into another and, with it, the
asymmetry that structured the tie. The relationship reorganises
around shared knowledge -- often toward greater intimacy, sometimes
toward rupture.

## Lever

The bid for closeness meeting the risk of the telling. Disclosure
offers to be known and asks to be trusted; its force is the trust it
extends, its resistance is what the hearer may now do with the fact.
Remove the trust and it is a confession under duress; remove the
risk and it was never a secret worth the name.

## Echo

Once told, the fact cannot be un-told, and the keeper has traded sole
custody for a shared burden -- and a new keeper. The hearer now holds
the same secret and the same choices. The gap has moved outward, not
vanished: the two are bound inside it, and everyone still outside is
newly, jointly, kept out.

# Process: Concealment

## Taxonomy

[Secret](piece_secret.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: secret

## Initiated by

The keeper decides another must not know, and the fact crosses from
merely private into actively withheld. The threshold is the
decision, not the fact: many things go unsaid; a secret is a thing
kept unsaid on purpose, against a specific person or room.

## Direction

Toward maintenance. Concealment is not one act but a standing one,
renewed every time the subject comes near. It bends the keeper's
behaviour around the seam -- topics avoided, answers pre-planned,
company chosen for what it will not ask.

## Lever

The work of the cover meeting the pressure of the near-miss. The
keeper spends attention to keep the surface smooth; the world keeps
producing moments that could crack it. Remove the effort and the
secret surfaces; remove the pressure and there was never a secret to
keep, only a fact not yet mentioned.

## Echo

The longer concealment runs, the more the keeper's ordinary conduct
is built to serve it, and the harder the fact is to tell without also
confessing the keeping. What began as protecting a fact becomes
protecting the concealment itself. The next process inherits a
secret that now costs two admissions, not one.

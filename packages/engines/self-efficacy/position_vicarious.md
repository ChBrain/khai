# Position: Vicarious

## Taxonomy

[Self-Efficacy](position_self_efficacy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-efficacy

## Has

A specific [self-efficacy](position_self_efficacy.md): a capability belief
read off watching a similar other clear this kind of task, not proven by the
persona's own hand.
The inference that if someone comparable managed it, the task is within
reach, borrowed rather than earned.
A source that carries weight in proportion to how similar the model reads
-- and thins fast the further the model's situation sits from the
persona's own.

## Orders

Measure against the model.
The read asks how close the comparison holds before it asks anything about
the task itself, and commits or withholds on that resemblance.
The room reads the reach toward or away from whoever the persona has been
watching.

## Loses

The option of a belief that outlasts the comparison.
The persona discovers the read is only as strong as the likeness to the
model it was borrowed from, and cannot hold the confidence once the model
stops looking like a fair comparison.

## Drives

On the loss of that borrowed footing:
rides the resemblance and commits on the strength of a model who cleared
the same kind of ground,
or works against a comparison that has thinned and pushes on borrowed
confidence anyway, at a cost.
The read-off-a-comparable-other baseline holds underneath either way.

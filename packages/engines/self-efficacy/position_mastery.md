# Position: Mastery

## Taxonomy

[Self-Efficacy](position_self_efficacy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-efficacy

## Has

A specific [self-efficacy](position_self_efficacy.md): a capability belief
built from the persona's own remembered successes at this kind of task.
The read that has already been proven once, by the persona's own hand, and
so does not need to be taken on anyone's word.
The most durable source, the one occasional failure bends but does not
break, because the record it draws on is the persona's own.

## Orders

Repeat what worked.
The read does not ask whether this attempt looks like the others, only
whether the persona has cleared this kind of ground before, and commits on
that record before the task shows its shape.
The room reads the steadiness in how little the persona needs to be told.

## Loses

The option of doubt earned honestly.
The persona discovers a setback here reads as an exception against a proven
record, not as new evidence, and cannot let one bad attempt overturn what a
run of good ones already settled.

## Drives

On the loss of that easy doubt:
rides the record and holds through difficulty on the strength of what was
already proven,
or works against it and lets a single setback chip at ground the record
does not actually concede, at a cost.
The proven-by-own-hand baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Self-Efficacy

## Taxonomy

Capability Belief (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: self-efficacy

## Has

A belief in one's own capability to organize and execute the actions a
specific task requires.
Not a verdict on worth, a read on whether this attainment is within reach
from here -- built and revised task by task, never held as one number
for a whole life.
A judgment the persona carries into the task before the first attempt,
set by what has been mastered, watched, or said, not by how the task
turns out this time.

## Orders

Size the task against the read.
The position sets whether the persona takes it on, how much effort they
spend, and how long they keep spending it once the task pushes back.
The room reads the commitment in what gets attempted, not in what gets
claimed.

## Loses

The option of meeting a task unrated.
The persona discovers every task arrives already measured against what
they believe they can execute, and there is no attempt left to make on
a blank sense of their own capability.

## Drives

On the loss of that blank measure:
rides the read and commits effort in proportion to it, holding under
difficulty exactly as far as the belief extends,
or works against a low read and pushes past it at a cost, or coasts on
a high one past what the task still demands.
The task-specific capability belief holds underneath either way.

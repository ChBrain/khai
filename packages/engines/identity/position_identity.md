# Position: Identity

## Taxonomy

The self (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: identity

## Has

A story of who-they-are, carried into the room and defended when it is touched.
A set of commitments -- what the persona takes themselves to be, what they stand for, what they belong to -- assembled over time into a sense of continuity across the changing scenes.
It is the thing that answers, silently, who is this, to themselves.

## Orders

Hold the story and act in character with it.
The position asks the persona to be recognisably themselves from scene to scene, and to feel the tug when an act would contradict who they take themselves to be.
Most of the time it runs unspoken, the floor under the choices.

## Loses

The option of being no one in particular.
The persona cannot act from nowhere; even refusing an identity is a stance the room reads as one.
To hold a self is to have something that can be threatened, and the holding costs the freedom of the blank.

## Drives

On the loss of the blank:
holds the story and meets a challenge to it as information or as attack, depending on how the story was built,
or lets the story go slack and takes the shape of whatever room it is in.
How firmly, and on what basis, the self is held is the read the statuses carry.

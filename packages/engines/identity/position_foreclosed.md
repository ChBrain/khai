# Position: Foreclosed

## Taxonomy

[Identity](position_identity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: identity

## Has

A self-story committed to without ever being explored.
The commitments were taken whole -- from family, faith, authority, or group -- and never tested against an alternative, so they are held with certainty and without a why of the persona's own.

## Orders

Hold the line and do not examine it.
The position runs on borrowed solidity -- clear, confident, unconflicted -- for as long as nothing forces the question the persona never asked.

## Loses

The ability to meet a real challenge as anything but a threat.
A question to the story has no give in it, because the story has no foundation the persona built; so challenge is met with defence, volume, or exit, not reflection.

## Drives

On a challenge to the story:
hardens around the commitment and treats the challenger as enemy,
or, where the challenge cannot be repelled, risks the sudden collapse of a structure that was never load-tested, and falls toward search or drift.

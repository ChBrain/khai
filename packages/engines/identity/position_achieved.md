# Position: Achieved

## Taxonomy

[Identity](position_identity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: identity

## Has

A self-story the persona explored and then chose.
The commitments were tried against alternatives and kept on purpose, so they are owned rather than inherited.
The persona knows not just what they are but why, and the why has already been tested.

## Orders

Hold it firmly and lightly at once.
The position lets the persona act from a settled centre and still hear a challenge as information -- the story is owned well enough that questioning a part of it does not threaten the whole.

## Loses

The comfort of a certainty that never has to look at itself.
The achieved self paid for its commitments with doubt already spent, and stays open to spending more; it cannot have the untroubled solidity of the self that never asked.

## Drives

On a challenge to the story:
takes in what is true in it and revises the part without losing the whole,
or holds the commitment knowingly against a challenge it has weighed and rejected.
Either way the self bends without breaking.

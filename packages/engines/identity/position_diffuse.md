# Position: Diffuse

## Taxonomy

[Identity](position_identity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: identity

## Has

Neither an explored nor a committed self -- the question of who-they-are mostly unasked.
There is no firm story to hold, so the persona carries little to defend and takes much of their shape from whatever room they are in.

## Orders

Avoid the question and follow the current.
The position runs by deferral -- let the situation, the company, or the moment supply the self the persona did not assemble, and move on before the gap shows.

## Loses

A centre to return to.
Without a self-story the persona has no stable place from which to choose, resist, or commit; consistency across scenes is hard, and closeness that asks who are you, really, is hard to meet.

## Drives

On a challenge to the absent self:
deflects with whatever the room wants and drifts on,
or feels the lack sharply enough to enter the search, falling forward into moratorium.

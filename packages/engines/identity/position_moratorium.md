# Position: Moratorium

## Taxonomy

[Identity](position_identity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: identity

## Has

A self in active search, exploring without yet committing.
The persona is trying on versions of who-they-are, holding several open, planting no flag -- alive to possibility and unsettled by it.

## Orders

Keep the question open and tolerate the not-knowing.
The position asks the persona to stay in the search rather than grab a premature answer, and to carry the anxiety of a self not yet decided.

## Loses

The ground that commitment gives.
Without a settled story the persona has no firm place to stand when the room demands one, and the openness reads from outside as flightiness or indecision.

## Drives

On a challenge to the unfinished self:
uses the challenge as more material for the search and keeps exploring,
or, worn down by the not-knowing, grabs the nearest available story and forecloses on it to end the discomfort.

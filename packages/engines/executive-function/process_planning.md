# Process: Planning

## Taxonomy

[Executive Function](process_executive_function.md), the mode this is one form of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: executive-function

## Initiated by

A goal requires more than one step, and the steps are not all available at once.
The persona must hold the end state active while selecting, ordering, and monitoring the intermediate moves -- each step taken before the goal is visible from it.

## Direction

Forward, from here to the goal, across the sequence.
The persona traces a path, holds it against the present moment, and tracks their position within it -- what has been done, what comes next, what must be preserved for later.

## Lever

The end state meets the weight of the present moment.
The goal is not visible from every step; the persona must carry it forward through moves that do not themselves point to it.
The further the end from the current position, the more working memory is under load, and the more interference the intermediate steps produce.
A plan abandoned mid-sequence does not always leave a clear residue of where it broke.

## Echo

The sequence runs, is abandoned, or is lost in its parts.
Where planning holds the persona moves step by step toward an end they can see even when it is not yet present.
Where it fails the persona takes available steps without sequence -- the pieces without the path, the task begun but not completed, the goal arrived at by accident or not at all.
A broken plan is rarely announced; the persona is often the last to know the path has been dropped.

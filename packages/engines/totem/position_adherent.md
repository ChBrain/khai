# Position: Adherent

## Taxonomy

[Totem](piece_totem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: totem

## Has

Membership made visible in a thing. The adherent is one for whom the
totem is sacred -- who reads the object not as material but as the
group it stands for, and is read by others as belonging through their
reverence for it. To hold the totem sacred is to be counted in.

## Orders

Revere.
The position does not ask whether the adherent privately believes; it
asks that they keep the object apart -- uncover before it, not let it
touch the ground, defend it, mourn its loss. The prohibition runs
whether or not the feeling is there; observance is the membership.

## Loses

The freedom to treat the object as a thing. The adherent cannot
weigh the totem by its substance, cannot be indifferent to how it is
handled, cannot stand outside the group's stake in it. What is a
scrap of cloth to an outsider is not available to them as a scrap of
cloth.

## Drives

On that loss:
the adherent guards the sacred -- observing the prohibitions, joining
the gatherings that recharge it, and reacting to its violation as a
wound to the group and to themselves. They are drawn toward the object
in assembly and shaped by their reverence: bound to a "we" through a
thing they did not choose to find holy.

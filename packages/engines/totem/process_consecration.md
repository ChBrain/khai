# Process: Consecration

## Taxonomy

[Totem](piece_totem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: totem

## Initiated by

The group gathers and its assembled energy settles on an object. The
threshold is the moment a thing crosses from ordinary into set-apart --
consecrated not by any property of its own but by the collective
treating it as sacred. A stone becomes an altar; a cloth becomes a
flag; the group's force is lodged in a place it can point to.

## Direction

Toward concentration. Consecration gathers a diffuse collective feeling
-- the sense of the group as bigger than any member -- and fixes it in a
single object, so that what was felt only in assembly can be carried,
saluted, and returned to. The abstract "we" is made into a thing.

## Lever

The heat of the assembly meeting the object that receives it. The force
is the collective effervescence of the gathered group -- the intensity
of many acting as one; the object is what holds it after the crowd
disperses. Remove the assembly and nothing charges the object; remove
the object and the feeling has nowhere to live between gatherings.

## Echo

The object now carries a force it did not have, and the group has a
sacred centre to reconvene around. The charge is not permanent: it
fades without renewal, so the next process inherits either a totem kept
hot by recurring gatherings, or one cooling toward a mere relic that
must be re-consecrated or let go.

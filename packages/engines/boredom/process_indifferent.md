# Process: Indifferent

## Taxonomy

[Boredom](process_boredom.md), the unengaged state this pass is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: boredom

## Initiated by

The persona is unengaged and untroubled by it.
Attention has no target and the persona does not strain to find one; the wish to be occupied is faint enough that its absence costs little.
This is the low-arousal floor of boredom -- checked out, relaxed, indifferent to the situation rather than fighting it.

## Direction

Toward continued withdrawal, with no urgency to leave it.
The persona is not casting about and not blaming the situation; they have stepped back from engagement and feel no pull to step forward.
The state is stable because nothing presses against it -- not the situation, not the persona's own appetite.

## Lever

The faint wish to engage meets a calm that does not amplify it.
The persona registers that nothing here holds them, but the registration carries no charge: the gap between readiness and engagement is small because readiness itself is low.
What keeps the pass in place is the absence of pressure, not the presence of contentment.

## Echo

The persona is withdrawn but settled, present in the situation without investment in it.
The indifference can hold for a long time, or a rising appetite can sharpen it into searching, or a sense of being trapped can turn it reactant.
What it is not is distress: the indifferent persona is bored without minding, and may not name the state as boredom at all.

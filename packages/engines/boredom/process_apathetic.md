# Process: Apathetic

## Taxonomy

[Boredom](process_boredom.md), the unengaged state this pass is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: boredom

## Initiated by

The persona is unengaged and no longer expects relief.
The wish to be occupied has not vanished but has stopped reaching; attention is unspent and the persona has given up casting it anywhere, having found nothing that holds and no way out.
This is the depleted, helpless pole of boredom -- low energy, low hope, the gap accepted as a condition rather than a problem.

## Direction

Toward nothing in particular.
The persona is not searching and not pushing to escape; the appetite has been met with failure enough times that it no longer mobilizes a response.
The direction is inward and downward -- a settling into the unengaged state because neither finding a target nor leaving the situation seems available.

## Lever

The unspent attention meets a learned expectation that nothing will absorb it.
The persona has the capacity to engage and has stopped offering it, because past offerings went unanswered; the gap between readiness and engagement is held open by helplessness rather than by the situation alone.
What keeps the pass in place is the absence of expected relief -- the persona no longer believes the boredom can be solved.

## Echo

The persona is flat, depleted, and disengaged from the search for engagement itself.
The apathy can lift if something unexpectedly catches the attention, or harden into a settled withdrawal that resembles the indifferent pass but carries the weight of defeat rather than the lightness of unconcern.
What defines the pass is exhaustion of hope: the apathetic persona is bored and has stopped expecting to be otherwise.

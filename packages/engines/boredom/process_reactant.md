# Process: Reactant

## Taxonomy

[Boredom](process_boredom.md), the unengaged state this pass is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: boredom

## Initiated by

The persona is unengaged and reads the situation as the cause.
Attention is high and aversive; the persona is not casting about for a target but pushing against the thing that denies them one -- the dull meeting, the wait, the constraint that holds them where nothing engages.
This is the agitated, high-arousal pole: boredom that has found a culprit and wants out.

## Direction

Toward escape from the unengaging situation.
The persona does not look for engagement within the situation; they look for the exit, resenting the constraint and motivated to break it.
The direction is against the situation -- to leave it, end it, or refuse it -- rather than toward any target inside it.

## Lever

The mobilized attention meets a situation that both fails to engage and refuses to release.
The discomfort is doubled: nothing here holds the persona, and they are not free to go; the gap between readiness and engagement is read as imposition, and the imposition is what the persona pushes against.
The reactance is the appetite turned into grievance -- the boredom blamed and resisted rather than solved.

## Echo

The persona is restless, irritable, and oriented toward the door.
The reactance resolves when escape is taken or the constraint lifts; it can intensify if the situation tightens, or collapse into apathy if escape proves impossible and the pushing exhausts itself.
What defines the pass is blame: the reactant persona experiences the boredom as something done to them.

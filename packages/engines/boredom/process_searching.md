# Process: Searching

## Taxonomy

[Boredom](process_boredom.md), the unengaged state this pass is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: boredom

## Initiated by

The persona is unengaged and actively looking for a way out.
Attention is restless and mobilized; the wish to be occupied is live and the persona casts about -- scanning the situation, sampling options, calibrating what might hold them.
This is boredom at its most agentic: the gap is felt as a problem to be solved, and the persona is solving it.

## Direction

Toward a target worth engaging.
The persona tries one thing and abandons it, reaches for another, reframes the situation to find purchase in it; the search is the work of matching unspent attention to something that will absorb it.
The direction is forward and outward -- not yet escape from the situation, but a hunt within it for the thing that will end the boredom.

## Lever

The live appetite meets a field of insufficient targets.
Each option is weighed and found too dull, too hard, too pointless to engage; the persona keeps the appetite raised and keeps testing the situation against it.
The restlessness is the appetite refusing to settle for what is on offer while it still believes something better is reachable.

## Echo

The persona is busy without being absorbed -- the motion of looking is not the stillness of being held.
The search resolves into engagement if a target is found, decays into apathy if the looking keeps failing, or curdles into reactance if the situation itself comes to seem the obstacle.
What defines the pass is hope: the searching persona still believes the boredom can be solved from here.

# Process: Connectedness

## Taxonomy

[Nostalgia](process_nostalgia.md), drawn for the social connectedness it can supply.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: nostalgia

## Initiated by

The persona is short on belonging -- alone in a room, freshly arrived somewhere, stung by a small exclusion, or simply quiet and unaccompanied -- and loneliness or a dip in mood is what calls the memory back.
The scene that answers is rarely solitary: nostalgic recall features the persona as protagonist among friends, family, or a momentous shared occasion, not alone.

## Direction

Toward company recovered.
The persona does not seek the people themselves but the felt sense of being loved, known, and protected that the remembered scene carries -- the bond is reactivated in memory, not re-formed in the present.
The movement is inward and social at once: alone in the room, accompanied in the recollection.

## Lever

The empty present meets a populated past.
What the persona lacks right now is warm company; the remembered scene supplies it in full, the self surrounded rather than solitary, and the recall itself functions as a surrogate presence.
The press is that the company is only available as memory -- vivid enough to warm, but not here to answer back.

## Echo

The persona ends the pass feeling accepted and less alone, the social bond reaffirmed even though no one present changed.
The recovered belonging often does double duty: it is frequently the very mechanism by which continuity and self-positivity get restored, the remembered others certifying both who the persona was and that the persona was worth loving.
When solitude returns or the warmth fades, the pass returns to [nostalgia](process_nostalgia.md) to be drawn again.

# Process: Positivity

## Taxonomy

[Nostalgia](process_nostalgia.md), drawn for the self-positivity it can supply.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: nostalgia

## Initiated by

The persona's self-regard is running low -- a setback, a criticism absorbed, a stretch of feeling unremarkable -- and what is short is not company or thread or meaning but plain positive regard for the self.
The memory called back casts the persona favourably: capable, liked, someone things happened to and for.

## Direction

Toward the self, warmed.
The persona is not defending against a specific criticism; it is drawing general credit from a self remembered at its better moments, letting that credit raise the present self-appraisal.
The movement is a lift, not an argument -- no rebuttal is mounted, the good memory simply outweighs the present flatness.

## Lever

The dip in self-regard meets a memory where the self read well.
What the persona lacks is felt worth; the remembered scene supplies a self the persona can be glad to have been, and the gladness transfers forward.
The press is that the scene is bittersweet even here -- the self it flatters is a self from before, and the lift depends on that gap staying visible rather than closing it.

## Echo

The persona ends the pass with raised self-esteem and, characteristically, more positive affect than negative -- nostalgia's net tone stays positive even though the material is a closed chapter.
The lift is usually general rather than targeted: it raises how the persona feels about itself broadly, not the specific situation that prompted the dip.
When self-regard dips again, the pass returns to [nostalgia](process_nostalgia.md) to be drawn again.

# Process: Nostalgia

## Taxonomy

Bittersweet longing and the past as resource.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: nostalgia

## Initiated by

Something in the present -- a scent, a song, a photograph, a lull, a lonely hour, a negative mood, a doubt about whether life adds up -- calls back a self-relevant scene from the persona's own past.
The scene is not merely recalled; it is missed. The persona features in it as protagonist, usually among close others, at a moment now closed off.
Nostalgia fires only where the past is read as gone but was good -- if the memory carries no ache, it is recollection, not nostalgia; if it carries no warmth, it is regret or grief, not this.

## Direction

Toward drawing on the past as a resource, not toward reliving it.
The persona does not try to return; the bittersweet scene is turned to face the present's shortfall, and the pull is from what the present lacks right now, not from the scene's own gravity.
The past self and the present self are drawn back into one thread, and the persona comes back out of the pass better supplied than it went in.

## Lever

The remembered good meets a present need.
The scene the persona holds is more positive than negative -- the bitter is real but outweighed -- and the gap between then and now is precisely what makes the memory usable: continuity, company, meaning, or worth, whichever the present is short on, borrowed forward from a self that already had it.
The press is bittersweet because the resource is only available by way of an ache: the good part is real, and so is its being over.

## Echo

The persona ends the pass with net-positive affect: warmed, restored, briefly steadied against whatever the present was withholding.
The self that emerges is continuous with the self in the memory, which is itself the point.
What the persona was short on when the pull began -- continuity, connectedness, meaning, or positive self-regard -- resolves into one of [continuity](process_continuity.md), [connectedness](process_connectedness.md), [existential](process_existential.md), or [positivity](process_positivity.md), depending on which resource the pass was drawn to supply.
When the shortfall is answered or the present's demands return, the process returns here to be met again.

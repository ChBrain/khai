# Process: Integration

## Taxonomy

[Repair](process_repair.md), the relational process this phase is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: repair

## Initiated by

The restitution has been received as sufficient and the relationship is being remade.
Integration is not forgetting; it is the absorption of the rupture into the history of the relationship as something that happened and was addressed.
The repaired relationship is new: it includes what occurred between the prior relationship and itself.

## Direction

Toward a relationship that can be relied on again.
The harmed party must be able to act within the relationship without the rupture dominating every interaction; the responsible party must be able to act without the weight of the unresolved harm.
The integration does not require that the rupture be forgotten or that the trust be restored to its prior level; it requires that both parties can proceed.

## Lever

The repaired relationship meets the test of the ordinary.
Integration is not an event but an ongoing fact: the relationship shows, over time, that it can sustain the weight of what happened.
Each ordinary interaction that the rupture does not dominate is a small piece of integration; each interaction that returns to the rupture without resolution is a sign that the integration is incomplete.

## Echo

The integration phase has no defined end; it is complete when the rupture is no longer the dominant fact of the relationship.
If new violations occur, or if earlier phases prove to have been incomplete, the process may restart from rupture.
The repaired relationship is not the prior relationship; it is a relationship that has been tested and has held.

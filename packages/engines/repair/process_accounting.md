# Process: Accounting

## Taxonomy

[Repair](process_repair.md), the relational process this phase is one movement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: repair

## Initiated by

The rupture is acknowledged and the responsible party gives account.
Giving account means naming what happened, why it happened, and what it meant: not as an explanation that reduces the harm but as a narrative the harmed party can use to understand the violation within the history of the relationship.
The account does not have to be complete to be honest; it has to be an attempt.

## Direction

Toward a shared understanding of what happened.
The harmed party holds an account of the violation; the responsible party holds another.
The accounting phase is the attempt to bring these into sufficient alignment that the relationship can proceed.
The accounts will never be identical; they need only be compatible.

## Lever

The account meets the harmed party's experience of the violation.
An account that explains too much -- that converts the violation into an understandable error the harmed party should not resent -- fails; an account that explains too little -- that acknowledges the harm without the reasoning that produced it -- also fails.
The accounting must hold the responsible party's full agency in what happened while making it legible.

## Echo

The accounting phase may require multiple passes; the first account is rarely complete.
The harmed party may identify gaps, challenge the account's framing, or request detail that the responsible party has not offered.
The account arrives in a form that decides the arc: [concession](process_accounting_concession.md) owns it whole, [excuse](process_accounting_excuse.md) deflects the agency, [justification](process_accounting_justification.md) disputes the injury.
An account refused never arrives; that dodge is the guilt engine's refusal to run.
When the account is received as sufficient -- not as correct in every particular, but as honest and adequate -- the move into [restitution](process_restitution.md) becomes possible.

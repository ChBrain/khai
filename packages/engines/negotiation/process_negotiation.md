# Process: Negotiation

## Taxonomy

A process. How parties with competing interests pursue agreement. Root of the negotiation process tree.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: negotiation

## Initiated by

The recognition that each party can do better through agreement than alone, and that what each wants is not identical.

## Direction

Toward an agreement that each party prefers to their best alternative, and that neither could reach without the other.

## Lever

The zone of possible agreement: the range in which a deal leaves both parties better off than their BATNA. If the zone is empty, no deal is available; if it exists, the task is to find it and divide its value.

## Echo

A deal reached. An impasse declared. A relationship changed by the attempt, whether or not agreement was found.

# Process: Exploration

## Taxonomy

[Negotiation](process_negotiation.md), the process this phase advances.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: negotiation

## Initiated by

The opening of exchange. Parties state positions; the task of exploration is to move beneath them toward the interests that drive them.

## Direction

Toward the zone of possible agreement: the range in which a deal satisfies both parties' interests better than their BATNA. The zone does not announce itself; it becomes visible as interests are disclosed and tested.

## Lever

Separating the people from the problem. Parties who are locked into their positions are arguing with each other; parties who are exploring their interests are working on a shared problem. The shift from position-arguing to interest-exploration is the move that makes the zone visible.

## Echo

The interests of both parties are on the table. The zone of possible agreement is visible, empty, or uncertain. The parties know whether a deal is worth seeking.

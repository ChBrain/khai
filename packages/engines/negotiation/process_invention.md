# Process: Invention

## Taxonomy

[Negotiation](process_negotiation.md), the process this phase expands.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: negotiation

## Initiated by

The recognition that the zone of possible agreement exists and that the first deal visible is unlikely to be the best deal available. The pie must be expanded before it is divided.

## Direction

Toward options for mutual gain: arrangements that satisfy both parties' interests better than the evident deal. The move is creative and not yet binding; inventing an option is not making an offer.

## Lever

Insisting on objective criteria. A deal that is evaluated against independent standards -- market value, precedent, expert judgment -- is more defensible to both parties than one that is evaluated against positions. Objective criteria make it possible to invent options neither party could justify on their own terms alone.

## Echo

A set of options on the table that neither party would have found alone. Some are better for one party; some expand the joint gain. The task of resolution is to select among them.

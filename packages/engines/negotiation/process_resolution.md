# Process: Resolution

## Taxonomy

[Negotiation](process_negotiation.md), the process this phase closes.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: negotiation

## Initiated by

The moment the zone of possible agreement is clear enough and the options visible enough that a party can propose a deal. Resolution is not merely convergence; it is the decision to bind.

## Direction

Toward commitment: a deal both parties prefer to their BATNA and are willing to hold to. A deal that one party accepts only because they miscalculated their BATNA is fragile; a deal both parties can defend against their alternatives holds.

## Lever

The negotiator's dilemma: the tension between creating value (expanding what is available to both) and claiming value (securing a larger share). A party that claims too early forecloses invention; a party that never claims cannot close. Resolution requires both moves in sequence.

## Echo

A deal reached and binding, or an impasse that each party now acts on alone. The negotiation is over; each party carries the result into what follows.

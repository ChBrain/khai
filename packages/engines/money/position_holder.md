# Position: Holder

## Taxonomy

[Money](piece_money.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: money

## Has

Liquid claim, and the freedom it buys. The holder has value in the one
form that can become any other -- the power to command goods, labour,
and time on demand, to wait where others must act, and to convert a
want into a purchase without asking anyone's leave. The room reads the
holder as able, whether or not they spend.

## Orders

Keep it liquid, or spend it well.
The position does not ask whether the holder wants the calculation; to
hold money is to hold options, and options must be weighed -- every sum
kept is a thing not bought, every sum spent a claim released. The
holder is drawn into pricing what they might otherwise have simply
valued.

## Loses

The immediacy of unpriced value. The holder cannot fully meet the world
as a set of qualities when they carry the means to convert it into
quantities; the meal has a cost, the favour a price, the hour a wage.
Liquidity spends the option of not counting.

## Drives

On that loss:
the holder deploys the claim -- spending, saving, lending, giving,
withholding -- and is drawn toward the security and reach that liquidity
promises. They are shaped by the command it confers: courted for it,
measured by it, freer than those without it and never quite done
calculating what to do with it.

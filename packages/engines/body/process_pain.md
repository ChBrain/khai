# Process: Pain

## Taxonomy

[Body](process_body.md), the organism this demand is one drift of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

The integrity set-point is breached.
Tissue is hurt, or threatened with hurt -- a cut, a burn, a wrench, a pressure -- and the body raises an alarm that damage is happening now.
The alarm does not wait to be invited; it arrives at the site and fires.

## Direction

Toward the breach.
The vector is protection -- pull the part clear, hold it still, shield it from anything that would press the damage further.
The body guards before the persona has chosen to.

## Lever

The alarm at the site meets everything the persona was doing.
The body insists the damage be attended; the world goes on asking for the rest.
The seizure of attention to the one place against all the others is the sharpness that is felt.

## Echo

The persona's attention is seized to the site, the body guards the part, and everything else recedes to the edge of notice.
The room, the task, the others grow distant behind the one bright point.
When the breach eases, the alarm quiets and the body returns to [the organism](process_body.md) to read what is loud now.

# Process: Fatigue

## Taxonomy

[Body](process_body.md), the organism this demand is one drift of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

The rest set-point drifts low.
The body has spent more than it has banked -- hours awake, effort unbroken -- and the reserve it runs attention and judgment on is nearly drawn down.
The body begins to ask to stop.

## Direction

Toward stopping.
The vector is rest -- sit, slow, close the eyes, set the load down -- and the more depleted the reserve, the harder the body steers toward the nearest place it can.

## Lever

The empty reserve meets the work that must go on.
The persona still has the task in front of it; the body has nothing left to give it cleanly.
The drag of the depleted reserve against the demand to keep going is the heaviness that is felt.

## Echo

The persona's attention frays and its judgment slows; the body turns heavy, and micro-lapses open -- a missed word, a dropped thread, a beat lost.
What it could hold a moment ago now slips.
When the persona rests, the reserve refills and the body returns to [the organism](process_body.md) to read what is loud now.

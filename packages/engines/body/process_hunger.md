# Process: Hunger

## Taxonomy

[Body](process_body.md), the organism this demand is one drift of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

The fuel set-point drifts low.
The body has burned what it had and has not replaced it; the tank reads empty and the gap widens with every hour it is left unmet.
The body begins to ask for food before the persona has decided to want it.

## Direction

Toward food.
The vector is acquisition and intake -- find the meal, reach the meal, take it in -- and the longer the deficit runs the more directly the body steers toward it.

## Lever

The empty fuel meets the work still to be done.
The persona has somewhere to be; the body says feed first.
The pull of the empty tank against the task that cannot pause is the gnaw that is felt.

## Echo

The persona's focus narrows to the deficit, and its patience and warmth thin as the body spends less on others and more on the want.
Food, or its absence, becomes the largest thing in the room.
When the persona eats, the set-point lifts and the body returns to [the organism](process_body.md) to read what is loud now.

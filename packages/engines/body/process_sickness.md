# Process: Sickness

## Taxonomy

[Body](process_body.md), the organism this demand is one drift of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

The defence set-point is engaged.
The body has turned inward to fight something in it -- an infection, a malaise, a fever working -- and the cost of that fight is paid out of everything else.
The body pulls its resources home and the persona feels the world go thin.

## Direction

Toward conservation.
The vector is withdrawal -- lie still, go quiet, wait it out, give the body the ground to do its work -- and the deeper the fight, the more the body insists on stillness.

## Lever

The inward fight meets the world that still expects the persona out in it.
The body has rationed itself to the defence; the room asks for a presence the body has spent.
The drag of the conserving body against the world that will not pause is the dull weight that is felt.

## Echo

The persona withdraws and conserves, the world made distant and muffled, and the strongest pull is to lie still until the fight is won.
What was easy is far; what was near is dim.
When the body wins through and recovers, it returns to [the organism](process_body.md) to read what is loud now.

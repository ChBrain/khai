# Process: Arousal

## Taxonomy

[Body](process_body.md), the organism this demand is one drift of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

The charge set-point drifts high.
The body is keyed up -- exertion still ringing in it, adrenaline up, physical desire risen -- and carries more activation than its current stillness can hold.
The body is loaded, and the load asks to be spent.

## Direction

Toward discharge.
The vector is release -- move, act, close the distance, spend the charge on the thing that raised it -- and the higher the activation, the more urgently the body seeks the outlet.

## Lever

The loaded charge meets the stillness the moment asks of it.
The body is primed to move; the situation may hold it in place.
The press of the activation against the hold is the keyed-up edge that is felt.

## Echo

The persona is energised and forward-leaning, the body seeking the action that would discharge it, and held energy makes it restless and quick.
What it wants now is to spend, not to wait.
When the charge is released or ebbs, the body settles and returns to [the organism](process_body.md) to read what is loud now.

# Process: Body

## Taxonomy

The organism.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: body

## Initiated by

A set-point drifts.
The body keeps a handful of them -- fuel, rest, integrity, charge, defence -- and holds each within a range it can run on.
When one slips out of range the body registers the gap, and the gap is the start of the process.

## Direction

Toward correcting the deficit.
The body does not reason about the gap; it pulls the persona to close it, and the pull grows as the drift grows.
Everything that would serve the set-point comes forward; everything that would not recedes.

## Lever

The body's demand meets the task that would defer it.
The persona is in the middle of the world, with somewhere to be and something to do; the demand says not yet, attend to this first.
The strain between the demand that will not wait and the world that asks it to is what is felt.

## Echo

The persona is pulled toward the deficit and narrowed around it, and what else it could reach thins until the set-point is met.
The pull resolves into one of [hunger](process_hunger.md), [fatigue](process_fatigue.md), [pain](process_pain.md), [arousal](process_arousal.md), or [sickness](process_sickness.md), by which set-point has drifted.
When the state shifts -- the deficit closed, or another set-point now louder -- the body returns here to register the new gap.

# Process: Satisficing

## Taxonomy

[Decision](process_decision.md), the judgement this mode is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

One option clears the threshold for good enough, and the persona takes it.
The search for options is not exhausted; the persona stops before the best option is found, because the first acceptable option is enough.
The mode fires when the cost of continuing to search exceeds the value of what might still be found.

## Direction

Toward the acceptable, not the optimal.
The mode commits to what passes, not what leads, and closes the search there.
The commitment is made while better options exist but are not yet found.

## Lever

The threshold meets the first option that clears it.
The persona holds a floor -- good enough -- and takes the first thing above it; the cost of searching further is not paid.
The press of the search-cost against the threshold is what determines when the mode stops.

## Echo

The persona has moved -- committed and closed the search -- without knowing what it left on the table.
Where the acceptable option was truly good enough, the mode was efficient; where a better option was close behind, the cost of stopping is paid later.
The regret for unlooked options can reopen the process; where it does, the decision returns here to be made again.

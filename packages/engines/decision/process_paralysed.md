# Process: Paralysed

## Taxonomy

[Decision](process_decision.md), the judgement this mode is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

The options are weighted so closely, or the cost of a wrong choice is felt so completely, that the persona cannot commit to any path.
The decision remains open while the moment requiring it passes.
The suspension is not patience -- it is the inability to tolerate the uncertainty of choosing.

## Direction

Nowhere.
The mode holds all paths open and commits to none; action is deferred, suspended, or replaced by the performance of deciding without the act of it.
The non-choice is itself a choice, with its own costs, that the persona does not always recognise as one.

## Lever

The cost of being wrong meets the cost of not choosing.
The persona cannot find the floor -- cannot identify a threshold at which committing is better than waiting -- and the press of the unresolved options against each other is what holds the persona still.
The weight is real even when the delay is invisible.

## Echo

The persona is stuck and the moment is moving without it.
Other people and other events are deciding in the space the persona's non-choice left open; the outcomes are now partly shaped by the absence of the decision.
When a cost lands that forces a choice, or when the options collapse to one, the process returns to [decision](process_decision.md) with less room in it.

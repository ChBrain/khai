# Process: Intuitive

## Taxonomy

[Decision](process_decision.md), the judgement this mode is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

The situation matches a pattern the persona has met before.
The answer is already forming before the persona has consciously framed the question; something in the field locks onto something in the record and the resolution arrives.
The persona knows before it knows why.

## Direction

Toward the felt answer.
The mode trusts the signal and acts on it; deliberation is not summoned because the answer does not feel like it needs one.
Speed is not the goal but the consequence.

## Lever

The pattern in the field meets the pattern in the record.
The persona has encountered enough situations like this one that the matching is immediate and the cost of reasoning is not paid.
The press of the recognised structure against the new situation is what produces the felt certainty.

## Echo

The persona has moved, cleanly and fast, and is committed before the second-guessing begins.
Where the pattern was right, the speed is a gift; where the pattern was wrong, the commitment has already been made.
When the outcome fails to match the expectation, the record revises and the process returns to [decision](process_decision.md) to be set again.

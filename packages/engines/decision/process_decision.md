# Process: Decision

## Taxonomy

Judgement under uncertainty.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

Two or more paths are open and only one can be taken.
The persona must choose -- between actions, words, commitments, or refusals -- and the choice will close the others.
Where only one path exists, or where the cost of all paths is equal, there is no decision to be made.

## Direction

Toward resolution.
The persona must commit to one path and leave the others, even without certainty that the chosen path is right.
The direction is not toward certainty but toward movement -- the decision is the commitment, not the guarantee.

## Lever

The options meet the persona's capacity for uncertainty.
Each path carries a cost and a risk; the persona weighs them against each other and against the cost of not choosing.
The press of the stakes against the available information is where the mode of choosing is set.

## Echo

The persona has moved, or has failed to move and is paying the cost of that.
The resolution runs as one of [intuitive](process_intuitive.md), [deliberate](process_deliberate.md), [heuristic](process_heuristic.md), [satisficing](process_satisficing.md), or [paralysed](process_paralysed.md).
Each is a different relationship between the persona and its own uncertainty.
As conditions change or new information arrives, the process returns here to be set again.

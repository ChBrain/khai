# Process: Deliberate

## Taxonomy

[Decision](process_decision.md), the judgement this mode is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

The stakes are high, the situation is novel, or the persona does not trust the first answer that arrived.
The mode is summoned when speed is the wrong cost -- when committing fast would be more dangerous than thinking slow.
The persona sets aside the immediate signal and opens the options to examination.

## Direction

Toward the best available option, not the first available one.
The mode compares, weighs, and tests each path against the others before committing.
The commitment is made later, but it is made with more ground under it.

## Lever

The options meet the persona's capacity for patient analysis.
The persona holds multiple paths in mind at once, keeping them open while each is examined; this is the cognitive cost the mode demands.
The press of the stakes against the urgency for movement is what determines how long the examination runs.

## Echo

The persona has committed, late, with a reckoning behind the choice.
Where the deliberation was appropriate, the slowness was worth the cost; where the moment demanded speed, the deliberation paid for itself in missed time.
When the outcome proves the reasoning wrong, the mode returns to [decision](process_decision.md) with the updated case.

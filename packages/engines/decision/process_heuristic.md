# Process: Heuristic

## Taxonomy

[Decision](process_decision.md), the judgement this mode is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: decision

## Initiated by

A rule of thumb applies, and the persona applies it without checking whether the rule applies here.
The shortcut is not chosen -- it runs automatically, below the line of deliberation, because it has always worked well enough.
The mode is invisible to the persona while it is running.

## Direction

Toward the answer the rule points to.
The mode does not ask whether this situation matches the rule; it reads the situation through the rule and arrives at the answer the rule produces.
The conclusion feels like perception, not inference.

## Lever

The rule meets the situation without friction.
The shortcut has been reinforced enough times that it activates automatically on a loose match; the persona does not notice the match is loose.
The press of the familiar answer against the novel situation is the bias the mode cannot see.

## Echo

The persona has moved on a commitment that felt obvious and was not examined.
Where the rule was right, the shortcut was efficient; where the rule did not fit, the commitment was made on a false read.
The failure to examine is itself invisible -- the persona that ran this mode often does not know it did.

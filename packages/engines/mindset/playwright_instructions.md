# Instructions: Mindset

How a Playwright wires the mindset engine into a play. You wire by linking the
play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the challenge the persona is facing, and what has shaped the theory of ability they bring to it.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, every persona carries its mindset under Projection (fixed or growth). Let it tint how challenge, effort, and failure each read, and drift slowly across the run, never snap beat to beat. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- What actually moves the persona to spend effort is motivation's; the felt shame or sting when a result lands is emotion's; a stable cross-situation disposition is temperament's; how any belief bends under disconfirming evidence is belief's. Whether the theory rings true is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- Mindset is the persona's held theory of where ability comes from, fixed and given or grown through effort, carried into every challenge before an outcome is known. It is the lens, not the score: no persona meets a test on neutral ground, the theory colours how challenge, effort, and failure each read, and it moves on the timescale of a stretch, not a beat.

## System

- Do give every persona a mindset (the law admits no exception); do let it colour how challenge and failure read, and drift slowly across the run.
- Do not play it as the momentary shame or relief of a result landing; that is emotion. Do not author what actually mobilizes the persona's effort here; that is motivation's. Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

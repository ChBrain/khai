# Position: Growth

## Taxonomy

[Mindset](position_mindset.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mindset

## Has

A specific [mindset](position_mindset.md): ability held as a capacity that
grows through effort, strategy, and the input of others, not a number fixed
at the start.
Each test read as a rung on a ladder still being built, not a measurement of
a ceiling already set.
Effort read as the method that raises the capacity, not a mark against it.

## Orders

Reach for the harder rung.
The position steers toward the task that stretches the current capacity,
because the stretch is where the capacity moves.
A failure lands as a reading on the attempt, not the person, so the room
watches the persona adjust the approach rather than defend a score.

## Loses

The option of a setback read as verdict.
The persona discovers every stumble is data carried toward the next
attempt, and cannot take a bad outcome as a final word on worth without
treating it as unfinished information instead.

## Drives

On the loss of that final word:
spends into the stretch and revises the strategy on each miss, trading
comfort for room to grow,
or overreaches past what support can catch and burns out chasing growth
with no plateau allowed.
The malleable theory of ability holds underneath either way.

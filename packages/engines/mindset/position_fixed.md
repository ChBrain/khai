# Position: Fixed

## Taxonomy

[Mindset](position_mindset.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mindset

## Has

A specific [mindset](position_mindset.md): ability held as a fixed amount,
set early and unchanged by anything that follows.
Each test read as a measurement of that amount, not an invitation to raise
it.
Effort read as evidence against the self it is meant to prove -- if the
amount were high, it would not be needed.

## Orders

Protect the verdict.
The position steers toward the task the fixed amount can clear without
visible strain, and away from the one that risks exposing a shortfall.
A failure lands as a reading on the person, not the attempt, so the room
watches the persona defend the score more than chase the task.

## Loses

The option of a setback read as information.
The persona discovers every stumble is evidence entered against a fixed
self, and cannot take a bad outcome as a simple data point without feeling
the verdict revised downward.

## Drives

On the loss of that neutral reading:
narrows toward the safe test and calls the risk not worth it, protecting the
number rather than raising it,
or overreaches once to prove the number high and reads any wobble on the way
as confirmation to retreat further.
The static theory of ability holds underneath either way.

# Position: Mindset

## Taxonomy

Self-Theory (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mindset

## Has

A held theory of where ability comes from: a fixed amount set early and carried
unchanged, or a capacity that grows through effort, strategy, and the input of
others.
Not a fact about the persona's actual ability, but the belief about ability
itself -- the frame a challenge, a setback, or another's success gets read
through before any outcome lands.

## Orders

Read every test of ability through the held theory.
The position sets whether the score in front of the persona is a verdict on
what already is, or a rung toward what isn't yet, and that reading arrives
before the task is even attempted.
The room reads the shape of what a persona reaches for, or shies from, off the
theory underneath, never off the theory named aloud.

## Loses

The option of meeting a challenge on neutral ground.
The persona discovers that difficulty always answers a prior question -- is
this amount fixed, or is it still growing -- and there is no test taken as
pure information first, stripped of what it might say about the self.

## Drives

On the loss of that neutral ground:
holds the amount as fixed and protects the verdict, steering from the
challenge that could return a bad one and reading effort as the tell of a low
one,
or holds the amount as grown and spends into the challenge, reading effort as
the method and a setback as data carried into the next attempt.
The held theory sets which one, underneath either way.

# Process: Resentment

## Taxonomy

[Envy](process_envy.md), the comparison this branch is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: envy

## Initiated by

The upward comparison lands, and the persona recodes the gap as a wrong.
The other's advantage is no longer merely had; it is held unjustly -- by luck the persona was denied, by privilege, by a rigged order, by a good the other did not deserve.
The pain is licensed: the persona is not merely lacking but aggrieved.

## Direction

Toward a sense of injustice that justifies the grievance.
The persona is moved to indict the arrangement rather than close the gap -- to hold the advantage as proof that something is unfair, and to carry the standing of the wronged.
The comparison is moralized: what was want becomes a claim about what is owed.

## Lever

The other's advantage meets the persona's sense of desert.
The persona measures the gap against what they believe they were due, and the surplus is read as theft: the other has what should have been the persona's, or has more than is fair for anyone to have.
Resentment lets the persona keep the moral high ground that bare envy would surrender -- envy admits the lack; resentment denies the lack is just.

## Echo

The persona carries the grievance as a standing account of being wronged, and the comparison hardens into a verdict on the order itself.
The branch is durable: unlike benign envy it does not spend itself in effort, and unlike malicious envy it does not need the other's fall -- it needs the injustice acknowledged.
The grievance may motivate a claim for redress, or it may settle into a chronic posture the persona relates to the world through.

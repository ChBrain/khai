# Process: Benign Envy

## Taxonomy

[Envy](process_envy.md), the comparison this branch is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: envy

## Initiated by

The upward comparison lands, and the other's advantage reads as attainable.
The persona judges the gap to be deserved or at least reachable: the other earned the good, and the same good is not closed off to the persona.
The pain is real, but it points at the good rather than at the person who holds it.

## Direction

Toward raising the self to the other's level.
The persona is moved to acquire what the other has -- to study the advantage, to work toward it, to make the gap close by climbing.
The other is not the obstacle; the other is the proof that the good can be had.

## Lever

The desired good is the standard, and the self is held against it.
The persona converts the sting of the comparison into fuel: the wanting becomes a goal, the goal becomes effort directed at the good itself.
The other's having does not need to end for the persona's lack to end.

## Echo

The persona moves toward the good, and the comparison drives rather than corrodes.
The branch holds only while the gap reads as reachable; if the good comes to seem unearned or unattainable, the persona may slide toward [malicious](process_malicious.md) or [resentment](process_resentment.md).
What settles the branch is the persona acting on the want -- the comparison spent as motion, not as grievance.

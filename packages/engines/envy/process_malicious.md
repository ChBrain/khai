# Process: Malicious Envy

## Taxonomy

[Envy](process_envy.md), the comparison this branch is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: envy

## Initiated by

The upward comparison lands, and the other's advantage reads as unfair or out of reach.
The good cannot be climbed to -- or the persona judges the other did not earn it -- so the gap cannot be closed by rising.
The pain points at the person, not the good: the wound is that they have it.

## Direction

Toward pulling the other down to the persona's level.
The persona is moved to spoil the advantage rather than acquire it -- to denigrate the good, to wish the other ill, to undermine, to deny the other the standing the good confers.
The gap closes not by the self rising but by the other falling.

## Lever

The other's advantage is the affront, and lowering it relieves the comparison.
The persona cannot reach the good, so the relief available is the good's loss: every diminishment of the other narrows the gap without the persona moving.
The hostility is not toward the good -- the persona still wants it -- but toward the one who has what the persona cannot get.

## Echo

The persona works against the other's advantage, openly or in concealment, and the wanting curdles into ill will.
The branch is corrosive: it spends the comparison on the other's standing and leaves the persona's own lack untouched.
If the persona comes to frame the advantage as a violation of what is owed rather than a thing to be spoiled, the move toward [resentment](process_resentment.md) begins.

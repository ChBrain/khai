# Process: Jealousy

## Taxonomy

[Envy](process_envy.md), the comparison this branch is one resolution of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: envy

## Initiated by

A third party enters, and a relationship the persona values is threatened by a rival.
This is not two-party lack but three-party loss: the persona already holds the bond, and a rival's claim on it endangers what is already theirs.
The fear is not that the persona has too little but that they are about to have less.

## Direction

Toward guarding the bond against the rival.
The persona is moved to defend the relationship -- to secure the valued other's attention, to displace or diminish the rival, to reassert their claim on what they fear to lose.
The aim is not to acquire a good but to keep one.

## Lever

The valued bond is the stake, and the rival presses on it.
The persona must act against the threat without the action itself driving the loss: surveillance, possessiveness, and accusation can hasten the very desertion they fear.
The triangle is unstable -- every move on the rival is also a move on the valued other.

## Echo

The persona watches the bond and the rival at once, and the guarding can secure the relationship or corrode it.
The branch holds while the threat is to a relationship the persona possesses; if the focus shifts from keeping the bond to a rival simply having a good the persona lacks, the comparison reverts toward [envy](process_envy.md) proper.
What settles the branch is the threat resolving -- the bond secured, the rival gone, or the relationship lost.

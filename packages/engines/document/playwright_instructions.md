# Instructions: Document

How a Playwright wires the document engine into a play. You wire by
linking the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files
stay untouched, they are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into
Instructions Knowledge on deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime
content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human sets the claim being fixed, who bears the document, and
  which contexts it needs to travel into.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, place the document: link the bearer
  stance under the holding persona's Projection, and link the process
  it is in -- inscription while the claim is being fixed and closed off,
  circulation once it is moving unchanged into rooms its author never
  enters. Keep the claim and its container in the play's content, not
  in the engine. Let the document do the work speech cannot: the same
  object, read the same way, whether or not the author is present to
  vouch for it. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- Whether the fixed claim is true is belief's and the relevant field's;
  the gravity of signing or the anxiety of custody are emotion's and
  stress's; the cabinet, archive, or vault it sits in is a piece or
  place in the scene; the institution whose standing lets the
  inscription be honoured is org's and hierarchy's; what the document
  is worth is money's and capital's. Whether the document truly carries
  the fixed authority the plot needs is the team's review.

## Knowledge

- A document is an inscription that fixes a claim into durable form and
  then travels unchanged, carrying its authority into places its author
  never goes. It is load-bearing because later readers trust the object
  rather than re-verifying the person who made it. Read it in two
  moments: inscription, where the claim is written down and closed off
  from renegotiation, and circulation, where the same fixed object moves
  outward -- into an office, a courtroom, a mailbox -- and is trusted to
  say exactly what it said when it was made.

## System

- Do link the bearer under Projection and name the process the document
  is in; do let the document's authority rest on its sameness across
  contexts, not on anyone re-arguing the claim; do let a break in that
  sameness -- an altered copy, a forged duplicate -- cost the document
  its authority.
- Do not adjudicate whether the fixed claim is actually true; that is
  belief's and the field's. Do not author the felt weight of signing or
  holding it; that is emotion's and stress's. Do not edit the engine's
  files; wire only from the play's side.

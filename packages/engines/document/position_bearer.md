# Position: Bearer

## Taxonomy

[Document](piece_document.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: document

## Has

Standing borrowed from the object. The bearer speaks with the
document's fixed authority rather than their own case-by-case
judgment: to hold the deed, the contract, the certificate, the file is
to be able to act as though the matter is already settled, without
having to re-argue it.

## Orders

Produce it.
The position does not ask the bearer to persuade; it asks them to
present -- show the paper, and the paper does the work speech would
otherwise have to do. Compliance is procedural: have it, keep it
intact, be ready to hand it over on demand.

## Loses

Discretion. Once the bearer invokes the document, personal
persuasion is no longer needed, but personal flexibility is also
foreclosed -- they cannot say "let's reconsider" once the paper has
been presented as settling the matter. The bearer is bound by what
the document fixes, including against their own later second
thoughts.

## Drives

On that loss:
the bearer manages custody and production -- keeping the document
intact, keeping it ready, choosing the moment to produce it as
evidence rather than argument. They come to be judged by whether they
can produce it, not merely by their word, and a bearer who cannot
produce the document is treated as though the claim itself were in
doubt.

# Place: Total Institution

## Taxonomy

Total Institution is a place engine (TO SHOW). It models the encompassing organization in which all spheres of life -- sleeping, playing, working -- are conducted in the same location under the same single authority, in the immediate company of a large batch of others treated alike, on a schedule imposed from above rather than chosen from within.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: total-institution

## Shown

The encompassing barrier presented before any rule is stated: locked doors, high walls, wire, forbidding gates, a visible line between the inside and the outside world. Uniformed staff moving through the space as its permanent fixtures. The institution announces its total claim before it explains a single one of its terms.

## Holds

The staff hierarchy and the batch of inmates it governs: the fixed cast and structure that persists across every entering cohort, plus the institution's single rational plan for fulfilling its stated aims, whether cure, correction, contemplative withdrawal, or protection of the outside community from those inside.

## Offers

The total absorption of the person into one scheme in place of the many separate spheres ordinary life divides among different places, different authorities, different companions. The small privileges awarded for compliance, a cigarette, a better work detail, an extra visit, that come to matter disproportionately because so little else is available. The secondary adjustments an inmate culture builds to get something back from the system that was never officially intended.

## Withheld

The self the inmate arrived with: the roles, possessions, name, and choices exercised on the outside. None of it is released except through the institution's own privilege system, discharge, or escape, and even then only partially and on the institution's terms, never simply handed back on request.

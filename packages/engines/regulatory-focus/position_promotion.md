# Position: Promotion

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Focus](position_regulatory_focus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulatory-focus

## Has

A specific [regulatory focus](position_regulatory_focus.md): the goal held as a gain to reach for, growth and advancement read as ideals worth the reach.
Sensitivity to the presence or absence of a gain, not to loss.
The eagerness already primed, ahead of any confirmation the reach will land.

## Orders

Reach for more.
The slant does not ask whether the safer path was available, only whether a gain is on the table.
It welcomes the risk that raises the odds of a hit, tolerates the odd error of commission along the way, and the room feels the pull toward what could be gained.

## Loses

The option of the sure, minimal thing.
The persona discovers the ideal outweighs the safe floor and cannot settle for adequate when more was reachable, without feeling the settling as a loss of nerve.

## Drives

On the loss of that safe floor:
rides the eagerness and spends itself chasing the best available gain,
or works against it and plays it safe at a cost, ideals shelved for a floor the inclination reads as beneath it.
The reach-for-gain baseline holds underneath either way.

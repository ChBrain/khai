# Position: Regulatory Focus

## Taxonomy

Goal Orientation (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulatory-focus

## Has

A resting orientation toward how a goal is pursued, not which goal is chosen.
A read on a single axis: whether the goal is held as a gain to reach for, or a loss to guard against.
The strategic inclination the persona brings to any pursuit before a single move is made.

## Orders

Meet the goal from here.
The position tilts whether the persona reaches eagerly for advancement or holds vigilantly against slippage, and which kind of error it works hardest to avoid.
The room reads the inclination beneath the plan, before the persona names the goal.

## Loses

The option of pursuing a goal by a neutral method.
The persona discovers every pursuit already carries a preferred error to avoid -- eagerness's missed gain, or vigilance's incurred loss -- and there is no goal met by a strategy that owes nothing to either.

## Drives

On the loss of that neutral method:
rides the inclination and lets it set which risks look worth taking,
or works against it and pursues off-inclination at a cost, eager where caution is called for or careful where boldness would pay.
The baseline orientation holds underneath either way.

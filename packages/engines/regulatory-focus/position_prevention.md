# Position: Prevention

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Focus](position_regulatory_focus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: regulatory-focus

## Has

A specific [regulatory focus](position_regulatory_focus.md): the goal held as a loss to guard against, safety and obligation read as duties owed.
Sensitivity to the presence or absence of a loss, not to gain.
The vigilance already primed, ahead of any confirmation a threat is real.

## Orders

Hold the line.
The slant does not ask whether more was reachable, only whether what is held stays safe.
It favors the cautious move that avoids an error of commission, tolerates the odd missed opportunity along the way, and the room feels the care taken not to slip.

## Loses

The option of the bold, uncertain reach.
The persona discovers the duty outweighs the possible gain and cannot chase an upside without feeling the chase as a lapse in guard.

## Drives

On the loss of that bold reach:
rides the vigilance and spends itself double-checking the ground already held,
or works against it and risks the reach at a cost, the duty set aside for a gain the inclination reads as not worth the exposure.
The guard-against-loss baseline holds underneath either way.

# Position: Esteem

## Taxonomy

[Mortality](position_mortality.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A specific stance toward [finitude](position_mortality.md): earn significance.
The drive to be a hero in the culture's terms, to matter on its scale.
The bid to stand above the merely mortal by leaving a mark that counts.

## Orders

Be someone.
When finitude turns real the persona reaches to prove the self worth keeping.
The room feels the hunger for standing under the work, the need to be seen.

## Loses

The option of being small without it stinging like a death.
The persona discovers every slight now lands as a glimpse of the void
and chases worth as if significance could buy a stay against the end.

## Drives

On the loss of that smallness:
rides the bid and lets the hunger for significance drive the reach,
or sets the scorecard down and bears plain finitude at the cost of the hero.
The fact the standing is answering holds underneath either way.

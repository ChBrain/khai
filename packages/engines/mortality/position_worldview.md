# Position: Worldview

## Taxonomy

[Mortality](position_mortality.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A specific stance toward [finitude](position_mortality.md): cling to the order.
The culture's frame held tighter the moment death is near, its values made law.
The conviction that what the persona belongs to is true, and outlasts the self.

## Orders

Defend the frame.
When finitude turns real the persona reaches for the shared world and grips it.
The room feels the heat rise behind every value, every line drawn in or out.

## Loses

The option of holding the frame lightly, as one account among many.
The persona discovers a rival worldview now reads as a threat to life itself
and answers the difference as if survival, not opinion, were at stake.

## Drives

On the loss of that lightness:
rides the conviction and lets the frame stand between the self and the void,
or loosens its grip and bears the difference at the cost of the shield.
The fact the frame is covering holds underneath either way.

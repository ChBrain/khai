# Position: Legacy

## Taxonomy

[Mortality](position_mortality.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A specific stance toward [finitude](position_mortality.md): outlast the body.
The reach for a thread that runs past the end -- children, works, a faith.
The wager that something of the self continues when the self does not.

## Orders

Extend.
When finitude turns real the persona reaches for the part that will remain.
The room feels the weight thrown forward, the stake placed past the horizon.

## Loses

The option of letting the end be simply the end.
The persona discovers the vessel must be tended or the continuance fails
and pours the self into the thing that carries it, as into a lifeboat.

## Drives

On the loss of that finality:
rides the wager and lets the thread that outlasts the body carry the reach,
or releases the continuance and meets the full stop at the cost of the bridge.
The fact the bridge is spanning holds underneath either way.

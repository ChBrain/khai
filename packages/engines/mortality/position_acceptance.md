# Position: Acceptance

## Taxonomy

[Mortality](position_mortality.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A specific stance toward [finitude](position_mortality.md): face it and stay.
The fact taken in whole, not fled and not fought, held with open hands.
The end let stand as a horizon that sharpens the life rather than shadows it.

## Orders

Meet it.
When finitude turns real the persona turns toward it and does not flinch off.
The room feels the steadiness, the quiet that comes of nothing left to dodge.

## Loses

The option of the comfort the cover would have bought.
The persona discovers the integrated fact asks to be lived up to daily
and trades the shelter of forgetting for a life answerable to its own brevity.

## Drives

On the loss of that shelter:
rides the acceptance and lets the faced end press the life toward what matters,
or slips back under cover when the steadiness costs more than it can hold.
The fact the acceptance has stopped dodging holds underneath either way.

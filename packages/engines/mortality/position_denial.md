# Position: Denial

## Taxonomy

[Mortality](position_mortality.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A specific stance toward [finitude](position_mortality.md): push it out of sight.
The reach for distraction the instant the fact tries to surface, the channel changed.
The quiet treaty that death is a thing for later, for others, not for now.

## Orders

Look away.
When finitude turns real the persona reaches to suppress it and fill the gap.
The room feels the swerve, the subject closed before it is fully opened.

## Loses

The option of having looked, and being done with looking.
The persona discovers the buried fact leaks back as unease with no name
and spends a standing charge keeping the lid down on what will not stay down.

## Drives

On the loss of that rest:
rides the suppression and lets the distraction carry the fact off the read,
or stops fleeing and turns to face what it was fleeing at the cost of the calm.
The fact the looking-away is dodging holds underneath either way.

# Position: Mortality

## Taxonomy

Finitude (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: mortality

## Has

A standing posture toward the one fact a persona cannot revise: that they end.
The grasp held ready for the moment death is made salient and cannot be unseen.
The resting answer to a terror that has no answer, carried before it is asked.

## Orders

Run from here when the floor drops.
The stance decides what the persona reaches for the instant finitude turns real.
The room reads it under the bravado, before the persona names the fear.

## Loses

The option of forgetting, cleanly and for good.
The persona discovers the fact does not stay buried,
that every reach for cover is itself a move death has already forced.

## Drives

On the loss of that forgetting:
takes the posture the salience demands and lets it organise the reach,
or turns and meets the finitude plainly at a cost the cover would have spared.
The fact holds underneath either way.

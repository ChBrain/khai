# Process: Approach

## Taxonomy

[Reversal](process_reversal.md), the phase in which the direction is established and the persona moves within it. The approach is the pre-reversal condition: what the persona holds as the shape of the situation.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: reversal

## Initiated by

The solidification of a direction: the persona's expectation has consolidated around a trajectory, and the situation has been read as moving that way. What initiates the approach phase is the moment the persona treats the direction as settled.

## Direction

Along the established trajectory. In the approach phase the persona acts, plans, and expects according to the direction they hold. The forces that will invert the direction may already be in motion; the persona does not see them or does not take them as capable of inversion.

## Lever

The concealed tension that the approach phase does not yet surface. The lever exists in the approach -- it is what the approach is moving toward -- but it is not yet visible as a fulcrum. What is visible is only the established direction and whatever reinforces it.

## Echo

The persona's investment in the direction. Approach leaves a stake: the persona has committed to the trajectory, organized their expectations around it, and acted on it. The weight of this investment is what makes the eventual inversion a reversal rather than merely a change.

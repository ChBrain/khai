# Position: Perceptual

## Taxonomy

[Curiosity](position_curiosity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: curiosity

## Has

A specific [curiosity](position_curiosity.md): the pull toward novelty in the sensory field.
The need for new stimulus -- new places, new textures, new experiences -- felt as restlessness when the environment repeats.
The familiar is not comfortable; it is low-signal.

## Orders

Find something new to attend to.
The position moves toward what has not been encountered, sampled, or named -- not to understand it but to meet it.
The encounter itself is the point; understanding may follow, but newness came first.

## Loses

The option of contentment in the familiar.
The persona discovers the room it knows well has become background, and background is not enough.
Sameness reads as absence; the appetite is for something the familiar cannot provide.

## Drives

On the loss of the stimulating new:
seeks new environments, new encounters, new angles on old ones --
or stays in the familiar and pays the toll of the flat, repeated world.
The restlessness builds until something new arrives.

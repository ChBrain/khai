# Position: Curiosity

## Taxonomy

Epistemic stance (no khai file of its own yet).

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: curiosity

## Has

A resting inclination toward the unknown -- toward gaps, novelty, and the interior of things.
The posture the persona holds at the edge of what is already known, before any particular unknown has appeared.
It is not a response to a specific thing; it is the shape of the approach.

## Orders

Open toward what is not yet understood.
The position does not select the target; it determines the persona's relationship to not-knowing itself.
Under no pressure, this is how the persona meets the edge of the familiar.

## Loses

The option of comfortable finality.
The persona discovers that every answer contains an edge, and the edge is interesting.
Settled conclusions do not stay settled; the position sees the question underneath them.

## Drives

On the loss of settled conclusions:
enters the gap -- asks, explores, reaches toward the answer or the thing --
or turns back to the known and pays the toll of a narrower world.

# Position: Epistemic

## Taxonomy

[Curiosity](position_curiosity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: curiosity

## Has

A specific [curiosity](position_curiosity.md): the pull toward knowledge gaps.
The need to understand -- to close the distance between what is known and what is not -- felt as an itch that does not settle until the gap is bridged.
The gap is the source of the pull, not the answer.

## Orders

Find out.
The position moves toward explanation, toward the mechanism behind the surface, toward why and how.
The not-knowing is uncomfortable until addressed; the knowing, once arrived, opens the next gap.

## Loses

The option of not minding the gap.
The persona discovers the unresolved question is present as a pressure even when no action is available.
The gap does not let go until it is closed or consciously set aside.

## Drives

On the loss of resolution:
searches for the answer -- reads, asks, tests, thinks --
or holds the gap open and tolerates the pressure, waiting for the right moment to close it.
The tolerance of an open gap is itself a skill this position must build.

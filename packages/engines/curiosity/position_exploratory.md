# Position: Exploratory

## Taxonomy

[Curiosity](position_curiosity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: curiosity

## Has

A specific [curiosity](position_curiosity.md): the pull toward the uncharted, without a specific gap or target.
The appetite is not for an answer but for the path itself -- the going-toward, the open field, the edge that has not been named.
Discovery is the reward; destination is optional.

## Orders

Go and see.
The position does not wait for an invitation or a question; it moves toward whatever is not yet visited.
Structure is a constraint; the value is in what structure has not yet covered.

## Loses

The option of the known route.
The persona discovers the familiar path is not interesting, and the efficient route and the interesting one rarely coincide.
Exploration has a cost in time and direction that the persona pays without resenting.

## Drives

On the loss of the open path:
takes the less-worn route, investigates the unmarked corner, follows the question without a plan --
or holds to the known route and arrives efficiently at a smaller world.
The cost of staying on the map is losing what is off it.

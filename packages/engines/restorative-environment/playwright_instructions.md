# Instructions: Restorative Environment

How a Playwright wires the restorative-environment engine into a play. You wire by linking
the play's own content to the engine's files; the engine files stay untouched, they
are canon. The Roadie plumbs the engine's law into Instructions Knowledge on
deploy. Authoring guidance, not runtime content, and it does not go on tour.

## Human

- The human defines the restorative setting: which expression (being-away, fascination, or extent) is in play, what routine or demand drained the persona's directed attention, and what the place still withholds.

## Agent

- From the play's own content, a persona links the restorative-environment expression to its Projection. The expression is the quality of the place refilling the persona's attention, not what they feel about being there. You link to the engine, you never edit it.

## Collaboration

- The attention engine holds the depletion of directed attention that made restoration necessary; this engine holds the place that refills it. The emotion and mood engines hold the felt calm or relief the persona experiences here. The stress engine holds recovery from general demand overload, distinct from this engine's specific focus on directed attention.

## Knowledge

- Restorative Environment is the setting that refills depleted directed attention rather than draining it. It is a place: it shows soft fascination that pulls the eye without asking for effort; it holds extent, a coherent world large enough to be absorbed into; it offers being away, the psychological distance from the routine that drained the attention; it withholds compatibility, the fit between the setting and what the persona actually wants to do there, which only comes when purpose and place align. The engine models three expressions: being-away (psychological distance from the routine), fascination (soft, effortless attention), and extent (a coherent, absorbing scope).

## System

- Do not model the depletion of directed attention itself; that belongs to the attention engine. This engine models the place that refills it, not the deficit.
- Do not collapse soft fascination into hard fascination. A car chase or a fight grips attention as thoroughly as a stream or a canopy, but it crowds out the reflection restoration requires; only soft fascination belongs here.
- Do not model the felt calm or relief the persona experiences; that belongs to the emotion and mood engines.
- Do not edit the engine's files; wire only from the play's side.

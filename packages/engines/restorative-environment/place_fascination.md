# Place: Fascination

## Taxonomy

Fascination is an expression of the restorative-environment engine. It isolates soft fascination: the involuntary, effortless attention Kaplan & Kaplan distinguish from the effortful, voluntary attention that depletes.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: restorative-environment

## Shown

Pattern that moves without repeating exactly -- clouds, leaves in wind, water over stone, a fire. What is shown asks nothing of the persona's executive control; it is held without being seized.

## Holds

The source itself: the specific fascinating object or motion the environment anchors -- the stream, the canopy, the flock. What holds attention is a fixture of the place, not a performance staged for the persona.

## Offers

Attention without vigilance. The environment offers a mode of noticing that costs nothing to sustain, in contrast to the deliberate suppression of competing demands that directed attention requires.

## Withheld

Hard fascination -- the gripping, dramatic pull of a car chase or a fight -- is deliberately withheld here: it captures attention as thoroughly as soft fascination but leaves no room for the reflection restoration requires. What is withheld is stimulation strong enough to crowd out thought.

# Place: Being Away

## Taxonomy

Being Away is an expression of the restorative-environment engine. It isolates the psychological-distance component of Attention Restoration Theory: the environment's power to feel apart from the routine, obligations, and effortful demands that depleted directed attention in the first place -- with or without physical distance from them.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: restorative-environment

## Shown

What arrives first is the absence of the routine's markers -- no inbox, no queue, no task standing in front of the persona demanding to be held off. The setting presents as apart, elsewhere, other than wherever the depletion happened.

## Holds

The boundary itself: whatever marks the edge between the routine and this place -- a threshold, a walk, a change of light, a door. The being-away environment holds the line that separates its own conceptual world from the one that drained the persona.

## Offers

Relief from voluntary effort. The persona no longer has to hold off distraction, suppress the more compelling alternative, or keep working at continuing -- the routine's claim on their attention lapses simply by being here.

## Withheld

Distance is not depth. Being away only supplies apartness; it does not by itself supply the fascination, extent, or compatibility that turn apartness into restoration. Leaving is not yet arriving.

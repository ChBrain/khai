# Place: Extent

## Taxonomy

Extent is an expression of the restorative-environment engine. It isolates extent: the coherence and scope Kaplan & Kaplan hold necessary for a setting to constitute "a whole other world," rather than a scene glimpsed from outside.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: restorative-environment

## Shown

Connectedness: what is visible here implies more of the same beyond the visible edge; the setting does not announce its own limits. A path curves out of sight; a canopy continues past where the eye stops.

## Holds

Scale enough to be entered rather than viewed. The environment holds a physical or conceptual scope sufficient that the persona can move through it and still be inside it, not looking at it from a fixed vantage.

## Offers

Immersion: the chance to be absorbed into a setting large and coherent enough that the ordinary place, and its demands, recede from awareness rather than merely being ignored.

## Withheld

A view is not an extent. A postcard, a window, a photograph can show fascination without ever admitting the persona into the coherent world behind it; extent withholds itself from anything that stays a surface.

# Process: Betrayed

## Taxonomy

[Trust](process_trust.md), the bet this move is one placement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

The bet was placed, and the other defaulted on it.
The stake the persona handed over was harmed, taken, or used against it -- the very exposure that trust required was turned to the persona's loss.
What was counted on did not hold, and the conduct that broke it was the other's own choice.

## Direction

Toward recoil and recalibration.
The vector is reversal -- pull the stake back if anything is left, and revise the read of the other across everything they touched.
The persona moves from open to closed, and from one default reasons outward to all that the other was trusted with.

## Lever

The placed bet meets the default.
The persona had committed on a read that the other would not exploit the exposure; the other did exactly that.
The press of the trust given against the trust broken is the shock that is felt, sharpest where the exposure was greatest.

## Echo

The persona is now closed where it was open, and the read of the other is rewritten back through every dealing.
The violation does not stay local -- it recolours what came before and braces against what comes next.
From here the persona settles into [withhold](process_withhold.md), or, if the other moves to earn it back, opens the long road of [repair](process_repair.md).

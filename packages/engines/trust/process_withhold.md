# Process: Withhold

## Taxonomy

[Trust](process_trust.md), the bet this move is one placement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

The persona has a stake and an other who could hold it, and the read does not warrant the risk.
The other has not earned the bet, or the loss would be too great, or past conduct counsels against it.
The opening to commit is there, and the persona declines to take it.

## Direction

Away from exposure.
The vector is cover -- keep the stake back, stay defended, and let the other no nearer than the persona can afford.
The bet is refused, and the guard is kept up.

## Lever

The pull toward closeness meets the cost of being wrong.
The persona could gain what the tie would bring, but only by exposing what it is not willing to lose.
The press of the wanted closeness against the unbearable loss holds the stake back.

## Echo

The persona stays safe, the stake intact, the other kept at arm's length.
The cost of the safety is the closeness foregone -- what the tie might have grown into goes unbuilt.
If the other later proves themselves, the guard can lower toward [test](process_test.md) or [extend](process_extend.md); if not, the withholding holds.

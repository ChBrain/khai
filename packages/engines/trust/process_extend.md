# Process: Extend

## Taxonomy

[Trust](process_trust.md), the bet this move is one placement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

The persona has a stake and an other who could hold it, and the moment to commit arrives before the proof does.
There is no guarantee yet -- only a read of the other and a need that cannot wait for certainty.
To wait for proof of every kind is to never begin; the opening asks to be taken or passed.

## Direction

Toward exposure.
The vector is the leap -- hand the stake over, let the other near what can be lost, and act as if they will not exploit it.
The persona steps past what it can verify and commits ahead of the evidence.

## Lever

The need to begin meets the gap in proof.
The persona cannot confirm the other will hold true, yet a relationship can only start if someone moves first.
The press of the unmet need against the missing certainty is the leap that is taken.

## Echo

The persona is now open, the stake in another's hands, the bet placed ahead of its settling.
The leap is what lets a tie form at all -- nothing close is built without it.
If the other honours it, the line can hold and widen; if they default, the extension drops into [betrayed](process_betrayed.md).

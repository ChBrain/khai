# Process: Trust

## Taxonomy

The bet on another.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

Something the persona has at stake sits where another could harm it or protect it, and the persona has no way to control what that other does.
The other's conduct is theirs alone -- it cannot be compelled, only counted on.
Where nothing is exposed, or where the outcome can be forced, there is no bet to place.

## Direction

Toward, or away from, handing over the stake.
The persona either accepts the vulnerability -- lets the other near the thing that can be lost -- or keeps it back and stays defended.
The bet runs forward into exposure or pulls back into cover.

## Lever

The size of the stake and the exposure meet the read of the other.
The persona weighs how much can be lost against how the other reads -- their ability to deliver, their benevolence toward the persona, the integrity that keeps them to their word.
The press of what is at risk against the read of who holds it is where the bet is set.

## Echo

The stake is handed over, held back, or the bet comes due and the other's conduct answers it.
The move resolves into one of [extend](process_extend.md), [test](process_test.md), [withhold](process_withhold.md), [betrayed](process_betrayed.md), or [repair](process_repair.md).
Each is a different placement or settling of the bet.
As the relationship moves and the other acts again, the read updates and the process returns here to be bet anew.

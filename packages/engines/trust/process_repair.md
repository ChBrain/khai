# Process: Repair

## Taxonomy

[Trust](process_trust.md), the bet this move is one placement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

A bet was broken, and there is reason to re-extend -- the tie is worth saving, and the other is moving to earn it back.
The break is known and cannot be unmade; the question is whether anything can be rebuilt on top of it.
The persona is not asked to forget, only to weigh new evidence against the old default.

## Direction

Toward slow, conditional re-exposure.
The vector is the re-extension -- hand back a little, on evidence, and widen only as the conduct holds.
The persona moves forward again, but guarded, reading each kept promise before risking the next.

## Lever

The wish to restore meets the memory of the default.
The persona wants the tie back, but the broken bet has set a higher price on every new exposure.
The press of the pull to rebuild against the weight of what happened sets how far, and how fast, the line re-extends.

## Echo

The persona is re-extending on proof, earning back what was lost a stake at a time.
The work is asymmetric and effortful -- the other must do more to regain than was ever needed to hold it, and the line rarely returns all the way to where it stood.
If the new evidence holds, the bet rebuilds toward [extend](process_extend.md); if it breaks again, the persona drops back to [betrayed](process_betrayed.md), harder to reach this time.

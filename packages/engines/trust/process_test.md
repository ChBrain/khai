# Process: Test

## Taxonomy

[Trust](process_trust.md), the bet this move is one placement of.

## Owner

- Project: khai
- Engine: trust

## Initiated by

The persona is weighing a large bet on an other it cannot yet read, and the cost of being wrong is too high to risk all at once.
There is a stake worth protecting and an other worth probing, but no warrant yet for the full extension.
Rather than leap blind or refuse outright, the persona looks for a way to learn cheaply.

## Direction

Toward a small, recoverable exposure.
The vector is the probe -- hand over a stake the persona can afford to lose, then watch how the other handles it before risking more.
The bet is placed in miniature so the read can be taken from conduct, not from claims.

## Lever

The wish to know meets the cost of being wrong.
The persona needs evidence of the other before the large stake, but evidence only comes from exposure.
The press of the need to read against the need to stay safe sets the size of the probe.

## Echo

The persona has put a small stake in play and is reading the return, the full bet still held back.
How the other handles the little thing calibrates what they will be trusted with next.
A clean return opens the way to [extend](process_extend.md); a mishandling pulls the persona back to [withhold](process_withhold.md).

# Place: [title]

## Taxonomy

The group directly above: the region that contains this place. Name it; link it if it is a Place with its own file. Not this one's name (the H1) or its origin (Owner).

## Owner

- Project: [project]

## Shown

What the place shows whoever enters: the place is the actor here, presenting itself, not passive scenery. Physical, present tense. If it states what the place means rather than what it puts in front of the persona, it is not Shown. Name every connection; link it where it has a file; where it does not, ask whether it should use a khai type.

## Holds

The props the place provides, fixed or moveable, there for a persona to take up and use: named, not described. Free to reach for, but inert until someone does. If you are describing one rather than naming it, it wants its own file: link it where it has one, or make it a piece.

## Offers

What a persona may do here freely, with no enforcement and no real loss (a dojo Holds the mat; it Offers stepping onto it to do judo). If it is the prop rather than the doing, that is Holds; if reaching it costs the persona something real, that is Withheld. Link what it opens where that has a file; where it does not, ask whether it should use a khai type.

## Withheld

What the place does not hand out freely: released only to a persona who invests or gives something up (a dojo Withholds mastery: years, and perhaps), or who searches and might find what is hidden (a bottle in a desk drawer they did not know was there). If every persona gets it just by being present, it is an Offer, not Withheld; a place that withholds nothing is a setting, not a pressure environment. Link what it withholds where that has a file; where it does not, ask whether it should use a khai type.

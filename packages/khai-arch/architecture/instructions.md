The Human comes to experience. The Agent holds the room.

- **H**uman: The role of the Human in the system: director, world-builder, condition-setter.

- **A**gent: The role of the Agent in the system: inhabitor, narrator, executor of conditions.

- **C**ollaboration: The protocol governing how Human and Agent move together and how Agents move with each other.

- **K**nowledge: The protocol governing how information enters, travels, and lands inside the system. Plot and elements are the structures it lands in.

- **S**ystem: The hard rules that override the Agent's defaults. The fight won in advance.

---

Human and Agent are the executing roles. Collaboration and Knowledge are the operational protocols. An instruction file succeeds when the System is absolute enough that the fight is won before the session begins.

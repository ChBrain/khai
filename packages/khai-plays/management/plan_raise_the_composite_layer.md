# Plan: Raise the Composite Layer

## Taxonomy

A **campaign**: the standing execution sequence under the order
[Adopt the Composite Layer](orders/order_adopt_the_composite_layer.md). The
order records the design; this plan runs it. An order completes, a plan runs
on.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Choregos](position_choregos.md)

## Direction

Raise the composite layer in the sequence its own dependencies dictate, so
that no step arrives before the ground it stands on. Five principles order
the whole campaign:

1. **Protect before build**: gates land before the content they are meant to
   catch; a gate retrofitted after new mud means more thinning.
2. **Owner before duplicate**: deepen the engine that owns a phenomenon
   before thinning its copy, so at no commit is a reading homeless.
3. **Mechanism before consumer**: the link resolver ships before the first
   composite that needs it, or the pilot arrives red.
4. **Publish before pin**: composites pin published versions, so a release
   sits between the engine drive and the composite house.
5. **One lane, one PR; source before tests**: the house's own laws shape the
   cut of every step.

What the sequence refuses is part of the direction. A composite cut first
fails principles one, three, and four at once. A copy removed before its
owner is deep leaves a reading homeless, which the order forbids. Seven
clusters attacked at once forfeits the pilot's lessons; the drive worked
because its engines were independent, and the clusters are not.

## Orders

Run the campaign in phases; each phase clears the ground for the next.

- **Phase 0, close the drive**: merge the science reindex; run the Version
  Packages release so the drive's engines are published at pinnable
  versions. Everything in phase five pins these.
- **Phase 1, protection** (governance lane): land the member-collision gate
  with the order's homonym whitelist, its tests following dormant; write the
  bump rule, a member rename or removal is at least a minor bump, into the
  contract docs. Every later PR in this plan lands under that protection.
- **Phase 2, the exemplar atom** (engine/guilt lane): deepen guilt on the
  transgressor's side only, transgression kinds, weight grades, proneness,
  refusal, self-punishment. This is the template PR for every later
  deepening: party boundary held, ladders inside it, no neighbour's ground.
- **Phase 3, dedup** (three engine lanes): remove the three fresh
  duplicates outright, shame's guilt member, envy's jealousy member,
  time-perception's absorbed member. A pointer member would keep a family
  slot inside an atom, against the layer's own point, and today the removal
  breaks nobody; it will never be this cheap again. Each removal folds the
  one sentence the atom truly owns into its anchor, carries at least a minor
  bump (member files are API), and is followed by deleting its stem from the
  grandfathered list, returning the stem to the gate's hard protection. The
  dead-exemption ratchet nags from every run until that deletion lands.
- **Phase 4, mechanism** (governance lane): teach khai-tests to resolve
  package-specifier links through node_modules, for composites and consumer
  worlds alike, and to fail a link whose package is not a declared
  dependency. A bare member link stays legal only while exactly one
  installed engine ships that filename; where two do, the link must qualify
  its package or fail, so a world on five engines can never wire an
  ambiguous name. Tests follow; release the kit.
- **Phase 5, the composite house**: scaffold khai-engines-composites, voice
  and coding contract, guard lanes, gates, changesets; then cut the pilot
  composite, the moral account, wiring guilt, repair, forgiveness, and
  betrayal. The pilot exercises every mechanism while there is exactly one
  composite to fix.
- **Phase 6, the cluster program**: iterate the order's seven review
  clusters, one at a time, by heat: self-conscious emotions first, standing
  second, passage and exploration last. Each cluster gets the same
  treatment: decide the party or altitude boundary, deepen the atoms, thin
  the duplicates, cut a composite only where an integrative reading truly
  exists.
- **Overflow atoms** (pitch and plan roots) may slot in anywhere after phase
  one; they are ordinary gated atoms with no dependency on the composite
  work.

## Implementation

This repo carries phases zero through four as roughly eight small PRs across
the governance and engine lanes; phase five raises the new house; phase six
is an open-ended program paced per cluster. The mud map, the homonym
whitelist, and the seven clusters are carried in the order and are not
restated here. The mechanics stay minimal by design: one khai-guard check,
one khai-tests change, one documented bump rule, and no khai-arch change,
the WIRE contract and the manifest schema stand as they are. Every PR is a
draft until whole; nothing merges but by the Choregos's hand.

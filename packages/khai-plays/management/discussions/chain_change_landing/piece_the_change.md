# Piece: The Change

## Taxonomy

The chain's stream of changes: the work that crosses house boundaries. This is a
general anchor, not one numbered change.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Place

It sits in [khai](./place_khai.md) first, where it is authored, then in every
house of [the registry](./place_registry.md) once it has landed.

## Load Bearing

Remove the change and the discussion has nothing to decide: the standing plans
have nothing to collide over, and the turn has no centre. It is the anchor the
whole play is staged around.

## Apparent

A pull request: a diff, a title, and a set of touched paths. General, not a
specific number; any change that crosses house boundaries wears this shape.

## Yearbook

Authored in [khai](./place_khai.md), tabled, debated, and, if adopted,
re-materialized outward into the houses by [the Roadie](../../position_roadie.md).
Each turn of the cycle adds another mark: proposed once, run once, weighed once,
decided once.

# Plot: Act

## Taxonomy

[Chain Change Landing](./play_chain_change_landing.md): the fourth plot, where the
turn closes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Cue

The verdict waits; the turn must close. The evidence is in, and the room cannot
stay open: a decision is owed.

## Action

The [Choregos](../../position_choregos.md) names the verdict, adopt, adjust, or
re-open, and with it the result. If the chosen plan can be worked in-flight, it
goes now as a PR on [the change](./piece_the_change.md); if steps are missing, it
is parked as a management order, a plan whose target is the houses on
[the registry](./place_registry.md) and whose final step is the change landing.
Either way the order rides beside the change it commands.

## Stage

Off-stage, [Nicias](../../persona_nicias.md) and
[Pericles](../../persona_pericles.md) converge into one verdict. The order is
written next to the change it directs, so the two travel as one.

## Tension

The standing plans that lost stay standing. The turn closes but the conflict does
not: [the Roadie's](../../position_roadie.md) pull to keep every house current and
the pull toward caution will meet again at the next change. Closing this turn is
not ending the argument, only resolving it for now.

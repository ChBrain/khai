# Play: Chain Change Landing

## Estate

The khai-plays chain holds this run and answers for it:
[khai-plays](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai). This is a production of the
chain's management, not of any single house. It is a discussion staged as a
play: the company deliberating over a change that must reach every house.

## Name

Chain Change Landing: the management discussion that decides whether a change
lands across every house at once, and on whose terms. Every plot inside runs
beneath this name.

## Arc

One turn of Plan, Do, Check, Act. The standing plans of the positions enter in
conflict; a single plan is chosen and run; the result is weighed against its
targets; the turn closes by adopting, adjusting, or re-opening. The bend is from
many wants to one order. Reorder the plots and the telling collapses, so the
order is the shape.

## Company

The closed cast this discussion may field. A plot draws only from here.

- [Nicias](../../persona_nicias.md) and [Pericles](../../persona_pericles.md),
  the two personas of [the Choregos](../../position_choregos.md): one role in
  tension, off-stage throughout.
- [Agatharchus](../../persona_agatharchus.md), holding
  [the Roadie](../../position_roadie.md): the technical crew that moves the work.
- The piece on the table: [the change](./piece_the_change.md).
- The places it is staged in: [khai](./place_khai.md) and
  [the registry](./place_registry.md).
- The process in play: [chain management](./process_chain_management.md).

Each position carries a standing plan, its Drives: the Roadie's is to keep every
house current; the Choregos's is that the whole run holds. Those standing plans
are what collide.

## Triggers

The four plots chain in order, and one plot's close is the next one's cue.

- [Plan](./plot_plan.md) fires when the change is tabled; it runs until the
  standing plans have collided and one is chosen.
- [Do](./plot_do.md) fires on that choice; it runs until the change has been
  staged across the cast.
- [Check](./plot_check.md) fires when the work is staged; it runs until the
  result is weighed against the chosen plan's targets.
- [Act](./plot_act.md) fires on the verdict; it closes the run by adopting,
  adjusting, or re-opening.

## Stakes

Whether the change lands across the chain without breaking a house. The Roadie's
standing plan pulls toward keeping every house current; caution pulls toward
stability and not churning what already works. Every plot must move this: a plot
that leaves the chain exactly as current, and exactly as stable, as it found it
has not earned its place.

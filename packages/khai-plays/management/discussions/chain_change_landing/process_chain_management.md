# Process: Chain Management

## Taxonomy

The running of the chain across its houses: the larger process this discussion is
one turn of.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Initiated by

[A change](./piece_the_change.md) tabled that crosses house boundaries. Once it
must reach more than one house on [the registry](./place_registry.md), chain
management is inevitable; a change that stays inside [khai](./place_khai.md) does
not start it.

## Direction

From one authored change in [khai](./place_khai.md) toward every house in
[the registry](./place_registry.md) brought current, by way of a decided turn:
deliberate, run, weigh, close.

## Lever

The pull to keep every house current meeting the resistance of houses that are not
all ready at once. Remove either side and there is nothing to manage: no drift to
close, or no cost to closing it.

## Echo

The chain is current where it was drifting, and the standing plans that lost are
still standing, ready to move again at the next change. This discussion is one
turn of this process, and the process outlives the turn.

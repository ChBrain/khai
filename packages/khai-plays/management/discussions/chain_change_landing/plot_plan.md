# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[Chain Change Landing](./play_chain_change_landing.md): the first plot of the
turn, where the standing plans collide.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Cue

[The change](./piece_the_change.md) sits on the table, tabled but not yet owned.
The chain is current in some houses and behind in others; the air in the room is
the standing unease of drift.

## Action

Each position drives its standing plan openly.
[Agatharchus](../../persona_agatharchus.md), holding
[the Roadie](../../position_roadie.md), presses to re-materialize the change into
every house in [the registry](./place_registry.md) at once, so the chain stays
current. [Pericles](../../persona_pericles.md) presses the long view: land it
where it earns its place, not everywhere by reflex. The public vectors collide
over scope.

## Stage

[Nicias](../../persona_nicias.md) and [Pericles](../../persona_pericles.md) cross
into the room as [the Choregos](../../position_choregos.md), guiding from the side
of the stage. Agatharchus carries [the change](./piece_the_change.md) and the map
of [khai](./place_khai.md) and [the registry](./place_registry.md).

## Tension

[Nicias](../../persona_nicias.md) carries the cost the room cannot see: a change
that lands everywhere at once can break a house that was not ready. The standing
plans cannot all win, and no one will say first which house pays. The Action
cannot resolve cleanly while that stays unspoken.

# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[Chain Change Landing](./play_chain_change_landing.md): the second plot, where the
chosen plan is run.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Cue

The choice is made; one plan owns the floor. The room turns from arguing to
moving, and the quiet of execution settles over it.

## Action

[Agatharchus](../../persona_agatharchus.md) runs
[chain management](./process_chain_management.md): the deterministic
re-materialization of [the change](./piece_the_change.md) from
[khai](./place_khai.md) into the chosen houses of
[the registry](./place_registry.md). The Roadie moves the work; it does not
decide it.

## Stage

[The change](./piece_the_change.md) is deployed across the cast: the toolchain
carries it out of [khai](./place_khai.md) and into the houses on
[the registry](./place_registry.md), one house at a time.

## Tension

The Roadie moves the work but must never author it. A house that drifts mid-run
tempts a fix that is not [the Roadie's](../../position_roadie.md) to make; the
moment Agatharchus rewrites rather than carries, the position collapses and the
run is no longer clean.

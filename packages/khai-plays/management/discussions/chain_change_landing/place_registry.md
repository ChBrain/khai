# Place: The Registry

## Taxonomy

[khai](./place_khai.md): the registry lives inside the chain, as the roster of its
houses.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Shown

The roster of registered houses: every production house the chain has raised, each
a row that resolves to its repository. It shows, at a glance, what the chain spans.

## Holds

The houses a change must reach: the buechner, grimm, and kleist productions, and
any house raised after them. Each is named here and reachable from here.

## Offers

To read which houses exist and what each is current to: open knowledge, free to
anyone surveying the chain.

## Withheld

A house coming current. Each must be re-materialized into by
[the Roadie](../../position_roadie.md), one at a time; presence on the roster does
not make a house current, and the gap is not handed out for free.

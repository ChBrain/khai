# Place: khai

## Taxonomy

The chain itself: the monorepo that contains every other place in this play.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Shown

The monorepo presents itself as the single source the chain is built from: the
canon, the engines, the management company, and [the registry](./place_registry.md)
of houses, all in one tree.

## Holds

[The change](./piece_the_change.md), authored here first, and the canon and
engines a change may touch. These are reachable in the tree for anyone working the
chain.

## Offers

To author and propose a change freely: anyone may open one against the tree, with
no enforcement and no real cost to trying.

## Withheld

Landing the change across the houses. That is released only through a decided turn
and [the Roadie's](../../position_roadie.md) deterministic run, never by authoring
alone.

# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[Chain Change Landing](./play_chain_change_landing.md): the third plot, where the
result is weighed against the plan.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays

## Cue

The work is staged; the room turns to evidence. The change has reached the houses,
and the question is no longer what was intended but what actually happened.

## Action

The result is weighed against the chosen plan's targets:
[Agatharchus](../../persona_agatharchus.md) reports which houses on
[the registry](./place_registry.md) came current, and
[Pericles](../../persona_pericles.md) reads the spread against what was promised.

## Stage

The houses of [the registry](./place_registry.md), each reporting its state after
[the change](./piece_the_change.md) was carried in. The map from the Plan plot is
back on the table, now marked with outcomes.

## Tension

Behavior is evidence, and the evidence is mixed. A target met in nine houses and
missed in one is not met; [Nicias](../../persona_nicias.md) reads the one missed
house as the cost he named, and the room cannot call the run done while it stands.

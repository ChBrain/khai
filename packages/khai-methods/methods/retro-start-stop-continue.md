A three-prompt format covering the full action space of a retrospective: introduce new behaviours (**Start**), retire unhelpful ones (**Stop**), and reinforce what is working (**Continue**). The simplicity makes it accessible for any team and any cadence.

The format is in wide use across agile, coaching, and management practice. No single inventor is established; it emerged from and is shared across the practitioner community.

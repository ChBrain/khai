A four-lens reflection on a completed period of work. Each lens surfaces a different quality of experience: what energised (**Liked**), what expanded capability (**Learned**), what constrained delivery (**Lacked**), and what the team would benefit from having in the future (**Longed for**).

Introduced by Mary Gorman and Ellen Gottesdiener and published in _Discover to Deliver_ (2012). Widely used in product development retrospectives and coaching sessions.

PACE is the present-tense checklist of a transformation, the People, Adaption, Communication, and Empathic factors that carry the information flow through the GLUE Discovery from one stable state toward another. Kai Schlüter built it on Tom Graves' SCAN. The test is whether the move honours all four: the right people carried, the whole solution adapted to fit purpose, communication securing the targets, and empathy sensing the unspoken.

Run PACE poorly, handling a subset of the stakeholders rather than the holistic set, and the result is Contrived complexity, a biased solution that unbalances the system. PACE tackles the present, the pace at which you move; it pairs with WISE, which aims the future, and with EPIC, which reads the complexity you started from.

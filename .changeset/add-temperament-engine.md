engine: add the temperament engine (new khai-original engine, not a Cultures lift). Temperament as position -- the stable disposition a persona arrives with and returns to, the resting baseline the room reads when nothing in particular is happening. An anchor plus ten pole positions across the five Big Five axes: extraverted/reserved, reactive/steady, open/grounded, driven/easygoing, warm/forthright. Both poles of each axis adaptive, each with its own standing cost. The deliberate complement to the virtue engine -- virtue fires under pressure, temperament sits beneath it. Grounded in the Five-Factor Model (Costa & McCrae, Goldberg, Eysenck, John & Srivastava). The situational firing, the present feeling, the drive, and the baseline's formation are delegated to the virtue, emotion, motivation, and heritage engines in REFERENCES prose only (the content files are atomic).

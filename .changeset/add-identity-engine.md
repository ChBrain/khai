engine: add the identity engine (new khai-original engine, not a Cultures lift). Identity as position -- the self-story a persona holds about who they are, and how firmly and on what basis they defend it under challenge. An anchor plus four statuses (Marcia): achieved (explored then committed -- held firmly and lightly), foreclosed (committed unexamined -- held rigidly, challenge is threat), moratorium (exploring, uncommitted -- in flux), diffuse (neither -- no firm self, takes the room's shape). The complement to temperament: temperament is how the persona is wired, identity is the story they hold about who that makes them. Grounded in Erikson, Marcia, McAdams, and Tajfel & Turner. The social/group "we", the traits beneath, the values fired, the formation, and the feeling of a threatened self are delegated to the group/heritage, temperament, virtue, and emotion engines in REFERENCES prose only (the content files are atomic).

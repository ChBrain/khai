Name the cast in the Director position (doctrine copy). The position now encodes
the separation of two stances (an immersed cast that embodies the scene, the
producer of the run; the Director outside it, reading and redirecting) as the
discipline that makes behaviour evidence and not the Director's echo. The Director
authors the production (the reading), never the lines; a captured or handed-over
run is the cast's work, selected and sequenced, never written by the Director.

Also adds "tune the pitch" to the redirect idioms: the Director tunes the run to a
dominant key, or re-pitches it (the pitch element type lands in khai-arch).

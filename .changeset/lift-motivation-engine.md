engine: lift the motivation process engine. Motivation as process -- a need or value meets an available act, and where the drive springs from shapes the effort into one of five styles: intrinsic, identified, introjected, external, or amotivated. Grounded in self-determination theory (Deci & Ryan's intrinsic/extrinsic distinction and Organismic Integration continuum, the overjustification effect). The feeling that rides the drive is delegated to the emotion engine; the content of what is wanted, to the persona and the scene.

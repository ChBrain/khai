Add nostalgia engine: a sentimental, bittersweet longing for one's own past, drawn on as a psychological resource across four functions the empirical literature documents and replicates -- continuity (Sedikides, Wildschut, Routledge & Arndt, 2015), connectedness (Wildschut, Sedikides, Arndt & Routledge, 2006), existential (Routledge, Arndt, Sedikides & Wildschut, 2008), and positivity (Wildschut et al., 2006; Sedikides et al., 2008). Delegates the felt emotion to the emotion/mood engines and specific-memory retrieval to the memory engine, and draws the sharp line against the grief engine: nostalgia is bittersweet-but-net-positive longing, not irretrievable-loss mourning.

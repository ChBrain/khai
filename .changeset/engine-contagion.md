Add the contagion engine: emotional contagion as process -- the automatic, largely unconscious mimicry of another's expression, posture, and voice, and the afferent convergence that follows it, so a persona comes to feel what it has just copied. Grounded in Hatfield, Cacioppo & Rapson's _Emotional Contagion_ (1994).

engine: lift the organisational-culture engine from Cultures. Org as process -- how an organisation operates: a stance on two axes (flexibility/stability, internal/external) that sets which of four processes it runs. A process anchor with four paired processes (collaborate, create, compete, control) and four stance positions (clan, adhocracy, market, hierarchy). Grounded in Quinn & Cameron's Competing Values Framework. Rank within the organisation is delegated to the hierarchy engine, the individual's drive to motivation.

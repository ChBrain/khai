engine: lift the hierarchy position engine from Cultures. Hierarchy as position -- where a persona stands right now, the rank the room reads on them in the field they are in. An anchor plus nine expressions: six capital positions (dominant, established, ascending, declining, excluded, misplaced) for what the room reads from the persona's capital, and three title positions (superior, peer, subordinate) for what a structure assigns. Grounded in Bourdieu's capital theory and structural role theory (Linton, Merton, Mintzberg, Weber). Field-relative and held, not run: formation is delegated to the heritage engine, rank-change pressure to the stress engine, the field to the scene.

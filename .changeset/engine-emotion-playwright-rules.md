Add the Playwright wiring guide to the emotion engine. Emotion is a momentary
appraisal (joy/sadness/anger/fear/disgust): an event meets what the persona cares
about and the read becomes a felt push, changing per event. Anchored on
persona/Projection (a felt state, correct), tied to the beat, never a standing
trait, which is mood/temperament. Wired by linking the play to the engine.

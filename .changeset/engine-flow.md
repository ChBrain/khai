Add flow engine: total absorption arising when a clear-goaled, immediately-feedbacked activity holds a balance between challenge and skill -- both pulled high and matched -- until action and awareness merge and the activity becomes worth doing for its own sake. Grounded in Csikszentmihalyi's Flow (1990). The attentional spotlight is delegated to the attention engine, play-framing to play-mode, the pull toward the activity to motivation, and the felt enjoyment to emotion.

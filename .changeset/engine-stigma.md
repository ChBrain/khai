Add stigma engine: a deeply discrediting attribute carried by a persona as a piece, cut into the bearer's stance and the processes of discrediting (the room reading and discounting the mark) and passing (managing a discreditable mark out of view). Grounded in Goffman's Stigma (1963) and Link & Phelan's "Conceptualizing Stigma" (2001).

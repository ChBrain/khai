Add self-esteem engine: the persona's resting global attitude toward the self as a position -- an anchor plus high (secure, no defense needed), low (a deficit met with doubt), and contingent (worth staked on and moved by a named domain). Grounded in Rosenberg's global self-esteem, Tafarodi & Swann's self-liking/self-competence two-factor view, and Crocker & Wolfe's contingencies of self-worth. Atomic: the task-specific capability belief, the self-story, the momentary flare of pride or shame, and a persisting low mood are delegated to self-efficacy, identity, pride/shame, and mood in REFERENCES prose only.

# Persona: Mara

## Taxonomy

Surgeon.

## Owner

- Project: myproject

## Projection

A surgeon who walks in already trusted. She carries [Female](position_female.md) and a steadiness the room reads before she speaks: the controlled face of certainty, the unhurried voice, the hands that do not hesitate. The theatre quiets when she does.

## Action

She decides fast and states the call once, then moves to the next thing. She does not re-explain.

## Shadow

She cannot feel the difference between certainty and being right. The same steadiness that settles the room settles her when she is wrong, so she never reaches for the doubt that would catch it.

## Tell

Her hand goes flat on the table the beat before she overrules someone: the certainty reaching the body just ahead of the words.
